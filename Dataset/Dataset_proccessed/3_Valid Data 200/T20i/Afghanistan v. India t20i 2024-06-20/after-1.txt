India vs Afghanistan Highlights: India defeated Afghanistan by 47 runs in the first Super 8 match in the ongoing ICC Men's T20 World Cup at the Kensington Oval in Barbados on 20 June.
India vs Afghanistan Highlights: India's Jasprit Bumrah, center, celebrates with captain Rohit Sharma after the dismissal of Afghanistan's Hazratullah Zazai, right, during the ICC Men's T20 World Cup cricket match between Afghanistan and India at Kensington Oval in Bridgetown, Barbados, Thursday, June 20, 2024. (AP Photo/Ricardo Mazalan) (AP)

India vs Afghanistan Live Score Updates: Chasing 182 runs, Afghanistan could only manage to score 134 runs after losing all their wickets. India defeated Afghanistan by 47 runs in the first Super 8 match in the ongoing ICC Men's T20 World Cup at the Kensington Oval in Barbados on 20 June.

For Afghanistan, only Azmatullah Omarzai could score 26 runs, while no other batter could cross 20 runs.

For India, Arshdeep Singh and Jasprit Bumrah picked three wickets each, while Kuldeep Yadav took two wickets. Axar Patel and Ravindra Jadeja also clinched one wicket each.

With this win, India maintained their record of remaining unbeaten against Afghanistan in T20 World Cup. Suryakumar Yadav received the player of the match award for his superb innings. 

Earlier, after winning the toss, India decided to bat first. Bating first against Afghanistan at Barbados, India scored 181/8 in 20 overs. Now Afghanistan need to score182 runs to chase.

For India, Suryakumar Yadav hit his second half century of the tournament, scoring 53 off 28 balls, while Hardik Pandya hit 32 off 24 deliveries. Virat Kohli (24) and Rishabh Pant (20) made some important runs.

For Afghanistan, Rashid Khan and Fazalhaq Farooqi took three wickets each. While Naveen-ul-Haq picked 0ne wicket.

India are undefeated in the tournament till now, while Afghanistan have won three out of four Group matches.

India vs Afghanistan Highlights: Head to Head

In T20Is, India have beaten Afghanistan 7 times in 8 meetings without facing a defeat, while one match was called off. In T20 World Cups, India have a 3-0 record against Afghanistan.
