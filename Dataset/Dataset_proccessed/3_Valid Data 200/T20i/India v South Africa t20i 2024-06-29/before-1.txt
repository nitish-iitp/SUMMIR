IND vs SA in 2024 T20 World Cup Final: India set up date with South Africa in ICC competition finale

India qualify for the T20 World Cup 2024 final after beating England by 69 runs in the second semi-final in Guyana on Thursday.

The Men in Blue will now take on South Africa in the grand finale at the Bridgetown in Barbados on Saturday. Earlier on Thursday, the Proteas had defeated Afghanistan in the first semi-final.

South Africa defeated Afghanistan in the first semi-final of the T20 World Cup 2024. The Proteas qualified for their first-ever World Cup Final. 

India, on the other hand, beat defending champions England at the Providence Stadium in Guyana. The Rohit Sharma-led will face the Proteas in the 'battle of the unbeaten teams' at the T20 World Cup 2024 Final.

Which teams will play the Final of T20 World Cup?

India will lock horns against South Africa in the T20 World Cup 2024 final in Barbados on Saturday. India won the inaugural title in 2007, while South Africa have reached a World Cup final for the first time in history.

When is the Final of T20 World Cup 2024?

The T20 World Cup  2024 final will take place on Saturday, June 29 2024.

Where is the Final of T20 World Cup 2024?

The grand final of the T20 World Cup 2024 will be played at the Kensington Oval in Bridgetown, Barbados.
