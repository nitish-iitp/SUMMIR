Dream 11 Team Prediction
Wicketkeeper

Jos Buttler (England – c & wk): Buttler is not only England’s captain but also their wicketkeeper and a powerhouse with the bat. Expect him to score big points both behind the stumps and with the bat.

Batsmen

Jonny Bairstow (England): Bairstow’s aggressive batting style makes him a dangerous player, capable of scoring runs quickly and providing crucial breakthroughs for your Dream 11 team.

Aqib Ilyas (Oman): As Oman’s captain, Ilyas leads by example with the bat. He’s a dependable batsman who can anchor the innings and accumulate valuable runs.

Harry Brook (England): Brook has shown promise as a young batsman, capable of playing match-winning innings. He’s a smart pick for your Dream 11 team.

All-rounders

Moeen Ali (England): Moeen Ali’s dual ability to contribute with both bat and ball makes him an invaluable asset. Expect him to rack up points with his all-round performance.

Zeeshan Maqsood (Oman): Maqsood is a key all-rounder for Oman, contributing with both bat and ball. He can provide crucial points in both departments.

Mark Wood (England): Wood’s raw pace and ability to pick up wickets make him a valuable asset for England. Expect him to trouble the opposition batsmen and earn you valuable points.

Bilal Khan (Oman): Bilal Khan is Oman’s premier fast bowler, known for his ability to strike early and break partnerships. He’s a smart choice to bolster your Dream 11 bowling attack.
