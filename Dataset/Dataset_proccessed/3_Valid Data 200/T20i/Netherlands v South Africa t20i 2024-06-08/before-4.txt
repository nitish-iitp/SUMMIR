When do the Proteas play again at T20 World Cup 2024

The Proteas face bogey team the Netherlands in their next match at the T20 World Cup on Saturday at New York’s Nassau Stadium.

By James Richardson
05-06-24 09:05
in Cricket

The ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2024 in USA and West Indies got underway on 1 June.

A total of three venues in the USA and six in the Caribbean will be used for the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2024 after the groups and fixtures for the ninth edition of the 20-over showcase were confirmed.

Ten of the 20 teams will play their first match of the tournament in the USA. There will be 16 contests held in Lauderhill, Dallas and New York and the blockbuster clash between India and Pakistan is scheduled for the new Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in Long Island on 9 June.
