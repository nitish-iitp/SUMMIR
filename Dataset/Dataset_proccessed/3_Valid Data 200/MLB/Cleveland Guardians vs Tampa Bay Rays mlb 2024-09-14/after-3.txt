Cleveland Guardians vs. Tampa Bay Rays, Sept. 15, 2024
By Paul Hoynes, cleveland.com

CLEVELAND, Ohio — On a perfect Saturday night for a baseball game, the Guardians played one of their better games of the season.

The Guardians, after losing the first two games of this four-game series to the Rays, seemed to take their time to enjoy their 6-1 win in front of over 30,000 fans at Progressive Field.
