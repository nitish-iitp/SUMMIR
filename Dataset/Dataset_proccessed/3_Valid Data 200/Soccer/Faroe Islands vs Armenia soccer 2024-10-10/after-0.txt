Eduard Spertsyan to miss Faroe Islands-Armenia match

Midfielder Eduard Spertsyan will miss the UEFA Nations League match between Armenia and Faroe Islands due to a slight injury, the Football Federation of Armenia reported.

This will be the first meeting between the two nations.
