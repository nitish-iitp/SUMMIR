Sri Lanka vs India (SL vs IND) 3rd ODI Match Preview:

It’s time for the third and the decider one. After two thrilling matches in the ODI series, cricket fans across the globe are waiting for the final result of the ODI series. After the first match ended in a tie, Sri Lanka bounced back in the second match. From batting to bowling, they nailed the game in every way. On the flip side, despite having a great start, Team India lost their flow. As a result of that, they faced a 32-run defeat. The excitement mounts over the third match as it is going to decide the future of the series. Currently, Sri Lanka is leading the series. In the last match, India will conquer hard to level the series. Fans are waiting to watch who will win the series. 

In the 2nd ODI of the India tour of Sri Lanka, Sri Lanka set a target of 241 runs, finishing at 240/9 in 50 overs. India, however, fell short and was all out for 208 in 42.2 overs, losing by 32 runs. During Sri Lanka’s innings, Washington Sundar was effective with 3 wickets for 30 runs in 10 overs. Kuldeep Yadav took 2 wickets for 33 runs in 10 overs, and Axar Patel added 1 wicket for 38 runs in 9 overs. In India’s chase, Rohit Sharma scored 64 off 44 balls, Axar Patel contributed 44 off 44 balls, and Shubman Gill made 35 off 44 balls, but their efforts were not enough for a win.

In the last match, Sri Lanka batted first and scored 240/9 in 50 overs. Kamindu Mendis and Avishka Fernando each made 40 runs, and Dunith Wellalage added 39 runs. In the second innings, India’s chase was cut short as they were all out for 208 in 42.2 overs. Jeffrey Vandersay was outstanding with 6 wickets for 33 runs in 10 overs, and Charith Asalanka, the captain, took 3 wickets for 20 runs in 6.2 overs. Kamindu Mendis also bowled 3 overs, giving away 19 runs without taking a wicket.
