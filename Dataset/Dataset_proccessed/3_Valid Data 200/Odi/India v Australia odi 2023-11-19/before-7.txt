IND vs AUS | The pitch has been used before, but it looked pretty firm, says Cummins
A black soil track, rolled heavily to ensure slowness, is on offer. Batting will be difficult under the lights if opposition has two quality spinners operating in the middle overs.

Updated - November 19, 2023 07:37 am IST - Ahmedabad

SHAYAN ACHARYA
READ LATER
PRINT

Australia captain Pat Cummins inspects the pitch and clicks photos ahead of the ICC Cricket World Cup 2023 final against India in Ahmedabad on November 18, 2023. | Photo Credit: Robert Cianflone

Australia captain Pat Cummins confirmed that Sunday’s World Cup final will be played on a ‘used surface’.

As reported by Sportstar earlier, the black soil pitch that was used for India’s game against Pakistan last month will be persisted with, and addressing the media on the eve of the marquee clash, Cummins referred to it as a ‘pretty good wicket’.
