India vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI, Highlights: Match ends in tie, India ALL OUT; fail to score 1 run
32 min read . Updated: 02 Aug 2024, 10:17 PM IST
Aman Gupta, Sounak Mukhopadhyay, Saurav Mukherjee
India vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI, Highlights: Chasing a target of 231 runs in the first ODI at Colombo, India managed to tied to level the score Sri Lanka on 2 August, but lost all the wickets. No Super Over today.
India vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI, Highlights: India's captain Rohit Sharma plays a shot as Sri Lanka's Kusal Mendis watches during the first ODI cricket match between Sri Lanka and India in Colombo, Sri Lanka, Friday, Aug. 2, 2024. (AP Photo/Eranga Jayawardena) (AP)

India vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI, Highlights: Chasing a target of 231 runs in the first ODI at Colombo, India managed to tied to level the score Sri Lanka on 2 August, but lost all the wickets. No Super Over today.

For India, Rohit Sharma scored 58 off 47 balls, while Axar Patel (33) an KL Rahul (31) scored some valuable runs. Apart from them, all other players seen struggling ahead of Sri Lankan bowlers.

For Sri Lanka, Wanindu Hasaranga and Charith Asalanka took three wickets each. Dunith Wellalage took two wickets, while Asitha Fernando and Akila Dananjaya clinched one wicket each.

The second ODI will take place on Sunday from the same stadium in Colombo.

After winning toss at the first ODI in Colombo on Friday, Sri Lanka posted a total of 231 for India to chase. Sri Lanka scored 230 off 8 wickets.

For Sri Lanka, Dunith Wellalage scored an unbeaten 67 off 65, while Prathum Nissanka scored 56 of 75 balls. Other batters could not score more than 25 runs.

While for India, Asrshdeep Singh and Axar Patel took two wickets each. Mohammed Siraj, Shivam Dube, Kuldeep Yadav and Washington Sundar picked one wickeyt each.

Sri Lanka skipper Charith Asalanka won the toss and opted to bat first at the R Premadasa stadium in Colombo. Indian skipper Rohit Sharma said that it looked like a good surface and he was fine with batting second here. 

Notably, the Men in Blue had completely dominated the T20I leg of the series, winning all the 3 matches. However, the hosts would be looking to make a comeback during upcoming matches. 

India and Sri Lanka squads:

Sri Lanka: Pathum Nissanka, Avishka Fernando, Kusal Mendis(w), Sadeera Samarawickrama, Charith Asalanka (c), Janith Liyanage, Wanindu Hasaranga, Dunith Wellalage, Akila Dananjaya, Asitha Fernando, Mohamed Shiraz

India: Rohit Sharma(c), Shubman Gill, Virat Kohli, Shreyas Iyer, KL Rahul(w), Shivam Dube, Washington Sundar, Axar Patel, Kuldeep Yadav, Arshdeep Singh, Mohammed Siraj
