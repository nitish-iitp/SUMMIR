Pittsburgh Pirates Betting Preview

Over the weekend the Pirates played the Kansas City Royals. Pittsburgh lost the first two games in that set before a 4-3 win Sunday. After losses 4-0 and 3-1 to the Cardinals, on Wednesday Pittsburgh fell again 10-5.

In the starter slot for the finale, the Pirates are going with Luis Ortiz. This year Ortiz is 6-6 with a 3.45 ERA in 35 games (13 starts). In his career, Ortiz is 11-13 with a 4.03 ERA in 57 games (32 starts). the Pirates will face the Reds following this series.

St. Louis Cardinals Betting Preview

Over on the Cardinals’ side, they played the Toronto Blue Jays in their last set. St. Louis lost all three of those games 4-3, 7-2, and 3-2. On Wednesday the Cards posted 14 hits and six runs in the seventh inning alone.

Erick Fedde will start for the Cardinals on Thursday. This season Fedde is 8-9 with a 3.45 ERA in 29 starts. Fedde is 29-42 with a 4.89 ERA in 131 career games (117 starts).

Pittsburgh Pirates vs St. Louis Cardinals Prediction

I’ll lean toward the Cardinals. Probably not touching this one though. Neither starter has been that great lately. Fedde is 0-2 in his last three with eight earned runs in 15.0 innings total. As for Ortiz, he’s 0-2 over his last pair with seven earned in 9.2 innings on 11 hits and four walks.

On Wednesday the Cardinals blew an early 4-0 lead but were able to come back with a wild seventh inning. St. Louis needs much more consistency on Thursday.
