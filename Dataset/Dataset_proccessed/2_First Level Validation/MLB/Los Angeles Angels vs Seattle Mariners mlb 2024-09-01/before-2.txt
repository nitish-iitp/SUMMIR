The San Francisco Giants have amassed 3,553 putouts on the campaign, in addition to 1,081 assists and 73 errors. The Mariners have converted 71.8% of balls hit into play into outs in their 10,659 innings on the field, which has them sitting 1st in pro baseball.

Miller has taken the hill for 282 innings and has totaled 262 strikeouts so far in his MLB career. Miller (18-14 mark in his career) sits with a FIP of 3.68 while he has faced 1,127 opposing hitters in the majors. His ERA is 3.73 (117 ER's allowed) and his career WHIP is 1.057. Los Angeles has accumulated 130 home runs for the year as well as 488 RBIs. They have put up 197 two-baggers, while walking 396 times and scoring 522 runs. The Los Angeles Angels have compiled an OBP of .303 in addition to a team batting average of .231 on the season. The Angels hold a slugging % of .370 and they earn 3.90 runs per game (27th in MLB). They have been rung up on 1,149 occasions (13th in MLB) and have notched a total of 1,022 hits.

The Angels have a WHIP of 1.321 and are the owners of a FIP of 4.58 as a unit on the year. They come in at 11th in the league as a pitching staff in total hits relinquished with 1,075. The Los Angeles pitching staff have conceded 647 runs for the year while having a team ERA of 4.55 (598 earned runs relinquished). They currently have a K/BB ratio of 7.90 (1,038 strikeouts vs 488 walks). They have conceded 160 homers and they relinquish 4.92 runs per 9 innings (27th in the league).

With 108 save situations, the Angels have 59 holds in addition to 15 blown saves. Los Angeles has had relievers enter the contest in 47 save chances and they have come away with 32 saves. Their relievers have come in 121 times in high leverage situations in addition to 112 occasions with runners on. Los Angeles bullpen pitchers hold an inherited score percentage of 34.3% of 178 inherited base runners. They sit at 5th in the league holding a save percentage of 68.1%, and they have dispatched 422 relievers to the hill on the year.

In their 10,653 innings played, the Angels hold a efficiency on defense of 70.9% (4th in baseball). The Los Angeles Angels have turned 119 double plays and own a fielding percentage of .985 (17th in the majors). The Angels have 1,187 assists, 72 errors and have accumulated 3,551 putouts for the season.

So far in his MLB career, Fulmer has surrendered 199 base knocks while accumulating 203 strikeouts in 221 innings. Fulmer (7-15 record in his career) is the owner of a 5.42 earned run average while giving up 8.1 hits per 9 innings. He has given up a total of 133 earned runs while holding a WHIP of 1.457 and being the owner of a FIP of 5.3.
