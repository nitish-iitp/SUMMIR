Toronto Raptors (2-7, 13th in the Eastern Conference) vs. Los Angeles Clippers (5-4, ninth in the Western Conference)

Inglewood, California; Saturday, 10:30 p.m. EST

BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: Clippers -7.5; over/under is 227

BOTTOM LINE: Los Angeles comes into a matchup against Toronto as winners of three games in a row.

Los Angeles went 51-31 overall last season while going 25-16 at home. The Clippers averaged 16.2 points off of turnovers, 12.6 second-chance points and 36.3 bench points last season.

Toronto went 25-57 overall with an 11-30 record on the road last season. The Raptors averaged 112.4 points per game last season, 16.3 from the free-throw line and 34.5 from deep.

INJURIES: Clippers: Mo Bamba: day to day (knee), P.J. Tucker: day to day (personal), Kawhi Leonard: out (knee).

Raptors: Garrett Temple: day to day (back), Kelly Olynyk: day to day (back), Bruce Brown: out (knee), Immanuel Quickley: day to day (pelvis), Ja'Kobe Walter: out (shoulder), Scottie Barnes: out (eye).
