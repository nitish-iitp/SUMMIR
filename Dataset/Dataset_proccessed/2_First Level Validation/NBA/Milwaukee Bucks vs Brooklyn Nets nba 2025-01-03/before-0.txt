BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: Bucks -14; over/under is 218.5

BOTTOM LINE: Brooklyn comes into the matchup against Milwaukee as losers of three games in a row.

The Bucks are 15-13 against Eastern Conference opponents. Milwaukee is third in the NBA with 35.1 defensive rebounds per game led by Giannis Antetokounmpo averaging 9.6.

The Nets are 6-17 against Eastern Conference opponents. Brooklyn is 8-14 against opponents with a winning record.

The Bucks are shooting 47.8% from the field this season, 0.9 percentage points lower than the 48.7% the Nets allow to opponents. The Nets average 14.6 made 3-pointers per game this season, 0.7 more makes per game than the Bucks allow.

TOP PERFORMERS: Antetokounmpo is averaging 32.6 points, 11.6 rebounds and six assists for the Bucks.

D'Angelo Russell is averaging 12.7 points and 4.8 assists for the Nets.
