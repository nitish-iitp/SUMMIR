TORONTO — Giannis Antetokounmpo had 11 points, 13 assists and 12 rebounds for his fourth triple- double of the season, Damian Lillard scored 25 points and the Milwaukee Bucks beat the struggling Toronto Raptors 128-104 on Monday night.

Antetokounmpo needed three stitches to close a cut near the base of his right pinkie, but returned to finish his third career triple-double against the Raptors and fourth triple-double of the season.
