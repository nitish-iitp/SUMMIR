LIVE Pakistan vs Netherlands, ICC Cricket World Cup 2023 Score Highlights: Pakistan defeats Netherlands by 81 runs
1 min read . Updated: 06 Oct 2023, 10:49 PM IST
Livemint
Pakistan vs Netherlands ICC Cricket World Cup 2023 Score Highlights: Haris Rauf and Hasan Ali led Pakistan's pace attack to stop Netherlands at the score 205 and the Babar Azam-led squad defeated Netherlands by 81 runs
Pakistan vs Netherlands, ICC Cricket World Cup 2023 Score Updates: Pakistan players celebrate the wicket of Netherlands' Colin Ackermann during the ICC Men's Cricket World Cup match between Pakistan and Netherlands (AP)

Pakistan vs Netherlands, ICC Cricket World Cup 2023 Score Highlights: Haris Rauf and Hasan Ali led Pakistan's pace attack and Netherlands are all-out at the score of 205. Bas de Leede scored the highest 67 runs while Vikramjit Singh smashed 52 runs for Netherlands. Pakistan defeats Netherlands by 81 runs. 

Netherlands openers started steady against dangerous Pakistan pacers but Hasan Ali took the first wicket as he dismissed Max O'Dowd. Colin Ackermann was looking a great posture, but soon he was also dismissed by spinner Iftikhar Ahmed. 

Bas de Leede bowled a wonderful spell to restrict Pakistan innings to 286 runs. The Netherlands pacers dominated in initial 10 overs as they clinched 3 early wickets, but then Mohammad Rizwan and Saud Shakeel played in a wonderful partnership to pull back Pakistan from a low-scoring end. 

Aryan Dutt provided the team a breakthrough as he dismissed Saud Shakeel. Bas de Leede took from there and dismissed Mohammad Rizwan, Iftikhar Ahmed, Shadab Khan and Hasan Ali to derail the Pakistan batting. 

Netherlands dominated the first 10 overs of the game as they successfully dismissed three Pakistan batters including skipper Babar Azam. But, then Mohammad Rizwan and Saud Shakeel came to stabilise the innings are led the comeback of the team.
