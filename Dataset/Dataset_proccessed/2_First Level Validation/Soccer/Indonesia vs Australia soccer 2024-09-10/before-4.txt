How To Watch: Indonesia v Australia | FIFA World Cup 2026™ Third Round Qualifier
Socceroos.com.au \\ Photo Credit: Albert Perez/Getty Images
Updated: 09:02am Sep 6, 2024
The Subway Socceroos face Indonesia in their second FIFA World Cup 2026™ Third Round Asian Qualifier at Gelora Bung Karno Stadium in Jakarta on Tuesday, 10 September at 10:00pm AEST.

The match will be the second meeting between the two sides this year after the pair crossed paths at the AFC Asian Cup™ in January.

Australia will be determined to bounce back from a frustrating 1-0 loss to Bahrain and get their campaign on track against Team Garuda, who played out a 1-1 draw away to Saudi Arabia.

Here is everything you need to know to catch the action.

How To Watch

The match against Indonesia will be streamed live and exclusive on Paramount+ from 9:30pm AEST.

International fans will need to check their local guides to watch the match.

Match Details

Indonesia v Subway Socceroos
Tuesday 10 September 2024 
Gelora Bung Karno Stadium, Jakarta
Kick-off: 7.00pm local / 10.00pm AEST
Broadcast: Paramount+
