The semi-finals of the biggest tournament in Africa, AFCON 2023, are slowly taking shape, and here are key details such as countries that have qualified, fixtures to be played, where the games will be played, and so on.

Date and Time of Kick off for AFCON Semi-Finals fixtures

The semi-finals of the Africa Cup of Nations 2023 (AFCON) will commence on Wednesday, February 7, 2024, at 6 pm West Africa Time.
