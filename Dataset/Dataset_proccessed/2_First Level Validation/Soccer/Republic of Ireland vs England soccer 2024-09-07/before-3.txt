What is England's record vs Ireland? Three Lions head-to-head stats against the Boys in Green
England
Ireland

GOAL brings to you the all time head to head stats between England and Republic of Ireland.

The football rivalry between England and the Republic of Ireland is one of the most intriguing and historic in international football.

Spanning over seven decades, this rivalry has seen numerous memorable matches, moments of controversy, and a passionate fanbase on both sides.

Here, GOAL takes a deep dive into the head-to-head record and previous meetings between the two sides.

Article continues below
Getty Images Sport
What is England's overall record vs Ireland?

The first official encounter between England and the Republic of Ireland took place on September 30, 1946, at Dalymount Park in Dublin. England emerged victorious with a 1-0 scoreline, setting the stage for a rivalry that would evolve over the years.

The second match, held in 1949, marked a significant milestone as Ireland became the first non-British team to defeat England on their home soil, winning 2-0 at Goodison Park, Liverpool.

As of now, England and the Republic of Ireland have faced each other a total of 17 times in international football. The overall record stands as follows:

When was the last meeting between England and Ireland?

England's 3-0 victory over the Republic of Ireland in a 2020 friendly match marked the end of a six-game draw streak between the two teams, which had lasted since Ireland's 1-0 triumph at the 1988 European Championship. Jadon Sancho, Harry Maguire and Dominic Calvert-Lewin got onto the scoresheet that day.

What was England's biggest win against Ireland?

England's biggest win against the Republic of Ireland in football occurred on May 8, 1957, when they defeated Ireland 5-1 during the 1958 FIFA World Cup qualification match. Tommy Taylor bagged a well-taken hat-trick, while John Atyeo came away with a brace. Ireland's Dermot Curtis scored the consolation for the Boys in Green that day.

What was England's biggest defeat against Ireland?

Ireland's last triumph over England dates back to Euro 1988. England's biggest defeat against the Republic of Ireland in football occurred on September 21, 1949, when they lost 0-2 in a friendly match at Goodison Park, Liverpool.
