Both Vietnam and Indonesia will eye a comeback in their respective AFC Asian Cup 2023 campaigns.

As the AFC Asian Cup 2023 is underway, Group D is gearing up for the second round of fixtures where Vietnam are going to play against Indonesia on 19th January at Abdullah Bin Khalifa Stadium, Doha. With both teams losing their first match, either side will look to salvage a comeback, making the contest even more exciting. The fans of both teams are very eager to see how Vietnam and Indonesia perform.

Kick-Off:

Friday, 19 January 2024 at 14:30 GMT

Location: Abdullah Bin Khalifa Stadium (Doha)

Indonesia (in all competitions): D-L-L-L-L
