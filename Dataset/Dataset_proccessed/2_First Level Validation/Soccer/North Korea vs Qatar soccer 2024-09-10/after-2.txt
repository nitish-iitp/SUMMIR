Qatar and North Korea draw in the second Group A match of 2026 FIFA World Cup Asian Qualifiers

Qatar met North Korea in their second Group A match of the 2026 FIFA World Cup Asian Qualifiers at New Laos National Stadium on 10 September 2024.

Ri Il-Song scored the first goal for North Korea in the 19th minute. In the 31st minute, Akram Afif scored a penalty for Qatar. After which, Almoez Ali turned the game around in the 44th minute with a right-footed shot from outside the area.

At halftime, Qatar led the scoreboard by 2-1. The game was halted due to rain, but once it resumed, Kuk-Chol equalized the game for North Korea. The match ended in a draw with a score of 2-2. Qatar will now play against Kyrgyzstan on 10 October 2024.
