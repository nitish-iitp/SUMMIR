Showmax is your front-row seat to heart-stopping EURO action
Football05 June 2024 19:55| © SuperSport
Share
Cristiano Ronaldo of Portugal, Bruno Fernandes celebrating © Getty Images

 As the excitement builds for the 2024 Uefa EUROs, catch all the thrills and spills with some exhilarating EURO friendlies live on Showmax between 5 June and 11 June, available to all Showmax PL subscribers at no additional cost.

The EURO friendlies that will be live streaming on Showmax in the next few days ahead of the tournament, which kicks off on 14 June 2024, include Belgium v Montenegro, Netherlands v Canada, England v Iceland, Portugal v Croatia, Spain v Northern Ireland, France v Canada, Netherlands v Iceland, and Portugal v Republic of Ireland.

Aside from the friendlies on offer on Showmax, there will also be plenty of other EURO content to keep fans entertained ahead of the spectacle.

Dive deep into the teams competing at this year’s EUROs, as Showmax previews all the nations, players and the host nation as Germany prepares for football’s biggest event of 2024.

There are also 11 episodes of the Uefa Preview Magazine, a show that looks into the tournament ahead, available to stream on Showmax.

BUT WAIT, THAT’S NOT ALL…

Showmax PL also offers a series of high-end documentaries that offer viewers a unique and enthralling insight into one of football’s most revered competitions: the Uefa EURO.

There’s also Premier League World Beaters, a series that looks into Premier League players who will be competing at the EUROs.

The special features players such as Liverpool’s Virgil Van Dijk and Dominik Szoboszslai, Man City’s Phil Foden, Kevin de Bruyne, and Rodri, Arsenal’s Bukayo Saka, William Saliba and Kai Havertz, Manchester United’s Rasmus Hojlund and Bruno Fernandes, and many others.
