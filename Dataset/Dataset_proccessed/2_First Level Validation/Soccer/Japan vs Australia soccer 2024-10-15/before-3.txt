How To Watch: Japan vs Australia | FIFA World Cup 2026™ Third Round Qualifier
Socceroos.com.au \\ Photo Credit: Aleksandar Jason/Socceroos
Updated: 02:37am Oct 15, 2024
The Subway Socceroos face Japan at Saitama Stadium 2002 on Tuesday, 15 October in its second FIFA World Cup 2026™ Third Round Asian Qualifier this month.

It will be Tony Popovic's second game in charge since becoming the new head coach of the Subway Socceroos.

The match will also be the 28th encounter in 'A' internationals between the two nations and first since March 2022.
