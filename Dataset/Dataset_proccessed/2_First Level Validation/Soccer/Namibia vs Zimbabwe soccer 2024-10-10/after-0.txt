Warriors Defeat Namibia 1-0 in Afcon Qualifier match
By Anna -
October 10, 2024

Zimbabwe Warriors defeated Namibia 1-0 in the 2025 Afcon Qualifier match held today at Orlando stadium in South Africa.

This marks thr Warriors first win of after opening with two consecutive goalless draws last month.

Meanwhile, Zimbabwe will host Namibia at the same venue on Monday.
