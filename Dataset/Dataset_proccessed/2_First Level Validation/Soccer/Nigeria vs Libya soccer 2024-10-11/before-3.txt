AFCON Qualifiers: Super Eagles of Nigeria players in camp as of now revealed
SHARE

Here are all the players currently in camp for the Super Eagles of Nigeria ahead of their must-win AFCON 2025 qualifiers for October 2024.

As the AFCON 2025 qualifiers for Nigeria for October 2024 draws closer, the list of 20 players who have arrived in camp ahead of the fixture have been revealed by the official account of the Super Eagles on social media.

With the first game for the Super Eagles which would be against Libya scheduled for Friday, October 11, 2024, at the Godswill Akpabio Stadium in Uyo and the second fixture which would be the return leg scheduled for October 15, Austin Eguaveon will be looking to secure all six points against a familiar foe and also place Nigeria in a good spot ahead of the tournament.
