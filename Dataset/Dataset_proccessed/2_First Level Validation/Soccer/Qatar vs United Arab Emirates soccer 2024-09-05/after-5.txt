UAE wins against Qatar in the third round of 2026 FIFA World Cup Asian Qualifiers

Qatar played against UAE for the third round of the 2026 FIFA World Cup Asian Qualifiers at the Ahmad Bin Ali Stadium in Doha on 5 September 2024. The match kicked off at 7 pm.

Qatar's Ibrahim Mohammad Al Hassan Ibrahim Mohammad Ali scored the match's first goal in the 38th minute with an assist from Akram Afif. Qatar took the lead with a 1-0.

In the match's second half, UAE's Harib Abdalla scored a goal in the 68th minute, assisted by Yahia Nader, equalizing the score to 1-1.

In the 80th minute, UAE's Khaled Ibrahim scored another goal for the team, taking the lead to 1-2.

Shortly into extra time, UAE's Ali Saleh scored the match's last goal in the 90+4th minute, securing the team's win at 1-3 by full-time.
