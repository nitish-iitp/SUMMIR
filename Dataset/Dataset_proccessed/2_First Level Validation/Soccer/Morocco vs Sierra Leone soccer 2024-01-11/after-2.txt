2023 AFCON Preparation: Morocco Beat Sierra Leone (3-1)

The Atlas Lions’ goals were scored by Youssef En-Nesyri (30th, 55th), and Sofiane Boufal (35th).

This match is part of the national team’s preparations for the 2023 AFCON, scheduled from January 13 to February 11 in Côte d’Ivoire.

The national team, which kicks off its CAN campaign against Tanzania on January 17, is drawn in Group F, which also includes DR Congo and Zambia.

 

 

Moroccan national football team beat its Sierra Leonean counterpart 3-1 in a match to prepare for the Africa Cup of Nations (2023 AFCON), played on Thursday in San Pedro, Côte d'Ivoire.
11 January 2024
San Pedro (Côte d'Ivoire)
