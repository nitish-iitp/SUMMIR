Asian Cup. The referees of the Uzbekistan-Syria match are known
11 январь, 2024, 16:05
0
Футбол

The officials who will work in the first round of the Asian Cup, which will start tomorrow, January 12, on the green fields of Qatar, have been announced. According to him, the match of the national team of Uzbekistan against Syria will be led by Omani Ahmad Abu Bakar Said al-Kaf as the chief referee. He is assisted by his compatriots Abu Bakar Salim al-Amri and Rashid Hamid al-Haiti, and Mohanad Qasim Sarray from Iraq works as a reserve judge.

For information, Ahmed al-Kaf worked in 11 matches this season. Among them, there are 6 OCL matches, 3 matches of the Oman Championship and 1 World Cup-2026 qualifying match, as well as 1 friendly match. During these matches, he took out a total of 24 yellow cards and awarded 1 penalty.
