Both Uzbekistan and Syria are strong contenders to secure qualification into the AFC Asian Cup knockouts.

Uzbekistan and Syria will meet on 13 January 2024 at the Jassim Bin Hamad Stadium in Doha, Qatar for their opening 2023 AFC Asian Cup fixture. Both countries will back themselves to join tournament favourites Australia in the knockout stages after securing one of the top two spots in Group B which also includes India.Â 

Uzbekistan and Syria will likely be direct competitors for the second and third spot in the group considering the quality of both their national teams. As a result, securing three points in the opening game against direct competitors goes a long way. Winning the game will hand a huge advantage to either team as they are expected to beat India unless the Blue Tigers cause an upset.Â
