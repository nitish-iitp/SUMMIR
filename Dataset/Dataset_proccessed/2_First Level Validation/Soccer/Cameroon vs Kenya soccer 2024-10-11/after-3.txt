Cameroon-Kenya : Decisive Clash
By Elizabeth MOSIMA 11 Oct. 2024 11:02 0 Likes 
  

The encounter is a crucial one for the Indomitable Lions who can secure a spot for the 2025 Africa Cup of Nations in case of victory.

The Indomitable Lions of Cameroon will take on the Harambee Stars of Kenya today October 11, 2024 at the Yaounde Ahmadou Ahidjo Stadium at 5:00 p.m. It will be a day three qualifier for the 2025 Africa Cup of Nations in Group J before travelling to play the return leg in Kampala on October 14, 2024 in a day four duel. Today’s encounter between the Indomitable Lions and the Harambee Stars is a crucial one for the Lions. 
The two sides will play two matches over three days which always go ahead to shape the group’s final standing. Cameroon needs to get at least four points to be sure of a qualification ticket. The two countries are leading the group with Kenya having an upper hand for scoring two goals as compared to Cameroon that scored just one. However, they are tied on four points and a plus one goal difference. A victory for Cameroon today will increase the chances of qualification for the Indomitable Lions. 
Ahead of the encounter, the coach Marc Brys had published a list of 24 players to defend the colours of Cameroon in the competition. All the players called to camp trained intensively at the Ngoa-Ekelle Stadium in Yaounde. From all indications, the choice of Coach Marc Brys is promising given the caliber of the players.
