Al-Nashama to face S. Korea Thursday in key World Cup Qualifier
 

09-10-2024 10:08 AM



Ammon News -
The national football team (Al-Nashama) is set to play a crucial match against South Korea on Thursday, at 5 p.m. at the Amman International Stadium. This fixture is part of the third and decisive round of qualifiers for the 2026 FIFA World Cup.

The team is completing its final preparations on Wednesday, with a win potentially solidifying its position at the top of Group B. Jordan shares the group lead with South Korea and Iraq, each holding 4 points. Kuwait follows with 2 points, Palestine with 1, while Oman has yet to secure any points.

The squad for this phase includes 28 players: Yazid Abu Layla, Nour Bani Attia, Abdullah Al-Fakhouri, Yazan Al-Arab, Abdullah Nasib, Saad Al-Rousan, Mohammad Abu Al-Nadi, Hijazi Maher, Hossam Abu Thahab, Ihsan Haddad, Yousef Abu Al-Jazr, Amer Jamous, Mohammad Abu Hashish, Raja’i Ayed, Nour Al-Rawabdeh, Ibrahim Saadeh, Nizar Al-Rashdan, Mohammad Abu Zrayq, Mahmoud Mardhi, Muhannad Samreen, Muhannad Abu Taha, Aref Al-Haj, Mousa Al-Tamari, Ahmad Ersan, Ali Alwan, Abdullah Al-Attar, Ibrahim Sabra, and Yazan Al-Naimat.

Following this fixture, Jordan will meet Oman next Tuesday at 7 p.m. on home turf, again at the Amman International Stadium. Petra
