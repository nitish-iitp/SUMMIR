Leone Stars Releases Line Up Against Djibouti

The Leone Stars of Sierra Leone are set to take on Djibouti today 5th June 2024 Burkina Faso on June 10, 2024.

These matches hold immense significance for Leone Stars as they strive to secure qualification for the 2026 World Cup, which will be jointly hosted by the USA, Canada, and Mexico.

The match will take place in El Jadida, Morocco, kicking off at 4 PM Sierra Leone time and 5 PM Moroccan time.
