World Cup Qualifiers: Players in camp ahead of must win fixtures for Nigeria revealed
SHARE

Over 17 players have arrived in camp for the Super Eagles of Nigeria ahead of the World Cup 2026 qualifiers which will kick off on Friday.

The build-up to the must-win qualifiers for the World Cup 2026 qualifiers which would commence on Friday, June 7, 2024, is reaching a new peak as over 17 players out of the Squad confimred by Nigeria for the fixtures have been confirmed to have arrived in camp ahead of the game.

On Friday, 7 June, at 8:00 p.m., the Nigerian national football team will compete against South Africa at the Godswill Akpabio Stadium in Uyo. Then, on Monday, 10 June, at 4:00 p.m. Ivorian time (5:00 p.m. Nigerian time), they will travel to Abidjan to take on Benin Republic at the Stade Felix Houphouet-Boigny.

Who will play for the Super Eagles in the Nigeria vs South Africa world cup qualifiers?
Wilfred Ndidi

Following the initial confirmation of a squad list by the new head coach Finidi George, here are the players currently in camp for the Super Eagles of Nigeria:

Semi Ajayi, Calvin Bassey, Bright Osayi-Samuel, Frank Onyeka, Paul Onuachu, Wilfred Ndidi, Kelechi Iheanacho, Nwabali Stanley, Benjamin Tanimu, Ibrahim Olawoyin, Samuel Chukwueze, Fisayo Bashiru, Victor Boniface, Raphael Onyedika, Chidozie Awaziem, Alex Iwobi, Ademola Lookman, Alhassan Yusuf, Maduka Okoye and Terem Moffi.
