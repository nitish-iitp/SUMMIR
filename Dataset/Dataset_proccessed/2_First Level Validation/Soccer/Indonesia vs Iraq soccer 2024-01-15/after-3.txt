Iraq beats Indonesia in Group D match of AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™

The second AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™ match held on 15 January 2024 was between Indonesia and Iraq at the Ahmad Bin Ali Stadium. The match kicked off at 5:30 pm.

Iraq's Mohanad Ali scored the match's first goal in the 17th minute, taking the lead to 1-0.

Marselino Ferdinan from Indonesia equalized the score with a goal in the 37th minute with an assist from Yakob Sayuri. This took the score to 1-1.


Osama Rashid secures the lead for Iraq with a goal in the 52nd minute, taking the score to 2-1 before the end of the first half.

In the 75th minute, Iraq's Aymen Hussein scored another goal, furthering their lead and securing the win with a score of 3-1.
