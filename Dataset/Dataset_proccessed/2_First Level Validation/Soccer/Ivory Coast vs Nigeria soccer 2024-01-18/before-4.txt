Ayoola Kelechi • 20:00 - 16.01.2024
The Super Eagles of Nigeria face their toughest test yet at the 2023 AFCON as they take on the hosts, Ivory Coast

The upcoming Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) match between Ivory Coast and Nigeria is scheduled for January 18, 2024, at 6 p.m. WAT.

Ivory Coast and Nigeria both have strong players in their squads. Ivory Coast will be without Sebastien Haller due to an ankle injury, but they still have the likes of Seko Fofana, who is known for his dynamic midfield presence. 

Nigeria, meanwhile, will rely heavily on Victor Osimhen, a key striker who has shown his goal-scoring abilities in Serie A. Nigeria's lineup also includes Premier League players like Iwobi, Onyeka, Bassey, and Aina​​​​.

Ivory Coast vs Nigeria tactical analysis

The match is expected to be tightly contested, reflecting both teams' recent forms and historical rivalry. Ivory Coast has an impressive home record, having suffered just one defeat at home since the start of 2023.

Super Eagle head coach Jose Peseiro || Credit: Imago

Nigeria, having last won the tournament in 2013, will be looking to reassert their strength in the competition.

Ivory Coast vs Nigeria betting tips and predictions

The predictions and betting tips suggest a potential for both teams to score, with an emphasis on a strong attacking display from both sides​​​​.

Where and how to watch Ivory Coast vs Nigeria 

For those interested in watching the match, it will be broadcast live on local and satellite television in Nigeria.
