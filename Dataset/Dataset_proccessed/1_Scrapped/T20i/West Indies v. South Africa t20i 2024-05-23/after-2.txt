The West Indies welcome the Proteas for a thrilling three-match T20I series, and we have provided everything you need to keep up with the Proteas as they prepare for the upcoming four-week World Cup tournament in the West Indies and the United States.

SQUADS

PROTEAS EVERY MOMENT - THE FULL BROADCAST SCHEDULE:

1st T20I: 23 May from 21:00 CAT @ Sabina Park, Jamaica on Grandstand & SS Cricket

2nd T20I: 25 May from 21:00 CAT @ Sabina Park, Jamaica on Grandstand & SS Cricket

3rd T20I: 26 May from 21:00 CAT @ Sabina Park, Jamaica on Grandstand & SS Cricket

Commentary will be in English, isiXhosa and Afrikaans.

HISTORICAL

West Indies and South Africa have faced each other in 19 matches in T20. Out of these 19 games, South Africa have won 11 whereas West Indies have come out victorious on 8 occasions.
