India vs Afghanistan Highlights Asian Games Final: India win gold after Afghanistan match gets called off due to rain
By 
HT Sports Desk
Oct 7, 2023 2:43 PM IST
India vs Afghanistan Highlights Asian Games Final: The Ruturaj Gaikwad-led side claimed the gold medal after rain washed out the summit clash
India vs Afghanistan Live Score Asian Games Final

India vs Afghanistan Highlights Asian Games Final: The women's team did it, now the men's team replicate it as well. Although it did not come off the manner in which they would have preferred. Ruturaj Gaikwad kept his playing XI the same as in the semifinal game against Bangladesh. It seeme a good toss to win as well with India want to bowl first in the moisture-laden track after rain caused a 20-minute delay. The bowlers quickly sent Afghanistan 5 down with Shivam Dube and Arshdeep getting the two openers inside the powerplay before the spinners on Ravi Bishnoi and Sai Kishore picked two more. But Afghanistan staged a stellar fightback in the second half of their innings. The fourth-wicket pair of Shahidullah Kamal and Afsar Zzai scripted a 37-run stand with ther former taking the charge before skipper Gulbadin Naib stepping in after Zazai's dismissal. The two scripted an unbeaten half-century stand while Kamal neared his own fifty as Afghanistan fought back from 52 for five in the 11th over to 112 for five in the penultimate over. However, rain interrupted the proceedings in Hangzhou once again. After a long wait, the officials eventually abandoned the game and India, being the higher seeded side of the two teams in the match, won the gold medal while Afghanistan claimed the silver for the third time in history.
