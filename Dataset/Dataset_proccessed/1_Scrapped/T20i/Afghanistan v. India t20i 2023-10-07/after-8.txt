India vs Afghanistan Highlights, Asian Games Final: Match Abandoned Due To Rain, India Win Gold
India vs Afghanistan Highlights, Asian Games 2023 Final: Team India clinched victory due to higher seeding
NDTV Sports Desk
Updated:October 07, 2023 03:39 pm IST
Read Time: 1 min
India vs Afghanistan Highlights: India clinch gold medal
© AFP

India vs Afghanistan Asian Games 2023 Final Highlights: Team India clinched the gold medal in the Asian Games 2023 men's cricket final against Afghanistan after the match got abandoned due to rain. The Ruturaj Gaikwad-led side were declared the winners due to their higher seeding. Opting to bowl first, India showed a dominant approach and kept on scalping wickets at regular intervals. Before the halt, Afghanistan's score read at 112/5 in 18.2 overs with Shahidullah Kamal (49*) and Gulbadin Naib (27*) are standing unbeaten at the crease. For India, Arshdeep Singh, Shahbaz Ahmed, Shivam Dube and Ravi bishnoi scalped one wicket each. (India vs Afghanistan SCORE | Asian Games 2023 Medals Tally | Asian Games 2023 Full Schedule)
