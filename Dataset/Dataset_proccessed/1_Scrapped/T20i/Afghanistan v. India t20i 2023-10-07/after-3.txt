India vs Afghanistan Asian Games men's cricket final live score. (AFP Photo)

India vs Afghanistan Asian Games Final Highlights: Team India has been declared the winner of match and won the gold medal being the higher-seeded team as the match was called off due to rain. The play stopped when Afghanistan were 112/5 in 18.2 overs. Earlier, India skipper Ruturaj Gaikwad elected to bowl first in the gold medal match. Indian men’s cricket team will seek to emulate their women’s counterpart when they take on Afghanistan in the gold medal clash of the Asian Games today. Indian women’s team had last month beaten Sri Lanka to win the gold and now the men’s team will be hoping for glory. India defeated Nepal in the quarter-final and then routed Bangladesh in the semi-final to make it to the title clash.
