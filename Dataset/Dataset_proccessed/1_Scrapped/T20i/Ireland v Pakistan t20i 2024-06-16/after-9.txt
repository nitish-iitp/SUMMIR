Pakistan vs Ireland Highlights, T20 World Cup 2024

Pakistan vs Ireland Highlights, T20 World Cup 2024: Despite failing to qualify for the Super 8s, Pakistan ended their T20 World Cup campaign on a good note, with a three-wicket win vs Ireland in their final group stage fixture, in Lauderhill on Sunday. Chasing 107, Pakistan managed to reach 111/7 in 18.5 overs in a closely-fought run-chase. Babar Azam top-scored with an unbeaten knock of 32* runs of 34 balls. Meanwhile, it was Shaheen Shah Afridi, who wrapped up the win for Pakistan with an unbeaten knock of 13 runs off five balls, smacking two sixes in the 19th over. For Ireland, Barry McCarthy took three wickets and Curtis Campher struck twice. Initially, Pakistan captain Babar Azam won the toss and opted to bowl. It proved to be the right decision in Florida as Shaheen Shah Afridi and Mohammadd Amir ran riot in the powerplay. Meanwhile Haris Rauf and Imad Wasim also joined them, with the former getting a wicket and the latter bagging three. Shaheen struck thrice and Amir got two wickets.
