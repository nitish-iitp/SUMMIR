Nepal vs Canada 1st T20I Live Streaming And Telecast: When And Where To Watch Online?
Nepal will take on Canada in the first match of the T20I series, also involving Oman. The two teams recently featured in the ODI tri-series, which was part of the ICC Cricket World Cup League 2. Who will prevail in this epic battle of associate nations? Here are the live-streaming details of the NEP vs CAN 1st T20I.
Written by:
Faham Uddin

Updated Sep 28, 2024, 22:21 IST

Nepal vs Canada 1st T20I Live Streaming And Telecast: When And Where To Watch Online? (Source: CAN/X)

Nepal and Canada will cross sword in the first match of the tri-series, also involving Oman. The three teams recently clashed in the ODI series, which was part of the ICC Cricket World Cup League Two 2023-2027. Nepal failed to win even a single game and lost twice to Canada and once to Oman. One game was called off due to rain. The team would certainly want to perform better in the T20Is, a format they are more comfortable in.
Meanwhile, Canada were at their best and won all games, defeating Nepal and Oman twice. Nepal's T20I form is also not great as they failed to win a game in the T20 World Cup. Canada, on the other hand, recently played a tri-series involving the USA and Netherlands, winning one and losing one against the Netherlands. One game against the USA was washed out due to rain and they suffered a defeat in the second meet.
Nepal vs Canada 1st T20I Date, Time And Venue
Nepal vs Canada first T20I will be played at the Maple Leaf North-West Ground, King City. The match is scheduled for September 28, 2024 at 9:30 PM IST
Nepal vs Canada 1st T20I Pitch Report
The wicket will have enough help for the bowlers, as we saw in the ODI series, where batters struggled to get going. The batting won't be easy, however, once a batter is set, he will find it easier.
Nepal vs Canada 1st T20I Weather Report
The weather will be mostly clear and the chances of rain interruption are low. It will be a warm day where the temperature can go up to 32 degrees, making the conditions sweaty.
Nepal vs Canada 1st T20I Live Streaming
There will be no TV broadcast of the T20 match. Fancode App streamed the ODI matches of the tour so we can assume the platform would offer live streaming of the T20I matches as well, however, it is not confirmed.
Nepal vs Canada 1st T20I Squads
Nepal Squad: Arjun Saud(w), Aasif Sheikh, Bhim Sharki, Rohit Paudel(c), Aarif Sheikh, Anil Sah, Gulsan Jha, Sompal Kami, Sandeep Lamichhane, Karan KC, Lalit Rajbanshi, Kushal Malla, Dev Khanal, Kushal Bhurtel, Dipendra Singh Airee
Canada Squad: Navneet Dhaliwal, Aaron Johnson, Pargat Singh, Harsh Thaker, Nicholas Kirton(c), Shreyas Movva(w), Saad Bin Zafar, Kaleem Sana, Dillon Heyliger, Parveen Kumar, Gurbaz Bajwa, Kanwarpal Tathgur, Dilpreet Bajwa, Ansh Patel, Akhil Kumar
