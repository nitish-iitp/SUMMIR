The 2024 Canada Tri-Nation T20I Series will start today (September 28). Here's all you need to know about the tri-series, including the full fixtures list, squads and live-streaming details.

Canada T20I tri-series 2024 schedule: Full fixtures list, match timings and venues

Six matches will be played among three teams – Canada, Nepal and Oman. Each side will play the other twice in a double round-robin format. The most successful side will be crowned champions.

Here's the full schedule:

*All timings are local

Thursday, September 28, Match 1: Canada vs Nepal, Maple Leaf North-West Ground (A), King City, Ontario, 12:00 PM

Friday, September 29, Match 2: Oman vs Nepal, Maple Leaf North-West Ground (A), King City, Ontario, 12:00 PM

Saturday, September 30, Match 3: Canada vs Oman, Maple Leaf North-West Ground (A), King City, Ontario, 12:00 PM

Sunday, October 1, Match 4: Nepal vs Canada, Maple Leaf North-West Ground (A), King City, Ontario, 12:00 PM

Monday, October 2, Match 5: Oman vs Nepal, Maple Leaf North-West Ground(A), King City, Ontario, 12:00 PM

Tuesday, October 3, Match 6: Canada vs Oman, Maple Leaf North-West Ground(A), King City, Ontario, 12:00 PM

Canada T20I tri-series 2024 squads: Full team lists and injury updates

Nicholas Kirton will captain hosts Canada, while Oman will be led by Aqib Ilyas and Nepal by Rohit Paudel. In the latest ICC Men's T20I rankings, Nepal currently sit at 17th place, Canada at 19th, and Oman in 22nd place.
