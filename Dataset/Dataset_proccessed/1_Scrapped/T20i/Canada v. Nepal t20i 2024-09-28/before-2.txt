The Canada T20I Tri-Series 2024 will start on Saturday, September 28 2024, and will end on Thursday, October 3 2024. Maple Leaf North-West Ground(A), King City in Ontario will be the host venue for the upcoming tri-series.

A total of three teams will be participating in the tri-nations series including Oman, Nepal and Canada. The team that finishes at the top of the points table after the conclusion of six matches, will be declared as the winner.

Oman played their last T20I in the ICC Men‚Äôs T20 World Cup 2024 against Scotland. Nepal lost to West Indies A in the most recent unofficial T20I series while Canada recently played the Netherlands Tri-Nation T20I Series 2024 in August.
