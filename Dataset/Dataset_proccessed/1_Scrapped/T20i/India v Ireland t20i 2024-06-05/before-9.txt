IND vs IRE T20 World Cup 2024 Preview: Likely Playing XIs, Key Battles, H2H and More About India vs Ireland Men’s T20WC Cricket Match in New York
India and Ireland are all set to lock horns against each other in the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 match number eight at the Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in New York. India have an edge over Ireland ahead of the clash and they will hope on beginning their campaign with a win.

India will be taking Ireland in their first assignment of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 at the Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in New York. The Rohit Sharma-led side, who have been the champions of the inaugural edition of the T20 World Cup will be hoping to begin their campaign in the competition with a win. On the other hand, Ireland have also played some tremendous cricket in the past and have showcased a lot of character. Happy Birthday Ben Stokes: Fans Wish England Test Captain As He Turns 33

Rohit Sharma and Co. are currently the top-ranked team in the ICC Men's T20 Team Rankings and are one of the favourite teams to lift the T20 World Cup 2024. The Indian squad consists the balance of both experience and talent which will prove to be very essential for the side in the ongoing edition of the T20 World Cup. The Indian team achieved a dominating victory over Bangladesh in the warm-up match and have all the boxes covered. However, the major point to be noted will, be who will open with Rohit Sharma. ICC T20 World Cup 2024 Points Table Group D: South Africa Emerge Victorious in Low-Scoring Contest Against Sri Lanka

Ireland on the other hand will be coming into the match after playing a lot of T20 cricket just ahead of the tournament. The Irish team gave tough competition to Pakistan in the three-match T20I series. Ireland have the potential to give a tough time to India in their inaugural T20 World Cup 2024 match. The Paul Stirling side consists of loads of experience which includes George Dockrell and Andrew Balbirnie. Harry Tector's performance can also leave huge impact for Ireland in the T20 World Cup 2024.

IND vs IRE Head-to-Head Record in T20Is

India have never lost to Ireland in the T20I format and have played seven matches so far against the Paul Stirling led side. Rohit Sharma and Co. will hope to continue the same performance in their inaugural T20 World Cup 2024 match.

IND vs IRE T20 World Cup 2024 Key Battles

The key battles in the IND vs IRE T20 World Cup 2024 match will be between Indian batsman Virat Kohli and Irish spinner George Dockrell. Other than this battle, the fans will also eye the captain's battle between Rohit Sharma and Paul Stirling.

IND vs IRE T20 World Cup 2024 Venue and Match Timing

The India National Cricket Team vs Ireland National Cricket Team ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 match will be played at the Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in New York on June 5, 2024. The IND vs IRE T20 World Cup 2024 match will begin from 08:00 PM IST (Indian Standard Time).

IND vs IRE T20 World Cup 2024 Live Telecast and Online Streaming

Star Sports Network has the broadcasting rights of ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 in India. Fans can tune into Star Sports 1/HD, Star Sports 2/HD, Star Sports 3, Star Sports 1 Hindi, Star Sports 1 Tamil, Star Sports 1 Telugu and Star Sports 1 Kannada TV channels to catch the live telecast of the IND vs IRE T20 WC 2024 match on TV in India. Disney Plus Hotstar has the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 online streaming rights in India. Fans can turn to the JioCinema app or website to catch the IND vs IRE T20 World Cup 2024 free live streaming in India.
