Highlights
The three foreign cricketers in the XI are Will Jacks of Chattogram Challengers, Sunil Narine of Comilla Victorians and Mujeeb Ur Rahman of Fortune Barishal.
Four cricketers from Comilla Victorians, runner-up Fortune Barishal, Chattogram Challengers, two cricketers from Khulna Tigers and one minister from Dhaka have been named in the XI.

The eighth edition of the Bangladesh Premier League (BPL) has come to a close with a tense final. Imrul Kayes' Comilla Victorians won the title for the third time by defeating Fortune Barishal by one run. Bdcrictime has created the best XI of the event with 11 of those who shined with both bat and ball throughout the season. 

Tamim has scored 406 runs in 9 innings

The three foreign cricketers in the XI are Will Jacks of Chattogram Challengers, Sunil Narine of Comilla Victorians and Mujeeb Ur Rahman of Fortune Barishal. Four cricketers from Comilla Victorians, runner-up Fortune Barishal, Chattogram Challengers, two cricketers from Khulna Tigers and one minister from Dhaka have been named in the XI.

Bdcrictime's BPL 2022's best XI:

Will Jacks (Chattogram Challengers):The right-handed English opener is the highest run-scorer of the tournament. He has scored 414 runs in 11 innings at an average of 41.40 and a strike rate of 155.05. The batter was consistent throughout the event.

Tamim Iqbal (Minister Dhaka):Tamim Iqbal was the only consistent performer in Minister Dhaka. He is the only Bangladeshi batsman in the list of top five run-scorer of the event. The opener has scored 406 runs in 9 innings with a strike rate of 132.57. The left-handed batter owns one of the four centuries of the event.

He scored 284 runs and took 16 wickets with the ball.

Sunil Narine (Comilla Victorians):Final game hero Sunil Narine batted 7 innings and scored 159 runs at an average of 31.80. The strike rate of 198.75 shows how devastating he was. He smashed a tornado innings of 57 off 16 balls in the qualifiers and 57 off 23 balls in the final. Narine was extremely frugal with the ball. The spinner, who took 4 wickets in 8 innings, has an economy of 5.61.

Mahmudul Hasan Joy (Comilla Victorians):This young right-handed batsman has scored 235 runs in 9 innings for Comilla Victorians. He has a strike rate of 115.19 and a batting average of 26.11.

Shakib Al Hasan (Captain) (Fortune Barishal):Shakib has been a great captain for Fortune Barishal. He was also great with bat and ball. The all-rounder has also been selected as the best cricketer of the tournament for his outstanding performance.

He scored 284 runs with the bat in 11 innings and took 16 wickets with the ball. Shakib has also built the reputation of being the best cricketer in five consecutive matches during the season.

Mrittunjoy Chowdhury has taken 15 wickets in 8 matches.

Yasir Ali (Khulna Tigers):Right-handed Yasir Ali became a symbol of confidence in the middle order for the Khulna Tigers. Batting in 8 innings, he scored 219 runs at an average of 31.28 and a strike rate of 139.49. His unbeaten 57 is the highest innings of the tournament.

Mushfiqur Rahim (Wicketkeeper) (Khulna Tigers):Khulna Tigers wicket-keeper and captain Mushfiqur Rahim scored 251 runs in 9 innings. He was unbeaten in two innings. Batting average and strike rate are 35.85 and 129.38 respectively.

Mujeeb Ur Rahman (Fortune Barishal):Spinner Mujeeb Ur Rahman bowled brilliantly throughout the match. He took 10 wickets in 8 innings at an economy rate of only 5.81. He also did a great job of tying the batters during the power-play.

Mustafizur Rahman (Comilla Victorians):Left-arm pacer Mustafizur Rahman is the highest wicket taker of the tournament with 19 wickets in 11 matches. He is also the owner of the best bowling figure of a match by taking 5 wickets for 27 runs.

Mrittunjoy Chowdhury (Chattogram Challengers):Young left-arm pacer Mrittunjoy Chowdhury has taken 15 wickets in 8 matches. He has also proved his effectiveness in death overs.

Shohidul Islam (Comilla Victorians):The right-arm pacer has 14 wickets in 8 matches. Shohidul also won the last over of the final.

Twelfth cricketer: Mahedi Hasan Rana

Besides, Andre Fletcher, Munim Shahriar, Dwayne Bravo, Moeen Ali were also in rhythm. However, they are not in the XI for team combination.
