The 9th ICC Men’s T20 World Cup will take place from June 1 to June 29, 2024. This tournament, organised by the International Cricket Council (ICC), will be hosted by the West Indies and the United States. It is the first time the T20 World Cup will be held in the United States and in any other part of the Americas besides the West Indies.

This edition will feature 20 teams, up from 16 in the previous tournament. The teams include the two hosts, the top 8 teams from the 2022 World Cup, the next 2 best-ranked teams, and 8 teams that qualified through regional tournaments.

The 20 teams will be split into 4 groups of 5. The top 2 teams from each group will move on to the Super 8 round, where they will be divided into 2 new groups of 4. The best 2 teams from each Super 8 group will reach the semifinals. The final will be held on June 29, 2024, at the Kensington Oval in Barbados. England, the 2022 champions, will defend their title.

Matches will be played at 9 venues: 3 in the USA (Florida, New York, Texas) and 6 in the West Indies (Antigua, Barbados, Guyana, St. Lucia, St. Vincent, Trinidad & Tobago). Teams finalised their squads by April 29, 2024, and can make unlimited changes until May 25. Warm-up matches will occur from May 27 to June 1, involving 20 teams.

T20 World Cup 2024 Schedule, Date and Times

The T20 World Cup 2024 will be held from June 1 to June 29, 2024. The tournament will feature 20 teams and a total of 55 matches.

The matches will be played across various venues in the United States and the West Indies, including:

Grand Prairie Cricket Stadium, Dallas

Guyana National Stadium, Guyana

Kensington Oval, Bridgetown, Barbados

Nassau County International Cricket Stadium, New York

Central Broward Regional Park Stadium Turf Ground, Lauderhill, Florida

Sir Vivian Richards Stadium, North Sound, Antigua

Brian Lara Cricket Academy, Tarouba, Trinidad and Tobago

Daren Sammy National Cricket Stadium, Gros Islet, St Lucia

Arnos Vale Ground, Arnos Vale, St Vincent

The tournament will start with the match between the USA and Canada on June 1, 2024, at 6:00 AM. Other key match timings include:

West Indies vs New Zealand on June 13 at 8:00 PM

India vs Pakistan on June 9 at 8:00 PM

Australia vs England on June 8 at 10:30 PM

The final will be played on June 29 at 8:00 PM in Barbados

The top two teams from each Super Eight group will qualify for the semifinals. The final will be played on June 29, 2024, in Barbados.

Some key details about the tournament:

20 teams will participate, the largest T20 World Cup ever

55 matches will be played across 9 venues in the USA and West Indies

Group A matches will be played entirely in the USA

The first 6 matches of Group D will be in the USA, with the remaining 4 split between St Lucia and St Vincent

All other groups will play their matches in the West Indies

Teams receive 2 points for a win, 1 for a no result. In case of a tie, a Super Over will decide the winner.

Fan would especially be looking forward to the T20 World Cup series 2024 because of the squads that are going to perform in the matches. The players on all the teams are a mix of experienced and emerging talents.

Three teams, USA, Uganda and Canada will make their T20 debuts this year, which will make the World Cup an even more entertaining experience for the audience worldwide.
