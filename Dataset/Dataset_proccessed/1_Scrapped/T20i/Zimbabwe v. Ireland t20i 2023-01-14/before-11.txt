The women’s cricket industry is booming at a rocketing pace and introducing a needful tournament to the industry has come from the International Cricket Council (ICC) launching the first-ever Under-19 Women’s Twenty20 World Cup that’s scheduled to get underway in South Africa in a few days time.

Complete Squad and Schedule List for ICC U19 Women’s World Cup 2023

 

The historic first of the U19 Women’s T20 World Cup that’ll take place in Benoni and Potchefstroom in South Africa from the 14th to the 29th of January has 41 matches on schedule to be played across 15 days.

The 16-team tournament constitutes 11 full ICC members and 5 associate teams have been split into four groups and squads of each have been detailed below. Teams eying qualification to the business end, the Super Six league stage will be required to finish in the top three in their respective groups.
