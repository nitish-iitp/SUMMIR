World Important Day on 30 April 2024 

Understand the significance of World Important Day and learn how to make a difference:

1. Death of Osama bin Laden (2011)

On May 2, 2011, Osama bin Laden, the mastermind behind the September 11, 2001 attacks in the United States, was killed by U.S. special forces in a covert operation in Pakistan. His death marked a significant milestone in the global fight against terrorism.

2. Beginning of Ferdinand Magellan’s Circumnavigation (1519)

On May 2, 1519, Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan embarked on his historic journey to circumnavigate the globe. Although Magellan himself did not complete the voyage, his expedition was the first to successfully navigate the Earth.

3. Fall of Berlin in World War II (1945)

On May 2, 1945, the capital of Nazi Germany, Berlin, fell to the advancing Allied forces during World War II. The surrender of Berlin marked a crucial victory for the Allies and hastened the end of the war in Europe.

4. First Performance of Gilbert and Sullivan’s “The Mikado” (1885)¨

On May 2, 1885, the comic opera ‘The Mikado,’ composed by Arthur Sullivan with libretto by W.S. Gilbert, premiered in London. The operetta became one of Gilbert and Sullivan’s most popular works, known for its witty satire and catchy tunes.

5. Apartheid in South Africa Ends (1994)

On May 2, 1994, South Africa held its first multiracial democratic elections, marking the official end of the apartheid system of racial segregation. Nelson Mandela, who had spent 27 years in prison for his anti-apartheid activism, was elected as the country’s first black president, symbolising a new era of equality and reconciliation.
