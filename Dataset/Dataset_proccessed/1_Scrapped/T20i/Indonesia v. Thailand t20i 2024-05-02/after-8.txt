World Important Day on 5 May 2024 

Understand the significance of World Important Day and learn how to make a difference:

1. First American in Space (1961)

Alan Shepard became the first American to travel into space aboard the Freedom 7 spacecraft, marking a significant milestone in space exploration.

2. Inauguration of London Eye (2000)

The London Eye, a giant Ferris wheel on the South Bank of the River Thames, was officially inaugurated, becoming an iconic landmark and tourist attraction.

3. First YouTube Video

The first video, titled “Me at the zoo,” was uploaded to YouTube, marking the beginning of the world’s largest video-sharing platform, revolutionising online media consumption.

4. Hawaii’s Kilauea Volcano Eruption (2018)

The Kilauea volcano on Hawaii’s Big Island erupted, causing widespread destruction and forcing thousands to evacuate, highlighting the power and unpredictability of natural disasters.
