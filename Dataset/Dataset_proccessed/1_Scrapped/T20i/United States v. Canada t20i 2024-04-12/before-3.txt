The Jawaharlal Nehru Technology University (JNTU) Hyderabad has initiated the online application correction window for the Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, Medical (or Pharmacy) Entrance Test (TS EAMCET) 2024 on its official website at eapcet.tsche.ac.in. The deadline for making modifications is April 12, 2024. The engineering exam is scheduled for May 9, 10, and 11, while the agriculture and medical exams will be held on May 7 and 8, respectively.
TS EAMCET correction window: The Jawaharlal Nehru Technology University (JNTU) Hyderabad has started the online application correction window for the Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, Medical (or Pharmacy) Entrance Test (TS EAMCET) 2024 on its official website at eapcet.tsche.ac.in.
The last date to modify the changes is April 12, 2024. The TS EAMCET 2024 for the engineering stream is scheduled to be conducted on May 9, 10, and 11, while for the agriculture and medical streams, the exams are scheduled for May 7 and 8.

Exam pattern
The TS EAMCET 2024 exam for all streams will be computer-based, lasting three hours and divided into two shifts: 9 a.m. to 12 p.m. and 3 p.m. to 6 p.m. The question paper will include 160 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), 40 in physics and chemistry and 80 in mathematics. Candidates will be awarded one point for each correct answer, with no negative marking for incorrect answers.
Further, starting this academic year 2023-24, TS EAMCET 2024 rankings will be determined solely by candidates' performance in the entrance examination. The previous 25% weightage assigned to intermediate or Class 12 marks in rank calculation has been removed.
