ICC Men's T20 World Cup Africa Sub Regional Qualifier A 2024: Full schedule, squads, match timings, and live-streaming details

Tanzania will host the ICC Men's T20 World Cup Africa Sub-Regional Qualifier A 2024 from Saturday, September 21 to Tuesday, September 26. The matches will take place at two venues in Dar-es-Salaam: the Dar Gymkhana Club Ground and the University of Dar-es-Salaam Ground.

The upcoming tournament is part of the qualification pathway for the 2026 ICC Men's T20 World Cup. Seven teams will compete: Cameroon, Ghana, Lesotho, Malawi, Mali, and the host nation Tanzania.

A total of 15 matches will be played, and the top two teams will advance to the regional final. There, they will be joined by Namibia and Uganda, who received byes for participating in the previous T20 World Cup, along with four teams from the sub-regional qualifiers B and C.

ICC Men's T20 World Cup Africa Sub Regional Qualifier A 2024: Telecast & Live-Streaming Details

The ICC Men's T20 World Cup Africa Sub-Regional Qualifier A 2024 will not be broadcast live on television in India. However, fans can watch the live stream of the tournament on the FanCode app and website. A match pass is available for Rs. 15, while a tour pass costs Rs. 49.
