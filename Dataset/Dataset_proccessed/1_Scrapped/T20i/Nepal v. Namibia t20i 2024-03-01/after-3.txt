By Republica
March 2, 2024 at 4:00 AM

KATHMANDU, March 2: Nepal is set to clash against the Netherlands in the ongoing triangular T20 cricket series today. The match is scheduled to commence at 11:30 am at the Tribhuvan University cricket ground in Kirtipur.




In the previous three encounters of the triangular T20 cricket series involving Namibia and the Netherlands, Nepal managed to secure victory in just one match.

Nepal must win today's match to make it to the finals of the series. Nepal defeated Namibia by three runs in the third match on Friday.




Nepal faced defeat in the initial match against Namibia by a margin of 20 runs, followed by another loss to the Netherlands by a mere two runs.




Netherlands holds the top position in the series with four points, having won both matches played so far. Nepal and Namibia trail with two points each. Nepal currently secures the second position on the points table, with Namibia at the bottom, based on run rate.
