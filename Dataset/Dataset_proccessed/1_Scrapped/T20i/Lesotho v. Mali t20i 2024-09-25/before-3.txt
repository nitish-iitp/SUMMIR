Match Preview

Mali struggled in their batting innings, setting a low total of just 57 runs before being bowled out in 16.4 overs. Lamissa Sanogo was the top scorer with 15 runs off 21 balls, while Sanze Kamate contributed 12 runs from 14 balls. Lassina Berthe added 7 runs off 5 balls. Despite individual efforts, the team’s performance was disappointing. In response, Cameroon chased down the target comfortably, scoring 58 runs for the loss of 4 wickets in 8.5 overs. Cheick Amala Keita stood out for Mali with his bowling, taking 2 wickets for just 7 runs in 1 over. Mohamed Coulibaly and Mahamadou Malle also chipped in with 1 wicket each. Ultimately, Mali lost the match by 6 wickets.

In Match 3 of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Africa Sub Regional Qualifier A, Lesotho faced off against Tanzania. Batting first, Tanzania set a challenging total of 183 runs, finishing at 183/7 in their 20 overs. Waseem Yaqoob excelled with the ball for Lesotho, claiming 4 wickets for 38 runs. However, Lesotho struggled in their innings, only managing to score 61 runs in 14.5 overs. Mpiti Lerotholi was the top scorer with 33 runs off 29 balls, but the rest of the batting lineup faltered. This match highlighted Tanzania’s dominance and Lesotho’s need for improvement as the tournament continues.
