PREVIEW

Cameroon will take on Lesotho in the 14th match of the Mens T20 World Cup Africa Sub-Regional Qualifier A. This match will be played at Gymkhana Club Ground. Cameroon have one win and three losses in three matches. On the other hand, Lesotho have lost all three matches and are fifth in the table. This promises to be an exciting match between these two sides.
