In the second match of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Africa Sub Regional Qualifier A, Ghana faced Cameroon. Ghana emerged victorious by 8 wickets. Obed Harvey, the captain, also impressed with 3 wickets for 5 runs in 2.2 overs, while Godfred Bakiweyem contributed with 1 wicket for 4 runs in 2 overs.

In response, Ghana chased down the target comfortably, finishing at 41 runs for the loss of 2 wickets in just 8.1 overs. Obed Harvey led from the front with the bat, scoring 17 runs off 16 balls, while Samson Awiah matched him with 17 runs off 26 balls. Philip Yevugah added 2 runs. Ghana’s strong bowling performance set the foundation for a convincing win, demonstrating their dominance in the match.

In the third match of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Africa Sub Regional Qualifier A, Tanzania faced Lesotho. Tanzania achieved a commanding victory, winning by 122 runs.
