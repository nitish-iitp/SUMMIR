Hosts Sri Lanka take on India in the first ODI of the 3-match series at the R. Premadasa Stadium in Colombo on Sunday afternoon. It is part of the ICC ODI Super League.

After a disastrous tour of England where they failed to win a single game, Sri Lanka come into this home series against an Indian team minus the usual names. They have lost their last three ODI series to the West Indies (3-0), Bangladesh (2-1), and England (2-0). Sri Lanka will be captained by Dasun Shanaka as their skipper Kusal Perera has been ruled out of the tour due to a shoulder injury. The suspensions of Kusal Mendis, Danushka Gunathilaka and Dickwella means Sri Lankaâ€™s batting line-up looks very inexperienced heading into this series.Â 

India have taken an alternate 20-man squad for this tour of Sri Lanka, with Shikhar Dhawan captaining the side. There are 6 uncapped players in the squad, including the likes of Devdutt Padikkal, Ruturaj Gaikwad and Varun Chakravarthy, who have hugely impressed in the Indian T20 League. The team is not short on star power, with the likes of Suryakumar Yadav, Hardik Pandya and Bhuvneshwar Kumar being part of it. Yuzvendra Chahal will be the prime spinner, with Rahul Chahar and Kuldeep Yadav vying for the second spot.

Despite being inexperienced on the international level, India head into this match as favorites against an unsettled and troubled host, who will look to gain confidence with some consistent performances at home. All in all, it will be an exciting start to the ODI series at the Premadasa Stadium on Sunday.
