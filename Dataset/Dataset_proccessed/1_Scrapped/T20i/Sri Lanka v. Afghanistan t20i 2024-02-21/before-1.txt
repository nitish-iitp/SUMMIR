Karachi Kings

Karachi Kings - a competitive franchise team in the Pakistan Super League, founded in 2015. Features star players including Babar Azam, Mohammad Amir, and Colin Ingram. Led by experienced coach Dean Jones. Plays home games at the historic National Stadium in Karachi. This page contains all the important information about Karachi Kings.

Karachi Kings Squad

Welcome to the Karachi Kings Squad for PSL T20 2024! As the excitement builds for the 9th season of the Pakistan Super League, the Karachi Kings are gearing up to showcase their cricket prowess. With a formidable lineup of batters, allrounders, and bowlers, the team is set to make waves in the tournament.

The middle-order boasts the talents of Irfan Khan, a right-hand batter with a knack for dominating the crease. Joining him is Muhammad Akhlaq, a seasoned wicketkeeper-batter known for his right-hand batting and medium-fast bowling skills. The explosive Tim Seifert, Shan Masood, and James Vince round out the power-packed batting lineup, promising thrilling performances in every match.

The allrounders' brigade includes the versatile Mohammad Nawaz, Jamie Overton, Kieron Pollard, Daniel Sams, and Shoaib Malik, each bringing a unique set of skills to the table. From slow left-arm orthodox bowling to right-arm fast, the Kings have a diverse range of allrounders ready to make an impact.

The bowling attack is spearheaded by Anwar Ali, Arafat Minhas, Hasan Ali, Mir Hamza, Mohammad Amir Khan, Tabraiz Shamsi, Sirajuddin, and the promising Saad Baig. This formidable group of bowlers is geared up to outsmart the opposition and secure victories for the Karachi Kings.
