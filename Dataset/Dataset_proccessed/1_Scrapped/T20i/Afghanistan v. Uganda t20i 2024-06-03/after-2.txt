T20 World Cup Live Score and Updates, AFG vs UGA: Afghanistan began as the overwhelming favourites, defeating Uganda by 125 runs at the Providence in Guyana. After a blistering opening partnership between Rahmanullah Gurbaz and Ibrahim Zadran, pacer Fazalhaq Farooqi registered his career figures – 5/9 in 4 overs – to hand a sensational win to Afghanistan.

Afghanistan did extremely well in the last ICC tournament, beating the likes of England, South Africa and Pakistan in the ODI World Cup in India last year, and they will certainly take heart from that showing in this tournament.
