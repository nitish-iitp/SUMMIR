WI vs SA Weather Report Of Trinidad

The West Indies vs South Africa 3rd T20I 2024 takes place in Trinidad on Tuesday, August 27.

According to the reports, the temperature of the city on Monday will be 26° Celcius during the day and 22° Celcius at night.

The sky will be partly cloudy during the day and night. The humidity will be 78% during the day and 92% at night. The chances of rain are 40% during the day and 15% at night.

WI vs SA Pitch Analysis

The West Indies vs South Africa 3rd T20I takes place in Trinidad at Brian Lara Stadium. According to stats, Brian Lara Stadium is a low-scoring ground where the average 1st innings total is 135 while the average 2nd innings total is 123.

Out of 14 matches at the stadium, the teams batting first has won 5 games while the teams batting second have won 9 matches. The average 1st innings total is 135 while the average 2nd innings total is 123.

The highest-ever total at the stadium is 267/3 posted by England in a clash against West Indies.

The lowest-ever total at the stadium is 149-6. In the ongoing series, West Indies has piled up 170+ totals in both innings.

Weather Impact

Since there is just a 24% to 48% chance of rain in Trinidad, Trouba on Tuesday, the WI vs SA 3rd T20I might be affected by rain.

However, since the chance of rain is less than 40%, the match will be able to complete like the previous ones.

The first two games between West Indies and South Africa were not affected by rain and the next game is also unlikely to be affected by rain.
