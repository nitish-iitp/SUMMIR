School Assembly News Headline for November 2, 2022 , is here. Students may refer to the top news headlines from national, international, and sports here. Also, November 2, is celebrated as “All Souls Day” and “Parumala Perunnal” to honour all the souls living and dead. Students may keep a moment of silence to appreciate life and pray for their souls.

List of Important Days in November 2022: National & International Days
National News for School Assembly November 2, 2022
Students may pick some of the trending and latest news from the national headline for the School Assembly November 2, 2022:
The Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) reports the Air Quality in Delhi under the “Severe” Category.
J.J.Irani, the Steel Man of India, who was associated with TATA Steel for decades passes away at the age of 86.
Union Minister, Rajeev Chandrasekhar, announces, Grievance Appellate Committee to be in place from November 30, 2022.
Droupadi Murmu, President of India, to visit Nagaland on November 2 to 3, 2022.
Rohith Vemula's mother joins Rahul Gandi on the "Bharat Jodo Yatra".
Congress demands a Judicial Inquiry on the Morbi Bridge Collapse case.
As per a study by the Ministry of Rural Development, the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) must be decentralised for better flexibility and results.
Scientists associated with the National Academy of Agricultural Sciences (NAAS), provide clearance to GM Mustard.
PM Narendra Modi visits and pays tributes to the tribals who were massacred by the British in 1913, at Mangarh in Rajasthan.

To support Education and Healthcare for Palestinian Refugees, India donates $2.5 million.
Pakistan saw a trembling earthquake with a magnitude of 4.8.
Afghan Generals claim that Russia is recruiting former Afghan Commandos trained by the USA.
Sri Lanka faces Urban Poverty tripled.
