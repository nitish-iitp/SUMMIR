Photos: India beats Bangladesh in T20 World Cup Super Eight stage
India moved to the top of Group 1 in the Super Eight with two wins from two matches.
India’s Shivam Dube plays a shot during the ICC men’s Twenty20 World Cup 2024 Super Eight cricket match between India and Bangladesh at Sir Vivian Richards Stadium in North Sound, Antigua and Barbuda, on June 22, 2024. (Photo by ANDREW CABALLERO-REYNOLDS / AFP) (Photo by ANDREW CABALLERO-REYNOLDS/AFP via Getty Images)
By NHAT MEYER | nmeyer@bayareanewsgroup.com | Bay Area News Group
UPDATED: June 24, 2024 at 4:14 AM PST

Hardik Pandya has blasted Bangladesh with a 27-ball half-century and Kuldeep Yadav’s three wickets have finished off India’s win at the Twenty20 World Cup in Antigua. Pandya struck four fours and three sixes in an unbeaten 50 to lead India to 196-5. Yadav then took 3-19 in four overs to limit Bangladesh to 146-8. India moved to the top of Group 1 in the Super Eight with two wins from two matches. Bangladesh is winless.
