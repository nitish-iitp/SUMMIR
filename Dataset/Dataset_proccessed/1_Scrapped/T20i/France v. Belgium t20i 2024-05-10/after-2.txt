National News Headlines in English for 14 May 2024

Stay updated with national news headlines in English. Learn about various sectors’ happenings in our country:

1. Supreme Court Leaves Decision on Arvind Kejriwal’s Post to Delhi LG: The Supreme Court of India stated on Monday (May 13) that “it is up to Delhi LG to act” regarding a plea seeking the removal of Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal from his post.

2. CBSE Announces Class 12 and 10 Results; Girls Outshine Boys: CBSE released Class 12 and 10 board results for the 2023-24 session. 93.6% of students passed, a 0.48% increase from last year. Girls performed better than boys.

3. China Overtakes US as India’s Largest Trading Partner: China surpasses the United States as India’s largest trading partner for the fiscal year 2023-24, with a bilateral trade volume reaching $118.4 billion, as per GTRI data.

International News in English for 14 May 2024

Explore global stories through international news in English. Discover updates from different countries:

1. Putin Nominates Belousov as New Defense Minister: Russian President Vladimir Putin proposes Andrei Belousov as the new defense minister, replacing Sergei Shoigu. Shoigu is set to become the secretary of Russia’s Security Council.

2. France Secures Investments from Amazon, Pfizer, and Morgan Stanley: France secures commitments from Amazon, Pfizer, and Morgan Stanley, promising new jobs and investments. Amazon pledges an additional investment of 1.2 billion euros, potentially creating 3,000 new jobs.

3. Pakistan PM Shehbaz Sharif Announces Privatization Plan: Sharif declares privatization of state-owned enterprises, except strategic ones, during a meeting in Islamabad. The Privatisation Program 2024-29 includes power distribution companies. Sharif instructs ministries to cooperate with the Privatization Commission.

Sports News in English for 14 May 2024

Get the latest sports news in English and follow your favourite sports events:

1. Rahul Dravid Unlikely to Reapply for Indian Team Head Coach Position: Rahul Dravid is unlikely to reapply for the Indian Team Head Coach position following the BCCI’s announcement to invite applicants after the 2024 T20 World Cup.

2. Djokovic Worried After Rome Open Upset Due to Head Injury: Novak Djokovic’s surprise exit from the Rome Open by Alejandro Tabilo worries him. He was hit by a bottle two days earlier, and experienced nausea and dizziness. Tabilo, ranked 32nd, defeated him 6-2, 6-3.

3. Arsenal Awaits Premier League Championship Drama: Manager Mikel Arteta’s hopes pinned on former club, Manchester City, to drop points. Arsenal’s title dream hinges on City’s results against Tottenham Hotspur and West Ham United.

World Important Day on 14 May 2024 

Understand the significance of World Important Day and learn how to make a difference:

1. Fatima Apparitions Begin (1917)

Three shepherd children in Fatima, Portugal, reported seeing visions of the Virgin Mary, sparking widespread interest and later culminating in what became known as the Miracle of the Sun.

2. Churchill Addresses Parliament (1940)

Winston Churchill delivers his famous “blood, toil, tears, and sweat” speech to the House of Commons, rallying Britain during World War II and marking a pivotal moment in history.

3. First Successful Flight of Polaris Missile (1958)

The U.S. Navy achieves a breakthrough in missile technology with the successful launch of the Polaris missile from a submerged submarine, marking a milestone in the Cold War arms race.

4. Assassination Attempt on Pope John Paul II (1981)

Mehmet Ali Agca attempts to assassinate Pope John Paul II in St. Peter’s Square, Vatican City, sparking shock and global concern over the safety of world leaders.

5. Final Episode of “Family Ties” Airs (1989)

The beloved American sitcom “Family Ties” concludes its seven-season run, leaving a lasting impact on television and popular culture, especially with its portrayal of the Reagan era.
