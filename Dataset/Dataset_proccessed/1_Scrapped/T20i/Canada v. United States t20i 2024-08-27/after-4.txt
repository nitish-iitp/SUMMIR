PREVIEW

The 5th T20I of the Netherlands T20I Tri-Series will witness a clash between the United States of America and Canada on 27th August 2024 at Sportpark Duivesteijn, Voorburg. 

The USA is currently at the bottom of the points table with 1 point, having played 2 matches with 1 loss and 1 no-result. Canada, on the other hand, is also struggling with just 1 point from 2 matches, placing them just above the USA on the points table.

As the team gears up for the upcoming clash, all eyes will be on the thrilling competition set to unfold on the cricket field. Ahead of the game, here is a look at the Dream11 predictions, the fantasy tips, the pitch report, the top player picks, and the best-predicted XIs for the fantasy contests of the match.
