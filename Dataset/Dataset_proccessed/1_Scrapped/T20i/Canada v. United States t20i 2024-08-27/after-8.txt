The USA vs Canada match will be played in Voorburg. (Photo: @officialcricketcanada/Instagram)

USA vs CAN 5th T20I Dream11 Prediction

USA will be up against Canada in match 5 of the tri-series at the Sportpark Duivesteijn in Voorburg on Tuesday, August 27.

USA are yet to register a win in the ongoing competition whereas Canada will be heading into the contest on the back of a eight-run win over the Netherlands.

Notably, the Netherlands, the USA and Canada are engaged in a six-match tri-series which will run till Wednesday, August 28.

This will be Canada's fourth and final match of the series whereas the USA will take field in their third fixture.

TOSS: The match toss between the USA and Canada will take place at 5:00 PM IST.

Time: August 27, 5:30 PM IST.

Venue: Sportpark Duivesteijn, Voorburg

 

USA vs CAN 5th T20I 2024 Live Streaming Details: The live streaming of the match in India will be helmed by the FanCode app and website.
