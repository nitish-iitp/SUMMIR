India Sign Off With Thumping Win Over Namibia, Fans Come Out In Support
1:30
Nov 09, 2021;
Rohit Sharma and KL Rahul's fiery partnership helped Team India defeat Namibia by nine wickets in the final Super 12 match of the ongoing T20 World Cup at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium on Monday. India failed to progress to the semi-finals after Afghanistan failed to defeat New Zealand on Sunday. Fans were disappointed that India couldn't reach the semis but still came out in support of the team. (Video Credit: ANI)
