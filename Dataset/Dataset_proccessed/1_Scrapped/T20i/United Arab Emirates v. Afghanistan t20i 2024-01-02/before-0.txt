UAE vs Afghanistan 2023-24, T20I series: Broadcast, Live Streaming details: When and where to watch in India, USA, Pakistan & other countries
Published - Dec 29, 2023, 07:07 UTC | Updated - Dec 29, 2023, 07:07 UTC
by Akshat Gaur 
United Arab Emirates will host Afghanistan in a three-match T20I series.
The first T20I will take place at  Sharjah Cricket Stadium on December 29.

In an eagerly anticipated cricket showdown, the United Arab Emirates (UAE) is set to host Afghanistan in a thrilling three-match T20 International (T20I) series, commencing this Friday, December 29. Cricket enthusiasts are gearing up for an exciting clash between the two formidable teams, and anticipation is high for the series that promises to be a cricketing spectacle.

This marks the second bilateral series between the UAE and Afghanistan in the Emirates this year, with their previous encounter taking place in February. During that series, Afghanistan emerged victorious, securing a 2-1 win over the hosts. The upcoming series provides the UAE with an opportunity to turn the tables and redeem themselves on their home turf.

Muhammad Waseem, a seasoned campaigner, will be leading the UAE side, bringing his wealth of experience to the captaincy role. On the opposing side, Ibrahim Zadran has the honour of captaining the touring Afghan team. Both skippers will be looking to inspire their squads to deliver top-notch performances and secure bragging rights in this T20I series.

Afghanistan tour of UAE 2023-24, T20I series, Fixtures, Venues and Timings:
December 29, 2023: First T20I, Sharjah Cricket Stadium, 7:30 pm IST/ 2 pm GMT/ 6 pm local
December 31, 2023: Second T20I, Sharjah Cricket Stadium, 7:30 pm IST/ 2 pm GMT/ 6 pm local
January 2, 2024: Third T20I, Sharjah Cricket Stadium, 7:30 pm IST/ 2 pm GMT/ 6 pm local
