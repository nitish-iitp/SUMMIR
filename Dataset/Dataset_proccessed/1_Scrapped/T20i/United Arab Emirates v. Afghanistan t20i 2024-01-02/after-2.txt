UAE vs AFG Dream11 Prediction For 3rd T20I: United Arab Emirates vs Afghanistan Fantasy XI For Series Decider In Sharjah

UAE vs AFG Dream11 Prediction 3rd T20I: Check the fantasy 11 for the series decider T20I match between the United Arab Emirates and Afghanistan in Sharjah on Tuesday.

By Ankit Bisht
Tue, 02 Jan 2024 02:30 PM (IST)
Source:JND
UAE vs AFG Dream11 Prediction 3rd T20I: Afghanistan will eye to win the series decider and seal the series 2-1 in Sharjah. (Photo: @ACBofficials/ X)

UAE vs AFG Dream11 Prediction 3rd T20I: United Arab Emirates and Afghanistan will lock horns in the third and final ODI of the series in Sharjah on Tuesday. The three-match series is currently levelled at 1-1 and the winner of the match will clinch the series.

In the second T20I, UAE openers Muhammad Waseem (53) and Aryan Lakra (63) struck half-centuries to guide the side to 166/7 after opting to bat first. In reply, fiery spells from Muhammad Jawadullah (4-26) and Ali Naseer (4-24) clinched four wickets each to all out visitors for 155 in 19.5 overs.

In the opening match of the series, Rahmanullah Gurbaz struck 100 off 52 balls to steer Afghanistan to 203/3. Chasing the target, Vriitya Aravind's unbeaten 70* went in vain as UAE fell 72-run short of the target.

UAE vs AFG Dream11 Prediction for 3rd T20I

Toss: The toss for the match will take place at 7 PM IST.

Time: The match will start at 7:30 PM IST on Tuesday, January 2.

Venue: Sharjah Cricket Ground
