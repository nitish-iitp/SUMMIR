Indian Veteran Premier League 2024: Complete Fixtures, Timings and Venue details
Published - Feb 22, 2024, 01:59 UTC | Updated - Feb 23, 2024, 12:34 UTC
by Akshat Gaur 
The inaugural season of the Indian Veteran Premier League 2024 is scheduled to start on February 23.
The tournament will feature yesteryear cricketing legends and regional stars.
IVPL 2024, Schedule and Match timings (Image Source: @IVPLT20)

Table of Contents

Teams and Captains
Indian Veteran Premier League schedule: Full schedule, match timings and venue

Cricket enthusiasts eagerly anticipate the inaugural season of the Indian Veteran Premier League (IVPL) 2024, a tournament set to take them on a nostalgic journey through the annals of cricketing history. Featuring yesteryear cricketing legends and regional stars, the IVPL promises to be an exciting event showcasing the timeless talent of revered players.

Taking place from February 23 to March 3, 2024, the tournament will unfold at the prestigious Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex in Greater Noida, serving as the battleground for six formidable teams.

Teams and Captains:
Chhattisgarh Warriors (Captain: Munaf Patel)
Mumbai Champions (Captain: Virender Sehwag)
Rajasthan Legends (Captain: Praveen Kumar)
Red Carpet Delhi (Captain: Herschelle Gibbs)
Telangana Tigers (Captain: Chris Gayle)
VVIP Uttar Pradesh (Captain: Suresh Raina)

Telangana Tigers vs Rajasthan Legends at 2 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
VVIP Uttar Pradesh vs Red Carpet Delhi at 7 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida

Telangana Tigers vs VVIP Uttar Pradesh at 2 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
Chhattisgarh Warriors vs Mumbai Champions at 7 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
February 27
Red Carpet Delhi vs Mumbai Champions at 2 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
Telangana Tigers vs Chhattisgarh Warriors at 7 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
February 28
Rajasthan Legends vs Chhattisgarh Warriors at 2 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
VVIP Uttar Pradesh vs Mumbai Champions at 7 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
February 29
VVIP Uttar Pradesh vs Chhattisgarh Warriors at 2 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
Rajasthan Legends vs Red Carpet Delhi at 7 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
March 1
Rajasthan Legends vs Mumbai Champions at 2 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
Red Carpet Delhi vs Telangana Tigers at 7 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
March 2
Semi-final 1 at 2 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
Semi-final 2 at 7 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
March 3
Final at 7 PM, Shaheed Vijay Singh Pathik Sports Complex, Greater Noida
