Match Preview:

In the second T20I of Bangladesh’s tour to the United States, the USA won by a narrow margin of 6 runs. Batting first, the USA team scored a total of 144 runs for the loss of 6 wickets in their 20 overs. Monank Patel was the top scorer with 42 runs, followed by Aaron Jones with 35 runs and Steven Taylor with 31 runs.

In response, Bangladesh needed 145 runs to win but could only manage to score 138 runs in 19.3 overs, falling short of the target. Despite efforts from Najmul Hossain Shanto, who scored 36 runs, and Shakib Al Hasan and Towhid Hridoy, the team couldn’t chase down the score. The USA bowlers, led by Ali Khan who took 3 wickets, restricted Bangladesh’s batting lineup. Saurabh Netravalkar and Shadley van Schalkwyk also played crucial roles, taking 2 wickets each. This collective effort ensured the USA’s victory by defending their total successfully, securing a 6-run win in the match.
