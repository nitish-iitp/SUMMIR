All You Need To Know about Rwanda Cricket Association Women’s T20 2024 League

The forthcoming 2024 edition of the Rwanda Cricket Association (RCA) Women’s Twenty20 (T20) League will feature five teams competing in a total of 13 games from the 23rd of March to the 18th of May at the Gahanga International Cricket Stadium in Kigali. From squads and schedule to broadcasting details and format, below is everything you need to know about the tournament.

Format: Rwanda Cricket Association Women’s T20 2024

The tournament will follow a round-robin format, where each team will compete against the others once in league games. Following this, the top four teams from the five-team points table will progress to the semi-finals, with the winners advancing to the final. Notably, a third-place playoff game has not been scheduled at this time.
