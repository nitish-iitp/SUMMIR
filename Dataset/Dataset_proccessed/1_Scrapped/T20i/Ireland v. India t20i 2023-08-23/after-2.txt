India vs Ireland 3rd T20I Highlights: Match abandoned due to rain, IND win series 2-0
By 
HT Sports Desk
Aug 23, 2023 10:34 PM IST
India vs Ireland 3rd T20I Highlights: The third T20I between India and Ireland has been called off due to rain. India win three-match series 2-0
India vs Ireland 3rd T20I Highlights

India vs Ireland 3rd T20I Highlights: The third T20I between India and Ireland has been abandoned due to rain and wet ground conditions. With this India bag the three-match series 2-0. India had won the first T20I by two runs as per the DLS method, and secured a 33-run victory both played at The Village, Dublin. Team India will now be seen in action in the Asia Cup, which gets underway next week. India start their campaign against Pakistan on September 2, following which they take on Nepal two days later.
