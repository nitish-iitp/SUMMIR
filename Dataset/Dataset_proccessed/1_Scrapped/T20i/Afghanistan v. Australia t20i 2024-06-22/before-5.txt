T20 World Cup 2024 Super 8 Schedule: Date, Match Time, TV Channels & Live Streaming details
Published - Jun 17, 2024, 04:15 UTC | Updated - Jun 18, 2024, 12:33 UTC
by Anirudh Singh 
All eight teams for the next round of ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 have been confirmed.
Bangladesh became the final team to join the elite group.

The stage is set for an intense battle for the T20 World Cup 2024 title as eight teams have secured their spots in the highly anticipated Super 8 stage.

India, Australia, Afghanistan, West Indies, South Africa, USA, England and Bangladesh emerged victorious from the respective group stages to continue their T20 World Cup journey.

England’s qualification was confirmed by a combination of their win against Namibia and Scotland’s subsequent loss to Australia on June 15. Bangladesh became the final team to join the elite group with a crucial 21-run victory over Nepal in Match 37.

Super 8 Format: Round robin battles

The Super 8 stage pits these eight teams in a round-robin format, divided into two groups – Group 1 and Group 2. Each side will face the other three teams in their respective groups, with the top two sides from each group progressing to the semifinals.

Group 1 and Group 2: A look at the match-ups

Group 1: India, Afghanistan, Australia, Bangladesh
Group 2: USA, England, West Indies, South Africa

The action kicks off on June 19th with USA taking on South Africa. Cricket fans can look forward to a rematch of the 2023 World Cup final as India takes on Australia on June 24th.
