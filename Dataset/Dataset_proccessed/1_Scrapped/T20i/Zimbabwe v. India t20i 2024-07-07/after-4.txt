India vs Zimbabwe Highlights, IND vs ZIM 2nd T20I: Abhishek Sharma smashed a quick-fire century to set the foundation for a comfortable 100-run victory for India over Zimbabwe in the second T20I at the Harare Sports Club. July 7, 2024 8:10 PM IST
'If it's the first ball, I will go after it': Abhishek Sharma

Abhishek Sharma was elated with his match-winning century and said he felt it was his day.

"I think it was pretty much good performance by me, the defeat we had yesterday we didn't have it easy for us. I felt today it was my day and I made it count. I feel T20 is about momentum and I took it to the end," Abhishek said after receiving Player of the Match.

The left-handed batter also asserted that he won't shy away from start hitting from the first ball if it comes in his arc.

"Special mention to the coach and team management, I always feel that as a youngster if it's your day, if you have to express yourself. We were talking after every ball and he said to me that you should take it on. I always believe in my ability, if there's in my arc and even if it's the first ball, I will go after it," he added.

July 7, 2024 8:07 PM IST
'Always good to have more options rather…': Shubman Gill

Shubman Gill heaped praised on Abhishek Sharma to take on the bowlers in the powerplay and gave India a positive start.

"Very happy, great to be back on the winning note back again. With the way Abhishek and Rutu batted, it was not easy especially in the powerplay, with the ball moving around," Shubman said after the win.

The Indian skipper was also elated to bounce back in an emphatic fashion after losing the series opener.

"Yesterday, it was more about not able to handle the pressure, it's a young side and it was their first international exposure. We have got three matches to go. It's always good to have more options rather than not having options," he added.
