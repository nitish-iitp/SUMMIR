By 
HT Sports Desk
Oct 17, 2022 1:15 PM IST
India vs Australia, warm-up match highlights T20 World Cup 2022: Australia get off to strong start in response to India's 186/7. However, Mohammed Shami's final-over heroics guided India to a 6-run win over Australia. 
KL Rahul slammed a quick-fire half-century against Australia

India vs Australia, warm-up match highlights T20 World Cup 2022: KL Rahul and Suryakumar Yadav smashed half-centuries as India piled a stiff 186/7 in the warm-up match against Australia at The Gabba in Brisbane on Monday. In response, Aaron Finch and Mitch Marsh got Australia to a strong start in the run chase. They got Australia past 50 in 5 overs, before Bhuvneshwar Kumar removed Mitchell Marsh on 35 in the final over of the powerplay. Finch played a crucial knock for the hosts. However, his batting heroics went in vain as Mohammed Shami-inspired India secured a 6-run win over Australia. ...Read More
