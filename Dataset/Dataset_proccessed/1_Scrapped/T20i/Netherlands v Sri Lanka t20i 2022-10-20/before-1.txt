CUBLNGTON CRICKET CLUB AGM 2021 TUESDAY 22nd November, 2022 7.30pm at the Biggs Pavilion, the Orchard Ground
AGENDA
The business of the Annual General Meeting shall be to:
a) Confirm the minutes of the previous Annual General Meeting and
any General Meetings held since the last Annual General Meeting
b) Receive the draft or audited accounts for the year from the Treasurer
c) Receive the annual report of the Committee from the Chairman
d) Elect a Chairman
e) Elect the Officers of the Club
f) Review club subscription rates and agree them for the forthcoming year
g) Consider any proposed alterations to the Constitution
h) Transact such other business received in writing by the
Chairman from members 14 days prior to the meeting and included on
the agenda.
