South Africa's cricket team, led by Aiden Markram, is set for the ICC T20 World Cup with a strong squad announcement creating excitement. The Proteas boast talent in batting, all-rounders, and bowlers, gearing up to face Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Netherlands, and Nepal in Group D.
South Africa team (X Photo)
NEW DELHI: South Africa's cricket team is getting ready for the ICC T20 World Cup, and the announcement of the squad has stirred up a lot of excitement.
Aiden Markram is set to lead the Proteas, who boast a strong roster with skills in every area.
South Africa players list for T20 World Cup 2024:



Batters:
Aiden Markram will not only lead the team but also serve as a key anchor in the batting line-up.
He will be joined by the explosive Quinton de Kock, Reeza Hendricks, Heinrich Klaasen, David Miller, along with the promising Ryan Rickelton and Tristan Stubbs.
With such depth and firepower, South Africa's batting order is poised to give opposing bowlers a tough challenge.
All-Rounders:
Adding versatility to their arsenal is Marco Jansen, whose prowess with both bat and ball will be crucial in pivotal moments of the game.

Bowlers:
South Africa boasts a formidable and intimidating bowling lineup. Leading the charge are the pace duo, Kagiso Rabada and Anrich Nortje.
Supporting them, you'll find the expert spin of Tabraiz Shamsi and Keshav Maharaj.
Emerging talents like Ottniel Baartman, Gerald Coetzee, and Bjorn Fortuin further bolster the attack. South Africa's bowlers have the capabilities to trouble any batting order.
Also see: T20 World Cup 2024 Schedule || T20 World Cup Points Table
Group D Fixtures:
Placed in Group D, South Africa will compete against Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, the Netherlands, and Nepal, promising a thrilling display of cricketing prowess. The Proteas will kick off their campaign against Sri Lanka on June 3rd in New York, setting the stage for an electrifying start to their T20 World Cup journey.

T20 World Cup 2024 Super Eight Matches Involving South Africa
1. T20 World Cup 2024 Super Eight Match 1:
- Date: June 19, 2024, Wed - 08:00 PM
- Teams: United States vs South Africa
- Venue: Sir Vivian Richards Stadium, North Sound, Antigua and Barbuda
South Africa faces a must-win match against a spirited USA in their Super Eight opener of the T20 World Cup. Despite their unbeaten run, South Africa’s batting has struggled, failing to surpass 120 runs in all four games on challenging tracks. Their top order, including Quinton de Kock and Aiden Markram, is under pressure to perform. Anrich Nortje, previously a concern, has excelled with nine wickets, forming a formidable attack with Ottneil Baartman. The USA, an eclectic team, has played aggressively and seeks another upset after defeating Pakistan. They hope for skipper Monank Patel’s return from injury.
