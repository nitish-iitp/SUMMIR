NED vs CAN 1st T20I Dream11 Prediction: A six-match tri-series will be played between the Netherlands, Canada and the United States. It will run from August 23 to Wednesday, August 28.

By Himanshu Badola
Fri, 23 Aug 2024 04:55 PM (IST)
Source:JND
Netherlands players in a frame. (Photo: @KNCBcricket/X)

NED vs CAN 1st T20I Dream11 Prediction

Netherlands will be up against Canada in the first T20I of the tri-series at the Sportpark Maarschalkerweerd, Utrecht on Friday, August 23.

A six-match tri-series will be played between the Netherlands, Canada and the United States. It will run from August 23 to Wednesday, August 28.

The Netherlands will play four matches in the competition, the United States will play five whereas Canada will feature in three games.

USA had a commendable outing in the T20 World Cup 2024 a couple of months ago where they managed to reach the Super 8 stage. They defeated Pakistan in their group-stage match.

On the other hand, the Netherlands and Canada were eliminated from the competition in the group stage.

TOSS: The match toss between the Netherlands and Canada will take place at 7:00 PM IST.

Time: August 23, 7:30 PM IST.

Venue: Sportpark Maarschalkerweerd, Utrecht


NED vs CAN 1st T20I 2024 Live Streaming Details: The live streaming of the match in India will be helmed by the FanCode app and website.
