Ireland Tour Of Afghanistan: When, Where To Watch Afghanistan Vs Ireland 1st T20I Live In India On TV, Online
Check below everything you need to know about Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I of the three-match AFG vs IRE T20I series live streaming, telecast details.
By : ABP News Bureau | Updated at : 14 Mar 2024 04:54 PM (IST)
Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match will take place at the Sharjah Cricket Stadium in Sharjah.
Source : @ACBofficials / X

Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I live streaming, telecast, venue, date, time: Afghanistan is set to face Ireland in a three-match T20I series, beginning from Friday, March 15, in Sharjah. Afghanistan secured a 2-0 victory in the recently concluded ODI series against Ireland, with one match being abandoned due to rain. 

The upcoming AFG vs IRE T20I series marks the return of the star leg-spinner Rashid Khan to the national team after successfully recovering from injury.

AFG vs IRE head-to-head record: In the head-to-head encounters between Afghanistan and Ireland in T20 internationals, Afghanistan has emerged victorious in 16 out of the 23 matches played so far, while Ireland has secured wins in 7 matches.

When will Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match be played?

AFG vs IRE date: Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match will take place Friday (March 15).

Where will Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match be played?

AFG vs IRE venue: Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match will take place at the Sharjah Cricket Stadium in Sharjah.

At what time will Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match be played?

AFG vs IRE timings: Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match will kickstart at 09:30 pm IST.

When, where to watch Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match live streaming in India?

AFG vs IRE live streaming: Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match live streaming in India will be available on FanCode app and website.

When, where to watch Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match live telecast in India?

AFG vs IRE live telecast: There won't be any live telecast for Afghanistan vs Ireland 1st T20I match in India.

Afghanistan vs Ireland T20I series - Complete schedule

Match	Date	Venue	Time
AFG vs IRE 1st T20I	March 15, 2024	Sharjah Cricket Stadium	9:00 PM IST
AFG vs IRE 2nd T20I	March 17, 2024	Sharjah Cricket Stadium	9:00 PM IST
AFG vs IRE 3rd T20I	March 18, 2024	Sharjah Cricket Stadium	9:00 PM IST
