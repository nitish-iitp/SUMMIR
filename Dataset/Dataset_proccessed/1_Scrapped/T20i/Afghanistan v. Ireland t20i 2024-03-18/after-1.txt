With the series currently tied at 1-1, the victor of the upcoming match will lift the trophy.

Afghanistan vs Ireland in UAE, 2024, 3rd T20I:
Date and Time: March 18, 2024, 08:00 pm local time/04:00 pm GMT/ 09:30 pm IST
Venue: Sharjah Cricket Stadium, Sharjah
Sharjah Cricket Stadium Pitch Report:

The Sharjah Cricket Stadium, know for its short boundaries, hosts a pitch that entices batsmen to unleash powerful shots. Despite the pitch’s slower nature, the closeness of the boundaries enhances the probability of achieving substantial scores. This unique combination frequently leads captains to choose to chase after winning the toss, seeking to capitalize on the assistance spinners can receive from a wearing pitch in the later stages of the match.
