CWU trumpet player wins national soloist competition

Corinne “Corie” Williamson knew she’d found a piece of her heart in music, but she never expected how far it would take her.

The Central Washington University senior won the undergraduate solo division at the National Trumpet Competition in Texas last month, and all she had to say was, “It doesn’t feel real.”
