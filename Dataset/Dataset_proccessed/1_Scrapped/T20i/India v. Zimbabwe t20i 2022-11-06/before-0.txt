IND vs ZIM Dream11 Prediction Tips & Fantasy Team

The 42nd match of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2022 will see India (IND) take on Zimbabwe (ZIM) at the Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG) on Sunday, November 6. Ahead of the match, here's everything you need to know about the IND vs ZIM Dream11 prediction.

India have done well in the ICC T20 World Cup, winning three out of their four matches. They have clicked as a unit with the likes of Virat Kohli and Arshdeep Singh standing out with the bat and ball, respectively. A win would assure India of a place in the semi-finals as the group toppers.

Zimbabwe, meanwhile, have blown hot and cold in the tournament. While they have lost their last two games against the Netherlands and Bangladesh, Zimbabwe's only win in the Super 12 phase came against Pakistan. While they will start as underdogs, Zimbabwe will bank on their pace attack to pave the way for a surprise win.

IND vs ZIM pitch report for ICC T20 World Cup 2022, Match 42

The pitch at the MCG is a decent one to bat on with the average score being 160. Most of the scheduled matches at the venue have been impacted by rain but the pacers have enjoyed the conditions, picking up over 60 percent of the wickets. Wickets in hand will be key, with the dimensions of the ground also bound to come into play. Chasing could be the preferred option, with the pitch not likely to change much during the course of the game.

IND vs ZIM Dream11 match top picks
Top Wicketkeeper Pick
Regis Chakabva (48 matches, 682 runs, SR: 123.77)

Regis Chakabva has been moved around in the Zimbabwean batting unit but to little avail. However, Chakabva can hold his own against both pace and spin with a strike rate of 123.77 in 48 matches. With Dinesh Karthik batting lower down the order, Chakabva is a top pick for your IND vs ZIM Dream11 prediction team.

Top Batter Pick
Virat Kohli (4 matches, 220 runs, Average: 220.00)

Virat Kohli is the leading runscorer in the tournament at the time of writing with 220 runs in four matches. He has three unbeaten fifties to his name, holding him in good stead. Given his form and batting prowess, Kohli is another top pick for your IND vs ZIM Dream11 prediction team.

Top All-rounder Pick
Sikandar Raza (7 matches, 185 runs, 9 wickets)

Sikandar Raza is Zimbabwe's go-to player with both the bat and ball, with 185 runs and nine wickets to his name in seven matches. Raza is striking at nearly 150 with the bat and has used his variations to good effect on the bowling front. With the conditions likely to suit Raza, he is a must-have in your IND vs ZIM Dream11 prediction team.

Top Bowler Pick
Arshdeep Singh (4 matches, 9 wickets, Average: 14.67)

Arshdeep Singh has been India's best pacer in this T20 World Cup, picking up nine wickets in just four matches. He has impressed in the powerplay and death overs, averaging just 14.67 with the ball so far. With Arshdeep in brilliant form and Zimbabwe's top-order looking scratchy, he is a good addition to your IND vs ZIM Dream11 prediction team.
