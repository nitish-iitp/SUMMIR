About Heena Hotchandhani:

NAME – Heena Hotchandhani
DOB – October 22, 2000
ROLE – All-rounder
BATTING STYLE – Right-hand bat
BOWLING STYLE – Slow left-arm orthodox
TEAMS – United Arab Emirates Women

Heena Hotchandani for UAE. PC: Female Cricket
Bio:-

Heena Hotchandani’s journey began with her debut in Women’s Twenty20 International cricket in July 2018, when she faced the Netherlands during the ICC Women’s World Twenty20 Qualifier. Since then, she has featured in 8 innings with the bat and accumulated a total of 42 runs and has also demonstrated prowess in bowling, with 15 wickets to her name in just 11 innings. She has proven to be a valuable asset in the bowling department, maintaining an impressive economy rate of 3.54.

Her standout performance came against Thailand on February 16, 2014, where she achieved her best bowling figures of 4 wickets for just 8 runs. Hotchandani’s all-round skills make her a key player in the UAE women’s cricket team, contributing significantly with both bat and ball and enhancing the team’s overall performance.
