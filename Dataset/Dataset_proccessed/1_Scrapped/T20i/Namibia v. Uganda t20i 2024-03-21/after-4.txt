In-Depth TOZO NC7 Review: Are These Affordable ANC Earbuds Worth It?

Finding the perfect pair of earbuds that offer great sound quality, comfort, and durability at an affordable price can be a challenge. The TOZO NC7 earbuds claim to provide all of these features and more, making them an attractive option for those on a budget.

In this review, we’ll dive into the various aspects of the TOZO NC7 earbuds, from design and sound quality to battery life and connectivity, to help you decide if they’re the right fit for you.

Design and Build Quality

Design	Sleek, minimalist, and eye-catching streamlined appearance
Build Quality	Durable construction with IPX6 waterproof rating
Comfort and Fit	Ergonomic design with soft silicone ear caps for a secure and comfortable fit

Design aesthetics

The TOZO NC7 earbuds boast a sleek, minimalist design with an eye-catching streamlined appearance that exudes simplicity and modesty. The smooth and round charging case is aesthetically pleasing and easy to carry and provides a beautiful touch feeling. Available in multiple color options, these earbuds are designed to suit various personal styles.

Build quality

Featuring an IPX6 waterproof rating, the TOZO NC7 earbuds can withstand water depths of 1.5 to 30 meters without leakage or performance issues, allowing users to enjoy their music in any environment. This high level of water resistance adds durability and longevity to the earbuds.

Comfort and fit

With an ergonomic design based on extensive data on the most suitable cochlear shapes, the TOZO NC7 earbuds fit the curve of the inner contour of the ear perfectly. The round, soft silicone ear caps provide additional wearing stability and comfort during use. Whether you’re running or jumping, these earbuds will stay securely in place, avoiding common issues like ear congestion often associated with the long-term use of truly wireless Bluetooth earphones.

Sound Quality
Audio performance

The TOZO NC7 earbuds deliver an impressive audio performance, especially considering their affordable price. Let’s break down the different aspects of their sound quality:

Bass response The earbuds produce deep and powerful bass, ensuring an immersive listening experience, whether you’re enjoying hip-hop, electronic, or pop music. The bass doesn’t overpower the mids and highs, maintaining a well-balanced sound signature.
Mid-range performance The mid-range is clear and detailed, allowing vocals and instruments to shine through without being overshadowed by the bass. This makes the TOZO NC7 earbuds suitable for various genres, from rock and jazz to podcasts and audiobooks.
Treble clarity The treble on these earbuds is crisp and articulate, providing a bright and lively sound without being harsh or fatiguing. The high frequencies are well-represented, ensuring that cymbals, high-pitched instruments, and vocal harmonies are accurately reproduced.
Soundstage and imaging

The TOZO NC7 earbuds offer a surprisingly spacious soundstage for in-ear headphones, creating an immersive audio experience that makes you feel like you’re in the middle of a live performance. The imaging is accurate, allowing you to easily pinpoint the location of different instruments and vocals within the mix.

Modern Appearance and Good Battery Life

The TOZO NC7 All-Function Hybrid Active Noise Cancelling Wireless Earbuds boast a modern appearance and compact design that make them a great companion for anyone who loves to listen to music on the go.

Their sleek and minimalist look, coupled with their lightweight and comfortable fit, makes them perfect for daily use. But that’s not all that makes them stand out. The TOZO NC7s also have an impressive battery life, lasting up to 10.5 hours on a single charge, which is more than enough for a long day out or even a transatlantic flight.

With an attractive price point, these earbuds represent an excellent value and are one of the best budget noise-cancelling earbuds under $50. All of these features combine to make the TOZO NC7 earbuds the perfect choice for anyone looking for high-quality wireless earbuds that deliver great sound, long battery life, and a stylish and contemporary design.

Perfect for Working Out and Listening to Music

The TOZO NC7 earbuds are perfect for those who enjoy an active lifestyle. They are stable, so they won’t fall out during a workout, and they deliver exceptional sound quality that immerses you in your music.

The earbuds’ compact design and long battery life make them a great option for people who like to listen to music while jogging or working out. You won’t have to worry about the earbuds slipping out of your ears while you move, and you’ll be able to enjoy a healthy dose of bass, coupled with clear high and low notes.

Additionally, the TOZO NC7’s balanced sound signature and rich vocals make them a great option for casual listening sessions as well. Overall, the TOZO NC7 earbuds are an excellent choice for anyone who wants the convenience of wireless earbuds without compromising on quality.

Balanced Bass and Clear Vocals

Moving on to the audio performance, the TOZO NC7 earbuds have a precise acoustic tuning that accurately adjusts every detail to achieve a perfect balance of bass, midrange, and treble. The overall sound signature has solid bass, good forward vocal and highs, making them a great choice for music enthusiasts.

Additionally, the bass is powerful, and midrange is mellow, resulting in a well-balanced sound that is perfect for all genres of music. What’s more, the channel separation is excellent, and the produced soundstage is above average for TWS earphones. The imaging is better than expected, further enhancing the overall listening experience.

The vocals on TOZO NC7 earbuds sound clearer and more natural than other Tozo earbuds, even when there is noise around. Overall, the balanced bass and clear vocals on TOZO NC7 earbuds make them an impressive choice for all music lovers who seek high-quality sound at an affordable price.

Comparison to competitors

When comparing the TOZO NC7 to other earbuds in a similar price range, such as the TOZO T10, there are some noticeable differences in terms of sound quality.

Bass response

While both the NC7 and T10 provide powerful bass, the NC7 has a more controlled and balanced bass response. The T10, on the other hand, tends to emphasize bass, which may appeal to bass lovers but could potentially overpower the mid-range in certain tracks.

Mid-range performance

The TOZO NC7 outperforms the T10 in terms of mid-range clarity and detail. The NC7’s well-defined mid-range allows vocals and instruments to stand out, while the T10’s mid-range can sometimes be overshadowed by its bass emphasis.

Treble clarity

Both earbuds offer crisp and clear treble, but the NC7 has a slightly more articulate and brighter high-frequency response compared to the T10. This results in a more lively and engaging listening experience, especially for genres that rely heavily on high-pitched instruments and vocal harmonies.

Soundstage and imaging

The TOZO NC7 earbuds have a more spacious soundstage and better imaging than the T10. The NC7’s ability to create an immersive audio experience and accurately represent the placement of instruments and vocals within the mix is superior to the T10.

Overall, while the TOZO T10 is a solid option for those looking for a bass-heavy sound signature, the TOZO NC7 offers a more balanced and detailed audio performance, making it a better choice for a wider range of genres and listeners. Additionally, the NC7’s superior soundstage and imaging provide an added level of immersion that sets it apart from its sibling, the T10.

Frequently Asked Questions
Does the TOZO NC7 Have Good Warranty Coverage?

The TOZO NC7 earbuds come with a 30-day return window and a 1-year limited warranty against defects. This warranty coverage is fairly standard for budget earbuds, offering some protection and peace of mind for customers in the event of any manufacturing issues.

Are TOZO headphones worth it?

Yes, TOZO headphones, particularly the NC7 model, offer a great combination of design, comfort, and sound quality at an affordable price, making them a worthwhile purchase for budget-conscious consumers.

Can you take a shower with Tozo earbuds?

While the Tozo NC7 earbuds have an IPX6 water resistance rating, it’s not recommended to use them in the shower, as the water pressure and steam may potentially cause damage.

Do Tozo earbuds have good bass?

Yes, Tozo NC7 earbuds provide powerful and deep bass without overpowering the mids and highs, ensuring a well-balanced sound signature suitable for various music genres.

What is the IP rating of Tozo NC7?

The Tozo NC7 earbuds have an IPX6 rating, providing a high level of water resistance, suitable for various environments, and protecting against damage from splashes and sweat.

Why do my TOZO earbuds keep falling out?

If your TOZO earbuds are falling out, try adjusting the ear tips and wings to find a more secure and comfortable fit for your ear canal, ensuring better stability during use.

Why is one of my TOZO earbuds so quiet?

If one of your TOZO earbuds is quieter than the other, it may be due to connectivity issues or accumulated debris blocking the sound. Try resetting the earbuds and cleaning the speaker grills gently to resolve the issue.

Final Verdict: Is the NC7 Worth Buying?

After carefully evaluating the TOZO NC7 earbuds in terms of design, comfort, sound quality, active noise cancellation, battery life, and connectivity, it’s clear that they offer a lot of value for their affordable price. With their stylish minimalist design, ergonomic fit, and impressive audio performance, these earbuds cater to a wide range of listeners and music genres.

While there are a few limitations, such as the moderate effectiveness of the ANC and the sensitive touch controls, these drawbacks are relatively minor compared to the overall quality and feature the NC7 earbuds provide.

Overall, if you’re looking for a pair of affordable, comfortable, and great-sounding true wireless earbuds with some added features like ANC, the TOZO NC7 is definitely worth considering. These earbuds stand out as an excellent choice for those on a budget who still want a high-quality listening experience.
