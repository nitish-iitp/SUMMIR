Zimbabwe tour Bangladesh for a T20I series from May 3. Here is all you need to know about where to watch the BAN vs ZIM 2024 T20Is live across TV and digital, including streaming and TV channel details.

Subscribe to the Wisden Cricket YouTube channel for post-match analysis, player interviews, and much more.

The first three matches will be played on May 3, 5 and 7 at Zahur Ahmed Chowdhury Stadium in Chattogram. Shere Bangla National Stadium in Dhaka will host the remaining two matches on May 10 and 12.

Here’s the full schedule of the Bangladesh-Zimbabwe 2024 T20I series

Najmul Hossain Shanto will lead a 15-member Bangladesh squad, while Sikandar Raza will captain the tourists.

In the history of T20Is, both sides have played against each other in 20 matches so far, with Bangladesh winning 13 times and Zimbabwe prevailing on seven occasions.

The teams will reconvene again in the format for the first time since Bangladesh beat Zimbabwe by 3 runs at the 2022 T20 World Cup in Brisbane, thanks to Taskin Ahmed’s 3-19

T Sports will televise the series in Bangladesh, while T Sports App will provide live-streaming services.
