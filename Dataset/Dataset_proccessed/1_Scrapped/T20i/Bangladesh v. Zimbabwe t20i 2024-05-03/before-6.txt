As the Mumbai Indians gear up to battle the Kolkata Knight Riders in a much-anticipated showdown, anticipation reaches fever pitch. In their latest game against Lucknow Super Giants, Mumbai Indians faced a tough challenge as they aimed to recover from previous losses. While batting first, they struggled. While batting in the second innings, Phil Salt showcased his masterclass again. He played a knock of 68 runs in 33 balls. Sunil Narine and Rinku Singh were dismissed early. Shreyas Iyer was not out on 33 runs and Venkatesh Iyer was unbeaten on 26 runs. The duo secured the win of 7 wickets.
