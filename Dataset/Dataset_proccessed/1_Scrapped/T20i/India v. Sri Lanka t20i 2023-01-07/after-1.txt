India vs Sri Lanka, 3rd T20I highlights: Suryakumar's sublime century guides Pandya and Co. to series win at Rajkot
By 
HT Sports Desk
, New Delhi
Jan 7, 2023 10:46 PM IST
India vs Sri Lanka 3rd T20I highlights: Leading the batting charge against Dasun Shanaka-led Sri Lanka in the 3rd T20I at Rajkot, Suryakumar Yadav smashed his 3rd T20I century to help India post a massive total of 228/5 in 20 overs on Saturday. In reply, Sri Lanka only mustered 137 in 16.4 overs as Hardik Pandya-led India crushed the visitors by 91 runs to clinch the series 2-1 at the Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium. 
India's Arshdeep Singh, center, celebrates the dismissal of Sri Lanka's Pathum Nissanka, left, during the third T20 match

India vs Sri Lanka 3rd T20I highlights: Suryakumar Yadav played a sensational knock against Sri Lanka in the third and final T20I of the bilateral series at Rajkot on Saturday. Launching an all-out attack against the Sri Lanka bowlers, Suryakumar slammed his third T20I century to help Hardik Pandya-led India post a challenging total (228-5) in the 20-over contest. Arshdeep Singh's improved show in the series decider between India and Sri Lanka paved the way for the hosts to register a comfortable win over the visitors. Pacer Arshdeep bagged three wickets while spinner Yuzvendra Chahal (2) shared four wickets with speedster Umran Malik (2) as Sri Lanka folded for 137 in 16.4 overs. Under the leadership of all-rounder Pandya, Rohit Sharma-less Team India registered a massive 91-run win over Sri Lanka to seal the series 2-1 at Rajkot.

Jan 7, 2023 10:40 PM IST
IND vs SL, 3rd T20I LIVE updates: No surprises! Suryakumar collects Player of The Match award

Suryakumar Yadav played a sublime knock of 112* off 51 balls to make sure India outclass Sri Lanka in the T20I series decider at the Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium. The middle-order batter has been named the Player of the Match for his batting masterclass. "It's really important to put pressure on yourself when you are preparing for a game. If you do that in practice, it gets a little easier while playing the game. There is a lot of hard work involved but it's about doing quality practice sessions. You should know what your game is and prepare accordingly. Boundaries behind are like 50-60 meters, so I was targeting them," Suryakumar said after the match. 

Jan 7, 2023 10:25 PM IST
IND vs SL, 3rd T20I LIVE updates: Chahal hails Suryakumar after India's massive win over Sri Lanka

"After the first match, I had a discussion with the coaches - on what pace and line to be bowling. (On Suryakumar Yadav) He's batting at a different level and I'm happy that I'm in the same team as him. Axar is a perfect package. It's tough to bowl to him as well as he's a clean hitter," spinner Chahal said after the match.
