Asia Cup 2022 at FairPlay Club

The Asian Cricket Council Asia Cup is a one-day international (ODI) and twenty-twenty international (T20I) cricket tournament for men. It was founded at the same time as the Asian Cricket Council (ACC), which was introduced in 1983 as a “tool” to promote goodwill among Asian countries. It was initially intended to take place every 24 months. The winning team is proclaimed Asia’s champions in the only cricket continental competition, the Asia Cup. The ODI and T20I formats are alternated every two years.

Asia Cup 2022: Key Details

The 15th Asia Cup cricket competition, which will take place in the United Arab Emirates in August and September of 2022, is known as the Asia Cup 2022. The event’s initial September 2020 date had to be postponed due to the COVID-19 epidemic. Then, it was slated to occur in Sri Lanka in June 2021, and then it was put off once again. Pakistan was slated to host the competition after winning the right to host the 2022 edition. But the ACC announced in October 2021 that Sri Lanka would host the event in 2022, while Pakistan would host the 2023 edition.
