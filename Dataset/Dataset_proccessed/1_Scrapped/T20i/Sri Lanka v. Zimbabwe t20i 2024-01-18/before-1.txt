With the Asia Cup and Twenty20 (T20) World Cup slated for later this year, the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) has made an exciting announcement, revealing a six-team ‘National Women’s T20 Tournament.’ From format, prize money, and broadcasting details to squads and schedule, here’s everything you need to know about this 17-day competition.

All You Need To Know about Pakistan’s National Women’s T20 Tournament 2023
Format: Pakistan’s National Women’s T20 Tournament 2023

The league games of the tournament will follow a double round-robin format, with each team competing in 10 matches. The top two seeded teams from this stage will progress to the final, scheduled for January 31, 2024.

Broadcast:

The 10 matches held at the Shoaib Akhtar Stadium will be live-streamed on the PCB’s YouTube channel, while scorecards for all matches will be available on the PCB’s match center web portal.

Prize Money:

The winning team will receive PKR one million, with the runners-up securing PKR 0.5 million. The player of the tournament will be honored with PKR 50,000, and the player of the match in every game will receive PKR 20,000. Additionally, the tournament’s top performers – best batter, best bowler, and best wicket-keeper – will be rewarded with PKR 25,000 each.
