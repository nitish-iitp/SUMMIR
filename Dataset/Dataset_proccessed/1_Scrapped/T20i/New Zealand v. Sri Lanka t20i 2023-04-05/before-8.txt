The FairBreak Twenty20 (T20) tournament returns to its home, Hong Kong for the 02 edition that’s scheduled to be played at the Kowloon Cricket Club, Hong Kong from the 3rd to the 16th of April. From fixtures, and broadcast to squads and format, below is all you need to know about the 2023 Hong Kong FairBreak T20 Invitational.

All you need to know about the FairBreak Invitational 2023 | Squad, Schedule, Broadcast

 

The tournament features 6 franchises and each is star-studded with a few of the best international players taking part alongside a few of the best players from associate nations as 90 players feature from as many as 36 territories. The tournament is one of a kind and was well welcomed by fans as the tournament registered a global viewing audience of an estimate of between 30-40 million from its broadcast to 141 territories.

The Tornadoes come into the tournament as defending champions and the format is that for each win teams will be awarded 3 points, a point for a tie, and 0 points for a loss. In case a game is abandoned or there’s no result, the teams share a point each and there’s a bonus point to the team with the highest score after 10 overs of the batting innings as well.
