Ketrina Chingaipe on FemaleCricket.com
Bio:-

Ketrina Chingaipe, born on December 6, 2006, is an all-rounder cricketer representing Malawi. She made her debut at 16 years old on September 2, 2023, against Lesotho during the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup Africa Region Division Two Qualifier at Gaborone (Oval 2). In her impressive debut, she picked a wicket and finished her bowling spell with 1/12.

Since her debut, Chingaipe has featured in 3 T20 International matches, accumulating a total of 16 runs and collecting 4 wickets. Her best bowling figure is 2/26, achieved against Botswana during the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup Africa Region Division Two Qualifier on September 5, 2023, at Gaborone (Oval 1).
