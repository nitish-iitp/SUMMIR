AUS vs WI 3rd T20 Live Score: Catch all the live updates of Australia vs West Indies third T20I from Perth

Australia vs West Indies Highlights, 3rd T20: Riding on the blistering knocks from Andre Russell (71 off 39 balls) and Sherfane Rutherford (67 off 40 balls), West Indies posted 220 for 6. The duo put on 139-run stand for the sixth wicket.

Opting to bat, West Indies were reeling at 17 for 3 with Johsnosn Charles, Kyle Mayers and Nicholas Pooran were back in the hut. Roston Chase (37 off 20 balls) and skipper ROvman Powell (21 off 14 balls) steadied the ship with a crucial 55-run stand.

For Australia, debutant Xavier Bartlett (2/37) was the pick of the bowlers. Jason Behrendoff, Spencer Johnson, Aaron Hardie and Adam Zampa picked up one wicket apiece.

In the chase, the Aussies were off to a positive start with opener David Warner smashing a 25-ball fifty. However, with wickets falling at regular intervals at the other end Warner eventually fell on 81, leaving the middle-order with an uphill task in the chase. Off-spinner Chase was the pick of the bowlers, picking two wickets for 19 as West Indies averted a series whitewash.
