PREVIEW

Thailand will take on Indonesia in the fourth match of the Bhutan T20I Quadrangular 2024 on Sunday, October 20, at the Gelephu International Cricket Ground in Gelephu. It will be the second match of the series for both teams.

Thailand defeated Bhutan in the first game by 77 runs after posting a total of 171 runs for the loss of six wickets. Chaloemwong Chatphaisan was the highest scorer for the team and made 52 runs off 36 deliveries. Sarawut Maliwan was the pick of the bowlers, who picked three wickets for 16 runs in four overs. Chatphaisan won the Player of the Match award for his batting performance.

Indonesia faced Maldives in their first game and elected to bat after winning the toss. They were bundled out for 95 runs in 19.2 overs. Only four batters managed to make a double-digit score for Indonesia. Maldives made 75 runs for the loss of two wickets in 12 overs before the match was interrupted due to rain. Maldives won the match by 23 runs by the DLS method. Gede Arta and Ferdinando Banunaek took one wicket each for Indonesia.
