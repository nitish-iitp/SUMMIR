Saint Lucia to host six thrilling matches of ICC T20 Cricket World Cup

Cricket fans from across the world can anticipate a thrilling display of talent at the popular Daren Sammy Cricket Ground in Saint Lucia.

Thursday, 13th June 2024

Saint Lucia is all set to be a key player in the 9th edition of the ICC Men’s T20 Tournament as it will be hosting six exhilarating matches from June 15 to 24, 2024. The island nation will be elevating the excitement of this cricket extravaganza as one of the scenic and vibrant venues of the Caribbean.

Cricket fans from across the world can anticipate a thrilling display of talent at the popular Daren Sammy Cricket Ground in Saint Lucia.

The island nation is an ideal destination for such events as it is known for its warm hospitality and robust cricketing culture. The island’s avid cricket community has excelled regionally as well as internationally and has positioned its status as a premier location for tournaments.

It is to be noted that the island promises an unforgettable experience for players as well as visitors alike during this prestigious international event.

While sharing the information about the Party Stand Tickets for the matches being held in Saint Lucia, the Tourism Authority shared the prices. The matches will take place on June 15, June 16, June 17, June 19, June 21, and June 24. All these matches will be held at the Daren Sammy Cricket Ground.

The ticket prices for these matches are as follows:

Saturday, June 15, 2024 – Australia vs Scotland ($100 – drinks inclusive)
Sunday, June 16, 2024 – Sri Lanka vs Netherlands ($100 – drinks inclusive)
Monday, June 17, 2024 – West Indies vs Afghanistan ($100 – drinks inclusive)
Wednesday, June 19, 2024 – B1 vs C2 ($100 – drinks inclusive)
Friday, June 21, 2024 – B1 vs D1 ($25)
Monday, June 24, 2024 – B2 vs A1 ($200 – drinks inclusive)

In addition to this, on June 17, the Tourism Ministry is also hosting Out of this World entertainment during which local performers Aidonia, JW, Ricky T and Shemmy J will be entertaining the guests with live music and performances.
