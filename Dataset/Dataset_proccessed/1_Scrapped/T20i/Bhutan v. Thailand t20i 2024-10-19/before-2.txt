Suprit Pradhan of Bhutan receiving the Player of the Match award (credits: X / BhutanCricket)

The Bhutan Quadrangular T20I Series 2024 is set to showcase an exciting clash among four nations: the host country, Bhutan, alongside Thailand, Maldives, and Indonesia. The upcoming T20 series will kick off on Saturday, October 19, 2024, with all 10 T20I matches taking place at the Gelephu International Cricket Ground, Bhutan.

Leading the Bhutanese squad is Thinley Jamtsho, supported by key players like Suprit Pradhan, Tenzin Wangchuk, and Sonam Yeshey. On the other hand, Austin Lazarus will captain the Thai team, which features talented players such as Akshay Kumar Yadav, Jandre Coetzee, and Mukesh Thakur.

The Indonesian side will be guided by Kadek Gamantika, with promising talents like Padmakar Surve, Anjan Tadarus, Ferdinando Banunaek, and Maxi Koda. Meanwhile, the Maldives team will be led by Azyan Farhath, alongside notable players Umar Adam and Ibrahim Hassan.
