BHU vs TL Match Preview:

The Bhutan T20I Quadrangular 2024 is set to kick off with an exciting clash between Bhutan and Thailand.

Bhutan, led by the experienced Thinley Jamtsho, will be looking to make a strong start to the tournament. The team boasts a talented squad, including Ranjung Dorji, Sherab Loday, Tenjin Rabgey, and Namgay Thinley, who have been in good form.

Thailand, on the other hand, will be led by Austin Lazarus and will be hoping to make a statement with their strong batting lineup, featuring Chaloemwong Chatphaisan, Yodsak Saranonnakkun, and Anucha Kalasi.

Both teams have a history of playing competitive cricket, and a thrilling match is expected.
