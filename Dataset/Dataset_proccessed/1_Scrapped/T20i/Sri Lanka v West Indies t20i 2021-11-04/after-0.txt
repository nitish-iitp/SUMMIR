T20 World Cup, West Indies vs Sri Lanka Highlights: SL win by 20 runs in Abu Dhabi
T20 World Cup, West Indies vs Sri Lanka (WI vs SL) Highlights: Sri Lanka bowed out with a 20-run win, eliminating defending champions West Indies in the process.
By: Sports Desk
Updated: November 4, 2021 23:38 IST
Follow Us
West Indies vs Sri Lanka Highlights: The match was held in Abu Dhabi. (AP)

T20 World Cup, West Indies vs Sri Lanka Highlights: Defending champions West Indies crashed out of the ongoing ICC T20 World Cup after losing to Sri Lanka by 20 runs in a Super 12 Group 1 match in Abu Dhabi on Thursday.

Charith Asalanka smashed 68 off 41 balls as Sri Lanka put on board a challenging 189/3. Asalanka and opener Pathum Nissanka (51 off 41 balls) added 91 runs in 10.1 overs. Asalanka hit eight fours and a six in his innings. In reply, West Indies were restricted on 169/8. Wanindu Hasaranga had the best figures of 2/19 in four overs as Shimron Hetmyer scored an unbeaten 81 off 54 balls.

Brief Scores: Sri Lanka 189/3 in 20 overs (Pathum Nissanka 51, Cjarith Asalanka 68; Andre Russell 2/33). West Indies 169/8 in 20 overs (Nicholas Pooran 46, Shimron Hetmyer 81*, Wanindu Hasaranga 2/19)
