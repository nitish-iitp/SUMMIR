Top Ten Players Of The Oman Cricket Team In ODI’s
Written by Aamir Abbas

The Oman international playing squad is the squad that presents the nation of Oman and it is organized by the Oman Board of cricket, which then become an additional member of the International Cricket Council (ICC) in 2000, and was granted the Associative representation in 2015. The national squad side has played games at Twenty-20 International levels. On 25th April 2019, the nation received One-Day International representation for the only time until 2022. They got victory tournament against Namibia by five wickets in the 2019 ICC World Cricket tournament Division one.

Here are the top 10 best players in Oman national cricket team in ODI's-

10. Kashyap Prajapati

He was born on 12th October 1995. He is a national cricketer. He participates in the national cricket squad. In October 2021, Kashyap Prajapati was registered in the national One-Day International (ODI) team for round six of the ICC Cricket World Cup tournament of 2020–2023.

9. Naseem Khushi

Naseem was chosen in the team's One-Day International (ODI) team for the 2021 Oman quad-Nation league. Naseem made his ODI opening for the nation, opposite the United Arab Emirates, on 6th January 2020.

8. Ayaan Khan

In October 2021, Ayaan got "The player of the match" victory for his outstanding play in a one-day game opposite Mumbai. After October month, Ayaan was chosen in the team for the 2020 ICC Men's T20 World Cup tournament.

7. Khawar Ali

In September 2018, Ali was chosen as the vice-leader of the team for the 2019 Asia Cup Qualifying league. In 2019, Ali was chosen as the vice-leader of Oman's team for the 2018 ICC World Cricket tournament Division four league.

6. Suraj Kumar

He was born on 30th November 1988. He is a national cricketer. He participates as a wicket-keeper batsman for the Omani national playing squad. Suraj participated in the team's debuting schedule of the league, opposite Kenya on 10th November 2018.

5. Mohammad Nadeem

Mohammad participated in his initial games for the team in the 2015 ACC Premier tournament. Nadeem's international opening was opposite Nepal at Selangor Club on 2nd May 2014. Nadeem bowled five overs and faced 22 runs and scored 11 runs while batting in a three-wicket win for the team.

4. Sandeep Goud

He was born on 9th November 1991. Sandeep is a national cricketer. In March 2019, Sandeep was chosen in the nation's Twenty-20 International (T20I) team for the 2017–19 Oman Quad-match Series in Oman.

3. Jatinder Singh

In September 2018, Jatinder was chosen in the nation's team for the 2019 ICC World Cricket tournament Division two league. Jatinder was the top run-getter for the team in the league, with 226 runs in six matches.

2. Zeeshan Maqsood

Zeeshan is a national player and the leader of the national cricket squad. Zeeshan participated in the 2015 ICC World Cricket tournament Division five league. On 12th March 2020, opposite the United States, Zeeshan got his second century in ODI cricket, with 110 runs.

1. Aqib Ilyas

In September 2018, Aqib was chosen as a team for the 2019 ICC World Cricket tournament Division four league. Aqib got his hundred in the nation's match opposite the United States. In November 2018, Aqib was chosen in the nation's squad for the 2019 ACC Emerging Teams Asia Cup series.
