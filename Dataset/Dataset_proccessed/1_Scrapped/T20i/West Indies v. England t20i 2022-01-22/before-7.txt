Bangladesh Premier League 2022 Schedule, Live Telecast In India, Squad, Player List Live Streaming Details. Bangladesh Premier League 2022 is all set to commence on January 21 (Friday). Bangladesh’s franchise T20 league will witness a battle among six teams. Fortune Barishal, Khulna Tigers, Minister Group Dhaka, Chattogram Challengers, Sylhet Sunrisers, and Comilla Victorians are the six teams that will be part of Bangladesh Premier League 2022.

Bangladesh Premier League 2022 begins on January 21 with the first encounter between Chattogram Challengers and Fortune Barishal. A total of 34 matches is scheduled to be played during Bangladesh Premier League 2022. The tournament will take place in a double round-robin format. Hence, during the league stage, every team will face the other team twice.

The top 4 teams in the points table after the completion of the league stage will qualify for playoffs. The final of Bangladesh Premier League 2022 will take place on February 18, 2022. The Bangladesh Premier League 2022 will be hosted across three venues. They are- Shere Bangla National Stadium in Dhaka, Zahur Ahmed Chowdhury Stadium in Chattogram, and Sylhet International Cricket Stadium in Sylhet.

Bangladesh Premier League 2022 Live Telecast and Live Streaming Details

In India:

FanCode has bought the broadcasting rights of Bangladesh Premier League 2022 in India. Hence, fans can check out the FanCode app to watch the live streaming of Bangladesh Premier League 2022 matches in India.

In Bangladesh:

In Bangladesh, T Sports and Gazi TV will present live telecast and live streaming of all matches of the Bangladesh Premier League 2022. Viewers can also switch to the YouTube channel of T Sports to watch the live streaming of Bangladesh Premier League 2022 matches.
