India TV Sports Wrap.

Chennai Super Kings, Rajasthan Royals, Royal Challengers Bengaluru and Delhi Capitals will be in action in the second-last doubleheader day of IPL (Indian Premier League) 2024. With just three playoff spots left for grabs, the fans are expected to be treated with plenty of mouthwatering action. All of that and much more in today's sports wrap.

Today's Top 10 Trending Sports News Stories

Kolkata Knight Riders beat Mumbai Indians to qualify for IPL 2024 playoffs

KKR defeated Mumbai Indians by 18 runs to secure a spot in the IPL 2024 playoffs.

Sunil Narine scales huge T20 milestone, joins Dwayne Bravo and Rashid Khan in elite category

Sunil Narine has become the third player in history to pick 550 wickets in T20 cricket.

Chennai Super Kings to take on Rajasthan Royals

CSK will take the field against Rajasthan in the 61st match of IPL 2024.

RCB to face Delhi Capitals in 62nd game of IPL 2024

Royal Challengers Bengaluru will take on Delhi Capitals in the 62nd match of IPL 2024 on Sunday. 

Rafael Nadal crashes out of Italian Open 2024

Nadal lost 6-1, 6-4 to Hubert Hurkacz in the round of 64 to crash out of the Italian Open.

Burnley relegated from English Premier League

Burnley have been relegated from the English Premier League after a 2-1 loss to Tottenham.

Inter Miami beat Montreal in MLS

Lionel Messi's Inter Miami defeated Montreal 3-2 in a Major League Soccer game.

England women beat Pakistan women to draw first blood in T20I series opener

England women defeated Pakistan by 53 runs in the first T20I.

Zimbabwe choose to field against Bangladesh in fifth T20I

Zimbabwe have elected to field in the fifth and final T20I against Bangladesh.

Axar Patel to lead Delhi Capitals vs RCB

Axar Patel will be leading Delhi against RCB in the 62nd match of IPL 2024 in Rishabh Pant's absence.
