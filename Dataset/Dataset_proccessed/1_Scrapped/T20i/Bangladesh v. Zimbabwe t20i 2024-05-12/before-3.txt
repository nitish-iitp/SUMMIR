In match 58 of the Indian Premier League, Royal Challengers Bengaluru secured a commanding victory over Punjab. Batting first, Bengaluru posted a formidable total of 241/7 in their allotted 20 overs. In response, Rajasthan managed to score 201/8 in their 20 overs. Sanju Samson led the charge with a brilliant knock of 86 runs off 46 balls, supported by Riyan Parag’s 27 and Shubham Dubey’s 25.
