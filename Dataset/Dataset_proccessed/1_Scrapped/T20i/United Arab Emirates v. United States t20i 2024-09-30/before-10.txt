The month of August was filled with several franchise cricket action, The Hundred (Women’s) and the Women’s Caribbean Premier League. However, September will bring exciting international cricket, featuring multiple bilateral series that fans won’t want to miss. In this article, we provide a rundown of the women’s cricket series scheduled for September 2024, which includes bilateral series as well as the warm-up matches for the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2024.

Complete Women’s Cricket Calendar for September 2024 Dates, Fixtures, Timings. PC: Getty
Women’s Schedule in September 2024:
1) Women’s Delhi Premier League: 2 – 8 September

The inaugural edition of the Women’s Delhi Premier League will kick off on September 2. A total of six league matches will be played among the four participating teams in a single round-robin format. All matches will be held at the Arun Jaitley Stadium in Delhi. The top two teams will advance to the final, which will be played on September 8.

2) Namibia Women’s T20I Tri-Series 2024: 6 – 14 September

Namibia will host the United Arab Emirates and Zimbabwe in a tri-series commencing on September 6. A total of nine matches will be played throughout the series, with each team facing the others three times. The tournament will be played in a T20 format, and all matches will be held in Windhoek.

3) England Women’s tour of Ireland: 7 – 17 September

The England women’s side will tour Ireland for a limited series which will begin from September 7. The tour will begin with a three-match ODI series which will be followed by three T20Is. While Belfast will host the three ODIs with Dublin hosting the three T20Is.

4) South Africa Women’s tour of Pakistan: 16 – 20 September

The South Africa women’s team will tour Pakistan for a three-match T20I series, which will kick off on September 16. The series will serve as preparation for both teams ahead of the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2024. All three T20Is will be hosted at the Multan International Cricket Stadium.

5) New Zealand Women’s tour to Australia: 19 – 24 September

Australia will host New Zealand for a T20I series ahead of the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2024. A total of three T20Is will be played in the series. While the first two matches will be played at Mackay, the last T20I will be played at Brisbane. The series will begin on September 19.

6) ICC Women’s T20 World Cup Warm-up matches: Sept 28 to Oct 01

The much awaited, ninth edition of the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup will kick off with the warm-up matches in the UAE. These matches will commence on September 28 and will provide valuable insights, allowing teams to adjust their game plans in preparation for the main event of the Women’s T20 World Cup 2024. A total of 10 warm-up matches will be played.
