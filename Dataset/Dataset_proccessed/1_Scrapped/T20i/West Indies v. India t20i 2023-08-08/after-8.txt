IND vs WI Highlights, 3rd T20: India's Suryakumar Yadav plays a shot against West Indies during the third T20 cricket match at Providence Stadium in Georgetown, Trinidad and Tobago, Tuesday, Aug. 8, 2023. (AP)

India vs West Indies Live Score, 3rd T20:¬†Suryakumar Yadav pelted 83 runs off 44 deliveries to help India claim a seven-wicket win in the third T20I against the West Indies at the Providence Stadium in Guyana. Chasing 160, Hardik Pandya and Tilak Varma sealed the deal with 13 deliveries to spare and keep the series alive with two more games to be played.

Brandon King (42) and Rovman Powell (40) shined bright as the West Indies posted 159/5 batting first against India in the third T20I. For India, Kuldeep Yadav was the pick of the bowlers, managing to pick three wickets to continue his impressive run in white-ball cricket.

Earlier, Rovman Powell won the toss and the West Indies opted to bat first. The hosts made one change to their lineup with Roston Chase coming in for Jason Holder. India on the other hand brought in Kuldeep Yadav for Ravi Bishnoi while Yashasvi Jaiswal made his debut in place of Ishan Kishan. Follow IND vs WI 3rd T20I highlights
