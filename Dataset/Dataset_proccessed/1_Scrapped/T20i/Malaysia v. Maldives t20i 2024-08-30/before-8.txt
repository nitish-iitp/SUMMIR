MAS vs MLD Match Preview:

The ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Asia Qualifier A 2024 is set to begin with a highly anticipated clash between Malaysia and the Maldives. Both teams have assembled strong squads for the tournament, making it a promising match to kick off the competition.

Malaysia, led by Ahmad Zubaidi, boasts a talented batting lineup featuring the likes of Nazmus Sakib, Virandeep Singh, and Khizar Hayat Durrani. Their bowling attack is anchored by Sharvin Muniandy and Muhammad Wafiq.

On the other hand, the Maldives, under the leadership of Abdulla Ibrahim Mabsar, have a promising squad with players like Yoosuf Azyan Farhath, Mohamed Miuvan, and Gedara Piyal Kumara. Their bowling department is led by Mohamed Azzam and Mohamed Rishwan.

Both teams will be aiming to make a strong start to their campaign and set the tone for their tournament. It promises to be a competitive match with plenty of excitement on offer.
