UEFA Euro 2024: Check Euro 2024 Cup Schedule, Date, Venues and Timings in IST for Indian Football Fans

Less than two weeks remain for the start of Euro 2024. And there is a huge excitement among Indian football fans to witness this mega event. While the entire tournament will be held in Germany, the match timings in India will be in late evenings and midnights. Fans will be able to tune into Sony Sports Network and SonyLiv to live stream Euro 2024 games.

Euro Cup 2024 Schedule India Time

Euro 2024 in India starts on 15th June effective at 12:30 AM as hosts Germany take on Scotland in the opening game. For Indian fans there are three slots of timings for the Euro Cup 2024 games. These include evening matches at 6:30 PM and 9:30 PM. Apart from these two evening matches, there will be is another slot for mid night kick offs at 12:30 AM.

After the group stage matches, the rest of the knockout matches will be held at 9:30 PM and 12:30 AM time slots. And ultimately, the two semi finals and the final will be played at 12:30 AM timings slot.
