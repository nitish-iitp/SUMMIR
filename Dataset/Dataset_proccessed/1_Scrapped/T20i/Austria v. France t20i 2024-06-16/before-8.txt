UEFA Euro 2024: Full Schedule, Dates, Timings, Venues & More
Ahead of the UEFA Euro 2024, here’s a look at the tournament’s full schedule, groups, dates and more:

UEFA Euro 2024: UEFA Euro 2024 is set to begin on June 15 in Germany, with the host nation facing Scotland in the opening Group A match. 24 countries, divided into six groups, will compete in the tournament, with 51 matches scheduled across 10 stadiums in Germany. The final will be held on July 15. Defending champions Italy are in Group B and will start their campaign against Albania on June 16. In the previous edition of the tournament in 2020, Italy won the title by defeating England in the final on penalties.
