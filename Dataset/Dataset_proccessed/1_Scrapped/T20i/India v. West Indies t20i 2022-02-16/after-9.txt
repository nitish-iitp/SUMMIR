India vs West Indies 1st T20I 2022 Highlights
by
CricHighlightsVidz
Published on :
2/16/2022 10:58:00 PM
Watch the highlights of India vs West Indies 1st T20I 2022 - West Indies tour of India 3-match Twenty20 international series of the 1st T20I match played between West Indies and India at Eden Gardens, Kolkata in 16th February 2022.



Ravi Bishnoi strikes a pose after picking up a wicket © BCCI


Debutant Ravi Bishnoi's impressive double-strike in an over on international debut before Rohit Sharma's assault, Ishan Kishan's superb 35, Rapid Knocks from Suryakumar Yadav and Venkatesh Iyer steers to India crushing six-wicket victory over West Indies and take a 1-0 lead in the first T20I.


Match Stats : 


India chased 158 was their third highest successful run-chase against West Indies in T20 internationals.
West Indies' 157 was their ninth highest score against India in T20 internationals.
45 : Nicholas Pooran and Kieron Pollard set up the second highest sixth-wicket partnership for West Indies against India in T20 international and West Indies' eighth highest sixth-wicket stand in T20Is.
64 : Rohit Sharma and Ishan Kishan set up the joint fourth highest first-wicket partnership for India against West Indies in T20 internationals, jointly 64-run stand held by between Virat Kohli and Shikhar Dhawan at Kingston in 2017.
Ravi Bishnoi became the ninth Indian player to win the Man-of-the-match award on debut T20I match.
48* : Suryakumar Yadav and Venkatesh Iyer set up the third highest fifth-wicket partnership for India against West Indies in T20 internationals.


West Indies scored 157-7 in 20 overs with top scorer by Nicholas Pooran hammered a 61 off 43-balls including 5-sixes & 4-fours with strike rate of 141.86.


Kyle Mayers cracked a 31 off 24-balls including 7-fours and Kieron Pollard scored a unbeaten 24 off 19-balls including 2-fours & a six.


India best bowler by Debutant Ravi Bishnoi - who took 2-wickets on his debut T20I match, Harshal Patel takes 2-wickets, Deepak Chahar, Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Yuzvendra Chahal each took one-wickets.


India chased 162-4 in 18.5 overs with top scorer by Rohit Sharma hammered a 40 off 19-balls including 4-fours & 3-sixes with strike rate of 210.52.


Ishan Kishan scored 35 off 42-balls including 4-fours, Suryakumar Yadav blasted a unbeaten 34 off 18-balls included 5-fours & a six with strike rate of 188.88.


Venkatesh Iyer cracked a unbeaten 24 off 13-balls including 2-fours & a six and Virat Kohli 17.


West Indies best bowler by Roston Chase took 2-wickets and one for Fabian Allen - Sheldon Cottrell.


Ravi Bishnoi named Player of the match for his impressive debut bowling performance to 2-wickets for 17-runs in 4-overs with economy rate of 4.25.
