In the Zim Afro T10 2024, each team will compete in seven matches during the league stage. The top four teams on the points table will move on to the playoffs.

The top two teams will face off in Qualifier 1, with the winner securing a spot in the final. Meanwhile, the teams ranked third and fourth will battle in the Eliminator for a chance to advance.

The winner of the Eliminator will then take on the loser of Qualifier 1 in Qualifier 2, and the winner of that match will claim the final spot in the championship game.
