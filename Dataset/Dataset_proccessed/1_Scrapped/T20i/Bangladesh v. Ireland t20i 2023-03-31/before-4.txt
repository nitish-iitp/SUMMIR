IPL 2023 Schedule: Gujarat Titans will face Chennai Super Kings in the opener, in Ahmedabad.

With the 2023 edition of the Indian Premier League (IPL) scheduled to begin from March 31, defending champions Gujarat Titans will face Chennai Super Kings in the opener at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad. With new venues announced for this season, IPL 2023 will be held in Ahmedabad, Mohali, Lucknow, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Kolkata, Jaipur, Mumbai, Guwahati and Dharamshala.

Gujarat Titans won IPL 2022.(PTI)

IPL 2023 will consist of 70 league games, which will have 18 double headers. Each franchise will play seven home and away fixtures during the campaign. Rajasthan Royals will play their first two home matches in Guwahati, and then will play their remaining games in Jaipur. Meanwhile, Punjab Kings will play their five home matches in Mohali, and then play their last two home fixtures in Dharamshala.
