FairBreak Invitational 2023: Broadcast, live streaming details – When and Where to watch in India, Australia, US, UK, Canada & other countries
Published - Apr 03, 2023, 03:31 GMT | Updated - Apr 03, 2023, 03:31 GMT
by Anirudh Singh 
The second season of the FairBreak Global Invitational Women's T20 will take place from April 3 to 16 in Hong Kong.
All 20 games will be played at the Kowloon Cricket Club.
FairBreak Invitational 2023 Captains (Photo Source: Twitter)

Table of Contents

Fixtures of FairBreak Global Invitational Women’s T20 2023
Broadcast and Live Streaming details

The FairBreak Global Invitational Women’s T20 competition will kick-start on April 3 in Hong Kong. A total of six teams will compete in the tournament, with the final scheduled on April 16.

All teams featuring in the FairBreak Invitational 2023 are star-studded ones, and it is Stafanie Taylor who will be captaining the Tornadoes, Sana Mir will take charge of the Sapphires, Suzie Bates of the Falcons, Nicola Carey of the Spirit, Lauren Winfield-Hill of the Barmy Army and Sindhu Sriharsha of the Warriors.

All 20 games will be played at the Kowloon Cricket Club in Kowloon.
