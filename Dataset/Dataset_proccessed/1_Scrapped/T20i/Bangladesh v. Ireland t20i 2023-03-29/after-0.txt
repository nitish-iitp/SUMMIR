Top 5 Fastest fifties for Bangladesh in T20Is
Published - Apr 03, 2023, 04:09 UTC | Updated - Apr 03, 2023, 04:09 UTC
by Akshat Gaur 
Litton Das smashed the highest fifty in T20Is for Bangladesh (Image Source: Twitter)

Litton Das registered history in the second T20I of the three-match series against Ireland on March 29, 2023. Litton smashed the fastest half-century for Bangladesh in the shortest format.

Here are the top 5 Bangladesh batters with fastest fifties in T20Is:

5) Litton Das (24 balls)
Opponent: West Indies
Place: Lauderhill
Year: 2018
