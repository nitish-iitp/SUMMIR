Ireland Cricket team defeated in second T20 match against Bangladesh
MAR 29, 2023 14:16
BY RADIOKERRYSPORT

Ireland have been beaten by 77 runs in Chattogram in their second T20 match against Bangladesh.

The hosts finished their innings on 202 for the loss of 3 wickets in the reduced 17 overs meeting due to a rain-delayed start.

Ireland were bowled out for 129.
