About Sunida Chaturongrattana:

Name:- Sunida Chaturongrattana
Date of Birth:- 22nd January 2004
Bowling Style:- Right Arm Off-break
Batting Style:- Right Hand Bat
Place of Birth:- Mae Hong Son, Thailand
Role:- Bowler
Teams:- Thailand A

Sunida Chaturongrattana for Thailand. PC: Female Cricket
Bio:-

The 20-year-old young bowler from Thailand is an exciting talent to watch-out for in the coming years. The off-spinner made her international debut on 12th September 2022 in a T20I against the United States of America. She has picked up 12 wickets at a strike rate of 17.5 an impressive average of 9.25 and with a sensational economy of 3.15 in 14 innings so far with the ball for Thailand in the T20I format.

Her best performance with the ball for Thailand in the T20I format came on 6th September 2023 when she returned with exceptional match-figures (4-0-9-2) against Hong Kong in the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup Asia Region Qualifier. She played a crucial role in helping her side clinch a thumping 43-run victory.

She is a part of Thailand’s squad for the upcoming ICC Women’s T20 World Cup Global Qualifier in Abu Dhabi that will commence from 25th April 2024 and conclude on 7th May 2024.
