Chiedza Dhururu for Zimbabwe. PC: Female Cricket
Bio:-

The 28-year-old wicket-keeper batter from Zimbabwe made her international debut on 5th May, 2019 in a T20I against Mozambique. She has scored 244 runs at a strike rate of 81.87 in 21 innings. Her best performance with the bat for Zimbabwe in the T20I format came on 19th September 2021 when she scored 44 runs at a strike rate of 78.57 against Namibia in the final of the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup Africa Region Qualifier. She was awarded the Player of the Match for her match-winning performance with the bat for her side.

She made her ODI debut on 5th October 2021 against Ireland. She has scored 147 runs at a strike rate of 43.62 in 10 innings. Her best performance with the bat in the ODI format came on 21st January 2024 when she scored 46 runs at a strike rate of 62.16 against Ireland. She played a crucial role in helping her side tie the game in the 2nd ODI of the bilateral series, earlier this year.

She is a part of the Zimbabwe squad for the upcoming ICC Women’s T20 World Cup Qualifier in Abu Dhabi commences from 25th April 2024 and will conclude on 7th May 2024. The two finalists will book a spot for the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup in Bangladesh, later this year.
