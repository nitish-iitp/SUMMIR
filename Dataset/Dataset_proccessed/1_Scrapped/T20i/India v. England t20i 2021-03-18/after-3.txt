India vs England 4th T20 Highlights: India beat England by 8 runs to level the series 2-2
By 
hindustantimes.com
Mar 18, 2021 11:40 PM IST
India vs England, 4th T20 Highlights: Virat Kohli's Team India defeated Eoin Morgan-led England by 8 runs in the fourth T20I at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad. Follow IND vs ENG 4th T20I Highlights updates here.
India vs England 4th T20I Highlights

India vs England, 4th T20I Highlights: A determined India beat England by eight runs in the fourth T20 International to level the five-match series 2-2 on Thursday. Suryakumar's 31-ball effort was his maiden half-century in international cricket and Iyer made his runs in just 18 balls, hitting five fours and a six, as India managed to put up a challenging total. In reply, England were stopped at 177 for eight in their full quota of overs. Get IND vs ENG, 4th T20I Full Scorecard, and ball by ball updates.

Hello and welcome to the live blog of India vs England 4th T20I to be played at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad.
