PREVIEW

The third match of the T20 WC Sub Regional Africa Qualifier B 2024 is set to take place between Kenya (KEN) and Mozambique (MOZ). Ruaraka Sports Club Ground in Nairobi will host this clash on October 19 at 4:20 PM (IST). 

Kenya has won three games while losing only two in their last five T20Is; they are coming off a 4-wicket win against Nigeria following a 14-run defeat against the same opponent. They will be eyeing starting their campaign on a winning note with a win in this game against Mozambique (MOZ). 

Speaking of Mozambique, they have lost three while winning only two games in their last five fixtures. They defeated Rwanda by 5 wickets in the previous T20I encounter. Before that, they faced three successive defeats against Botswana, Malawi, and Uganda, respectively.
