Namibia vs USA 6th Match Tri-Nation T20I Series 2024 Article
by

Read the article of Namibia vs USA 6th Match Tri-Nation T20I Series 2024 - Namibia Tri-Nation Twenty20 international series of the 6th T20I match played between United States of America and Namibia at Wanderers Cricket Ground, Windhoek in 05th October 2024.



Milind Kumar claimed a career-best 5-for © USA Cricket / Twitter 


Spinner Milind Kumar's maiden five-wicket haul and Andries Gous' 19-ball 37 sets up to USA comfortable six-wicket victory over Namibia, despite a Gerhard Erasmus' fightback in a low-scoring game of the sixth match of a Triangular series.


Match Stats : 


USA chased 121 was their seventh lowest successful run-chase in T20 internationals.
Namibia's 120 was their lowest score against USA in T20Is, previously they have scored 168 at same venue earlier in this tournament.
Milind Kumar became the second USA bowler to take a five-wicket haul in T20 internationals after Saurabh Netravalkar.
Milind Kumar became the first USA bowler to claimed a five-for against Namibia in T20I cricket history.
Milind Kumar's 5 for 16 was the second best bowling figures for USA in T20Is after Saurabh Netravalkar's 5 for 12 against Singapore at Bulawayo in 2022 and the best-bowling-figures by an USA bowler against Namibia in T20Is, surpassing the previous record of 3 for 34 held by Nosthush Kenjige at same venue earlier in this tournament.


* UAE clinch the Tri-Nation series title with a superior net run-rate.


Namibia scored 120-9 in 20 overs with top scorer by Shaun Fouché hit a unbeaten 40 off 43-balls including 5-fours.


JP Kotze blasted a 29 off 20-balls including 6-fours with strike rate of 145 and Jan Frylinck blasted a 21 off 12-balls included 3-fours & a six with strike rate of 175.


USA best bowler by Milind Kumar 5-16, Harmeet Singh took 2-wickets and one for Nitish Kumar - Jasdeep Singh.


USA scored 124-4 in 15.1 overs with top scorer by Andries Gous hammered a 37 off 19-balls including 4-fours & 2-sixes with strike rate of 194.73.


Saiteja Mukkamalla struck 22 off 24-balls including 2-fours & a six, Milind Kumar hit a unbeaten 18 off 15-balls included a boundary and Harmeet Singh scored 16 off 12-balls contained one-six & a four.


Namibia best bowler by Gerhard Erasmus picked up 3-wickets for 23-runs in 4-overs and Bernard Scholtz took one-wicket.


Milind Kumar named Player of the match for his stunning spin bowling attack to claimed a career-best 5-wicket haul for 16-runs in 4-overs.
