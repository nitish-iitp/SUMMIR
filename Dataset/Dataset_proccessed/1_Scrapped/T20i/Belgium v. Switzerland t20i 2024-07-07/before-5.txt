Euro Cup 2024: Quarter final matches fixed, Portugal team will face France team
By Akshay - July 04, 2024 04:44 PM  

Euro 2024: The schedule of the quarter-final matches of the current season of Euro Cup has been decided in which Portugal will face France. Portugal defeated Slovenia in the Round of 16.

Euro Cup 2024: Quarter final matches fixed, Portugal team will face France team

The Netherlands team has confirmed its place for the quarter-finals by defeating Romania 3-0 in the Round of 16 of Euro Cup 2024. Now the Netherlands team will face Turkey in the quarter-finals. To reach the semi-finals in the Euro Cup, the matches of this round will start from July 5. At the same time, this time the Germany team, which is hosting the Euro Cup, will face the Spain team in the Euro Cup. This time the second quarter-final is going to be very exciting in which Cristiano Ronaldo's Portugal team will face Mbappe's France in which this match will be played on July 5. The Portugal team had to struggle a lot to win against Slovenia in the Round of 16.

This is Cristiano Ronaldo's last Euro Cup

Cristiano Ronaldo had already told before the start of Euro Cup 2024 that he is participating in this football tournament for the last time. The French team also won 1-0 against Belgium. This time in the third quarter-final, England and Switzerland, who were runners-up in the year 2022, will face each other. At the same time, the last quarter-final match between the Netherlands and Turkey team will be played on 7 July. Netherlands won an easy match against Romania 3-0 in the Round of 16. At the same time, Turkey defeated Austria 2-1.

Euro Cup 2024 quarter finals schedule
Spain vs Germany - July 5, 2024, 9:30 am IST
Portugal vs France - July 6, 2024, 12:30 am IST
England vs Switzerland - July 6, 2024, 9:30 am IST
Netherlands vs Turkey - July 7, 2024, 12:30 am IST
