MLD vs INA Match Preview:

The Bhutan T20I Quadrangular 2024 will continue with a fascinating match between Maldives and Indonesia.

The Maldives, led by the talented Azyan Farhath, will be aiming to build on their performance in the opening match. The team boasts a strong batting lineup, including Abdulla Ibrahim Mabsar, Ismail Ali, and Mohamed Shiyam.

Indonesia, on the other hand, will be led by Kadek Gamantika and will be hoping to make a statement with their talented bowling attack, featuring Anjar Tadarus, Febrianto Heo, and Kirubasankar Ramamoorthy.

Both teams have a history of playing competitive cricket, and a thrilling match is anticipated.
