PREVIEW

Maldives will take on Indonesia in the second match of the Bhutan T20I Quadrangular. This match will be played at Gelephu International Cricket Stadium. 10 matches will be played between four teams in this series. All the matches will be played at the same venue. Both these teams have a lot of players who have done well in this format and will be keen to showcase their skills.
