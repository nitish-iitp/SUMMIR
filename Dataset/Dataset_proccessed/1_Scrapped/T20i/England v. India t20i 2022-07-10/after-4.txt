India vs England 3rd T20 Highlights: Dawid Malan, Reece Topley heroics helps ENG eke out consolation win against IND
By 
HT Sports Desk
July 10, 2022 10:44 PM IST
India vs England Highlights 3rd T20I: Having lost the previous two T20Is, England managed to claim a consolation win against India in the third T20I on Sunday, at Trent Bridge in Nottingham. Follow highlights of the 3rd IND vs ENG T20I, at Trent Bridge in Nottingham.
IND vs ENG Highlights: Dawid Malan plays a shot.

IND vs ENG 3rd T20I Highlights: England managed to beat India by 17 runs to eke out a consolation win in the third and final T20I, at Trent Bridge in Nottingham on Sunday. Defending a target of 216 runs, the hosts restricted India to 198/9 in 20 overs, with Reece Topley taking three wickets. Meanwhile, Chris Jordan and David Willey were also in good form and took two wickets each. For India, Suryakumar Yadav slammed 117 runs off 55 balls but didn't receive support from his teammates. Initially, England posted 215/7 in 20 overs, courtesy of a knock of 77 runs off 39 balls by Dawid Malan. Meanwhile, Ravi Bishnoi and Harshal Patel took two wickets each for India.
