Match Preview

In Match 12 of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Asia Sub-regional Qualifier A, Kuwait beat Myanmar by 8 wickets. Myanmar batted first but struggled, finishing at 47/7 after 20 overs. Yasin Patel was outstanding with the ball for Kuwait, taking 3 wickets for 6 runs in 4 overs. Adnan Idrees also performed well, claiming 2 wickets for 10 runs, while Shiraz Khan Shereef added 1 wicket for 7 runs. In their chase, Kuwait quickly reached 48 runs in just 3.3 overs. Ravija Sandaruwan led with 20 runs off 7 balls, Clinto Velookkara Anto scored 14 runs from 7 balls, and Meet Bhavsar finished with 11 runs off 4 balls, ensuring a comfortable win.

In Match 11 of the same qualifier, Malaysia won by 1 run against Singapore. Singapore batted first, posting 181/7 in their 20 overs. Syed Aziz was key for Malaysia, scoring 53 runs off 22 balls, while Ahmed Aqeel Wahid contributed 48 runs from 22 balls. Virandeep Singh added 29 runs from 30 balls, helping Malaysia reach 182/6. Singapore came close but ended at 181/7. Pavandeep Singh took 3 wickets for 25 runs, Rizwan Haider took 1 wicket for 38 runs, and Virandeep Singh claimed 1 wicket for 39 runs. Malaysia’s strong batting and tight bowling secured a narrow victory.
