Match Preview

In Match 12 of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Asia Sub-regional Qualifier A, Myanmar suffered an 8-wicket defeat to Kuwait. Batting first, Myanmar struggled to post a competitive total, ending their innings at 47/7 in 20 overs. Pyae Phyo Wai was the top scorer with 16 runs off 38 balls, while Paing Danu added 14 runs from 23 balls. Thura Aung chipped in with 6 runs from 17 balls. Defending a modest target, Myanmar’s bowlers tried to make inroads, with Paing Danu taking 1 wicket for 23 runs in 1.3 overs and Pyae Phyo Wai also claiming a wicket, conceding 25 runs in 2 overs. However, Kuwait chased down the target in just 3.3 overs, securing a comfortable win.

In Match 10, Maldives lost to Hong Kong by 9 wickets. Batting first, Maldives managed to score 102/9 in their 20 overs. Adam Nasif Umar led the batting effort with 26 runs off 15 balls, while Gedara Piyal Kumara contributed 21 runs from 25 balls, and Abdulla Ibrahim Mabsar made 11 runs off 21 balls. Hong Kong, however, chased down the target with ease, reaching 107/1 in 9 overs. Mohamed Shiyam was the only Maldivian bowler to take a wicket, conceding 22 runs in his over, but their overall performance fell short as Hong Kong clinched a decisive victory.
