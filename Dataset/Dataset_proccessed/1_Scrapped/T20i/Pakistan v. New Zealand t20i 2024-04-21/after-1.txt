PAK vs NZ 3rd T20I: Rawalpindi Pitch And Weather Report, Fantasy 11, Head To Head Record
Babar Azam-led Pakistan defeated New Zealand by seven wickets at Rawalpindi on Saturday (April 20), after rain washed out the series opener at the same venue.

PAK vs NZ Pitch, Weather, Playing 11, Head To Head: Pakistan cricket team aims to extend series lead when they take on New Zealand in PAK vs NZ 3rd T20I on Sunday (April 21) at the Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium.

After rain washed out series opener in Rawalpindi, Babar Azam-led Pakistan defeated New Zealand by seven wickets at the same venue on Saturday (April 20).

PAK vs NZ 3rd T20I Weather Report

According to various weather reports, fans can expect an uninterrupted T20I clash between Pakistan and New Zealand on Sunday (April 21). There are no chances of rain showers during match hours of PAK vs NZ 3rd T20I in Rawalpindi.

PAK vs NZ 3rd T20I Pitch Report

The Rawalpindi pitch in Pakistan is renowned for its flat tracks, often resulting in high-scoring encounters. Pacers tend to thrive on the bounce offered by these tracks, while spinners become effective as the pitch slows down later in the match. In the recently-concluded second T20I, pacers found success. 

In the six T20I matches played between Pakistan and New Zealand at Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium, teams batting first have emerged victorious in four matches, while teams batting second have failed to win any. Additionally, two PAK-NZ matches in Rawalpindi have concluded without a result.

PAK vs NZ Head To Head Record In T20Is

In the T20 format, Pakistan (PAK) and New Zealand (NZ) have faced off in a total of 41 matches. Among these encounters, Pakistan has emerged victorious 22 times, while New Zealand has secured victory in 17 matches. Additionally, two matches between PAK and NZ have ended without a result (NR).
