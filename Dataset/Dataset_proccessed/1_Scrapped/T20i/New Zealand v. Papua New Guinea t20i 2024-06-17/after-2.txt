New Zealand vs Papua New Guinea Highlights, T20 World Cup 2024: New Zealand posted a 7-wicket win over Papua New Guinea in their fourth and final ICC T20 World Cup Group C assignment on Tuesday to close out their campaign.

The Kiwis bundled out PNG to 78 runs as Lockie Ferguson created history with four maidens in as many overs and a three-wicket haul! NZ chased down the target with 46 balls to spare as Devon Conway top-scored with 35 runs.
