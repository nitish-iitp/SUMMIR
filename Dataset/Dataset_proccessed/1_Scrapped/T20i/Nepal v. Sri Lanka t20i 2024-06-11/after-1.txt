KATHMANDU, June 12: The much-anticipated ICC T20 World Cup cricket match between Nepal and Sri Lanka was called off due to persistent rain today at the Broward County Ground in Florida. Originally scheduled for a 5:15 AM start, the match faced continuous downpours leading to a cancellation, with both teams being awarded one point each.
