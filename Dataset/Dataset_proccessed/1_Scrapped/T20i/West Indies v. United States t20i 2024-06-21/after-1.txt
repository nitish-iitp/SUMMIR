The Windies were hammered by England in their first Super 8 match. (Image Credit: Getty Images)

United States will take on West Indies in a Group 2 Super 8 match of the ICC Men's 2024 T20 World Cup at the Kensington Oval in Bridgetown, Barbados, on Friday, June 21. The game will get underway at 8:30 PM local time [June 21] and 6:00 AM IST [June 22].

After making a surprise entry into the Super 8 round of the T20 World Cup at the expense of Pakistan, the USA showed that they deserved the berth with a fighting performance against South Africa. Chasing a challenging target of 195 in Antigua, they finished on 176-6 as Andries Gous clobbered 80* off 47 balls. Briefly, United States gave the Proteas a scare, but South Africa pulled through.

On the other hand, West Indies suffered an eight-wicket hammering at the hands of defending champions England in their first Super 8 match in St Lucia. Batting first, the Windies put up a competitive 180-4 on the board. However, the Englishmen chased down the score in 17.3 overs as Phil Salt slammed 87* off 47.

What happened when United States and West Indies met in an ODI in 2023?

United States and West Indies clashed in the ICC Cricket World Cup Qualifier in Harare in June last year. The Windies won the match by 39 runs.

Expand Tweet

The USA won the toss and elected to bowl first in the game. Saurabh Netravalkar, Kyle Phillip and Steven Taylor claimed three wickets each. However, West Indies managed to put up 297 on the board as Johnson Charles, Shai Hope, Roston Chase and Jason Holder all hit half-centuries.

In the chase, United States were held to 258-7 despite a brilliant ton from Gajanand Singh (101* off 109). For the Windies, Kyle Mayers and Alzarri Joseph claimed two wickets each.
