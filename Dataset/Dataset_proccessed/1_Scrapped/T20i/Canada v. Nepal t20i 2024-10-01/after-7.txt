Metro spl night ops from Saptami to Nabami for pandal-hoppers
Ajanta Chakraborty / Oct 1, 2024, 04:18 IST
SHARE

+

Kolkata: Metro will operate trains till 4 am on the North-South line and till 1.45 am on the East-West Metro’s Howrah section on the main days of puja. From Chaturthi to Shashthi (Oct 7-Oct 9), services will start at 6.50 am. But on Chaturthi and Panchami, the last trains will be at 10.40 pm. On Shashthi, the last train will be at 12am. From Saptami to Nabami, services will start at 1pm and cease at 4am on the Esplanade-Dakshineswar corridor.
At 20-minute interval, services will start at 1 pm and end at 11.30 pm on East-West Metro’s Sector V-Sealdah section. On East-West Metro’s Howrah Maidan-Esplanade section, the first and last trains will be at 1.30pm and 1.45 am respectively. The services will operate at 12 and 15-minute intervals. On Oct 12, the North-South line services will run from 1 pm till midnight. On Dashami, East-West Metro’s Sector V-Sealdah section will run at 20-minutes interval from 2 pm to 9.45 pm. East-West Metro’s Howrah Maidan-Esplanade section will run at 15-minute intervals from 2 pm to 11.45 pm on Dashami. While N-S corridor will run services from 9 am to 9.40 pm on Oct 13, no services will be available on EW’s Sec V-Sealdah section that day though the Howrah section will provide services from 2.15 pm to 9.50 pm. On Oct 14-15, N-S line will run trains from 6.50 pm to 9.40 pm.
From Chaturthi to Shashthi, East-West Metro’s Sealdah section will operate from 6.55 am to 9.40 pm. The Howrah section will run trains from 7 am to 9.54 pm. New Garia-Ruby line will run services from 8 am to 8 pm from Chaturthi to Shasthi but none from Oct 10 to 13. The Joka-Majerhat line, too, will not operate Oct 10 to 13.

Paddy purchasebegins on Oct 1from West UP
Paddy procurement in Uttar Pradesh starts Oct 1 in western districts and Nov 1 in eastern areas, with MSP set at Rs 2,300-2,320 per quintal. Farmers get an additional Rs 20 per quintal for unloading and cleaning. Payments to farmers will be made within 48 hours, and 4,000 purchase centers have been established. 32,000 farmers registered.
New CR main line timetable likely from Oct 5
Central Railway will implement a revised timetable for its main line starting Oct 5. The update aims to enhance efficiency and reduce congestion by increasing train services from Dadar, Parel, and Kalyan. No changes are expected in the harbour line services. Currently, CR operates 894 services, with most originating from CSMT.
PM to flag off Metro 3 on Sat, services likely from Sunday
PM Modi is set to launch the first phase of Mumbai's Metro 3 and conduct groundbreaking ceremonies for the Thane ring Metro and NAINA projects. Safety certification for Metro 3 is still pending. Modi's visit will also include attending the Mukhyamantri Mahila Sashaktikaran Abhiyaan. Preparations are underway to accommodate about 40,000 attendees.
