India vs Zimbabwe, T20 World Cup 2022, Highlights: India Thrash Zimbabwe To Top Group 2, Set To Face England In Semi-finals
India vs Zimbabwe Highlights: India defeated Zimbabwe by 71 runs at the Melbourne Cricket Ground on Sunday.
NDTV Sports Desk
Updated:November 06, 2022 07:29 pm IST
Read Time: 1 min
India vs Zimbabwe, T20 World Cup Highlights: India won the game by 71 runs.
© AFP

India vs Zimbabwe, T20 World Cup, Super 12 Group 2 Highlights: India defeated Zimbabwe by 71 runs at the Melbourne Cricket Ground on Sunday to set up a semi-final clash with England. Defending 186 for 5, India bundled out Zimbabwe for 115 runs in 17.2 overs as Ravichandran Ashwin starred with 3 wickets for 22 runs in his four overs. Earlier, Suryakumar Yadav scored 61 not out off 25 balls as India posted a big total of 186 for 5 against Zimbabwe. Meanwhile, Sean Williams was the pick of the bowlers for Zimbabwe by returning figures of 2 for 9 in two overs. India skipper Rohit Sharma had won the toss and opted to bat first in the game. (SCORECARD)

India (Playing XI): KL Rahul, Rohit Sharma(c), Virat Kohli, Suryakumar Yadav, Hardik Pandya, Rishabh Pant(w), Axar Patel, Ravichandran Ashwin, Bhuvneshwar Kumar, Mohammed Shami, Arshdeep Singh
