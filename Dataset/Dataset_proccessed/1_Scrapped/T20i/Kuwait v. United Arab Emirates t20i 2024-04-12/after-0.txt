UAE vs KUW T20I Dream11 Prediction

In the upcoming fixture of the ACC Men’s T20I Premier Cup tournament, United Arab Emirates (UAE) is all set to take on Kuwait at the Al Amerat Cricket Ground United Arab Emirates Cricket (Ministry Turf 2) in the UAE on Friday.

The UAE team, under the leadership of captain Waseem Muhammad, presents a formidable lineup comprising talented players such as Asif Khan and Alishan Sharafu. Known for their skills and experience in the T20 format, the UAE squad is poised to showcase their prowess on the field and make a strong start to their campaign in the tournament.

On the opposing side, Kuwait, led by Mohammed Aslam, will enter the contest with a determined mindset, aiming to clinch victory against their formidable opponents.

Here is the ACC Men's T20I Premier Cup Dream11 Team Prediction - UAE vs KUW Dream11 Guru Tips Prediction and UAE vs KUW T20I Dream11 Team Prediction, UAE vs KUW T20I 2024 Fantasy Cricket Prediction, UAE vs KUW T20I Playing 11s 2024, Fantasy Cricket Prediction United Arab Emirates vs Kuwait, Fantasy Playing Tips - United Arab Emirates vs Kuwait

TOSS - The match toss between UAE and Kuwait will take place at 11 AM IST.

Time - April 12, 11:30 AM IST

Venue - Al Amerat Cricket Ground (Ministry Turf 2), Al Amerat 

UAE vs KUW T20I 2024 Live Streaming Details: The live streaming in India will be helmed by Fancode.
