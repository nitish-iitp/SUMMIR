Asian Games, India vs Bangladesh Semi-Final Match, Highlights: India Hammer Bangladesh By 9 Wickets, Enter Final
Asian Games 2023, India vs Bangladesh Semi-Final Match: Tilak Varma slammed an unbeaten 55 as India hammered Bangladesh by 9 wickets to reach the final of the Asian Games men's cricket event
NDTV Sports Desk
Updated:October 06, 2023 09:19 am IST
Read Time: 1 min
Asian Games 2023, India vs Bangladesh Semi-Final: India hammer Bangaldesh.
© X (Twitter)

India vs Bangladesh, Asian Games 2023, Live Updates: Tilak Varma slammed an unbeaten 55 as India hammered Bangladesh by 9 wickets to reach the final of the Asian Games men's cricket event. India are now assured of a silver medal at least. Earlier, R Shai Kishore took a three-wicket haul as India restricted Bangladesh for 96/9. For Bangladesh, Jaker Ali hit an unbeaten 24, while Rakibul Hasan played a 14-run cameo off just six balls. Team India will be coming to this clash after thrashing Nepal by 23 runs in their quarter-finals match. Young opener Yashasvi Jaiswal slammed his maiden T20I hundred and played a crucial role in team's victory. (Scorecard | Asian Games 2023 Medals Tally)
