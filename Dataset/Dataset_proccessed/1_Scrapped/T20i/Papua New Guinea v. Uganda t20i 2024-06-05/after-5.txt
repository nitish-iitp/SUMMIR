PNG vs UGA 2024, T20 World Cup 2024 Live Streaming: When and where to watch Papua New Guinea vs Uganda?
Papua New Guinea vs Uganda 2024, T20 World Cup 2024 Live Streaming: The Indian Express gives all the live streaming details of the T20 World Cup game between PNG vs UGA on Thursday.
By: Sports Desk
June 5, 2024 19:21 IST
2 min read
PNG vs UGA Live Streaming Details: Papua New Guinea and Uganda look for first win. (AP/PTI)

Papua New Guinea vs Uganda ICC T20 World Cup 2024 Live Streaming: Papua New Guinea will look to build on their impressive performance against the West Indies in the opening match when they take on a wounded Uganda outfit at the Providence Stadium in Guyana on Thursday.

PNG had almost given hosts West Indies a late scare when their bowlers led by captain Assad Vala put the two-times champions under tremendous pressure in a 137-run chase. WI needed the combined experience of Andre Russel and Roston Chase to get to a tricky target after losing a couple of quick wickets.

Uganda, meanwhile, was thoroughly thrashed an Afghanistan unit by 125-runs on Tuesday after they were bundled out for 58 while chasing 183. Playing in just their second R20 World Cup, Uganda would hope for a win against PNG in order to improve on their previous performance where they had won 1 match.

Here are the live streaming details of the Papua New Guinea vs Uganda match
When to watch the Papua New Guinea vs Uganda game of the ICC T20 World Cup 2024?

The Papua New Guinea vs Uganda game of the T20 World Cup 2024 will begin at 5:00 AM (IST) on Thursday, June 6.

Where will the Papua New Guinea vs Uganda T20 World Cup 2024 match be played?

The Papua New Guinea vs Uganda game of T20 World Cup 2024 at the Providence Stadium in Guyana.

Where to watch live streaming of Papua New Guinea vs Uganda T20 World Cup 2024 match?

Star Sports Network has the telecasting rights for the entire T20 World Cup 2024. On the other hand, live streaming of the match will be available on Disney+Hotstar.
