The ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 is the ninth edition of the Cricket tournament and will take place from June 01 onwards, which means June 02 as per Indian Standard Time (IST). The T20 World Cup 2024 is to be co-hosted by West Indies and the United States of America (USA). While West Indies will host the T20 World Cup for the second time, the USA will host the tournament for the first time. Meanwhile, you can download the PDF file of ICC T20 World Cup 2024 Complete Schedule for free which contains match timings in IST and venue details as well. ICC T20 World Cup 2024: A Look at Format, Groups, Participating Teams and Points System of Men's Twenty20 Cricket WC

A total of 20 teams will take part in the ICC T20 World Cup 2024. As per the T20 World Cup 2024 format, the teams have been divided into four groups of five teams each. After the first round, eight teams will head to the Super 8s round, which means top two teams from each group. In the Super 8s round, teams will be divided into two groups. Thereafter, the top two teams from these groups will advance to semifinals.

India and Pakistan are placed in Group A along with Ireland, Canada and the USA. England, Australia, Namibia, Scotland and Oman are placed in Group B. New Zealand, West Indies, Afghanistan, Papua New Guinea and Uganda are part of Group C. And in Group D, South Africa, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Netherlands and Nepal are stationed. The matches will be played across nine stadiums in West Indies and the USA. ICC T20 World Cup 2024 All Squads: Full Players List of All Teams for Ninth Edition of Men's Twenty20 Cricket WC
