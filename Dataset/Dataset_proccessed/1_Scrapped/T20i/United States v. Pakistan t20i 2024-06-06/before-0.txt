India vs Pakistan T20 World Cup Match: Check Ticket Prices And How to Book Online For Ind vs Pak Game

The ICC T20 World Cup 2024 is just around the corner, starting from June 1 to June 29 across venues in the USA and the Caribbean and fans are eagerly waiting for the biggest cricket showdown, India vs Pakistan. This intense rivalry match is set for June 9 in New York, and it going to be an unforgettable event for millions of fans. Cricket lovers from all over the world are excited to see these two teams compete on such a grand stage. In this article, we'll give you all the details about the match, including ticket prices and how to book them online, so you don't miss out on this epic clash.


India vs Pakistan T20 World Cup Match Details

India and Pakistan will play against each other on June 9, 2024, at the new Nassau County International Cricket Stadium with 34,000 seating capacity in Long Island, New York. This match is part of the group stages of the ICC T20 World Cup 2024, a tournament that promises thrilling cricket action. The rivalry between India and Pakistan is legendary, making this match one of the highlights of the World Cup.

Venues and Tournament Schedule

The T20 World Cup will be held at three venues in the USA and six in the Caribbean. The opening match will see co-hosts USA take on Canada on June 1, followed by West Indies against Papua New Guinea in Guyana. India and Pakistan, both in Group A, will start their World Cup journey by playing against each other in New York. The tournament will end with the final in Barbados on June 29.

Are Ticket Prices Touching Rs 16.5 Lakh Per Seat For IND vs PAK?

The ticket prices for the India vs. Pakistan game have caused controversy. Lalit Modi, former commissioner of the Indian Premier League, has criticised the International Cricket Council (ICC) for focusing more on making money than promoting cricket in the United States. Modi claims that seats in the exclusive Diamond Club section at Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in New York are being sold for as much as $20,000 (around ₹16,65,138) each.


India vs Pakistan Match Tickets and Hospitality Packages

Ticket prices for the India vs Pakistan match are rising as the date gets closer. The ICC offers several hospitality packages, but only a few are available for this big match.

Diamond Club

The Diamond Club is the top package, known as the 'jewel' of Nassau County International Cricket Stadium. It offers seats directly behind the wicket, high-quality food and drinks, air-conditioned comfort, and opportunities to meet cricket legends and access the field before the match.

Price: $10,000 (Rs 8,34,323)

Premium Club Lounges

These lounges are located behind the wicket in both the North and South Pavilions, offering great views and excellent food and drink options, including alcohol.

Price: $2,500 (Rs 2,08,585.25)

Corner Club

The Corner Club offers an exclusive atmosphere for up to 92 people per corner, with an outdoor buffet and bar.

Price: $2,750 (Rs 2,29,413.94)

How to Book Tickets Online

If you’re planning to witness the India vs Pakistan match live, you can book your tickets online via the ICC's official website. Follow these steps to secure your seats:

Visit the ICC's official website: https://www.icc-cricket.com/tournaments/t20cricketworldcup/matches
Navigate to the 'Buy Tickets' section under the ticketing tab.
Select the venue: Nassau County International Cricket Stadium, New York.
Apply filters and select the India vs Pakistan match.
Choose your desired ticket type.
Enter the number of tickets you wish to purchase.
Add them to your cart.
Proceed to checkout and complete your payment online.

FAQs

1. When and where is the India vs Pakistan match taking place?

The India vs Pakistan match is scheduled for June 9, 2024, at the Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in Long Island, New York.

2. How can I book tickets for the India vs Pakistan match?

You can book tickets online through the ICC's official website by selecting the venue and match, choosing your ticket type, and completing the payment process.

3. What are the available hospitality packages for the India vs Pakistan match?

The available hospitality packages include the Diamond Club, Premium Club Lounges, and Corner Club, each offering different levels of amenities and seating options.

4. How much do the tickets for the India vs Pakistan match cost?

Ticket prices vary depending on the package. The Diamond Club costs $10,000 (Rs 8,34,323), the Premium Club Lounges cost $2,500 (Rs 2,08,585.25), and the Corner Club costs $2,750 (Rs 2,29,413.94).

5. Where can I find more information about the ICC T20 World Cup schedule?

You can find detailed information about the schedule on the ICC's official website, including match dates, venues, and timings.
