Texas Super Kings vs Los Angeles Knight Riders Major League Cricket 2023 Highlights: Follow highlights of TSK vs LAKR, in Dallas.
Major League Cricket 2023 Highlights Texas Super Kings vs LA Knight Riders

Texas Super Kings vs LA Knight Riders Highlights Major League Cricket 2023: Texas Super Kings defeated LA Knight Riders by 69 runs in the Major League Cricket (MLC) 2023 opener, at the Grand Prairie Stadium in Dallas on Friday. Chasing a target of 182 runs, LAKR were bowled out for 112 in 14 overs, despite a half-century from Andre Russell (55). Meanwhile, Mohammad Mohsin bagged a four-wicket haul for TSK. Initially, Texas posted 181/6 in 20 overs, courtesy of good knocks from Miler (61) and Conway (55). For LAKR, Lockie Ferguson and Ali Khan took two wickets each.
