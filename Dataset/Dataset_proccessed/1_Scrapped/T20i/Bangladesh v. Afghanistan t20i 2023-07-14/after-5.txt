'Give Me Money': Putin's Mouthpiece Mocks 'Isolated, Lonely' Zelensky After NATO Setback
World News
Published on Jul 14, 2023 07:51 PM IST
 
Ukrainian President Zelensky found himself at the centre of controversy as memes emerged, portraying him in a mocking light during the summit. Memes showed Zelensky standing on a chair, pleading for financial aid from allies. These memes, intended to ridicule Zelensky, came after a last-ditch effort by Kyiv to secure an invitation from NATO to join the alliance 'failed'. Ukraine's allies in NATO have ruled out membership for Ukraine as long as the war with Russia rages on.
