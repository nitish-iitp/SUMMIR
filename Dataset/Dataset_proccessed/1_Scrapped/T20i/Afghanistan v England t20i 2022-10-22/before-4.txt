ICC Twenty20 World cup 2022 will be played among the world top ranking teams during the months of October and November 2022. The T20 matches among all teams will be aggressive and enjoyable and millions of of cricket fans spreading across the world will watch the live streaming of the matches direct on their tv screens while sitting in their homes.  International Cricket Council manages the ICC T20 WC 2022 on different venues in Australia. The cricket fans will watch the schedules of all matches, team squads, live score card, pictures and live telecast of all matches. When the matches will be finished, the video highlights of every match will be uploaded and the cricket enthusiasts will watch them on their tv screens to watch the aggressive moments of the match among all these teams. The cricket fans will get the ICC T20 Schedule 2022 of all matches online.

 

The ICC T20 World Cup 2022 will be played in two stages, in which first is the qualifying round, in which 8 teams are divided into two groups. In the group matches, the teams will play with other teams included in their groups and top two teams will be included in the main event of the ICC Twenty20 WC 2022.
