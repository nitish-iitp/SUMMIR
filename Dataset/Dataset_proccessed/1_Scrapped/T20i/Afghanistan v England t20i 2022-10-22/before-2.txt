DU Merit List 2022 Live Updates: First cut off list for UG admissions released
By 
HT Education Desk
Oct 19, 2022 7:41 PM IST
DU First Cut Off List 2022 Live Updates: DU UG first cut off list 2022 has been released today, October 19. Check latest updates below. 
DU Merit List 2022 Live Updates: First cut off for UG admissions shortly (HT Photo)

DU Merit List 2022 Live Updates: DU UG first cut off list 2022 has been published by Delhi University today, October 19, 2022. Candidates who have registered for the admission round can view the list on the official DU website at du.ac.in. From October 19 to October 21, candidates can accept their seats, and from October 19 to October 22, 2022, colleges can verify and accept their online applications. The deadline for candidates to pay their admission fees is October 24, 2022....Read More

Follow all the updates here:
Oct 19, 2022 7:41 PM IST
Cut Off List Of DU 2022: Acceptance of allocated seats 

Once a seat has been allocated in a particular round, the candidate must 'Accept' the Allocated Seat before the last date/time specified for the given allocation round. The provision for acceptance of a particular Allocated Seat will be valid only for the round in which the seat was allocated to the candidate.

Oct 19, 2022 7:26 PM IST
DU First Cut Off 2022: What candidates should do 

A candidate who is allocated a seat as per the Allocation Policy stated in the Common Seat Allocation System (CSAS 2022) must perform the following steps through their dashboard to ensure successful admission:

1. "Accept Allocation" under User Action Tab

2 . On receiving the "Approval from the College Principal", proceed to pay the fee to

confirm the admission.

3. Take a printout of the acknowledgement receipt.

Oct 19, 2022 6:45 PM IST
DU Admissions: College preferences

Oct 19, 2022 6:31 PM IST
DU First Cut Off List 2022: Merit list as per merit

The merit list for the UR category seats will comprise all candidates in order of merit. No one will be excluded from the same. In other words, the merit list for the Unreserved (UR) category will also include SC/ST/OBC-NCL /Minority/EWS candidates, irrespective of category, if they meet the criterion of merit for the UR category.

Oct 19, 2022 6:16 PM IST
DU Admissions 2022: What next?

Now, when the cut off list has been released candidates can accept their seats from October 19 to 21. During the same window, colleges can verify and accept online applications. The deadline for candidates to pay their admission fee is October 24, 2022.

Oct 19, 2022 5:59 PM IST
DU First Cut Off List: Admission can be cancelled anytime 

A candidate's participation in the admission process will be provisional. If, at any stage, it is found that eligibility requirements are not fulfilled, the admission, if granted, shall be canceled ipso facto and appropriate legal action will be taken against such candidate.

Oct 19, 2022 5:49 PM IST
DU Cut off List 2022: Released 

The DU cut off list 2022 has been released. Candidates can check it through their personal account on the website of DU at du.ac.in. 

Oct 19, 2022 5:40 PM IST
DU UG First Cut Off List 2022: How to check 

Go to admission.uod.ac.in/du.ac.in

On the home page, check the link for the 1st cut-of list.

Open the pdf file and check the college and course wise list.

Oct 19, 2022 5:30 PM IST
DU Cut-Off List: About document verification 

The candidate will be required to appear in person to verify the original documents as and when notified by the University/College.

Oct 19, 2022 5:21 PM IST
Cut Off List Of DU 2022 CUET: Not released yet 

Cut Off List Of DU 2022 CUET has not been released yet. Keep checking this space for latest updates. 

Oct 19, 2022 5:16 PM IST
Cut Off List Of DU 2022: Official website not responding 

The official website of Delhi University is not responding at the moment. Candidates are advised to keep a check on the website for first cut off list. 

Oct 19, 2022 5:08 PM IST
DU First Cut Off 2022: Seats on basis of CUET score 

Seat allocation to all UG Programs will be based solely on the scores obtained in CUET(UG) - 2022. The CSAS-2022 merit lists published on the Admission website shall be adhered to by all the Colleges of UoD.

Oct 19, 2022 5:02 PM IST
DU Admissions: Information regarding cut off list 

The candidates must ensure that they have secured their admission on the seat allocated to them, whenever offered. Only the candidates who are admitted to a Program in a College will be able to opt for upgradation in subsequent round/ s.

DU First Cut Off List: Check college wise preferences

Oct 19, 2022 4:36 PM IST
DU UG First Cut Off List 2022: Documents required for admission 

– Class 10 marksheet (self-attested)

– Class 12 marksheet (self-attested)

— Transfer certificate from school (if available)

— Government-issued photo identification card such as Aadhaar card, PAN card, driver's license or passport.

— A couple of passport-sized photos (which may have to be self-attested)

Oct 19, 2022 4:26 PM IST
DU Cut-Off List: All streams list to release today 

DU Cut-Off List will be released today for all streams- Science, Commerce and Arts. The lists will be available on the official site of DU at du.ac.in.

Cut Off List Of DU 2022 CUET

Last date of Online payment of admission fees by the Candidates: Till October 24, 2022

DU admission 2022: Number of rounds

DU plans to hold 3 rounds of CSAS allocation and admission, followed by one spot admission round, as per the schedule available on admission.uod.ac.in.

DU's rule to break ties

In case of a tie between 2 or more candidates in the merit list, it will be resolved based on marks of Class 12 board examination, DU had previously announced.
