The United Arab Emirates (UAE) and the USA will tour Namibia from September 29 to October 5, 2024, with all matches scheduled to take place at the Wanderers Cricket Ground in Windhoek.

The upcoming tri-nations series will feature three teams: the USA, the UAE, and the host nation, Namibia. The team that accumulates the most points across the six matches will be declared the winner.

United States of America had a poor run in the most recent Netherlands T20 Tri-Series 2024, finishing third with two losses and one victory in four games. Namibia's last T20 International was during the Men's T20 World Cup 2024, where they also finished third in Group B, losing three out of four matches.

Trending

Meanwhile, the UAE come into the tournament with renewed confidence, having triumphed in the ACC Men's Premier Cup 2024 by defeating Oman in the final by 55 runs.

Muhammad Waseem will lead the UAE in the upcoming series, Monank Patel will captain USA, and Gerhard Erasmus will be at the helm for Namibia.
