All You Need To Know About Guwahati Women’s League
February 29, 2024  Team Female Cricket

The stage is set for Assam Cricket Association’s (ACA) Guwahati Women’s League, a 6-team Twenty20 (T20) tournament, from the 1st to the 17th of March, 2024. From squads and schedule to broadcasting details and format, here’s everything you need to know about the tournament.

All You Need To Know About Guwahati Women’s League
Format:

The tournament will follow a double round-robin format, meaning each team will go up against the other twice in league games. The top four teams from the 6-team points table will then advance to the semi-finals.

Squads:

New Star Club – Hiramoni Saikia (WK), Swastika Gogoi, Bahrati Hassan, Rupshree Saikia, Baibhabee Das, Neha Chanda, Diya Barman (WK), Maini Payeng, Jagriti Kalita, Shahanas Yeasmin, Nebedita Deka, Jinan Hussain, Pahi Das, Sayanika Deka, Bhagyashree Barman, Sristi Singh
