How to watch Australia vs. West indies ODI series: TV and live streaming details

KIERAN FRANCIS

05 February, 2024
•
11:15 pm UTC

The West Indies will enter the one-day international series against Australia full of momentum after their famous Test win in Brisbane.

After their first Test victory in Australia in 27 years, the Windies will play three ODI matches against the green and gold at the start of February.

Will Australia prevail on home soil or can the West Indies provide another stunning upset?

MORE: Australia sinks to historic loss against West Indies but Steve Smith's return to form a welcome boost

The Sporting News has all the details on the Australia vs West Indies ODI series below.

When is the Australia vs West Indies ODI series?

After Australia and West Indies completed two Tests in Adelaide and Brisbane, a three match ODI series will be played in Melbourne, Sydney and Canberra.

The West Indies tour of Australia will then be completed with three T20 internationals in Hobart, Adelaide and Perth.

What time do the Australia vs West Indies ODI matches start?

All three ODI matches will start at the traditional time of 2:30pm local time with this AEDT across the games.

How to watch or live stream Australia vs West Indies ODI series

Every match of Australia's summer of cricket - including Tests, ODIs and T20Is - will be broadcast live on Fox Cricket and streamed on Kayo Sports.
