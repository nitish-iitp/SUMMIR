PREVIEW

Get ready for a thrilling encounter between Uganda (UGA) and Nigeria (NIG) in Match 12 of the T20 World Cup Africa Region Qualifier 2023, set to take place at the United Cricket Club Ground in Windhoek. Both teams come into this fixture with a competitive edge, Uganda having won two out of their three matches played in the tournament so far, while Nigeria secured victory in one out of their three encounters.

Uganda enters this fixture having secured victories in two out of their three matches. Their recent clash against Zimbabwe resulted in an impressive win by five wickets. Uganda successfully chased down the target of 136 runs set by Zimbabwe in 19 overs. Key contributors for Uganda included Riazat Ali Shah, with a crucial 42 runs, and Alpesh Ramjani, who added 40 runs to the tally. Additionally, Dinesh Nakrani's three-wicket haul played a pivotal role in restricting Zimbabwe.

On the other hand, Nigeria comes off a recent victory against Tanzania in their last match. Tanzania set a target of 139 runs, and Nigeria, displaying resilience, successfully chased it down in 19 overs, losing seven wickets along the way. Noteworthy performances for Nigeria included Prosper Useni's valuable 31 runs and the combined effort of Ridwan Abdulkareem, Isaac Okpe, and Sylvester Okpe, each claiming two wickets to contain Tanzania's total.
