INDIA’S T20 WORLD CUP 2024 MATCH TIME: UPCOMING MATCHES SCHEDULE OF INDIA IN ICC T20 WORLD CUP 2024

Before the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 Tournament, India is going to engage in a warmup match against Bangladesh at Nassau County Ground, New York

India’s T20 World Cup 2024 Match Time: Upcoming Matches Schedule of India in ICC T20 World Cup 2024

India’s T20 World Cup 2024 Match Time: As per the Most Recent Updates, the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 Tournament is all set to be held on June 2, 2024, at 6:00 following IST (Indian Standard Time) at the Grand Prairie Stadium, Dallas, Texas. In the Opening Match of the Tournament, the USA will face off against Canada as well as 20 teams are going to compete in this tournament against each other in a total of 55 Matches. The ICC T20 World Cup 2024 is going to take place in a total of three venues in the USA and Six Venues in the Caribbean from 1 to 29 while the final match will be held in Barbados. Also, Out of 20 Teams, 10 Teams are going to play their first match of the 29-day tournament in the United States of America (USA) along with 16 matches to be scheduled in Lauderhill, Dallas and New York.

The Most Anticipated Match of the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 – India vs Pakistan is slated to be conducted on June 9, 2024, at the new Nassau County International Cricket Stadium, Long Island, New York. Although before the T20 World Cup 2024 tournament, India is going to play against Bangladesh in a warm-up match, today, June 1, 2024, in Nassau County Ground at 10:30 AM local time (8 PM as per the Indian Standard Time). In addition, the India vs Bangladesh Match is crucial as this match will provide an idea about the difficulty of the T20 World Cup 2024 Tournament and the Indian Team will get used to the conditions prior to the Opening Match of the T20 World Cup against Ireland on June 5, 2024.

The Warmup Match of India vs Bangladesh will be played as per the following rules given below:

The Warm Up Match of India vs Bangladesh will include 20 overs from each side and it will not be considered as an International T20 Status.
Teams will be permitted to handle all individuals from their 15-player squad.
According to the Change in the previous rights cycle, Teams are given the chance to play Upto two warm-up matches based on their arrival time at the event.

India's T20 World Cup 2024 Match Time: Match Timings of India

So India will commence its first match of the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 by locking horns with Ireland in New York on June 5, 2024, at 8 pm as per the IST (Indian Standard Time). However, The Match will commence at 10:30 am according to the USA Eastern Time EDT (Eastern Daylight Time).
