ICC T20 World Cup 2024: Super 8 teams, full schedule, match timings, venue, and streaming details
This year's tournament has already seen its fair share of surprises and upsets, with heavyweight teams like New Zealand and Pakistan bowing out in the early stages.

The Super 8 stage features eight formidable teams divided into two groups. Each team will face the others in their group in a round-robin format. (Reuters)

As the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2024 progresses into its highly anticipated Super 8 stage, the cricketing world is abuzz with excitement. This year’s tournament has already seen its fair share of surprises and upsets, with heavyweight teams like New Zealand and Pakistan bowing out in the early stages. Their absence from the Super 8 adds a new dynamic to the competition, leaving fans eager to see which teams will rise to the occasion.

Super 8 Teams and Format

The Super 8 stage features eight formidable teams divided into two groups. Each team will face the others in their group in a round-robin format. The top two teams from each group will advance to the semifinals.

Group 1: India, Afghanistan, Australia, Bangladesh/Netherlands
Group 2: USA, England, West Indies, South Africa

The stage is set for a thrilling series of matches as these teams compete for a spot in the semifinals.

Broadcasting of Super 8 matches in India

Cricket fans in India can catch all the excitement of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2024 Super 8 matches on the Star Sports Network. The matches will be broadcast live on:

Star Sports 1 HD/SD with English commentary.

Star Sports Hindi HD/SD with Hindi commentary.

Live Streaming of Super 8 matches in India

For those who prefer to watch online, the live streaming of the Super 8 round of the T20 World Cup 2024 will be available on Disney+ Hotstar. This ensures fans can enjoy the matches on the go, through their preferred devices.
