Sanath Jayasuriya joins Sri Lanka Team In Bangladesh
March 6, 2024 @ 1:33 PMby Rohit Pawar
Written by Rohit Pawar

Former opener, current Tourism Brand Ambassador and Consultant Coach (full time) Sanath Jayasuriya Yesterday joined Sri Lanka national team in Bangladesh to mark his presence in the T20I, ODI and Test matches encounters against the home side, Cricket Age reliably learns.

All the matches of the Tigers’ home series against Sri Lanka – remaining two Twenty20s, three One-Day Internationals and two Tests – will take place in Chattogram and Sylhet in March and April.

Sri Lanka won the first T20I in a nail-biting thriller. Second T20I to be played today evening.
