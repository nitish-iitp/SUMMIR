Nepal players applaud the fans following the ICC Men's T20 Cricket World Cup West Indies & USA 2024 match between Netherlands and Nepal at Grand Prairie Cricket Stadium on 4 June 2024 in Dallas, Texas. Credit: Matt Roberts-ICC/ICC via Getty Images

The Nepali men's cricket team started their second ICC T-20 World Cup journey against the Netherlands on Wednesday, 5 June. Defeated by six wickets, the South Asian team showed some lack of momentum in the post-rain game. Nepalese Cricket Association of Northern Territory President Aayush Sapkota and member Deepak Shrestha spoke to SBS Nepali about the team's performance and upcoming match predictions.
