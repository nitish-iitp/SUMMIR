T20 World Cup 2022 | Netherlands secures first Super 12 win, beats Zimbabwe by 5 wickets
The Netherlands chased down Zimbabwe’s small target of 118 with two overs to spare to get their first win of the Super 12 stage of the T20 World Cup, though they are already out of the tournament

Updated - November 02, 2022 02:48 pm IST - Adelaide

PTI
READ LATER
PRINT

Netherlands’ Max O’Dowd top-scored with 52 in the T20 World Cup cricket match against Zimbabwe in Adelaide, Australia on November 2, 2022 | Photo Credit: AP

In-form opener Max O’Dowd slammed a fine half century after a clinical show by the pace bowlers as Netherlands beat Zimbabwe by five wickets for their first win in the Super 12 stage of the ongoing T20 World Cup here on Wednesday.

The Netherlands chased down the small target of 118 with two overs to spare with O’Dowd hitting 52 from 47 balls, reaching 120 for 5 to collect their first points — two — though they are already out of the tournament.
