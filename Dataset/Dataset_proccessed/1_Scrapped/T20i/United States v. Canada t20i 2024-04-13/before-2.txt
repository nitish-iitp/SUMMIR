USA vs Canada 2nd T20I Live Streaming: How To Watch Live In India On TV, Online
USA vs Canada T20I live streaming: Check below everything you need to know about USA vs Canada 2nd T20I live streaming, telecast in India. 
By : ABP News Bureau | Updated at : 09 Apr 2024 01:33 PM (IST)
USA vs Canada 2nd T20I live streaming, telecast, venue, date, time, squads - all you need to know.
Source : @usacricket / X

USA vs Canada 2nd T20I live streaming, telecast, venue, date, match timings: United States and Canada are all set to square off in USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match, slated to be played on Wednesday (April 10) in Houston. The series opener between the two teams witnessed a commanding performance from the host team, with USA securing a thumping 6-wicket win over Canada.

USA and Canada are scheduled to play a five-match T20I series, from April 7 to April 13, 2024. Noshtush Kenjige bagged three wickets as the United States bowled out Canada for 132 in the series opener. In the second innings, USA chased down the target in 17.3 overs to win the match by 6 wickets.

Check below everything you need to know about USA vs Canada 2nd T20I live streaming, telecast in India. 

When will USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match be played?

USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match will be played on Wednesday (April 10). 

Where will USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match be played?

USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match will be played at the Prairie View Cricket Complex in Houston.

What time will USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match be played?

USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match starts at 1:30 am IST onwards on Wednesday (April 10).

When, where to watch USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match live streaming in India?

USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match live streaming in India will be available on USA Cricket's YouTube channel.

When, where to watch USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match live telecast in India?

There won't be any live telecast available in India for USA vs Canada 2nd T20I match.

USA vs Canada T20I series - Squads

Canada: Saad bin Zafar (c), Ajayveer Hundal, Dilon Heyliger, Dilpreet Singh, Harsh Thaker, Ishwarjot Singh, Jeremy Gordon, Navneet Dhaliwal, Nikhil Dutta, Nicholas Kirton, Pargat Singh, Sahib Malhotra, Shahid Ahmadzai, Shreyas Mova (wk), Srimantha Wijeyeratne (wk), Uday Bhagwan, Aaron Johnson, Udaybir Walia, Yuvraj Samra. Reserve players: Juanoy Drysdale, Saiteja Mukkamalla, Shayan Jahangir.

USA: Monank Patel (c), Aaron Jones (vc), Andries Gous, Corey Anderson, Gajanand Singh, Harmeet Singh, Jessy Singh, Milind Kumar, Nisarg Patel, Nitish Kumar, Noshtush Kenjige, Saurabh Netravalkar, Shadley Van Schalkwyk, Steven Taylor, Usman Rafiq.
