Navaika is best wicket taker in Group B of T20 Women's World Cup Qualifier

Nasimana Navaika from the Vanuatu Holiday Inn Women’s Cricket Team has been named the T20 Best Wicket Taker during Group B Stage of the ICC Women’s Cricket World Cup Qualifier in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.

The 28-year-old showcased her skills on the cricket field, clinching the title after her stellar performance saw her secure 10 wickets across 4 innings, boasting an impressive average of 7.5 runs per over.
