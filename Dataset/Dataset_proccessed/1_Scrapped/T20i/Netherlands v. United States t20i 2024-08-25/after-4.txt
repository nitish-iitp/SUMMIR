NED vs USA 3rd T20I Dream11 Prediction

Netherlands will be up against the USA in match 3 of the tri-series at the Sportpark Maarschalkerweerd in Utrecht on Sunday, August 25.

The Netherlands, the USA and Canada are engaged in a six-match tri-series which will run from August 23 to Wednesday, August 28.

The Netherlands opened their campaign with a win over Canada by five wickets at the same venue whereas the second match of the series between Canada and the USA was washed out due to rain.

This will be the Netherlands' second out of four matches they will play in the series. The USA will play five games whereas Canada will feature in just three matches.

USA headed into the ongoing tri-series on the back of a promising campaign at the T20 World Cup 2024 where they defeated former champions Pakistan and even reached the Super 8 stage.

On the other hand, Canada and the Netherlands were eliminated from the competition in the group stage.

TOSS: The match toss between the Netherlands and the USA will take place at 7:00 PM IST.

Time: August 25, 7:30 PM IST.

Venue: Sportpark Maarschalkerweerd, Utrecht
