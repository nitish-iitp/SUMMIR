Women’s U19 T20 World Cup 2023: When and where to watch India Women vs New Zealand Women U19 semifinal in India?
The Women in Blue will now face New Zealand Women in the U10 T20 World Cup 2023 semifinal on January 27, 2023.

Shafali Verma-led Team India is having a dream run in the Women’s U19 T20 World Cup 2023. The side remained unbeaten in the Group D and finished at the top of the table in the Group D. Following this, the India Women also finished first in the Super Six Group 1 table with three wins and one loss in the four matches.


 

Follow all the updates on SportsTiger mobile app

 

The Women in Blue will now face New Zealand Women in the U10 T20 World Cup 2023 semifinal on January 27, 2023. The match will take place at the Senwes Park in Potchefstroom with the winner getting a direct qualification in the final, scheduled to take place on January 29 at the same venue.

 

Women’s U19 T20 World Cup 2023: India Women vs New Zealand Women

When and where will India Women vs New Zealand Women U19 T20 World Cup 2023 semifinal take place?
The Women’s U19 T20 World Cup 2023 semifinal between India Women and New Zealand Women will take place on Friday, January 27 at 01:30   p m IST at the Senwes Park, Potchefstroom.

 

Where to watch the live telecast of India Women vs New Zealand Women U19 T20 World Cup 2023 semifinal in India?
The live telecast of the Women’s U19 T20 World Cup 2023 semifinal between India Women and New Zealand Women will be available on Star Sports Network in India.

 

Where to watch the live stream of India Women vs New Zealand Women U19 T20 World Cup 2023 semifinal in India?
The live stream of the Women’s U19 T20 World Cup 2023 semifinal between India Women and New Zealand Women will be available on Fancode  in India.
