Nepal vs Oman 5th T20I Live Streaming And Telecast: When And Where To Watch Online Today?
Nepal and Oman will clash in the fifth T20I of the tri-series. Nepal have a win and two defeats in three games while Oman have a win and a defeat. Who will prevail in the battle of associates? Here are the live-streaming details of the match.
Written by:
Faham Uddin

Updated Oct 2, 2024, 19:08 IST

Nepal vs Oman 5th T20I Live Streaming And Telecast: When And Where To Watch Online Today? (UAE cricket/X)

Nepal and Oman will clash in the fifth match of the T20I series. Nepal played Canada in the first match and suffered a defeat by 14 runs. The following match saw them hammer Oman by eight wickets. They faced Canada for the second time in the series but the fate didn't change as they lost by four wickets. Meanwhile, Oman bounced back well and defeated Canada after losing the first game to Nepal. The eight-wicket win over Canada would give Oman massive confidence heading into the game against Nepal.
Speaking about the top performers for Nepal, Anil Sah has looked good, scoring 77 runs in three games while Aasif Sheikh has scored 65 runs. With the ball, Sandeep Lamichhane has taken six wickets in three games while Sompal Kami has taken four in two games.
For Oman, Shoaib Khan has scored 75 runs in two games while Aqib Ilyas has scored 55 runs in two innings. Aqib Ilyas is the leading wicket-taker with five wickets in two games while Fayyaz Butt has taken three in two matches.
Nepal vs Oman 5th T20I Date, Time And Venue
Nepal vs Oman fifth T20I will be played at the Maple Leaf North-West Ground, King City. The match is scheduled for October 2, 2024 at 9:30 PM IST
Nepal vs Oman 5th T20I Pitch Report
The wicket has been good for batting and teams should be able to post big runs. However, it is important for batters to get set before cutting loose.
Nepal vs Oman 5th T20I Weather Report
The weather will be mostly clear and the chances of rain interruption are low. The temperature will range between 17 and 8 degrees throughout the day.
Nepal vs Canada 1st T20I Live Streaming
There will be no TV broadcast of the T20 match in India. There are no details available for the live streaming of the T20I series in India. In Canada and the USA, the matches can be streamed live on Willow TV. For Nepal and the rest of the world, fans can catch the action live on STYX Sports
Nepal vs Oman 5th T20I Squads
Nepal Squad: Anil Sah, Aarif Sheikh(w), Rohit Paudel(c), Kushal Malla, Kushal Bhurtel, Gulsan Jha, Dipendra Singh Airee, Karan KC, Sandeep Lamichhane, Lalit Rajbanshi, Sompal Kami, Aasif Sheikh, Bhim Sharki, Dev Khanal, Arjun Saud, Rijan Dhakal
Oman Squad: Aqib Ilyas(c), Kashyap Prajapati, Shoaib Khan, Zeeshan Maqsood, Ayaan Khan, Khalid Kail, Pratik Athavale(w), Fayyaz Butt, Kaleemullah, Rafiullah, Shakeel Ahmed, Jay Odedra, Samay Shrivastava, Muzahir Raza, Bukkapatnam Siddharth, Mehran Khan, Jatinder Singh
