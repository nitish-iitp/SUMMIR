Pathum Nissanka most runs in first 20 Twenty20 Games
Saturday, June 11, 2022 - 01:00
Print Edition
Sports
Indika Welagedara
Pathum Nissanka

Sri Lanka opening batsman Pathum Nissanka continued his good batting form after completing 20, T20 international innings. Nissanka has a better record compared to the likes of legendary batsmen Kumar Sangakkara and T.M. Dilshan.

Most runs in the first 20, T20 international innings for Sri Lanka

598 – Pathum Nissanka
576 – Kumar Sangakkara
572 – TM Dilshan
544 – Kusal Perera
542 – Sanath Jayasuriya

Also the most 30+ scores for Sri Lanka in their first 20, T20 International innings is as follows,

10 Pathum Nissanka
9 – TM Dilshan
8 – Kumar Sangakkara
7 – Sanath Jayasuriya
