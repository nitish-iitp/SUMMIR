India vs Hong Kong, Asia Cup 2022 Highlights: IND beat HK by 40 runs, qualify for Super Four
By 
HT Sports Desk
Aug 31, 2022 11:21 PM IST
India vs Hong Kong, Asia Cup 2022 highlights: Suryakumar Yadav's astonishing assault and Virat Kohli's half century helped India scored 192/2 after which Hong Kong managed to score 152/5 in their 20 overs.
Kohli put up 98 runs off just 42 balls

IND vs HK, Asia Cup 2022 Highlights: Suryakumar Yadav and Virat Kohli scored a whopping 98 runs off just 42 balls. Suryakumar is unbeaten on a whopping 68 off just 26 deliveries, having hit six fours and as many sixes. He has a strike rate of 261.53. Kohli, meanwhile, is unbeaten on 59 off 44, with one four and three sixes. Rohit Sharma and KL Rahul got off to a rather quick start but tapered off, with the latter eventually falling for a 39-ball 36. Kohli and Suryakumar's recovery effort propelled India to 192/2.
