PREVIEW

Namibia cricket team is all set to lock horns against United Arab Emirates in the 5th game of the Namibia T20I Tri-Series on October 2, Wednesday at Windhoek. Notably, the two sides will be looking to win the upcoming game to boost their rankings in the ongoing series.

The hosts, Namibia, have lost two out of two matches in the series and place themselves at the third and last spot. The side must be eager to register their first win in the series. In contrast, the UAE side are sitting in the second spot, having won one out of the two matches they have played so far. They will be looking to win this clash to jump to the top of the table.

As the team gears up for the upcoming clash, all eyes will be on the thrilling competition set to unfold on the cricket field. Ahead of the game, here is a look at the Dream11 predictions, the fantasy tips, the pitch report, the top player picks, and the best-predicted XIs for the fantasy contests of the match.

It has a history of favoring bowlers, particularly pacers, as seen with 38 wickets taken by pacers and 24 by spinners in the last 5 matches. The average score at the venue is 148 runs, indicating that batters need to be cautious early on.
