GPSC CCE Prelims Result 2023
NEW DELHI: The Gujarat Public Service Commission has announced the GPSC CCE Prelims Result 2023 today, April 18, 2023. Candidates who appeared for the Combined Competitive (Preliminary) Exam for the Advt. No. 20/2022‐ 23, Gujarat Administrative Service, Class‐1, Gujarat Civil Service, Class 1 & 2 and Gujarat State Municipal Chief Officers’ Service, Class‐ 2 can check and download the prelims result from the official website - gpsc.gujarat.gov.in.
A total of 3806 candidates have been declared provisionally qualified in the GPSC CCE Prelims exam which was conducted on January 08, 2023.
Candidates can check the Roll Numbers who have provisionally qualified for appearing in the Main Written Examination in the result notification.
It shall be noted that the candidates are declared provisionally qualified for appearing in the Main Written Examination, subject to their fulfilling all conditions of eligibility of Advt. No. 20/2022-23.
"In accordance with the rules of the examination, all these candidates have to apply again for admission to the main examination in the detailed application form, as per the instruction of the commission," reads the official notification.

Apart from the result, the commission has also released the details of the category-wise qualifying standard (cut-off marks) and a category-wise total of qualified candidates.
How to download GPSC CCE Prelims Result 2023?
Step 1: Visit the official website at gpsc.gujarat.gov.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on the PDF link available for Eligibility List (Main) - 20/2022-23 - LECM-20-202223.
Step 3: A new PDF file will then appear on the screen.

Step 4: Check if your roll number is there in the list of provisionally qualified candidates for the Main exam.
Step 5: Download the same and take a printout for further reference.
Check Result Here

"All Candidates can view their obtained marks from 20.04.2023, 16:00 hours onwards by using his/her credentials on web-link to be displayed on Commission’s website: https://gpsc.gujarat.gov.in," reads the official notification.
The candidatures of two candidates have been cancelled by the Commission as they were caught with mobile in the examination hall during the exam.
