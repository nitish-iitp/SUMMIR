The ECS T10 Cyprus 2024 will start on June 1st, 2024, at the Ypsonas Cricket Ground in Limassol. Organized by European Cricket (ECS), the first edition of the ECS Cyprus T10 will feature sixteen (16) teams competing in 68 matches over thirteen (14) days. All matches in the ECS T10 Cyprus will be played at the Ypsonas Cricket Ground, Limassol. The ECS T10 Cyprus final will be held on June¬†14, 2024.

The 16 cricket teams are divided into two groups. Each team will play 7 matches against each other.

Each team will compete against the others seven times in a double round-robin format. The top four teams from each group will qualify for the quarter-finals; four quarter-final rounds, two semifinal rounds, and one bronze-final round are scheduled.
