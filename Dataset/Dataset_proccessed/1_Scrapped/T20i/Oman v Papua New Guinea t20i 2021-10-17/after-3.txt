ICC T20 World Cup 2021 Points Table, Round 1: Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Namibia, Scotland Qualify For Super 12
The Round 1 matches will be held from October 17 onwards and will end on October 22. From October 23 onwards the tournament proper will take place. In Super 12, two groups will feature six teams each.

Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Namibia, and Scotland have qualified for the Super 12 stage of the T20 World Cup 2021. Namibia in their first-ever world cup managed to advance to the next round and will be placed in Super 12 Group 2 who will also have Scotland joining them. Bangladesh and Sri Lanka will be placed in Group 1 of the Super 12 stage. Group A of Round 1 features Ireland, Namibia, Netherlands and Sri Lanka while Group B comprises of Bangladesh, Oman, Papua New Guinea and Scotland. Top two teams from each group will proceed to Super 12 and join other eight teams. Group A topper and the second-placed team from Group B will join Super 12 Group 1 while Group B topped and the second-placed team from Group A will join Super 12 Group 2.

The Round 1 matches will be held from October 17 onwards and will end on October 22. From October 23 onwards the tournament proper will take place. In Super 12, two groups will feature six teams each. Two teams from each group will qualify for semi-finals. The final of the ICC T20 World Cup 2021 will be held on November 14.
