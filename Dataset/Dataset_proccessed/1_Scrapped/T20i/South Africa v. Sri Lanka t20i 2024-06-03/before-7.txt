ICC T20 World Cup 2024: ICC World Cup Schedule Out; Know the Details of India Match, Group Team Details

ICC T20 World Cup 2024: As per the latest updates, the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 will commence soon at the Grand Prairie Stadium, Dallas, Texas. This World Cup is facilitated mutually by the United States of America and the West Indies and vows to be an exhilarating Cricket Festival. The competition will include a sum of 20 Teams engaging in 55 matches to be delegated title holders in the most popular tournament of the game. India, Australia, England and Pakistan are among this tournament’s popular participating teams and powerhouses while Nations like Nepal, Nambia, Uganda and the USA are rising gradually in these tournaments. Furthermore, the match venue has been circulated throughout the host nations.

According to the Indian Standard Time, The 9th edition of The ICC T20 World Cup 2024 is going to commence on Sunday, June 2, 2024, at the Grand Prairie Stadium, Dallas, Texas where the USA will encounter against Canada. As the 20 Teams are going to compete for the first time in the T20 World Cup’s 17 years history, Partnered countries could agitate Test-playing countries amid the changing elements of the game’s shortest format. The Most Demanding Match, India vs Pakistan will be held on June 9, 2024. Also, The Final Match is scheduled in Barbados on June 29, 2024. However, Prior to the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 Tournament, India is going to compete in a one-and-only warm-up match with Bangladesh on June 1, 2024.

ICC T20 World Cup 2024: Format of the Tournament

Men’s T20 World Cup First Stage will be staged in both the West Indies and USA whereas Super 8, the second round will be played in the nations of the Caribbean.

20 Teams are going to compete out of which five teams will be divided into four groups, in the First Round.
For the Super 8 Round, Only two teams from each specific group will be qualified.
Out of Eight Teams, four teams will be divided into two groups for the Super 8 Round.
In the Super 8 Round, Two teams from each group will be qualified for the semifinals.
At last, The Winner Teams from each group will compete against each other in the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 scheduled on June 29, 2024.
