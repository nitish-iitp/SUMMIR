NEET PG Counselling 2023: Round 1 registrations closing today; Apply here

The Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) is closing registrations for the first round of NEET PG counselling 2023 today. Candidates are urged to apply on the official website before the deadline. The counselling process will be conducted in four rounds for 50% All India Quota seats, while the state quota admissions are already underway. The article highlights the important dates and steps for the counselling process, emphasizing the need for eligible candidates to complete the registration within the specified timeframe for seat allotment.
Read More
The Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) is all set to close registrations for the first round of National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for Postgraduate (NEET PG) counselling 2023 today, i.e., on Tuesday, 1st August 2023.
Candidates who have not yet registered for NEET-PG Counselling are urged not to wait any longer and to apply immediately on the official website - mcc.nic.in, as the website may become unresponsive during the closing hours.

The registration window will remain open until noon, and the payment facility for NEET PG 2023 counselling round one will be available until 8 pm.
MCC conducts NEET PG counselling for 50% All India Quota seats in four rounds, namely, 1, 2, 3, and stray round. Meanwhile, PG medical counselling for 50% state quota admissions is already underway for states such as Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, and others.

The choice filling and locking process for NEET PG counselling 2023 will be conducted from 28th July to 2nd August 2023. Candidates have the option to fill their choices until 11:55 PM on August 2, 2023. The choice locking will start at 03:00 PM on August 2, 2023 and continued until 11:55 PM, as per the server time. The seat allotment process will take place from August 3 to 4, 2023, and the result will be declared on August 5, 2023.

Candidates will be required to upload their documents on the MCC portal on August 6, 2023. The reporting and joining dates were scheduled from August 7, to 13, 2023. Institutes will verify the data of the prospective candidates and will shareit with MCC from August 14 to 16, 2023.
In Round 2 of NEET PG counselling 2023, the registration facility will be available from August 17, 2023 to 21, 2023 until 12:00 Noon. Candidates who have not participated in the previous rounds or those who wish to upgrade their choices can register during this period. It is important for eligible candidates to complete the registration process within the specified timeframe to be considered for the seat allotment process in Round 2.
The Medical Council Committee (MCC) is the governing authority responsible for conducting NEET PG counselling 2023, offering approximately 1 lakh seats for postgraduate medical courses. The registration process started from 27th July to close today in online mode, and the counselling will be conducted in four rounds, all online. To be eligible for counselling, candidates must have qualified the NEET PG exam. The counselling fee is set at Rs 1000 for All India Quota seats and Rs 500 for Deemed and Central Universities. For further information, applicants can visit the official website mcc.nic.in.
Direct link to apply online
How to apply for the NEET PG Counselling 2023 Round One Registrations
Step 1: Visit the official website of the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) - mcc.nic.in.
Step 2: Click on the "NEET PG Counselling 2023" link on the homepage to begin the registration process.
Step 3: Create a new account or log in with your existing credentials to access the registration form.
Step 4: Fill in all the required details, including personal information, NEET PG exam details, and contact information.
Step 5: Choose the preferred counselling round and select the desired course and college choices from the available options.
Step 6: Pay the counselling fee of Rs. 1000 (for All India Quota seats) or Rs. 500 (for Deemed and Central Universities) through the provided payment gateway.
Once the registration is complete, you will receive a confirmation message. Ensure to keep a copy of the submitted form and payment receipt for future reference.
