IND-W vs SL-W Women’s Asia Cup T20 2024 Final Preview: Likely Playing XIs, Key Battles, H2H and More About India Women vs Sri Lanka Women Cricket Match in Dambulla
The Indian women's cricket team is all set to clash against Sri Lanka in the final of the Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 in Dambulla on July 28. The Indian team will be hoping to win yet another Asia Cup title.

The Indian women's cricket team is all set to clash against Sri Lanka in the final of the Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 at the Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium, Dambulla. The Indian women's team has showcased a terrific display of cricket in the current edition of the tournament and they will hope to repeat the same against Sri Lanka in the final. On the other hand, the Sri Lanka women's cricket team has played the same brand of cricket so far and they will want to win their first-ever Asia Cup title. Smriti Mandhana Gifts Mobile Phone to Specially-Abled Fan Adeesha Herath After IND-W vs PAK-W Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 Match, Heartwarming Video Goes Viral.

Harmanpreet Kaur and Co have several stars such as Smriti Mandhana, Shafali Verma, Deepti Sharma, Jemimah Rodrigues and Renuka Singh Thakur, who have showcased brilliant performances in the tournament so far. The Indian women's team management would want them to repeat their same performance against Sri Lanka Women in the finals. India Enter Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 Final; Renuka Singh, Radha Yadav Help Women in Blue Script Dominant 10-Wicket Victory Over Bangladesh in Semifinal.

Sri Lanka women's cricket team has been led by Chamari Athapaththu who has been the leading run-scorer of the team. The left-handed batter has already scored a hundred and the management would want her to perform the same against India in the final. The Sri Lankan women's team has made a place in the final of the Women's Asia Cup but are yet to win a title.

IND-W vs SL-W Head-to-Head Record in T20I Matches

India Women's team has faced Sri Lanka in 29 T20I matches so far out of which they have emerged victorious in 19 games whereas nine have been won by Sri Lanka. One match has ended with no result.

IND-W vs SL-W Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 Semi-Final Key Battles

The key battles in the IND-W vs SL-W Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 match will see the duo of Shafali Verma and Smriti Mandhana taking on Sri Lankan women's team bowlers. Other than this battle, it will be interesting to see the battle between Harmanpreet Kaur and Chamari Athapaththu.

IND-W vs SL-W Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 Semi-Final Venue and Match Timing

The India Women's National Cricket Team vs Sri Lanka Women's National Cricket Team Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 match will be played at the Rangiri Dambulla International Cricket Stadium in Dambulla, Sri Lanka on July 28, 2024. The IND-W vs SL-W Women's Asia Cup T20 2024 Semi-Final will begin at 07:00 PM IST (Indian Standard Time).
