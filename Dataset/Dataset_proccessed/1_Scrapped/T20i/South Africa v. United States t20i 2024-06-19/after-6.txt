South Africa vs United States Highlights, T20 World Cup 2024 Match Today: Despite a power-packed partnership between Andries Gous (80*) and Harmeet Singh (38) the United States fell 18 runs short of the 194 posted by South Africa in Antigua during the Group 2 Super-8 T20 World Cup match. Earlier, United States won the toss and opted to bowl against South Africa at the Sir Vivian Richards Stadium, North Sound in Antigua on Wednesday.
