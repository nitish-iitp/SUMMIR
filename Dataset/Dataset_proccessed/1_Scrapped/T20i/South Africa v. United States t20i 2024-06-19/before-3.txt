T20 World Cup 2024 Super 8: Updated list of qualified teams, match schedule, date and venues
T20 World Cup 2024, Super 8: Here is a look at the qualified teams, schedule, match dates and venues for the Super 8 stage of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 to be held in the West Indies.
By: Sports Desk
Updated: June 17, 2024 10:25 IST
4 min read
T20 World Cup 2024: India, Australia, South Africa and West Indies were the first four teams to qualify for the Super 8. (AP)

T20 World Cup 2024 updated schedule: The T20 World Cup 2024 is heading towards the Super 8 stage after a series of upsets and nail-biting results in the first-ever 20-team group stage tournament held in the West Indies and the United States.

Eight teams have now secured their spot into the next stage from the 20 that started the group stages. On Monday, Bangladesh (from Group D) joined India (Group A), USA (Group A), Australia (Group B), England (Group B), Afghanistan (Group C), West Indies (Group C) and South Africa (Group D) and Bangladesh (Group D) in the Super Eights.

In the Super 8, the teams will again split into two groups in a single-round robin league from which the top two teams in either group advance into the semi-finals. The teams will be separated according to their pre-determined seedings for the top-eight T20I sides.
