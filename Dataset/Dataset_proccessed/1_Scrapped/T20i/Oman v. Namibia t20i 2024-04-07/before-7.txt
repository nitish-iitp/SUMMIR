Oman vs Namibia (OMN vs NAM) 5th T20I Match Preview

In the 4th T20I of Namibia’s tour of Oman, Namibia secured a convincing victory by 24 runs. Batting first, Namibia posted a challenging total of 164/4 in their allotted 20 overs. Jean-Pierre Kotze starred with the bat, smashing a brilliant 78 runs off 51 deliveries, while David Wiese contributed a valuable 51 runs off 32 balls. Despite some resistance from Oman’s bowlers, including Samay Shrivastava who claimed 2 wickets, Namibia managed to set a formidable target.

In response, Oman faced an uphill battle in their chase, eventually finishing at 140/9 after their 20 overs. Naseem Khushi provided a quick start with a blistering 34 runs off just 14 balls, but Oman couldn’t sustain the momentum. Mohammad Nadeem and Aqib Ilyas also made notable contributions with scores of 30 and 24 runs respectively. However, Namibia’s bowlers, led by David Wiese with figures of 3/26, and supported by Gerhard Erasmus and Tangeni Lungameni, managed to contain Oman’s batting lineup.
