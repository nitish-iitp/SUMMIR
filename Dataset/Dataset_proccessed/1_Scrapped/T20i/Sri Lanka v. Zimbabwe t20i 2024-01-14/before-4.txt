Bangladesh Vs Sri Lanka ICC U-19 Men’s Cricket World Cup 2024 Warm-Up Live Streaming: When And Where To Watch BAN U-19 Vs SL U-19 On TV And Online

ICC Under-19 Men’s Cricket World Cup 2024 opens up with the warm-up matches. Here is all you need to know about the BAN U-19 Vs SL U-19 warm-up game - venue, timing, live streaming details

Bangladesh are the current Asian U-19 champions

With the ICC Men's U-19 World Cup 2024 just few days away, the 16 participating teams will be seen in action in the warm-up matches commencing from Saturday, January 13. (More Cricket News)

The tournament is set to be played in the Rainbow Nation. The first-ever U-19 World Cup was held in 1988, with the Aussies clinching the title. 

India have recorded the most U-19 titles (5), whereas the Aussies have won it thrice. Pakistah have two titles whereas England, South Africa and West Indies have one title to their name.

Live Streaming, Telecast Details

Here are the live streaming details for the BAN U-19 Vs SL U-19 warm-up match:-

When will the warm-up match be played?

The warm-up match will be played on Sunday, January 14, 2024 at 1:30pm IST.

Where will the warm-up match be played?

The match will be played at the Tshwane University of Technology Oval, Pretoria.
