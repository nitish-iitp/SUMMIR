The Pakistan National Women’s T20 Tournament 2024 will begin on January 15. Here is the schedule, including the full fixtures list and match timings.

Subscribe to the Wisden Cricket YouTube channel for post-match analysis, player interviews, and much more.

The National Women’s T20 Tournament 2024 will feature six teams – Karachi (led by Fatima Sana), Lahore (Nida Dar), Multan (Umm-e-Hani), Peshawar (Aleena Shah), Quetta (Saima Malik), and Rawalpindi (Aliya Riaz).

The six teams will play each other twice in a double round robin league format. Three matches will be played simultaneously every day, at the Ayub Park Ground in Rawalpindi, and the Diamond Cricket Ground and Shoaib Akhtar Stadium in Islamabad. These will be followed by a final.

The prize money includes PKR one million for the champion side; PKR 0.5 million for the runners-up; PKR 50,000 for the Player of the Tournament; PKR 20,000 for every Player of the Match; and PKR 25,000 for each of the best batter, bowler, and wicketkeeper of the competition.
