Hong Kong, Malaysia and Kuwait will prepare for the upcoming ICC Men's T20 World Cup Asia Sub-Regional Qualifier A with a tri-nation series at Kuala Lumpore. The tri-nation series will kick-off with the Hong Kong vs Kuwait match. The Hong Kong vs Kuwait Tri-Nation series match will be played at the  Selangor Turf Club, Kuala Lumpur and has a start time of 8:00 AM Indian Standard Time (IST) on August 21. Unfortunately, fans in India can't get the viewing option of the match on their TV sets due to the lack of a broadcast partner. The Hong Kong vs Kuwait Tri-Nation series match live streaming viewing option is likely to be available for the fans on the Malaysia Cricket Live and the Cricket Hong Kong, China YouTube channels.
