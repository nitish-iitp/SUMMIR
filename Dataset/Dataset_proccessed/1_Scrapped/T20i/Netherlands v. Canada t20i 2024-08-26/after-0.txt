PREVIEW

Canada will take on the Netherlands in the fourth match of the T20I Tri-Series. This match will be played at Sportpark Maarschalkerweerd in Utrecht. The Netherlands got over the line in the opening game of the tri-series against Canada and are the table toppers. They also won their previous match against USA. On the other hand, Canada lost their opening game, and their previous game ended in NR.
