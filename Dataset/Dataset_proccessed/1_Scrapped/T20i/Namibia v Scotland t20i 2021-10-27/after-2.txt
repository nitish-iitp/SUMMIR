By 
hindustantimes.com
Oct 27, 2021 11:00 PM IST
Scotland vs Namibia T20 World Cup 2021 Highlights: Scotland tumbled Bangladesh in the opening round of the tournament, while Namibia impressed with wins against the Netherlands and Ireland.
SCO vs NAM T20 World Cup 2021 Highlights

Scotland vs Namibia T20 World Cup 2021 Highlights: Namibia started their Super 12 campaign on a winning note as they beat Scotland by four wickets in the ongoing T20 World Cup in Abu Dhabi on Wednesday evening. After inviting Scotland to bat first, Ruben Trumpelmann helped Namibia start on a strong note, and restricted Scotland to 109/8 in 20 overs. Trumpelmann picked three wickets in the first over, a blow from which Scotland could never recover. In response, Namibia got off to a decent start, maintaining a check on the required run-rate. The team did lose three quick wickets in the middle overs but JJ Smit's cool composure helped Namibia complete the run-chase with five balls to spare. ...Read More
