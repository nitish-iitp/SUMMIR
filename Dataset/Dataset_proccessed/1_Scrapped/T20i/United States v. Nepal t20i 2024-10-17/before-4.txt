USA TEAM ANNOUNCED FOR THE STAKE STARS & SUMMIT TROPHY T20I NEPAL SERIES
By USA Cricket MediaOctober 13, 2024

October 13, 2024

USA Cricket today announced its 15-member team selected for the forthcoming T20I bilateral series against Nepal, also named as the Stake Stars & Summit Trophy.

The Stake Stars and Summits Trophy is set to kick off on October 17th, 2024 at Grand Prairie Cricket Stadium in Grand Prairie, Texas, and will feature three T20Is between Team USA and Nepal.

USA T20I Team: Monank Patel (Captain), Andries Gous, Abhishek Paradkar, Aaron Jones, Harmeet Singh, Juanoy Drysdale, Muhammad Ali Khan, Milind Kumar, Nosthusha Kenjige, Jasdeep Singh, Saurabh Netravalkar, Saiteja Mukkamalla, Shayan Jahangir, Utkarsh Srivastava, Yasir Mohammad.

Stake Stars and Summit Trophy Schedule: 
Venue – Grand Prairie Cricket Stadium, Start time – 1900 hrs CST

1st T20I – Thursday, Oct 17th, 2024
2nd T20I – Saturday, Oct 19th, 2024
3rd T20I – Sunday, Oct 20th, 2024

Additionally, to enhance its roster and develop emerging talent for the national team, USAC Selectors also unveiled the USA-A team. This team will participate in practice matches against Scotland and Nepal prior to the start of the CWC League 2 Series, set to take place at Grand Prairie, Texas, from October 25th. The two practice matches are scheduled for October 22nd and 23rd.

USA-A Team for Matches Against Scotland and Nepal: Rahul Jariwala (Captain), Sushant Modani, Nitish Kumar, Sanjay Krishnamurthi, Skanda Rohit Sharma, Utkarsh Srivastava, Kwame Patton Jr., Vatsal Vaghela, Ali Sheikh, Zia Shahzad, Abhishek Paradkar, Ayan Desai, Aarin Nadkarni.

ABOUT USA CRICKET

USA Cricket is the National Federation for cricket in the United States as recognized by the International Cricket Council. USA Cricket’s mission is to govern, regulate, develop and promote all forms of cricket at all levels in the United States, and to enable U.S. cricketers to achieve sustained competitive excellence and successfully compete in national and international competition. Through a diverse Board of Directors that has representation from most cricket stakeholder groups, USA Cricket will lead the sport to new heights by creating and administering value added programs to grow and develop the sport.
