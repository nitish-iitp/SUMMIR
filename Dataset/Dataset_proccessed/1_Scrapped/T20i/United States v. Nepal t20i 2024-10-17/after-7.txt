USA vs NEP Match Preview:

The first T20I between the USA and Nepal will be played on October 18, 2024, at Al Amerat Cricket Ground, Muscat, Oman. The USA will be led by Monank Patel while Rohit Paudel will be leading the Nepali side.

Aaron Jones, Saiteja Mukkamalla, and Shayan Jahangir are among the key players from the USA team. On the other hand, Dipendra Singh Airee, Gulsan Jha, and Kushal Malla are among the vital players from the Nepali team.

This will be the first-ever T20I match between the USA and Nepal, so both will be looking to start their journey on a winning note.
