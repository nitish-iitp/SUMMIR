By

India vs Afghanistan 3rd T20I Highlights: Afghanistan incredibly made 212/6 to tie the scores and the first Super Over ended in a tie as well.
India vs Afghanistan 3rd T20I Highlights:

India vs Afghanistan 3rd T20I Highlights: The match looked to be done and dusted when India had dismissed both of Afghanistan's openers after the 120th over as they defended a target of 213. However, Mohammad Nabi and Gulbadin Naib put up a sensational fightback that pegged back the Indians. Nabi fell for 34 off 16, thus ending a 56-run stand between him and Gulbadin that came in just 22 balls. However, Gulbadin carried on as wickets fell at the other end. He ended up being unbeaten on 55 off 23 and Afghanistan tied the scores, thus incredibly pushing the match into a Super Over. Both teams ended up scoring 16 runs in the first one, which meant that a second Super Over is now needed to decide the match. India scored 11 runs and lost two wickets in their second Super Over innings which means Afghanistan needed 12 runs to record their first-ever T20I match win over India.
