PREVIEW

Mongolia (MON) will take on Hong Kong (HK) in Match 6 of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Asia Qualifier A at Bayuemas Oval, Malaysia. Hong Kong had a strong start to the tournament, winning their opening match, and currently leads the points table. On the other hand, Mongolia lost their opening match and currently occupy the bottom position on the points table. 

As the team gears up for the upcoming clash, all eyes will be on the thrilling competition set to unfold on the cricket field. Ahead of the game, here is a look at the Dream11 predictions, the fantasy tips, the pitch report, the top player picks, and the best-predicted XIs for the fantasy contests of the match.
