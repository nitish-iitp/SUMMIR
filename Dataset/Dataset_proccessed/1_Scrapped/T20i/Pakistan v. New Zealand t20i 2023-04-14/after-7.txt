Pakistan vs New Zealand 2023 LIVE Streaming: Here's everything you need to know about Pakistan vs New Zealand 2023 Live Streaming, Schedule & Squads.
By : ABP News Bureau | Updated at : 14 Apr 2023 12:29 PM (IST)
Live telecast of PAK vs NZ T20 and ODI series in India will be available on TV channels of Sony Sports Network, fans can watch PAK vs NZ 2023 T20 & ODI series live streaming in India on SonyLiv app.
Source : Getty Images

Pakistan vs New Zealand 2023 T20 & ODI series Live Telecast: Pakistan vs New Zealand 2023 Live Broadcast, Schedule, Time Table & Squads | New Zealand tour of Pakistan 2023: New Zealand cricket team is scheduled to play 5 T20Is and 5 ODIs on their Pakistan tour. New Zealand tour of Pakistan will kick-off from April 14, while the last match between the two teams will be played on May 7. NZ tour of PAK will begin with a five-match T20 series and later a five-match ODI series will be played between Pakistan and New Zealand. Pakistani cricketers aren't allowed to participate in IPL but many New Zealand star players are currently playing in the 2023 edition of Indian Premier League (IPL). Pakistan will head into PAK vs NZ 2023 series after losing a T20I series to Afghanistan. New Zealand, on the other hand, will be without many key players.

PAK vs NZ T20Is: Pakistan's Gaddafi Stadium in Lahore will host PAK vs NZ 1st T20I on Friday, April 14. 

Ahead of high-octane PAK vs NZ T20Is, let's know all about Pakistan vs New Zealand 2023 Live Streaming, Schedule & Squads.

Pakistan vs New Zealand T20 Series 2023 Schedule

PAK vs NZ 1st T2O: April 14, 2023 - Gaddafi Stadium, Lahore 

PAK vs NZ 2nd T2O: April 15, 2023 - Gaddafi Stadium, Lahore 

PAK vs NZ 3rd T2O: April 17, 2023 - Gaddafi Stadium, Lahore 

PAK vs NZ 4th T2O: April 20, 2023 - Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium, Rawalpindi, Lahore 

PAK vs NZ 5th T2O: April 24, 2023 - Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium, Rawalpindi, Lahore 

Pakistan vs New Zealand ODI Series 2023 Schedule

PAK vs NZ 1st ODI - April 27, 2023 - Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium, Rawalpindi

PAK vs NZ 2nd ODI - April 29, 2023 - Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium, Rawalpindi

PAK vs NZ 3rd ODI - May 3, 2023 - National Stadium, Karachi

PAK vs NZ 4th ODI - May 5, 2023 - National Stadium, Karachi

PAK vs NZ 5th ODI - May 7, 2023 - National Stadium, Karachi

PAK vs NZ 2023 Live Broadcast in India

How to watch Pakistan vs New Zealand 2023 LIVE in India?

Live telecast of PAK vs NZ T20 and ODI series in India will be available on TV channels of Sony Sports Network, fans can watch PAK vs NZ 2023 T20 & ODI series live streaming in India on SonyLiv app.

PAK vs NZ 2023 Live Broadcast in United Kingdom: PAK vs NZ 2023 series will be broadcast live on Sky Sports Cricket and Sky Sports Main Event in United Kingdom, while live streaming of the same can be viewed online on the SkyGo platform.

PAK vs NZ 2023 Live Broadcast in Pakistan: PAK vs NZ 2023 series will be broadcast live on PTV Sports in Pakistan, while fans can watch Pakistan vs New Zealand 2023 live streaming online on Ary Zap app, Vidly.tv, Tamasha and tapmad.
