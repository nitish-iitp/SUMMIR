Cricket Point Table Asia Cup 2022 Super 4: Teams, full schedule, Super Four points table and more
As of now, Sri Lanka leads the Super Four stage of Asia Cup 2022 after beating Afghanistan in the first match in Sharjah. Sri Lanka 179/6 (19.1) beat Afghanistan 175/6 (20) by four wickets on Saturday.
   
Instead of a normal semi-final phase, the teams progressing from the two groups play each other in another round robin phase. Pic: Official Website/ PTI/ https://twitter.com/ACCMedia1

Cricket Point Table Asia Cup Super 4: Cricket Point Table Asia Cup Super 4: Asia Cup 2022 is in the Super Four stage. A total of six teams -- India, Pakistan and Hong Kong (Group A) and Sri Lanka, Bangladesh and Afghanistan (Group) faced each other in the group stage. Two teams from each group advanced to the Super Four stage -- India and Pakistan from Group A and Sri Lanka and Afghanistan from Group B.

All the Super Four teams will play again each other -- total 6 matches. The top two teams will be qualified for the Final game which will be played on September 11, Sunday.

Asia Cup is being played in the UAE this year. The venue was shifted from Sri Lanka due to the crisis the island nation is facing.

As of now, Sri Lanka leads the Super Four stage of Asia Cup 2022 after beating Afghanistan in the first match in Sharjah. Sri Lanka 179/6 (19.1) beat Afghanistan 175/6 (20) by four wickets on Saturday.

Pakistan is placed second on the Super Four points table after its win over India on Sunday. Pakistan 182/5 (19.5) beat India 181/7 (20) by five wickets. India, at 3rd spot, has zero points and an NRR of -0.126.

Instead of a normal semi-final phase, the teams progressing from the two groups play each other in another round robin phase.
