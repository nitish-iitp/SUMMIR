By

Lok Sabha Election 2024 highlights: Campaigning is underway for the fifth phase of the Lok Sabha elections.
Prime Minister Narendra Modi

Lok Sabha Election 2024 highlights: Campaigning is underway for the fifth phase of Lok Sabha elections scheduled for Monday. This phase will see voting in 49 parliamentary constituencies spread across six states and two Union territories. Notable candidates include Union ministers Rajnath Singh and Smriti Irani, along with Rahul Gandhi. Singh is contesting from Lucknow, Irani from Amethi, and Gandhi from Rae Bareli. Additionally, Gandhi is also contesting from Kerala's Wayanad....Read More

Lok Sabha Election 2024 LIVE: 'Central government failed to fulfill its promises of 'acche din',' says Congress leader Sachin Pilot

Lok Sabha Election 2024 LIVE: Addressing an election rally in Balasore Lok Sabha constituency in favour of party candidate and former Union minister Srikant Jena, Congress leader Sachin Pilot said,"People across the country have made up their minds to change the central government, which has failed to fulfill its promises of 'acche din'."

"BJP wants to make India opposition free. They have frozen the bank accounts of the Congress... two chief ministers were sent to jail," he added, reported news agency PTI.

Lok Sabha Election 2024 LIVE: Top leaders contesting in fifth phase of the Lok Sabha polls

Lok Sabha Election 2024 LIVE: The fifth phase of the Lok Sabha elections 2024 will be held on May 20, covering 49 seats across 6 States and two Union Territories.

Top leaders across political parties whose fate will be decided in this phase are Rahul Gandhi, BJP leaders Rajnath Singh, Smriti Irani, Rajiv Pratap Rudy, Piyush Goyal, Ujjwal Nikam, Karan Bhushan Singh, LJP (Ramvilas) chief Chirag Paswan, JKNC chief Omar Abdullah, and RJD leader Rohini Acharya.
