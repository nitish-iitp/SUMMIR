India vs England 2nd T20I 2021 Highlights
by
CricHighlightsVidz
Published on :
3/14/2021 11:00:00 PM
Watch the highlights of India vs England 2nd T20I 2021 - England tour of India 5-match Twenty20 international series of the 2nd T20I match played between England and India at Narendra Modi Stadium, Motera, Ahmedabad in 14th March 2021.



Ishan Kishan plays a shot © BCCI


Ishan Kishan's debut fifty and Virat Kohli's masterclass half-century powers to India comfortable seven-wicket victory over England and level the series with 1-1 in the second T20I.


Match Stats : 


26 : Virat Kohli hit the most fifty-plus scores in T20 international cricket history, surpassing the Rohit Sharma's record of 25 fifty plus scores.
Virat Kohli became the first batsman to score 3000 runs landmark in T20 international cricket history.
Ishan Kishan became the seventh Indian player to win the Man-of-the-match award on debut T20I match.
Ishan Kishan became the second Indian batsman to score a fifty or more runs on debut T20I match after Ajinkya Rahane.


England scored 164-6 in 20 overs with top scorer by Jason Roy struck 46 off 35-balls including 4-fours & 2-sixes.


Eoin Morgan hammered a 28 off 20-balls including 4-fours with strike rate of 140, Dawid Malan hit 24 off 23-balls included four-fours, Ben Stokes scored 24 off 21-balls including a boundary and Jonny Bairstow 20.


India best bowler by Washington Sundar and Shardul Thakur both took 2-wickets and one for Bhuvneshwar Kumar - Yuzvendra Chahal.


India chased 166-3 in 17.5 overs with top scorer by Virat Kohli hammered a unbeaten 73 off 49-balls including 5-fours & 3-sixes with strike rate of 148.98.


Debutant Ishan Kishan - who scored 56 off 32-balls on his debut T20I match and Rishabh Pant blasted a 26 off 13-balls included 2-sixes & 2-fours with strike rate of 200.


England best bowler by Sam Curran, Adil Rashid and Chris Jordan each took one-wickets.


Ishan Kishan named Player of the match for his match-winning whirlwind 56-runs knocked off facing 32-balls including 5-fours and 4-sixes with strike rate of 175.
