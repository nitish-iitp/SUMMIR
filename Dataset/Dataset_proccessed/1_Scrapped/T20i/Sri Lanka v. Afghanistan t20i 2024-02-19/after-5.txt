Sri Lanka are gearing up to take on Afghanistan in the second T20I of the ongoing Afghanistan tour to Sri Lanka. The encounter will take place at the Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium on Monday (February 19).

The hosts lead the three-match series by 1-0, winning the high-octane first T20I by four runs. They will be focused on sealing the series by winning the forthcoming battle. Conversely, the Afghans will be eager to mark a comeback and register their first tour victory.

Afghanistan tour of Sri Lanka 2024, 2nd T20I:
Date and Time: February 19, 2024, 7:00 pm local time/ 01:30 pm GMT/ 07:00 pm IST
Venue: Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium.
Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium Pitch Report:

The cricket pitch at Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium in Dambulla is predominantly favourable for batters, offering conditions conducive to their performance. The track provides substantial turn and bounce, making it especially advantageous for spinners expected to capitalize on these conditions. Teams winning the toss at this venue frequently choose to bowl first, anticipating that bowlers may find opportunities to limit the opposing team’s batting performance due to the nature of the pitch.
