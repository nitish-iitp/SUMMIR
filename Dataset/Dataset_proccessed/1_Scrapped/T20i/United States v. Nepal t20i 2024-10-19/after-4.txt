USA vs NEP Match Preview:

Nepal secured a decisive 19-run victory over the USA in the first match of the three-game T20I series, taking an early 1-0 lead.

The USA won the toss and chose to field, but Nepal dominated, posting 164 runs. Rohit Paudel starred with 49 runs. USA’s Saurabh Netravalkar, Jasdeep Singh, and Harmeet Singh claimed two wickets each.

In response, the USA managed 147 runs, with Shayan Jahangir top-scoring at 60. Dipendra Singh Airee led Nepal’s bowling attack, capturing three crucial wickets.
