USA and Nepal are playing in a three-match T20I series, which started on October 17 - here’s where you can watch the USA vs NEP 2024 T20Is live, including TV broadcast, live streaming, and match timing details.

USA vs NEP T20Is 2024 schedule: Full fixtures list, match timings and venues

After Nepal beat the hosts in the series opener, the second match will be held on October 19 and the third on October 20. Grand Prairie Cricket Stadium in Texas will host the entire series. All matches will start at 7 pm CST (Central Time Zone).

Thursday, October 17, 1st T20I: USA vs NEP

Saturday, October 19, 2nd T20I: USA vs NEP

Sunday, October 20, 3rd T20I: USA vs NEP

USA vs NEP T20Is 2024: Full squads

USA are led by Monank Patel, while Rohit Paudel is captaining the tourists.

USA T20I squad: Monank Patel (c), Andries Gous, Abhishek Paradkar, Aaron Jones, Harmeet Singh, Juanoy Drysdale, Muhammad Ali Khan, Milind Kumar, Nosthush Kenjige, Jasdeep Singh, Saurabh Netravalkar, Saiteja Mukkamalla, Shayan Jahangir, Utkarsh Srivastava, Yasir Mohammad.

Nepal T20I squad: Rohit Paudel (c), Aasif Sheikh, Dipendra Singh Airee, Kamal Airee, Kushal Bhurtel, Abinash Bohara, Sagar Dhakal, Gulsan Jha, Sundeep Jora, Karan KC, Kushal Malla, Sandeep Lamichhane, Pratis GC, Lalit Rajbanshi, Anil Sah, Sompal Kami.

USA vs Nepal 2024 T20Is: Where to watch live on TV channels and live streaming platforms
Where to watch in India

Sony Ten 5 will telecast the series for the viewers in India, while the Sony Liv app and its website (sonyliv.com) will provide live-streaming services.

Where to watch in USA

USA audience can view the matches live on Willow TV.

Where to watch in Nepal

Nepal audience can view the three T20Is live on Kantipur Max.
