Pakistan Vs New Zealand 2nd T20I Live Streaming: When & Where To Watch PAK-NZ Match In India
Pakistan will face New Zealand in the second T20I of the ongoing five-match series on Sunday (January 14). The match is scheduled to take place at Seddon Park in Hamilton. The Kiwis won the series opener played on Friday (January 12) in Auckland by 46 runs to go 1-0 up in the series.
Authored by:
TN Sports Desk

Updated Jan 14, 2024, 13:05 IST

The second T20I of the five-match series between Pakistan and New Zealand will be played at Seddon Park in Hamilton on Sunday (January 14). Picture Credit: X/@TheRealPCB

KEY HIGHLIGHTS
Pakistan will face New Zealand in the second T20I on Sunday (January 14).
The match is scheduled to take place at Seddon Park in Hamilton.
New Zealand won the series opener played in Auckland by 46 runs.
After losing the first T20I of the ongoing five-match series against New Zealand by 46 runs in Auckland on Friday (January 12), Pakistan will face the Black Caps in the second match at Seddon Park in Hamilton on Sunday (January 14). The match is scheduled to start at 11:40 a.m. IST and will be live streamed on Amazon Prime Video in India.
In the series opener, Daryl Mitchell scored 61 runs from 27 balls to help the Kiwis post 226 runs on the board, which in the end proved to be enough. Apart from him, New Zealand captain Kane Williamson also scored fifty. In reply to the 227-run target, Pakistan, despite a half-century from Babar Azam, got all out for 180 runs in 18 overs.
Pakistan vs New Zealand Live Score
For New Zealand, Tim Southee picked up four wickets for 25 runs in four overs. By taking four wickets in the first T20I, he also became the first bowler in history to take 150 T20I wickets. The Black Caps would like to maintain the same form in the second T20I as well and go 2-0 up in the series.
On the other hand, Pakistan, under the new leadership of Shaheen Afridi, would like to make a comeback and level the series. In the first T20I, Pakistani players dropped multiple catches, which proved to be the difference in the end.
Here’s all you need to know about the live streaming details of the second T20I between Pakistan and New Zealand in India.
The second T20I between Pakistan and New Zealand that is scheduled to take place at Seddon Park in Hamilton will start at 11:40 a.m. IST on Sunday (January 14), and the matches will be live streamed on Amazon Prime in India. There will be no live telecast available for the Pakistan-New Zealand T20Is in India.
Squads
New Zealand: Kane Williamson (C), Tim Seifert (WK), Devon Conway, Finn Allen, Mark Chapman, Glenn Phillips, Daryl Mitchell, Mitchell Santner, Ish Sodhi, Tim Southee, Matt Henry, Adam Milne, and Ben Sears
Pakistan: Shaheen Afridi (C), Mohammad Rizwan (WK), Babar Azam, Fakhar Zaman, Saim Ayub, Iftikhar Ahmed, Azam Khan, Aamer Jamal, Mohammad Nawaz, Haris Rauf, Zaman Khan, Usama Mir, Mohammad Wasim Jr., Abrar Ahmed, Sahibzada Farhan, Abbas Afridi, and Haseebullah Khan
