KUW vs MON Match Preview:

The ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Asia Qualifier A 2024 continues with a third exciting match between Kuwait and Mongolia. Both teams are looking to make their mark in the tournament.

Kuwait, led by Bilal Tahir, has a strong batting lineup featuring Clinto Anto, Muhammad Aqif, and Muhammad Umar. Their bowling attack is led by Usman Ghani, Adnan Idrees, and Mohammed Aslam.

Mongolia, led by Davaasuren Jamyansuren, has a promising squad with players like Nyambaatar Naranbaatar, Od Lutbayar, and Gandemberel Ganbold. Their bowling department is led by Sanchir Natsagdorj and Sodbileg Gantulga.

Both teams will be aiming to secure a victory and improve their position in the tournament. It promises to be a competitive match with plenty of action.
