Sri Lanka Vs New Zealand Weather Forecast, Pitch Report, Live Streaming & Probable XIs
SL vs NZ Rain Update & Pitch Report: Sri Lanka will have to be on their toes to beat a confident side like New Zealand, who will surely bring their 'A game' to continue their winning momentum. 
By : ABP News Bureau | Updated at : 28 Oct 2022 07:19 PM (IST)
There are absolutely no chances of rain in Syndey on October 29, 2022, Saturday, 01.30 pm IST.
Source : Getty Images

Sri Lanka vs New Zealand weather forecast: A confident New Zealand cricket team will aim to continue its winning momentum when they lock horns with Sri Lanka in Match 27 of ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022 in the Sydney Cricket Ground, Sydney, on October 29, Saturday. Both the teams are placed in Group 1. New Zealand started their T20 World Cup campaign with a win by thrashing defending champions Australia ay Sydney. The win meant a lot for the Kiwis as they thrashed Aussies Down Under convincingly. After this, New Zealand traveled to Melbourne for T20 WC fixture against Afghanistan, but the match was called off without a ball being bowled due to persistent rain.

Sri Lanka, like New Zealand, attained a win to kick off their T20 World Cup campaign. Sri Lanka defeated Ireland in their first Super 12 match but were thrashed brutally by Australia in their next fixture. The Asia Cup 2022 winners will now have to be on their toes to beat a confident side like New Zealand, who will surely bring their 'A game' to continue their winning momentum. 

Ahead of the much-awaited SL vs NZ T20 WC match, let us know about Syndey's latest weather forecast.

There are absolutely no chances of rain in Syndey on October 29, 2022, Saturday, 01.30 pm IST. The weather forecast indicates that it is a ideal day for playing a cricket match. The temperature in Sydney is expected to range between 12 and 24 degrees Celsius.

Sri Lanka vs New Zealand pitch report: Sydney Cricket Ground's (SCG) pitch is known to be batting friendly. The ball comes nicely onto the bat here, helping the batters to play aggressive strokes right from the start of the match. Spinners might come into play as the game progresses. 

Sri Lanka vs New Zealand probable playing XIs

New Zealand Probable Playing XI: Devon Conway (wk), Finn Allen, Kane Williamson (c), Glenn Phillips, James Neesham, Mark Chapman, Mitchell Santner, Tim Southee, Ish Sodhi, Lockie Ferguson, Trent Boult

Sri Lanka Probable Playing XI: Kusal Mendis (wk), Pathum Nissanka, Dhananjaya de Silva, Charith Asalanka, Bhanuka Rajapaksa, Dasun Shanaka (c), Wanindu Hasaranga, Chamika Karunaratne, Maheesh Theekshana, Lahiru Kumara, Pramod Madushan

Sri Lanka vs New Zealand Live Streaming Details 

Fans can watch Sri Lanka vs New Zealand Live Streaming on Disney+ Hotstar. Live Telecast of New Zealand vs Sri Lanka match can be seen on Star Sports Network.
