PAK vs NZ Live Streaming: How, When To Watch Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I Series Live In India And Pakistan
PAK vs NZ T20I series marks return of Imad Wasim and Mohammed Amir to the national team after announcing their retirements earlier.
By : ABP News Bureau | Updated at : 16 Apr 2024 04:02 PM (IST)
Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series live streaming, telecast, date, time, venue - all you need to know.
Source : PTI

PAK vs NZ T20I series live streaming, telecast, date, time, venue: Pakistan and New Zealand kickstart their T20 World Cup 2024 preparations with a five-match PAK vs NZ T20I series, starting with PAK vs NZ 1st T20I in Rawalpindi on Thursday (April 18). First three T20I matches between Pakistan and New Zealand are scheduled to take place in Rawalpindi, while the remaining two matches will be played at Lahore's Gaddafi Stadium.

PAK vs NZ T20I series marks return of Imad Wasim and Mohammed Amir to the national team after announcing their retirements earlier. On the other hand, New Zealand opted to field a second-string squad against Pakistan as many of their regular players are busy in IPL 2024.

Check below Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series complete schedule, match timings

1st T20I: Thursday, 18 April 2024 - Rawalpindi - 7 PM Local Time (PKT) / 7:30 PM IST / 2 AM NZST (Friday, 19 April 2024)

Pakistan vs New Zealand 1st T20I: Thurday (April 18), starts at 7:30 PM IST onwards; venue: Rawalpindi 

Pakistan vs New Zealand 2nd T20I: Saturday (April 20), starts at 7:30 PM IST onwards; venue: Rawalpindi 

Pakistan vs New Zealand 3rd T20I: Sunday (April 21), starts at 7:30 PM IST onwards; venue: Rawalpindi 

Pakistan vs New Zealand 4th T20I: Thursday (April 25), starts at 7:30 PM IST onwards; venue: Lahore 

Pakistan vs New Zealand 5th T20I: Saturday (April 27), starts at 7:30 PM IST onwards; venue: Lahore

Check below when and where to watch Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series live in India, Pakistan

When, where to watch Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series live streaming in India?

Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series live streaming in India will be available on Fancode app for tour pass that will cost Rs 89.

When, where to watch Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series live telecast in India?

Unfortunately, there won't be any live telecast of Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series in India.

When, where to watch Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series live in Pakistan?

Pakistan vs New Zealand T20I series will be available live and exclusive in Pakistan on A Sports HD.

Pakistan squad for PAK vs NZ T20I series: Babar Azam (captain), Abrar Ahmed, Azam Khan, Fakhar Zaman, Iftikhar Ahmed, Imad Wasim, Abbas Afridi, Mohammad Rizwan, Mohmmad Amir, Irfan Niazi, Naseem Shah, Saim Ayub, Shadab Khan, Shaheen Shah Afridi, Usama Mir, Usman Khan, Zaman Khan.

New Zealand squad for PAK vs NZ T20I series: Michael Bracewell (captain), Mark Chapman, Josh Clarkson, Jacob Duffy, Dean Foxcroft, Ben Lister, Cole McConchie, Jimmy Neesham, Will O’Rourke, Tim Robinson, Ben Sears, Tim Seifert, Ish Sodhi, Zac Foulkes, Tom Blundell.
