Schedule for the T20 World Cup 2024: Dates, Times, Locations, Ticket Information, and Detailed Team List





Prepare for cricket fever! The T20 World Cup 2024 is expected to occur in the United States and the Caribbean, with a whopping 50 matches scheduled.

On June 1st, 2024, the T20 World Cup will kick off with an exciting match between co-host USA and their northern neighbors, Canada, at Grand Prairie Stadium in Texas. This game sets the tone for an exciting tournament that will take place in a number of legendary locations throughout the US and the Caribbean. Cricket fans are especially excited about the June 9th high-stakes match between India and Pakistan, which will take place at the picturesque Eisenhower Park in Long Island, New York. Dramatic moments and fierce competition are promised in this match. The competition will come to a head in a spectacular and memorable showdown on June 29 in Barbados, where the top two teams will compete for the coveted T20 World Cup trophy.


As the US hosts T20 World Cup 2024 matches, feel the pulse of cricket all over Texas, Long Island, and Florida.
