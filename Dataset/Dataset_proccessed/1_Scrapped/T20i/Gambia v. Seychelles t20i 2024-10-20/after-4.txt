SEY vs GAM Match Preview:

Seychelles currently sits at the bottom of the points table, having suffered a defeat in their sole outing. Meanwhile, their upcoming opponent, Gambia, occupies fourth place despite losing their only game.

Conversely, Gambia’s relatively stronger position highlights the fine margins between victory and defeat. In contrast to Seychelles’ struggles, Gambia seeks to capitalize on its ranking. Notably, both teams share identical records, with one loss each.

As Seychelles faces the Gambia, both teams seek redemption. While Seychelles aims to break their losing streak, Gambia looks to solidify their top-four standing. Ultimately, the outcome will hinge on which team capitalizes on the other’s weaknesses.
