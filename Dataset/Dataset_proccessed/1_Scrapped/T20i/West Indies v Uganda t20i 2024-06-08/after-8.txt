West Indies win toss, batting first vs Uganda
written by Barbados Today 08/06/2024 0 min read  A+A-
Photo by: Windiesciricket
SHARE 

West Indies captain Rovman Powell won the toss and chose to bat first against Uganda in a Group C match of the T20 World Cup at the Guyana National Stadium on Saturday.

Teams:

West Indies – Rovman Powell (captain), Brandon King, Johnson Charles, Nicholas Pooran (wicketkeeper), Roston Chase, Sherfane Rutherford, Andre Russell, Romario Shepherd, Alzarri Joseph, Akeal Hosein, Gudakesh Motie.

Uganda: Brian Masaba (captain), Roger Mukasa, Simon Ssesazi (wicketkeeper), Robinson Obuya, Riazat Ali Shah, Alpesh Ramjani, Dinesh Nakrani, Juma Miyagi, Kenneth Waiswa, Cosmas Kyewuta, Frank Nsubuga.

Umpires: Kumar Dharmasena (SRI), Rashid Riaz (PAK)

TV Umpire: Adrian Holdstock (RSA)
