SENIOR WOMEN’S ONE DAY TROPHY 2021-2022
Senior Women’s One Day Trophy 2021-2022 | Squad | Schedule | Fixtures
October 25, 2021  Juili Ballal

The senior women’s one-day tournament will be played from October 28 to November 20 among 37 teams divided into six groups, namely, Elite Group A, Elite Group B, Elite Group C, Elite Group D, Elite Group E, and Plate Group. A total of 106 matches will be played across six venues, namely, Kolkata, Bangalore, Pune, Dehradun, Vizag, and Nagpur. Out of the 106 matches, 96 matches will be league matches and 10 will be knockouts.
