Match Preview

In Match 14 of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Asia Sub-regional Qualifier A, Kuwait defeated Malaysia by 8 wickets. Malaysia batted first and scored 149/7 in their 20 overs. Virandeep Singh was the top scorer with 43 runs off 37 balls. Ahmad Faiz added 28 runs off 10 balls, and Zubaidi Zulkifle contributed 28 runs from 28 balls. Kuwait chased down the target, reaching 150/2 in just 15.2 overs. Vijay Unni took 1 wicket for 25 runs in 4 overs, and Pavandeep Singh also took 1 wicket, giving away 42 runs in 4 overs. Ahmed Aqeel Wahid had a challenging day, conceding 11 runs in 1 over without taking a wicket. Despite their efforts, Malaysia couldn’t defend their total, and Kuwait secured a convincing win.

In Match 10 of the same tournament, Hong Kong defeated Maldives by 9 wickets. Maldives scored 102/9 in their 20 overs. Ehsan Khan was the standout bowler for Hong Kong with 3 wickets for 20 runs in 4 overs. Yasim Murtaza and Ateequl Rehman Iqbal also took 2 wickets each, giving away 13 and 24 runs, respectively. Hong Kong chased the target easily, reaching 107/1 in just 9 overs. Zeeshan Ali led with an unbeaten 50 off 21 balls, Nizakat Khan scored 35 off 13 balls, and Jamie Atkinson added 19 off 20 balls. Hong Kong’s strong performance secured a comfortable victory.
