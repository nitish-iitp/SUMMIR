The Brooks Macdonald Women’s Inter-Insular T20I series is to be hosted by Jersey Cricket at Saint Saviour, Jersey. The three-match series will be played on 25th and 26th June, 2022 at Grainville. The women’s national squads of Jersey and Guernsey will lock horns in the shortest format of the game.

 

Jersey Women’s Cricket Team at The Brooks Macdonald Women’s Inter-Insular T20I series. PC: Twitter

 

The last edition of the Women’s Inter-Insular series was played in 2019 when Guernsey Cricket hosted Jersey cricket and Guernsey cricket won a single WT20I match. The two teams have traditionally played the series annually in the 50-over format since 1950.

Jersey Women’s Cricket Squad 

Chloe Greechan, Charlie Miles, Maria Da Rocha, Rose Hill, Flo Copley, Trinity Smith, Georgia Mallet, Tea Brocklesby, Lily Greig, Analise Merritt, Erin Duffy, Erin Gouge, Mia Maguire, Grace Wetherall

Guernsey Women’s Cricket Squad

Hannah Eulenkamp, Jeanette Savage, Leigh Le Page, Elizabeth Willcocks, Katie Watson, Francesca Bulpitt, Rebecca Hubbard, Lucy Le Page, Carrie Eddie, Philippa Stahelin, Claire Jennings

Match schedule – The Brooks Macdonald Women’s Inter-Insular T20I series

 

Match No.	Day and Date	Timings	Venue
1.	Saturday, 25th June,2022	11 A.M	Grainville
2.	Saturday, 25th June, 2022	3 P.M	Grainville
3.	Sunday, 26th June, 2022	11 A.M	Grainville
