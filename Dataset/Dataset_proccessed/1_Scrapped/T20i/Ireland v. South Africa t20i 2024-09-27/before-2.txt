The South Africa T20 Challenge, also known as CSA T20 Challenge 2024 this league is scheduled to begin on September 27, 2024, at The Wanderers Stadium. Cricket South Africa organizes the tournament, and all the matches are hosted at various venues in South Africa. The final match will be held on October 27, 2024
