The first T20I between Ireland and South Africa in the Ireland tour of South Africa, hosted in the United Arab Emirates, is set for Friday, September 27, 2024. The match will take place at the iconic Sheikh Zayed Stadium in Abu Dhabi, with the action beginning at 9:00 PM local time. Both teams are looking to kick off the series on a strong note, making this an eagerly anticipated contest. With competitive squads on both sides, fans can expect an exciting start to this international series under the UAE lights.
