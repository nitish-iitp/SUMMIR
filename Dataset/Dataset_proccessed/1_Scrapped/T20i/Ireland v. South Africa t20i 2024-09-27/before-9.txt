Ireland vs. South Africa cricket teams to battle it out in Abu Dhabi
Updated: September 25, 2024
2 min read
491

Get ready for an exciting showdown as the Ireland and South Africa men’s cricket teams go head-to-head in a thrilling T20I series in Abu Dhabi.

From 27th September to 7th October, Zayed Cricket Stadium will be the battlefield for this highly anticipated contest. Cricket fans will witness five electrifying matches between these two talented squads, showcasing top-class international cricket right here in the UAE.

Ireland’s T20I Squad

Led by captain Paul Stirling, the Irish team features a mix of seasoned players and promising new talents.

Some key names to watch include Mark Adair, Ross Adair, Curtis Campher, and Harry Tector. With a dynamic squad that includes all-rounders and powerful batsmen, Ireland will be looking to make a strong impression and challenge the South African side at every turn.

South Africa’s T20I Squad

South Africa, captained by Aiden Markram, will arrive with a formidable line-up of skilled cricketers.

The team includes renowned names like Reeza Hendricks, Lungi Ngidi, and Bjorn Fortuin. Combining experience with fresh talent, the Proteas will aim to dominate the series, putting up a strong fight in Abu Dhabi’s sunny conditions.

Ireland vs. South Africa Match Schedule
27 September (Friday) – The match starts at 7:30 p.m., with the second innings wrapping up by 10:40 p.m.
29 September (Sunday) – Another evening match is kicking off at 7:30 p.m., set to finish by 10:40 p.m.
2 October (Wednesday) – This match starts earlier at 3:30 p.m., ending at 11:00 p.m. after an intense day of cricket.
4 October (Friday) – Same timing as the previous, starting at 3:30 p.m. and closing by 11:00 p.m.
7 October (Monday) – The final match starts at 3:30 p.m., promising a dramatic conclusion by 11:00 p.m.

This series promises excitement, with each match showcasing high-octane cricket action.

Whether you’re cheering for the Irish or the South Africans, this is a cricketing experience you won’t want to miss. Soak in the atmosphere, enjoy top-level sportsmanship, and watch your favorite players give their all on the field.
