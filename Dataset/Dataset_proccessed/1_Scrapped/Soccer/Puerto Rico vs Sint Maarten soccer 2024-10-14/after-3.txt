First Half Drama

The initial 45 minutes were marked by a close contest as both sides showcased their skills and strategies. San Martín initially appeared to have an edge with more possession, but Puerto Rico capitalized on vital opportunities, with Gerald Díaz striking a goal at the 44-minute mark, bringing the crowd to its feet.

Second Half Turnaround

Just as the second half commenced, Sint Maarten’s Q. Christina caught Puerto Rico off guard, scoring a quick goal at the 50th minute. This equalizer brought an electric tension as each team intensified their game.

The atmosphere was taut as both teams endeavored to tip the scales in their favor. With relentless effort and perseverance, Puerto Rico broke the deadlock when Ricardo Rivera hit the net at the 83rd minute, securing their lead once more.

Key Statistics

The game not only featured spectacular goals but was also a showcase of impressive team dynamics. While Sint Maarten boasted 54% possession, Puerto Rico was more precise with their attacks, managing to have more shots on target.

Overall, the match statistics revealed a gripping duel, with 8 total shots from Puerto Rico and 9 from Sint Maarten. However, Puerto Rico’s 4 shots on goal significantly outnumbered Sint Maarten’s 2, underscoring their efficient play.

Past Performances and Context
