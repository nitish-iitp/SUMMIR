Dates and Times for Match Day 3 & 4 of the FIFA (CAF) World Cup Qualifiers revealed
by ISMAEL KIYONGA
MAY 31, 2024

The Confederation of African Football along with the Federation of International Football Associations (FIFA) has confirmed the dates and times for the forthcoming the FIFA World Cup 2026 qualifiers.

Match Day 3 and Match Day 4 of the Qualifiers will be played between 05-11 June 2024.

For the 2026 World Cup final, nine African teams are guaranteed a place with a ten place at stake but another will participate in the Play-off Tournament as the global showpiece will host 48 teams for the first time ever.

Winners of each Group will automatically qualify for the FIFA World Cup 2026.

The four best runners-up (from the Groups) will play in a CAF Play-Off tournament.

The winner of the CAF Play-Off Tournament will play in the FIFA Play-off Tournament.

Below are the fixtures for the matches to be played over the next seven days. (all kick-off times in GMT)
