LAWRENCEVILLE — A chilly and windy Monday didn’t curb the celebration from a pair of breakthrough winners in the Gwinnett County Track and Field Championships.

The host Archer girls and the Mill Creek boys won county titles for the first time in each program’s history, rewarding finishes for teams that have been among the best at the county meet for years.
