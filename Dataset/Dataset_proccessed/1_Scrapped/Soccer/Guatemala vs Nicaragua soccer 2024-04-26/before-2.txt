Boutique hotel boom: First of five upscale properties popping up in downtown Toronto to open this fall

The 33-storey TOOR Hotel near Moss Park will include 181 rental units on the upper floors.

Updated April 26, 2024 at 12:45 p.m.
April 25, 2024
1 min read
Save

The hotel and upper floor residences of the TOOR Hotel on Jarvis will share an ‘amenities box’ featuring a fitness centre and yoga studio.

Manga Hotel Group artist rendering
By Reanna JulienStaff Reporter

The first of five upscale boutique hotels rising in downtown Toronto over the next five years, the TOOR Hotel, is scheduled to open its doors in September on Jarvis St. near Moss Park.

Manga Hotel Group founder, president and CEO, Sukhdev Toor, said the hotel at 203 Jarvis St. is in an “evolving” part of the city and will “bring the whole area up.”
