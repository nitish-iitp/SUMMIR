Myanmar to test Sri Lanka twice
October 9, 2024
18
 Share

Myanmar will test Sri Lanka twice this week as part of their preparation for the ASEAN Mitsubishi Electric Cup 2024 later this year.The FIFA international “A” friendly matches will be played at the Thuwanna Stadium.The two matches against Sri Lanka will be played tomorrow, 10 October 2024 (at 4pm local time) and 13 October 2024 (4pm). #AFF
