Heritage Festival set for Nov. 2

From mouth-watering food to live music and performances by local dancers, singers and the Alabama Coushatta Tribe of Texas, Lufkin Creative’s 2024 Heritage Festival will have something for everyone.

All activities will be in and around Cotton Square from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. Saturday, Nov. 2, in downtown Lufkin. If you want to come early, join a free yoga class at 9:30 a.m. with Jackie Johnston with Shepherd Street Yoga.
