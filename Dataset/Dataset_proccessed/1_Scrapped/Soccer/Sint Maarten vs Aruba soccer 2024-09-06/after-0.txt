RAVE THE VOTE: Electronic Acts Offer Up Free Music for US Voters Who Check Registration Status

Check your voter registration status and score free music from Carl Craig, TOKiMONSTA, Soul Clap, DJ Minx, and many more.

Cameron Holbrook
2 min •
Sept 6, 2024

Ahead of the upcoming 2024 US presidential elections on November 5th, Rave the Vote has partnered up with non-partisan voter engagement organization HeadCount to encourage the US dance music community to get out and vote by offering up a remarkable bundle of tracks and mixes from some of America's most talented DJs and producers.

By simply checking your registration status via this link, those who sign up will receive a free package of musical goodies from artists such as Ardalan, Carre, Carl Craig, DJ Holographic, DJ Minx, Hercules & Love Affair, Louie Vega, Mary Droppinz, Nala, Seth Troxler, Soul Clap, TOKiMONSTA, Walker & Royce and more.

Once checking your registration status and and opting in to text reminders about voting, this bundle of music will land in your inbox sometime in late September.

"Dance music in America is bigger than ever, it's crucial that our community gets out and votes, and ensures its voice is heard. There is a lot at stake in this election, and we need dance music fans to show up and vote for values that are synonymous with our culture; peace, love, unity and respect," said Rave The Vote in a statement.

Check your registration status and sign up for your free tracks & mixes here!
