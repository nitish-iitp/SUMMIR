 Inkuru ibabaje muri kano kanya Ku bakunzi b’umugwi nserukira gihugu w’Intamba mu rugamba 
 Youssouf Ndayishimiye yababaye mu rukino rwariko rubahuza na PSG .
 Ivyo vyaciye bituma uwo mukinyi asohoka mu kibuga Ku munota wa 55 w’urukino .
 Gushika muri kano kanya ubwoba ni bwinshi Ku Bakunzi b’umugwi nserukira gihugu w’Uburundi Intamba mu rugamba kuko niwe mukinyi twari twizeye narirya Nsabiyumva Frederic atazoboneka mu rukino rwa mbere ruzohuza Uburundi na Burkina Faso igenekerezo rya 10/10/2024.
 Mwomenyako urwo rukino rwarangiye ari igitsindo 1-1.
 Tubandanye tumusengera kugira turabe y’uko yokina urukino rwo ku musi wa kane .
