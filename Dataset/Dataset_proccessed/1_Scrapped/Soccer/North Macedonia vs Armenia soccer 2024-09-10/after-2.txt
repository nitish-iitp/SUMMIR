National League: Armenia to face North Macedonia in Group L
 Siranush Ghazanchyan 
Send an email
September 10, 2024, 19:35
 Less than a minute

Armenia will face North Macedonia in a Nations League match today. The Group L fixture will kick off at 22:45 Yerevan time (20:45 CET).

The teams have met 13 times so far. The Armenian national team has won four times, the North Macedonian has celebrated six victories, three matches have ended in a draw.

The upcoming match will be the 7th meeting between the teams at Toše Proeski Stadium in Skopje

The hosts won 3 times in the previous six matches, 2 matches ended in a draw, the Armenian national team won once.

Razmik Grigoryan scored the first goal for the Armenian national team at the Toshe Proeski Stadium (1995).

Armenia beat Latvia 4-1 on Saturday.
