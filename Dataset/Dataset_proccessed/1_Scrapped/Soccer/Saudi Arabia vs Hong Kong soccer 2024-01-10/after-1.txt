NT beat Hong Kong at the end of the 2023 AFC Asian Cup's preparations

2024-01-10
20:29:29

The Saudi Arabia national team concluded its preparatory camp, which was held in Sealine in the State of Qatar during the period (December 31 - January 10), on Wednesday evening, within the preparatory program in preparation for the 2023 AFC Asian Cup, which begin on the 12th of January in Qatar.
On the field, the Saudi national team defeated Hong Kong with a score of (2-0) in the match that brought them together this Wednesday evening at the Qatari club Al-Wakrah Stadium.
The first goal for the Saudi team was scored by Abdul-Rahman Gharib from a penalty kick in the 21st minute, before Fawaz Al-Saqour added the second goal four minutes before the end of the game.
On the other hand, the technical director will give the Green's players a free period tomorrow morning, Thursday, and the gathering will take place again in the evening of the same day at the camp headquarters in the Qatari capital, Doha.
It is worth noting that, during the fourth stage of the preparatory program, Saudi Arabia played three friendly matches against Lebanon, Palestine, and Hong Kong.
