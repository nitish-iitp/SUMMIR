Uzbekistan to face Iran in World Cup 2026 qualifiers

Today, October 10, Uzbekistan's national football team will play against Iran as part of the third stage of the 2026 FIFA World Cup qualifiers. 

Photo: UFA

The match will kick off at 19:00 at the Bunyodkor Stadium in Tashkent.

Both Uzbekistan and Iran currently top their groups with six points each after two matches. Following them are the UAE with 3 points, North Korea and Qatar with 1 point each, and Kyrgyzstan, which has yet to score any points.

Reportedly, all tickets have been sold out and those with tickets are reselling them for around 150-200 thousand UZS.
