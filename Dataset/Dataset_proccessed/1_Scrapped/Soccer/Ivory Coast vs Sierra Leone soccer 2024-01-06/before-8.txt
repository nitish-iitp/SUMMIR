Leone Stars Departs For Ivory Coast in Anticipation of Saturday’s Friendly

Sierra Leone National Football Team, the Leone Stars has embarked on a journey to Ivory Coast today January 4, 2024, for an eagerly anticipated friendly match scheduled for Saturday, January 6, 2024.

The departure was accompanied by an air of anticipation and enthusiasm as fans gathered to bid farewell to their football heroes. The Leone Stars, under the guidance of their seasoned coach, left no stone unturned in their preparations for the upcoming clash. This friendly encounter is poised to test the team’s mettle and fortify their synergy as they face off against formidable opponents on the Ivorian soil.

The national squad, consisting of a blend of seasoned veterans and emerging talents, aims to showcase their prowess and refine their strategies in this friendly fixture. The match not only serves as a platform for honing skills but also fosters camaraderie and sportsmanship between the two nations.
