Benin Republic Referees to Officiate Sierra Leone vs Ivory Coast Friendly Match
Last updated: 2024/01/03 at 8:52 AM
admin
Share
SHARE

The upcoming friendly match between Sierra Leone and Ivory Coast will be officiated by a team of officials from Benin Republic. The game is scheduled to take place on Saturday 6th January, 2024 at the Laurent Poukou De San Pedro Stadium1. The Ivorian Football Federation has announced a list of 27 players for their friendly clash with Leone Stars of Sierra Leone, while the Sierra Leone Football Association has announced a list of 21-man squad to face the Elephants of Ivory Coast in the upcoming international friendly1. The officiating team will consist of the following three FIFA badge Benin referees:

Center Referee: Ahomalnto Dejinnanchi
Assistant 1: Koutou Gbemassiandan Narcisse
Assistant 2: Dodometin Detondji Joe Cortex
