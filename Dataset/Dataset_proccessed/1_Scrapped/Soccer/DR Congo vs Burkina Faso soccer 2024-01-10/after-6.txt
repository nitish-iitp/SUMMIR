AFCON 2023 - DRC: Nuké Mfulu called in as backup

Omenuke Mfulu has been called up to the DR Congo national team for the 2023 Africa Cup of Nations, his club Las Palmas made official on Tuesday January 9. The Congolese midfielder is expected to replace the injured Edo Kayembe, who is doubtful for the tournament. The Leopards play their final warm-up match this Wednesday, January 10, against the Etalons of Burkina Faso.
