THOMASVILLE — Ledford rolled past crosstown foe East Davidson 9-0 in nonconference boys soccer Thursday at Brown Middle.

Valentino Ontiveros had four goals and two assists to lead the Panthers. Joaquin Leal-Godoy added two goals, while Jose Gomez scored two goals. Emauel Galindo and Diego Araujo each had a goal. Kevin Palacios, Raul Reyes and Brooks Burick each had an assist. Antonio Palacios made three saves.
