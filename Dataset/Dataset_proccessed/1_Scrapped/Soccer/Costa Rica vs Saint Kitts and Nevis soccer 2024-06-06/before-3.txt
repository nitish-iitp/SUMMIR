Lowbanks man dies following single-vehicle crash near Dunnville

OPP received a report at 10:40 p.m. June 5 that a pickup truck had plunged into a creek that runs parallel to North Shore Drive near Canal Bank Road.

Updated June 7, 2024 at 2:22 p.m.

Haldimand OPP are investigating a June 5 collision near Dunnville that claimed the life of a Lowbanks man.

Metroland file photo
By Mike PearsonReporter

One person is dead following a single-vehicle collision in Haldimand County on Wednesday evening.

Ontario Provincial Police are investigating the crash on the outskirts of Dunnville that claimed the life of a 69-year-old Lowbanks man.
