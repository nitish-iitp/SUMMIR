2026 World Cup Qualifiers: Morocco Bests Tanzania

   The Atlas Lions’ goals were scored by Hakim Ziyech (28th) and Bakari Mwamnyento (54th, OG).

The Moroccan national football team beat Tanzania 2-0, Tuesday evening at the Benjamin Mkapa Stadium in Dar Es-Salaam, in the second day (group E) of the African qualifiers for the 2026 World Cup.
21 November 2023
Dar Es-Salaam (Tanzania)
