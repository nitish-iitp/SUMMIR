DFA U20 league to commence in Dominica, know fixtures

Dominica Football Association U20 League is all set to return this week with the matches will kickstart on Wednesday at Newtown Savannah

Written by Scott Johnson

2024-09-04 09:57:02

Roseau, Dominica: The Dominica Football Association U20 League is all set to return this week with the matches will kickstart on Wednesday at Newtown Savannah. The schedule for the league has been unveiled as the matches will run through Saturday.

The first match will be held between Harlem Utd SC and Mahaut SS SC at 5: 30 pm on September 4, 2024. The second match of the series will be held between Middleham Utd FC and East Centre at Laplain Playing Field on September 5, 2024, at 6: 30 pm.

The third match will be held between WE United FC and Bombers FC on September 5, 2024 at 6: 30 pm at DFA Tech Centre.

The last match of the league will be held between Bath Estate FC and South East DC on September 6, 2024, at 6: 30 pm at Laplain Playing Field.

The fixtures for the DFA Women Premier League has also been unveiled with the four thrilling matches. The matches will kickstart on September 4, 2024 between South City FC vs South East FC at 6: 30 pm , DFA Technical Centre.

On September 6, 2024, two matches will be held at Benjamin Park and DFA Technical Centre. The first match will be held between Harlem and Goodwill Runners and the second match will be held between Bombers and Mighty Avengers.

The last match will be held between Kalinago Warriors and Mahaut Soca Striketts at 4: 00 pm at DFA Technical Centre on September 7, 2024.

The fixtures for the DFA Under-15 league have also been outlined that will also commence on September 4, 2024 with a match between Goodwill Runners and Pointe Michael at 4: 30 pm. The second match will be held between Taris United SC and South East FC at 4: 30 pm at DFA Technical Centre on September 5, 2024.

On September 7, 2024, two matches will be held between Calibishie and NJFA Harlem UTD SC and Goodwill Runners and Marigot United.
