Leone Stars wrapped up their second training session today at the Lacee Classique Training Centre in Abidjan as they prepare for the upcoming Afcon Qualifier against the Elephants of Ivory Coast, set for Friday, October 11, 2024.
The squad is feeling optimistic about securing a positive result at the San Pedro Stadium, thanks to the quality and depth across all positions.
Coach Karim and his technical team will conduct their final training session on Thursday at the San Pedro Stadium.
