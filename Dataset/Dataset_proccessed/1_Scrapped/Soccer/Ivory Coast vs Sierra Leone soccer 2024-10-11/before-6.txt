IVORY COAST VS SIERRA LEONE: PREVIEW, PREDICTION, TEAM NEWS & LINEUPS

Ivory Coast vs Sierra Leone Preview, Prediction, Team News, and Lineups | 2025 AFCON Qualifiers.

 

Match Overview

The highly anticipated clash between Ivory Coast and Sierra Leone is set to unfold on October 11, 2024, as part of the Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) 2025 Qualifiers. This match will be hosted at the Stade de San Pedro in Ivory Coast, kicking off at 7 PM GMT. Both teams are eager to leave their mark in this crucial stage of the tournament.

 

Current Standings

Ivory Coast currently holds a commanding position at the top of Group G, boasting two consecutive victories in their first two matches. They began their campaign with a 2-0 home triumph over Zambia, followed by another 2-0 victory against Chad away. With six points under their belt, Ivory Coast is looking to secure qualification for the next stage with two more wins during this international break.

Conversely, Sierra Leone finds themselves in a challenging situation, sitting third in the group with just one point. Their journey thus far includes a 0-0 stalemate against Chad and a disappointing 3-2 loss to Zambia. Although they have the same points as Chad, their position is compromised by a less favorable goal difference.

 

Ivory Coast vs Sierra Leone Team News
Ivory Coast

The Ivory Coast national team is in excellent form, with no injury concerns or suspensions reported ahead of this important match against Sierra Leone. Coach Jean-Louis Gasset will have all his key players available, providing a solid foundation for his strategy.

Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone, too, has a fully fit squad. The team’s coach, John Keister, will be keen to harness the available talent to upset the Ivorian giants and secure crucial points in the qualifiers.

Ivory Coast vs Sierra Leone Head-to-Head Record

Historically, Ivory Coast has enjoyed a favorable record against Sierra Leone, winning three of their last six encounters, while the other three matches ended in draws. Here’s a brief look at their last five meetings:

Ivory Coast 5-1 Sierra Leone – International Friendly (January 6, 2024)
Ivory Coast 2-2 Sierra Leone – AFCON Group Stage (January 16, 2022)
Ivory Coast 1-1 Sierra Leone – AFCON Qualifiers (September 3, 2016)
Sierra Leone 0-0 Ivory Coast – AFCON Qualifiers (September 6, 2015)
Sierra Leone 1-5 Ivory Coast – AFCON Qualifiers (November 14, 2014)
Key Players to Watch
Simon Adingra

Simon Adingra has emerged as a key figure for the Ivory Coast. His performance at Brighton & Hove Albion in the 2023-24 season has been impressive, with seven goals and three assists across competitions. Known for his agility and skill on the wing, Adingra has proven himself crucial for his national team, contributing two goals and four assists in 16 international appearances.

Augustus Kargbo

For Sierra Leone, all eyes will be on Augustus Kargbo. The forward, who plays for Cesena in Italy’s Serie B, has shown his prowess with four goals and three assists in 10 matches this season. As Sierra Leone seeks to compete against a stronger Ivory Coast side, Kargbo’s ability to hold up play and create scoring opportunities will be vital.

Match Prediction

Given their recent performances and overall quality, Ivory Coast is favored to secure a win against Sierra Leone. While Sierra Leone’s determination should not be underestimated, Ivory Coast’s class and depth in talent suggest they will dominate the match. Soccer Baba predicted the scoreline of Ivory Coast 3-1 Sierra Leone, with a strong showing from their key players expected.
