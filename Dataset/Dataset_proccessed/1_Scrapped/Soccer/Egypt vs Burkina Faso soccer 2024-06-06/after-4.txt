Metro Line 3 Extends Hours for Egypt vs Burkina Faso Football Match

Service hours will be extended until 1 AM on June 6th, with trains running every 4.5 minutes.

Cairo Scene

 Jun 06, 2024

Metro Line 3 is making special arrangements for the national Egyptian football team’s match against Burkina Faso at Cairo International Stadium today, June 6th.

The operating company for Metro Line 3 has announced a reduction in train intervals and an increase in the number of trains to ensure quick access to Stadium Station. Service hours will be extended until 1 AM, with trains running every 4.5 minutes. For passengers travelling from Cairo University Station or Rod El-Farag Axis Station to Kit Kat Station, the interval between trains will be 9 minutes.

Additionally, a customer service team will be stationed at various stops to assist passengers, ensuring smooth movement and easy access to and from the match.
