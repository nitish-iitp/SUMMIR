Fiji Bula Boys Share Special Moment with Their Children in Dominant Victory Over Samoa

 Vula Bilitaki  19 June 2024

As a football player, it brings me joy when I have my family on the sidelines watching and cheering for my team and I. Having your loved ones watching gives that extra “ummmph”, that extra push that boosts our confidence level and drives us to winning the game.

Fiji FA

It was a heartwarming sight at the HFC Bank Stadium in Suva as the Fiji Bula Boys stepped onto the field, hand in hand with their own children, ahead of their second match against Samoa in the OFC Men’s Nations Cup. This special moment, filled with pride and emotion, highlighted the deep connections and shared dreams between the players and their families. The presence of their children added a sentimental layer to the occasion, showcasing the importance of family in the players’ lives and the inspiration it brings to their game.

Fiji FA

As the match commenced, the Fiji Bula Boys quickly took control, with Setareki Hughes scoring a goal just two minutes in. This early lead set the tone for an exhilarating performance.

Fiji FA

Throughout the game, Fiji’s team displayed remarkable skill and coordination, ultimately securing a commanding 9-1 victory over Samoa. This triumphant win not only underscored Fiji’s prowess in the tournament but also made the special moment shared with their children even more memorable, symbolizing the unity and strength within the team and their supportive families.

The scorers:
Thomas Dunn
Roy Krishna (2 goals)
Setareki Hughes (2 goals)
Nabil Begg
Filipe Baravilala
Etonia Dogalau (2 goals)

Congratulations to our Fiji Bula Boys and all the best in your next game!
