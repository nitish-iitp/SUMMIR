Vanuatu prepares for OFC Nations Cup and U-19 qualifying tournament

The Vanuatu Football Federation (VFF) Local Organizing Committee is making preparations for the OFC Nations Cup, scheduled to take place from June 15- 30 with games to be played in both Port Vila and Luganville.

The regional tournament will feature teams from New Zealand, New Caledonia, Solomon Islands, Fiji, Papua New Guinea, Tahiti, Vanuatu and the winners of the qualifying playoffs between Cook Islands, Samoa, and Tonga.
