Tafea will play for third-place after semi-final loss

Vanuatu’s Tafea FC and Fiji’s Labasa FC will contest in the third-place playoff at the OFC Women’s Champions League after their losing in their semi-finals at Lawson Tama Stadium yesterday in Honiara, Solomon Islands.

Tafea became the first-ever Vanuatu team to participate at an OFC champions league and have managed to reach the semi-final stage after beating the Henderson Eels of the host country, Solomon Islands with a score of 3-0 in their third Pool A game. They also defeated Cook Islands’ Avatiu FC by 4-2 and draw with Papua New Guinea’s Hekari United.
