Key instructions for fans attending Qatar Vs. UAE 2026 FIFA World Cup Asian Qualifiers

On 4 September 2024, the Qatar Football Association announced key instructions for Qatar's match against UAE at the Asian Qualifiers for the 2026 World Cup. The match will be held at the Ahmad Bin Ali Stadium at 7 pm on 5 September 2024.

The following guidelines have been laid out for the fans attending the match:

Gates will open at 4 pm, and fans are advised to arrive at the stadium early.
The entire western parking lot will be designated for fans, as indicated on the fan parking maps.
No one will be allowed entry into the stadium without a valid ticket.
Flags and scarves will be distributed to fans in the stands.

Be an impactful fan by cheering enthusiastically for the players.
The best way to the match is by metro. Fans can take the Green Line to Al Riffa Station.

Fans are requested to follow the signs around the stadium to reach their designated parking area.
Tickets will not be sold at the stadium.
There will be several restaurants available to provide food and drinks for fans.

Multiple prayer areas are available in the stadium for Maghrib and Isha prayers.
