Format[edit]
League: May 15th - June 2nd, 2024
20 teams:
13 partnership teams (6 from Middle East & Africa, 2 from Europe, 5 from TÃ¼rkiye)
Top 3 teams from Middle East & Africa Qualifier
Top 2 teams from Europe Qualifier
Top 2 teams from TÃ¼rkiye Qualifier
3 weeks, each week consisting of 5 matchdays.
Each matchday consists of 6 matches. (30 matches total per week)
Teams are split into 5 groups of 4, each group plays a total of 24 matches.
Groups are reshuffled every week:
Week 1: Teams are grouped randomly.
Week 2: Teams are grouped based on their Week 1 placements.
Week 3: Teams are grouped based on their Week 2 placements.
Each week teams are awarded Bonus Points based on their placements.
Top 16 teams based on the overall rankings advance to Finals.
Finals: June 7th - 9th, 2024
16 teams.
Teams begin with 50% of their accumulated Bonus Points from League stage.
In case of a team's total bonus points is an odd number, the points will be rounded up to the nearest integer.
