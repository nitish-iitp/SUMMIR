EURO 2024 AMPUTEE FOOTBALL: UKRAINE FACES DEFEAT AGAINST NETHERLANDS

07.06.2024 19:05

Today, June 7, at the European Championship among amputee players, Ukraine's national team suffered a 2:3 defeat against the Netherlands in their fifth match of the tournament. Goals for the blue-yellow team were scored by Oleksiyenko (7th minute) and Sushch (11th minute).

It's worth reminding that Ukrainians finished third in their group and are now competing for the 9th to 16th places. Our next match will take place on June 8 against Scotland. In this match, they will vie for the overall 11th position.

EURO 2024 Amputee Football

Group A: France, Spain, Ukraine, Belgium.

Group B: England, Italy, Georgia, Netherlands.

Group C: Turkey, Ireland, Israel, Azerbaijan.

Group D: Poland, Germany, Greece, Scotland.

Ukraine's Team Matches

01.06. Ukraine - France - 0:1

03.06. Ukraine - Spain - 0:3

04.06. Ukraine - Belgium - 3:1

06.06. Ukraine - Georgia - 3:1

07.06. Ukraine - Netherlands - 2:3
