Kyrgyzstan and Thailand to play at Asian Cup
   

The Asian Football Cup is taking place in Qatar with the participation of national teams of 24 countries. The Kyrgyz Football Union reported on social media.

The team of Kyrgyzstan will play against the team of Thailand in Group F at the Abdullah bin Khalifa Stadium in Doha today, January 16.

The match will start at 8.30 p.m. Bishkek time.

The match between Kyrgyzstan and Thailand will be officiated by the referee from Jordan Adham Makhadmeh.

A fan zone has been organized for Kyrgyz fans in Asia Mall and Dordoi Plaza shopping centers.

Matches of the Asian Football Cup are broadcasted live in Kyrgyzstan on UTRK-Sport TV channel.
