Caf don cancel di traditional preliminary round wey dem bin dey use bifor.

Di new plan go see all African nations take part for a larger group stage - di 54 entrants dem split dem into nine pools, each group get six teams.

But, Caf plans spoil afta Eritrea late withdrawal from qualifying on 10 November, now Group E get only five teams.

All di kontris wey top dia qualifying group go automatically book dia place for United States, Mexico and Canada tournament.

While some of di (runners-up) dat one mean second position for table of di nine qualifying groups go get another chance to reach Canada, Mexico and USA.

Di four best-ranked second-placed sides go play for play-offs, wey go see dem play semi-final and final for November 2025 to determine which African side go advance to di intercontinental play-off tournament.

Eritrea withdrawal go affect dis rankings, Caf neva tok yet how dem go take solve dis matter.

Di final global qualifying round go involve six teams wey go compete for di final two places for di 2026 finals, and e dey expected to take place for March 2026.
