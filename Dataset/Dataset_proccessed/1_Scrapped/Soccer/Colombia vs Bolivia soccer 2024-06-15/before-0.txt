UEFA Euro 2024 and COPA AMERICA 2024 match schedule; Police warn of betting criminals
Government web portal
June 14, 2024
(Chinhphu.vn) – The European Football Championship (EURO 2024) and the South American Football Championship 2024 (COPA AMERICA 2024) will take place from June 15, 2024 to July 15, 2024. These are major sporting events that attract a large number of viewers. At the same time, it is also the time when football betting forms arise and increase, attracting a large number of players, causing many consequences and economic recession for families and society.
Warning about criminals organizing gambling and gambling in the form of soccer betting on Euro season and Copa America 2024 

The European Football Championship (EURO 2024) and the South American Football Championship 2024 (COPA AMERICA 2024) are scheduled to take place from June 15, 2024 to July 15, 2024. 

These are the events nutrition, big entertainment, attracting a large number of fans on world including Vietnam. At the same time, it is also the time when football betting forms arise and increase, attracting a large number of players, causing many consequences, economic recession of families and society. 

With a passion for football, faith in their favorite teams and the desire to have money to play, many people have been seduced and enticed to participate in football betting directly or on cyberspace. 

This is also the reason why other types of crimes and violations of the law increase and become complicated, such as theft, robbery, robbery, fraudulent appropriation of property, intentional injury, and lending at high interest rates in transactions. civil, human trafficking....

According to current law, the act of betting on soccer and losing money or in kind is considered gambling, depending on the level, it can be administratively handled or criminally prosecuted according to the provisions of Articles 321, 322 of the Penal Code.

Currently, gambling organizations have taken advantage of cyberspace to set up many soccer betting websites with server systems located abroad, and at the same time try to advertise and entice players to open accounts to participate in betting. football level.

To proactively prevent organized gambling crimes and gambling in the form of football betting during the EURO and COPA AMERICA seasons in 2024, the Criminal Police Department of the Provincial Police Thai Binh suggestion:

1. Every citizen needs to raise awareness of compliance with legal regulations related to social evils, passion and love for football but resolutely stay away from football betting, be aware of the consequences, The harmful effects of gambling on personal life, family and society.
2. Pay attention, manage, educate and mobilize relatives and people around to increase vigilance, avoid being lured into losses and enticed to participate in soccer betting.
3. Owners of food service establishments, coffee shops, beer bars, entertainment spots, cafes, etc. must strictly comply with the provisions of law on security and order. During the process of organizing football viewing, do not organize football betting and do not assist or cover up for subjects to take advantage of organizing football betting.
4. If you discover suspects related to football betting, you should promptly report the news to the nearest police station or report to the Criminal Police Department of Thai Binh Provincial Police via the hotline. 069.2760.273 for investigation and treatment measures to contribute to a healthy social environment.
Detailed match schedule of Copa America 2024 football tournament

Copa America 2024 has the participation of 10 teams from the South American region and 6 teams from the CONCACAF region (Confederation of North, Central American and Caribbean Football).

Copa America 2024 is the 48th edition of the South American Football Championship. The event takes place in the United States from June 21, 2024 to July 15, 2024.

This is the second time Copa America has been held in the US, after the first time in 2016 on the occasion of the 100th anniversary of the most important tournament of national teams in South America.

This year's tournament has the participation of 10 teams from the South American region and 6 teams from the CONCACAF region (Confederation of North, Central American and Caribbean Football).

Copa America 2024 will have 16 teams divided into 4 groups of four teams each. The top two teams in each group will advance to the quarter-finals.

The opening match will be the clash between defending champions Argentina and Canada (Group A) at Mercedes-Benz Stadium, Atalanta on 21/6.

The final will take place on 15/7 at Hard Rock Stadium in Miami, Florida with a capacity of 65.300 people.
