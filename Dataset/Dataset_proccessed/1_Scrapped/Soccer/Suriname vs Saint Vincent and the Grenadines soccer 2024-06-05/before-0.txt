Official Visit of the Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People’s Congress of the People’s Republic of China
June 3, 2024

The Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People’s Congress of the People’s Republic of China, the Honourable Mr Cai Dafeng, is currently leading a 16-member delegation on an official visit to Seychelles. He was welcomed on his arrival today by the Deputy Speaker of the Seychelles National Assembly, Honourable Gervais Henrie and the Principal Secretary for Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Vivianne Fock Tave.

During his 3-day visit, Honourable Dafeng will be paying a courtesy call on the Vice-President of Seychelles, Mr Ahmed Afif, at State House, followed by a meeting with the Speaker of the National Assembly, Mr Roger Mancienne. He will also visit various China funded projects.

Seychelles is Honourable Dafeng’s last destination in his ‘Africa Tour’ and will be departing Wednesday 05th June 2024.
