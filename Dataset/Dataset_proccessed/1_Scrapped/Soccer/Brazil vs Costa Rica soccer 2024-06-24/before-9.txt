BRAZIL PREDICTED LINEUP VS COSTA RICA: EXCITING PREVIEW, LATEST TEAM NEWS, PREDICTIONS, BETTING, COPA AMERICA 2024 GROUP D MATCHDAY 1
By Yazad Bagli
June 19, 2024

Football Fans eagerly anticipate numerous matches around the globe, particularly on a day that aligns with Copa America 2024. We at The Football Yeller are here to cover Brazil Predicted Lineup vs Costa Rica for the Copa America 2024 Group D Matchday 1 matchup that will take place on Monday, 24th June 2024, at SoFi Stadium in Inglewood, California, USA.

Brazil Predicted Lineup vs Costa Rica. Image Credits:- Goal.
Brazil Predicted Lineup vs Costa Rica Preview

Regardless of Brazil’s performance at the Copa America in the United States, it is bound to catch many people off guard. The new coach, Dorival Junior, has only been in charge of two friendlies, and his squad for the South American championship has not yet shown much evidence of his plans.

In addition, the team will be missing Neymar, who unfortunately sustained an ACL injury and will be unable to play. There have been some easily recognisable traits of the Brazil team over the past few decades.

Regardless of the outcome, there are frequently skilled dribblers in the forward positions, fullbacks who contribute to the attack, and solid defenders who are capable of scoring goals. Not this time. One player who stands out is Vinicius Junior, a strong contender for the Ballon d’Or.

Vinicius Junior showcased his skills by helping Real Madrid secure the Champions League title and even scoring in the final against Borussia Dortmund. He is set to shine as Brazil’s top player during the tournament, which takes place from June 20 to July 15.

Observers of Brazil’s football scene anticipate that the Copa America won’t offer much insight into the team’s composition for the 2026 World Cup. For most Brazilians, the World Cup is the sole tournament of significance.

However, the upcoming continental tournament presents an opportunity for the new coach to evaluate promising young talents such as strikers Endrick and Rodrygo, who are expected to start and don the team’s iconic No. 9 jersey.

Going into this fixture, Brazil has a win probability of 65%, whereas Costa Rica has a win probability of 20%.

Below is Brazil‘s Official Copa America Squad:

Goalkeepers:- Alisson (Liverpool), Bento (Athletico Paranaense), and Rafael (Sao Paulo).

Defenders:- Marquinhos (PSG), Danilo (Juventus), Eder Militao (Real Madrid), Gabriel Magalhaes (Arsenal), Guilherme Arana (Athletico Mineiro), Bremer (Juventus), Yan Couto (Girona), Lucas Beraldo (PSG), and Wendell (Porto).

Midfielders:- Lucas Paqueta (West Ham United), Bruno Guimaraes (Newcastle United), Douglas Luiz (Aston Villa), Andreas Pereira (Fulham), Joao Gomes (Wolverhampton Wanderers), and Ederson (Atalanta).

Forwards:- Vinicius Junior (Real Madrid), Raphinha (Barcelona), Rodrygo (Real Madrid), Gabriel Martinelli (Arsenal), Endrick (Palmeiras), Pepe (Porto), Savio (Girona), and Evanilson (Porto).
