In an exciting Group C encounter of the Africa Cup of Nations, Guinea face Gambia on Friday at Stade de Yamoussoukro stadium.

Guinea, currently occupying the 3rd position, seek a crucial win to climb the group ladder, while Gambia, positioned 4th, aim to secure victory and improve their standing in the competition.

Guinea vs Gambia Preview
Guinea Form

Inconsistency was a regular theme for Guinea across their past three matches. They first fell to a 1-0 defeat to Botswana before bouncing back by beating Nigeria 0-2. Their most recent outing resulted in a 1-1 draw in a hard-fought encounter with Cameroon.

Gambia Form

Gambia had nothing to show for their previous three games. They first fell to a 3-2 loss to Burundi before getting trounced against Ivory Coast by a 0-2 score. Last time out, more disappointment followed as Senegal ran out 3-0 winners.
