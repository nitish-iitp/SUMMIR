A top-of-the-table clash from CAF World Cup qualifiers

The November international break is been used to take care of some 2026 FIFA World Cup qualifiers. From the African region (CAF), Group D matchday 2 sees the Libya national football team take on Cameroon national football team. They last faced each other a decade ago.

Libya have opened their qualification campaign on a good note. Matchday 1 saw them get a 1-0 victory over South African nation Eswatini. They are now only second in Group D due to Cameroon's better goal difference.

Cameroon eased their opening-day nerves by beating Mauritius 3-0 at home. Now they have an away test which seemingly has proven difficult for them in the last 8 fixtures.
