Egypt faces Guinea Bissau in the 2026 World Cup African Qualifiers

BY

Egypt Today

-

Mon, 10 Jun 2024 - 07:00 GMT

CAIRO, June 10, 2024: The Egyptian national team, led by Hossam Hassan, is preparing to face Guinea-Bissau at the 24 de September Stadium in the city of Bissau, in the fourth round of the World Cup qualifiers.

 

The Egyptian national team's match comes within the fourth round of qualifiers for the 2026 World Cup finals, the African continent.

 

The expected formation of the Egyptian national team for the match against Guinea Bissau today, led by Mohamed Salah, is as follows:

 

Goalkeeper: Mohamed El-Shenawy.

Defense line: Mohamed Hani - Rami Rabia - Mohamed Abdel Moneim - Mohamed Hamdi.

Midfield: Marwan Attia - Ahmed Sayed Zizou - Hamdi Fathi.

Offensive line: Mohamed Salah - Mahmoud Hassan Trezeguet - Mustafa Mohamed.

 

The Egypt national team group in the World Cup qualifiers includes: Egypt - Guinea Bissau - Burkina Faso - Sierra Leone - Ethiopia - Djibouti.

 

The Egyptian national team leads the first group of the 2026 World Cup qualifying groups with 9 points.

 

The Egyptian team defeated Djibouti (6-0), Sierra Leone (2-0), and Burkina Faso (2-1). Guinea-Bissau comes in second place with 5 points. It won over Djibouti (1-0), tied with Burkina Faso (1-1) and tied with Ethiopia (0-0).
