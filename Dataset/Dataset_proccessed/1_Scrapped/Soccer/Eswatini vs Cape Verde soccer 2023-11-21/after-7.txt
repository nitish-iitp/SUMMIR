MTN LEAGUE GAMES BACK THIS WEEKEND

By SIPHOSETHU DLAMINI | 2023-11-21

SOCCER - THE MTN Premier League will be in full swing this weekend after the FIFA Week break.
The league took a break to allow players to join the national team Sihlangu for the World Cup Qualifiers.

Sihlangu has already played against Libya where they lost 1-0.
The nation’s pride will face-off with Cape Verde today at Mbombela Stadium.

First to take to the field will be Ezulwini United, who will be up against Moneni Pirates at the Prince of Wales Sports Ground on Thursday.

There will be a double header at Mavuso Sports Centre on Saturday where Denver Sundowns will host struggling hub giants Manzini Wanderers while army side Young Buffaloes will welcome capital city side Mbabane Swallows in the second match.

Another exciting encounter at Ka-Langa Stadium on Saturday will see log leaders Green Mamba take on Mbabane Highlanders.Also on Saturday at Mayaluka Stadium, Illovo will play against Manzini Sea Birds.

There will be another double header on Sunday at King Sobhuza II Stadium where Rangers will clash with police side Royal Leopard while Nsingizini Hotspurs will host Madlenya in the second match.
The fixtures were released by Premier League of Eswatini (PLE) Football Manager Pat Vilakati.

He announced that there were three pending fixtures that would be played next week.
The pending fixtures are between Manzini Sea Bords against Green Mamba, Illovo against Royal Leopard and Nsingizini Hotspurs against Mbabane Highlanders.
