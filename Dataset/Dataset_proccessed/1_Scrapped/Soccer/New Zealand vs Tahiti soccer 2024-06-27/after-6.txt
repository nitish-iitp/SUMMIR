Town mourns loss of 'intellectual heir' of The Foxboro Company
By JEFF PETERSON For The Foxboro Reporter Jun 27, 2024 
1 of 2

Among scores of accolades accumulated during a lifetime of achievement, Edgar Bristol’s most notable tribute just may have occurred in 2003, when he was recognized as one of the 50 most influential innovators in the history of the process controls industry.

The honor was especially sweet in that it included three other members of the Bristol family, founders and generational leaders of The Foxboro Company, the town’s largest employer for nearly a century.
