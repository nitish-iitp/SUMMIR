2023-24 Panini Select FIFA Soccer Cards Box Break Overview
Box Break Contents

Each box of the 2023-24 Panini Select FIFA Soccer Cards includes:

3 Autographed or Memorabilia Cards
7 Inserts or Insert Parallels
9 Base Parallels (Unnumbered)
5 Numbered Parallels
Product Highlights
Autographed and Memorabilia Cards
3 Autographed or Memorabilia Cards per Box: Collect stunning autographs and memorabilia cards from the world’s biggest soccer stars. Key sets to chase include:
Select Signatures
Pitchside Signatures
Autographed Memorabilia
Inserts and Insert Parallels
7 Inserts or Insert Parallels per Box: Look for a range of exciting inserts, with some featuring rare parallels.
Super Rare Case Hits: Keep an eye out for case-hit inserts like:
Stained Glass
Artistic Impressions
Visionary
Base and Numbered Parallels
9 Base Parallels (Unnumbered): These colorful and visually stunning parallels give collectors plenty of variety to chase.
5 Numbered Parallels per Box: Find a range of numbered parallels, which include some of Select's most sought-after variations:
Multi-Color
Camo
Tie-Dye
Zebra
Black
And many more!
