Who Will Make it to the Finals?

Comparing the Group E AFCON teams for their potential to reach the finals is tricky, as each team has unique strengths and weaknesses, and the tournament holds an unpredictable share of surprises. Here is a breakdown for each team:

Tunisia:
Strengths: Tunisia is the most experienced team in the group, with a strong technical game and tactical awareness. It boasts star players like Wahbi Khazri and Youssef Msakni.
Weaknesses: The Carthage Eagles have an aging squad with limited pace, and sometimes struggle defensively against physically strong teams.

The prospects for Tunisia are not looking good. Its weaknesses were apparent in the team’s unexpected 1-0 loss against Namibia in its opener. The defeat eclipsed player Youssef Msakni milestone of earning his 100th cap for the national team.

Advertisement
Mali:
Strengths: Mali has a talented young team with high energy and pace. It also has a strong midfield led by Adama Traoré and Amadou Haidara.
Weaknesses: Team Les Aigles can be inconsistent and undisciplined at times, lacking experience in big tournaments.

Mali soared out of its AFCON 2024 debut with a 2-0 win against South Africa. This was a landmark victory for the team given that it is the only team to have never received an AFCON trophy and its best performance dates back to 1972 with a 3-2 loss against Congo.

South Africa:
Strengths: South Africa has improved its performance under coach Hugo Broos, it is defensively solid and organised. It also has passionate home support, even with the tournament being held in Ivory Coast.
Weaknesses: Bafana Bafana has a lack of individual star power compared to other teams, and can be short on creativity in attack.

South Africa has quite a fight on its hands in its next match against Namibia. The odds are leaning against it. Not only was it devastatingly defeated in its opening match, but on average, its AFCON career has not been a stellar one.

Namibia:
Strengths: Namibia inspires an underdog mentality, can be defensively resolute, and hard to break down.
Weaknesses: The Brave Warriors are the least experienced team in the group, are a limited attacking threat, and might struggle against more skilled opponents.

Namibia surprised spectators by defeating Tunisia in its first AFCON 2024 appearance by a goal. The magnitude of this achievement is visible when comparing the current African Football rankings of the teams: Namibia (90th) and Tunisia (3rd). It would be interesting to see if the Brave Warriors will remain underdogs against Bafana Bafana.

Prediction:

It is difficult to choose a clear favorite. Tunisia has the experience and quality, but age and lack of pace could be an issue. Mali has the potential with its youthful exuberance, but consistency is a concern. South Africa is defensively strong but may lack firepower. Namibia is unlikely to go far but could upset teams with its fighting spirit. It is equally difficult to tell what will happen between Bafana Bafana and AFCON 2024.

Ultimately, the team that manages to find a good balance between experience, talent, and form during the tournament will have the best chance of reaching the finals. Factors like injuries, refereeing decisions, and even a bit of luck will also play a role.

So, keep an eye on all four teams in Group E! It promises to be a competitive and exciting group stage. Especially for Bafana Bafana and AFCON 2024.
