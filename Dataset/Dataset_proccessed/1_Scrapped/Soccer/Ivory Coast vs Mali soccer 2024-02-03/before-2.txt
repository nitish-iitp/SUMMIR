Ratiba ya Robo Fainali / Quarterfinals AFCON 2023

Ratiba ya Robo Fainali / Quarterfinals AFCON 2023 , which means the Schedule of the quarterfinals of the Ongoing AFCON 2023 / 24 Tournament. Out Of the 24 men’s football national teams that began the tournament hosted by Côte d’Ivoire, 8 have made it through to the Quarterfinals stage

This is the list of the 8 Quarter-finalists / Waliofuzu Robo Fainali of AFCON 2023

Cape Verde
Nigeria
Guinea
Angola
Mali
South Africa
DR Congo

As these last eight standing sides take on each other for Africa’s biggest football tournament, African & Global Soccer fans, will be expecting more Gripping football actions in the upcoming AFCON 2023 Quarterfinals as this tournament has been the AFCON of ‘Surprises’ with all the top 5 African Sides Morocco, Senegal, Algeria, Egypt and Tunisia eliminated.
