Mary Sloan started writing obituaries when she was a J-school student at the University of Missouri. She always said “I’m writing my own!”

Mary Elizabeth (Jacox) Sloan was born September 29, 1944 in Chadron, NE the daughter of Kermit and Johanna (Englehaupt) Jacox. The family – parents, sister Barbara and Mary moved to Scottsbluff, NE 2 years later, where Mary lived until graduating from Scottsbluff High School in 1962. Mary came to Columbia, Missouri to begin Journalism School and graduated with a degree in Broadcast Journalism in January 1967. She married Mike Sloan soon after and they were a couple until 1995. She married Scott Dressel in 1998.
