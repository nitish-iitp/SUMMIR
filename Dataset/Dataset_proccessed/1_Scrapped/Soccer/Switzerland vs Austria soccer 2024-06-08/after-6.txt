Euro 2024 squads: All 26-man selections from the 24 competing nations
By Luke Bosher
Jun 8, 2024

19

The 2024 men’s European Championship gets underway on Friday, June 14 with hosts Germany facing Scotland at the Allianz Arena in Munich.

Over the next four weeks, 24 teams will compete for glory, culminating with the final on Sunday, July 14 at Berlin’s Olympiastadion.

Many countries named enlarged preliminary selections ahead of the tournament but those had to be whittled down to a final 26 by midnight CET on Friday, June 7.

Here are the final squads for Euro 2024:

This article was last updated on Tuesday, June 18
