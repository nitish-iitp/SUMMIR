Debra “Debbie’ Jean King

Debra Jean King, lovingly known as Debbie, passed away Saturday, March 23, 2024, in Jacksboro, Texas. She was 67 years old. Debbie was born on July 25, 1956, in Olney, Texas to James Lee Voyles and Melba Jean (Banks) Voyles. Debbie accepted Jesus Christ as her Lord and Savior on December 6, 1964, at the Live Oak Baptist Church in Jacksboro, Texas.

A visitation will be held from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM on Tuesday, March 26, 2024, at the Bible Baptist Church in Jacksboro. Funeral services will follow beginning at 10 AM. The burial will take place at Oakwood Cemetery in Jacksboro, Texas. Services are under the direction of Coker Funeral Home in Jacksboro, Texas.

Debbie married James King on February 23, 1980, in Jacksboro, Texas. She attended Bible Baptist Church and was a medical coder until her health no longer allowed her to work. Most of all Debbie was a loving wife, mother, and Mimi. Debbie will be truly missed by her family and friends.

Debbie is preceded in death by her father.

Those left behind to cherish her memory include her husband, James King; mother, Melba Mower; children, Heather Reeves and Cody Carter and wife Jessica; brother, Mike Voyles, and wife Darla; 10 grandchildren; 9 great-grandchildren; along with many extended family and friends.
