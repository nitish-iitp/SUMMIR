Three warming centres open in Toronto and Scarborough

The centres offering around 150 spaces in total will be open 24 hours this winter.

Updated Nov. 21, 2023 at 11:31 a.m.
Nov. 20, 2023
1 min read
Save

Three warming centres in Toronto and Scarborough opened Sunday evening.

Steve Russell / Toronto Star file photo
By Nawa Tahir Staff Reporter

Toronto has opened three warming centres for the homeless as temperatures drop to -5 C in the city.

The centres opened at 5 p.m. on Sunday and will remain open until temperatures rise.
