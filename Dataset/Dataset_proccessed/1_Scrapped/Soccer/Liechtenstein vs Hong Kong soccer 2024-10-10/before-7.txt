Georgia-Pacific takes home top honors in Diboll Day Parade

DIBOLL — It was all smiles and high fives as Georgia-Pacific proudly claimed first place in the industrial division of the 2024 Diboll Day Parade. This year’s theme, “Building a Better Future,” was brought to life by the dedicated teams from Diboll Lumber and Diboll Particleboard.

Over the past month, employees collaborated to design and construct an eye-catching parade entry that highlighted the building products made right here in East Texas.
