WHAT HAPPENED?

Pulisic provided a game-changing moment for the USMNT against Brazil. The Milan star won a free kick at the top of the box before smashing it home to tie the game at 1-1 - scoring what was eventually the lone US goal of the match.

THE BIGGER PICTURE

Pulisic strike offered the US hope less than four days after an abysmal showing, getting them back into the game against one of the best side's in world soccer on the back of a 5-1 defeat to Colombia. It was his 29th goal for the USMNT.

WHAT NEXT FOR PULISIC AND THE USMNT?

Gregg Berhalter's side will look ahead to the start of Copa America following the conclusion of Wednesday evening's friendly. Their Group Stage consists of opponents Bolivia, Panama and Uruguay.
