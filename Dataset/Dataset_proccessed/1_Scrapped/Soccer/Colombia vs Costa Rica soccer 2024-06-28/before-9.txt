On Monday, June 24 at 6:00 PM ET, Colombia and Paraguay square off in Group D at the 2024 Copa America, the first match for each squad.

Keep reading to find out the info on how to watch this game on Fox Sports 1.

How to Watch Colombia vs. Paraguay
Match Day: Monday, June 24, 2024
Match Time: 6:00 PM ET
Venue: NRG Stadium
Location: Houston, Texas
TV Channel: Fox Sports 1 – Watch FREE on Fubo

Colombia vs. Paraguay Insights
Colombia scored 29 goals (1.7 per match) and Paraguay conceded nine overall (0.8 per match) in the past two years of international play.
In the past two years, Paraguay posted six goals in 11 games, and Colombia allowed 11 in 17 games.
With 29 goals scored and 11 allowed, Colombia had a positive goal differential.
Paraguay was outscored 9-6 in that time frame.
Colombia Leaders
Playing for Liverpool FC in the Premier League this past season, Luis Diaz had eight goals and five assists in 37 matches.
Jhon Arias had seven goals and six assists in 30 games for Fluminense FC RJ (Brasileiro) last season.
With Argentine Primera División club CA River Plate, Miguel Angel Borja posted five goals and one assist in 26 appearances.
Jhon Jader Duran Palacio’s club stats last season were five goals and no assists in 28 appearances for Aston Villa (Premier League).
