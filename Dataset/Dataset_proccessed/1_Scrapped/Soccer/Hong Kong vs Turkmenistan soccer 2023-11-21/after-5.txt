The match between the national teams of Turkmenistan and Hong Kong will be officiated by a team of referees from the UAE
13:4021.11.2023  0 12007

The match of the second round of the 2026 World Cup qualifying tournament in group “E” between the national teams of Turkmenistan and Hong Kong will be officiated by a refereeing team from the UAE.

The main referee will be 41 years old FIFA referee Adel Al-Naqbi. Al-Naqbi will be assisted by his compatriots - assistant referees Ali Al-Nuaimi and Sabet Al-Ali. The reserve referee will be Razlan Joffrey (Malaysia).

Let us remind that the match between Turkmenistan and Hong Kong will be held on November 21. The teams will play at Hong Kong Stadium. The game starts at 17:00 Ashgabat time.
