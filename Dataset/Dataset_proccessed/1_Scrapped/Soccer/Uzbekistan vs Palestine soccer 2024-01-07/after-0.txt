ADNOC Pro League's Impressive Stats: 289 Goals in Just 12 Matchweeks
07/01/2024
Worth sharing?

The ADNOC Pro League, before pausing for the UAE national team's participation in the upcoming AFC Asian Cup, has left fans excited with its performance in the first 12 matchweeks.

 

These initial rounds were not just packed with exciting moments but also remarkable statistics.

 

 

A total of 289 goals were scored in 84 matches, averaging 3.55 goals per match.

 

 

This high scoring rate emphasizes the league's attacking prowess.

 

 

In terms of attempts, there were 2,254 shots, with 818 accurately on target.

 

 

Al Ain led this chart with the highest number of shots on goal, amounting to 79, and their standout player, Laba Kodjo, contributed significantly with 26 shots.

 

 

The league's teams have also displayed impressive passing skills, collectively making 68,426 passes.

 

Al Jazira topped this category with 6,815 passes, while their player Fernando made the most individual contributions with 954 passes.

 

 

Penalties have been a notable part of the game, with 36 awarded across the 12 rounds.

 

Al Wahda earned the most, receiving 5 penalties.

 

 

Additionally, there were 877 corner kicks throughout the league, with Al Ain securing the highest number at 92.

 

 

Al Wasl, sitting at the top of the table with 30 points, boasts an unbeaten record of 9 wins and 3 draws.

 

They have scored 33 goals, marking them as the strongest offense, and conceded 15 goals.

 

Al Wasl also lead in second-half scoring with 17 goals and ranks third in first-half goals with 16.
