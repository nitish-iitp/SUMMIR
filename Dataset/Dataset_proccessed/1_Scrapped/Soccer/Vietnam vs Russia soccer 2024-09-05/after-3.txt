Opening of the LPBank Cup 2024 International Friendly Football Tournament
Vietnam
September 6, 2024

September 5, 2024 Day at My Dinh Stadium, international friendly football tournament LPBank Cup 2024 has officially opened, promising exciting football festivals for fans with many world-class stars. world and area.

My Dinh Stadium, 5/9 evening, a large audience eagerly came to enjoy the top match between the Vietnamese team and the Russian team. The opening match LPBank The 2024 Cup marks the first time the Vietnamese and Russian teams meet at the national team level. 

The Russian team is currently ranked 33rd on the FIFA rankings, considered one of the strongest teams in Europe and the world, with 11 appearances in the World Cup and reaching the semi-finals in 1966 (former Soviet Union team) and the quarter-finals in 2018. For the Vietnamese team, Russia is the strongest European team. “The Gold Star Warriors” have the opportunity to compete. Previously, in 2023, under the guidance of coach Troussier, the Vietnamese boys played friendly matches with the Korean, Uzbekistan and Chinese national teams, but only stopped at Asian teams.

Mr. Le Minh Tam - Permanent Vice Chairman of LPBank Board of Directors spoke at the Opening Ceremony

The LPBank Cup 2024 international friendly football tournament plays an important role for coach Kim Sang-sik to shape the appearance of the Vietnamese team, in which Russia and Thailand are considered squad tests to help the Korean coach's students demonstrate their abilities and tactics. "LPBank Cup 2024 is considered a quality test for the Vietnamese team to compete, gain more international competition experience to prepare to defend the AFF Cup championship and pass the 2027 Asian Cup qualifiers," coach Kim Sang Sik shared at a previous press conference.

Loc Phat Bank Vietnam (LPBank) is the main sponsor of this year's international friendly tournament. This activity continues to affirm LPBank's companionship with the development of nutrition Vietnam in general and football in particular. With the motto "Incorporating social responsibility into business", LPBank determines that continuously contributing to the future of the country's sports is the consistent orientation in the Bank's operations.

Representatives of the Sponsor and the Organizing Committee presented flowers and encouraged the players of the two teams Vietnam - Russia at the opening match.

Previously, LPBank was also known as one of the largest sponsors in the sports field when accompanying many domestic clubs and sports events. Most recently, LPBank officially became the main sponsor of the LPBank V.League 1 National Football Championship - 2024/2025, aiming for new and more professional developments for Vietnamese football.

In addition to its high professional quality, LPBank Cup 2024 also plays an important role in promoting the image of Vietnam and its people to the world. The tournament receives great attention from international media and is broadcast live on national television. Therefore, LPBank Cup 2024 is not only an attractive sporting event but also a valuable opportunity to enhance understanding and strengthen solidarity among nations. This event contributes to promoting international cooperation and connecting the three countries of Vietnam, Russia and Thailand.

The exciting opening match of the LPBank Cup 2024 International Friendly Football Tournament

At the opening ceremony, Mr. Le Minh Tam, Standing Vice Chairman of LPBank's Board of Directors, said with excitement: “LPBank Cup 2024 not only brings high-quality matches but is also a golden opportunity for the Vietnamese national team to compete with strong European teams, to improve physical strength, endurance, speed and confidence, and to prepare perfectly for important tournaments at the end of the year. This is the starting point for an exciting journey, with matches between Vietnam, Russia and Thailand promising to create tense and exciting moments, while opening up opportunities for deep exchange and connection. We expect the tournament to attract millions of fans and strengthen the friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and the international community”.

LPBank Cup 2024 is a golden opportunity for the Vietnamese national team to compete with strong European teams.

According to the schedule, after the opening match with the Russian team, the Vietnamese team will have a fateful match with the Thai team on 10/9. Between these two matches is the confrontation between Thailand and Russia taking place at 20:00 on 7/9. With high professional quality and competition in attractive matches, LPBank Cup 2024 is considered by experts to be a "feast" to treat domestic football fans.

On July 15, 2024, LPBank officially changed its name to Loc Phat Bank Vietnam, opening up more opportunities for cooperation and business development, while demonstrating the vision of the Bank's leaders in moving towards prosperity and well-being with customers, the community and the country. The change of name and brand identity marks the beginning of a new development phase of LPBank with a new business strategy, aiming for new values ​​for customers, partners and shareholders.

To date, LPBank is "present" in communes and districts across the country, its network stretches across 63 provinces and cities with more than 1.200 transaction points. Network strength is one of the main driving forces promoting the growth of residential capital mobilization and retail lending of the entire bank and is a strategic spearhead in the following years.

 

K.Oanh
