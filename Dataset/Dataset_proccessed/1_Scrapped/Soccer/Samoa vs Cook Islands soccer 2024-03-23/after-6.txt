SNELLVILLE — Anna Braziunas’ big game powered Parkview to a 6-3 victory over South Gwinnett in Region 4-AAAAAAA girls soccer on Friday.

Braziunas scored five goals for the Panthers, now 5-7 overall and 5-3 in region play.
