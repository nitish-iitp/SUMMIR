Portland Timbers introduce Jonathan Rodriguez to help bolster attack

The Portland Timbers finally concluded the process that had generated many rumors in both the US and Mexcio’s media when general manager Ned Grabavoy introduced the new designated player, forward Jonathan Rodríguez on Thursday, March 21 at the club’s training facilities in Beaverton.

Rodríguez signed a contract through 2026 with a club option in 2027, and will occupy a Designated Player spot and an international spot on the roster, pending a physical and a receipt of his International Transfer Certificate (ITC) and P-1 visa. The Timbers did not disclose the details of the transfer, but is rumored to make Rodriguez the highest paid player in club history.
