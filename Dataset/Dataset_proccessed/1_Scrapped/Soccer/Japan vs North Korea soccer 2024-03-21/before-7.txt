About[edit]

On March 19th, it was announced that Mirage's competition license has been revoked, and that the North America League has been reduced to nine teams.[1] Beastcoast's week 1 forfeit win against Mirage has been nullified.[2]

Format[edit]
Group Stage:
Round-Robin format
All games are Bo1
Top 6 teams advance to Playoffs
Bottom 2 teams join the Open Qualifiers Playoffs
Playoffs:
Modified GSL format
All games are Bo3
Placement
Top 3 teams qualify to the Major
4th to 8th teams goes to the Last Chance Qualifier
