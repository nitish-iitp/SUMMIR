Leone Stars Holds First Training in Liberia Ahead of Clash With Chad in AFCON Qualifiers
03 September, 2024
|

Leone Stars have completed their first training session at the SKD Practice Pitch in Pensville, Liberia, ahead of their 2025 AFCON Qualifiers match against Chad this Friday.

Sierra Leone’s head coach, Amidu Karim, recently unveiled a 25-man squad for the upcoming qualifiers against Chad and Zambia.

Leone Stars are gearing up for these crucial matches as they aim to secure their place in the tournament.
