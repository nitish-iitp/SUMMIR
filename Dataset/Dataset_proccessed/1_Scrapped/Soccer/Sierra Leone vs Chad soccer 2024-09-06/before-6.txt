 Leone Stars Complete First Training Session in Monrovia! 
Our Leone Stars have successfully wrapped up their first full training session at the SKD Stadium in Monrovia this evening! 
 The team is fully focused and ready to take on Chad in the upcoming 2025 AFCON Qualifiers match this Friday, September 6, 2024.
With the squad now complete, including our home-based and foreign-based players, the energy is high and the determination is stronger than ever. Let’s rally behind our boys as they represent Sierra Leone on the international stage!
Stay tuned for more updates as we prepare for the big match!
