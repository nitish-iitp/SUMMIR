Africa AFCON 2023: Nigeria, Guinea, Mali, and More Brace for Quarter-Final Showdowns

As the dust settles on the thrilling Round of 16 clashes in the 2023 Africa Cup of Nations, seven teams have secured their spots in the quarter-finals, with Morocco and South Africa set to determine the final entrant. Notable surprises include hosts Côte d’Ivoire overcoming defending champions Senegal in a penalty shootout, and Guinea securing a dramatic last-minute victory over 10-man Equatorial Guinea.

Cape Verde, buoyed by a late penalty, advances for the second time in their history. The quarter-finals promise more excitement, featuring matchups like Nigeria vs Angola and DR Congo vs Guinea.

In an unexpected upset, Egypt bows out to DR Congo in a penalty shootout. The stage is set for intense battles, and football fans are eagerly anticipating the unfolding drama in this prestigious tournament.

Full Quarter-Finals Fixtures:


Nigeria vs Angola
Date: Friday, February 2, 2024
Venue: Felix Houphouet-Boigny
Time: 6:00 pm
DR Congo vs Guinea
Date: Friday, February 2, 2024
Venue: Stade Olympique Alassane Ouattara
Time: 9:00 pm
Mali vs Cote d’Ivoire
Date: Saturday, February 3, 2024
Venue: Stade de Bouaké
Time: 6:00 pm
Cape Verde vs Morocco/South Africa
Date: Saturday, February 3, 2024
Venue: Stade Charles Konan Banny de Yamoussoukro
Time: 9:00 pm.
