Di qualifiers for di 2025 Africa Cup of Nations qualifiers don end.

We now know di 24 teams wey go take part for di tournament next year out of di 48 teams wey bin take part for dis campaign.

Confederation of African football divide di teams into 12 groups,.

Only di top two teams for di 11 groups wey no involve tournament hosts Morocco, qualify for di competition.

Di 35th edition of di ogbonge football competition go hold between 21 December and 18 January 2025.

Some of di big teams wey qualify include Nigeria, Algeria, Angola, Egypt, Senegal, Burkina Faso, Morocco, Cameroon and DR Congo, Equatorial Guinea and Ivory Coast.

Morocco dey prepare to host Afcon for di second time

Di North African Morocco nation dey prepare to host di continent ogbonge football tournament for di second time for dia history.

Di first time Morocco bin host di Africa Cup of Nations na 1988, 36-years afta di kontri don see ogbonge development for dia football.

For 1988, di tournament bin feature just eight teams and na Cameroon win am.

Di 2025 edition, 24 nations go take part, dis go reflect di growth of African football.

Many pipo dey tip key cities like Casablanca, Rabat, Marrakech, and Tangier to host di matches next year.

Tori be say di iconic Stade Mohammed V for Casablanca, wey bin host di 1988 final, fit play central role again.

Morocco hosting of Afcon 2025 go further confam di kontri as big force for football on and off di pitch.

Di nation successful bid to host di 2030 Fifa World Cup, jointly wit Spain and Portugal, show say dem get big dreams.
