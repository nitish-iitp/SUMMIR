Bermuda Team Selection - Concacaf Nations League Match 94 vs French Guiana
November 21, 2023

Our Gombey Warriors will step onto the pitch at Stade Pierre Allier at 3 PM and fight to finish at the top of their group. Their journey in the 2023/24 Concacaf Nations League has come with its challenges and today they will look to step up, perform, but most importantly enjoy this moment.




Make sure to set yourself a reminder and tune in live to see our Senior Men's National Team in action one last time this year as they represent Bermuda on the international stage.
