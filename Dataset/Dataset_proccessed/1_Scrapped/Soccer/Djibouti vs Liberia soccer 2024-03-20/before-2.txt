‘It was terrifying’: Two critically injured in multiple-alarm fire on Dalkeith Avenue

Ontario Fire Marshal says there were no working smoke alarms in home neighbours described as rooming house

Updated March 20, 2024 at 11:57 a.m.
March 19, 2024
3 min read
Save

Two people are in hospital in critical condition, including a woman who was initially without vital signs, after being pulled from a multiple-alarm fire at a house on Dalkeith Avenue.

Cathie Coward The Hamilton Spectator
By Nicole O’ReillyReporter, and Sebastian BronReporter

Two people remain hospitalized in critical condition after being pulled out of an engulfed east Hamilton home early Tuesday morning.

Emergency services responded to the two-and-a-half-storey residence at 8 Dalkeith Ave., between Ottawa Street North and Rosslyn Avenue North, around 1:15 a.m. for reports of a multiple-alarm structure fire.
