‘Age is just a number and I’ll go as long as I can’: Carol Burnett, 90, on her role in Apple TV’s Palm Royale

The comedy legend says she ‘hit the jackpot’ with her role as Palm Beach society doyenne Norma Dellacorte in the Apple TV series Palm Royale.

Updated March 20, 2024 at 11:45 a.m.
March 17, 2024
6 min read
Save

Carol Burnett plays Norma Dellacorte, a society maven in 1960s Palm Beach, Florida, in the Apple TV series “Palm Royale.” 

APPLE TV PLUS
By Debra YeoToronto Star

When you are a television legend of the stature of Carol Burnett, you don’t get out of bed for just any job.

In Apple TV’s “Palm Royale,” the 90-year-old found a gig that was not only worth her time but involved being in bed for the first few episodes.
