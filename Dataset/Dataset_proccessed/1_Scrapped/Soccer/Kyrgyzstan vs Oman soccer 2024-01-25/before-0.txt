AFC Asian Cup 2023: When to watch Oman vs Kyrgyzstan game?

Muscat : Oman's national football team will meet Kyrgyz national team on Thursday, January 25, 2024 at 7 pm. The match will be held in Qatari capital, Doha.

Oman News Agency (ONA), said in a statement :"Our first national football team will meet tomorrow with the Kyrgyz national team in its third match in the sixth group of the Asian Nations Cup, which is hosted by the Qatari capital, Doha, at exactly 7 pm MCT ."
