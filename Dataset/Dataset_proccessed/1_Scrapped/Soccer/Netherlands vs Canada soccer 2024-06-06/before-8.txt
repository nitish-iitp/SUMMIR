Another exciting international friendly clash to watch out for

Netherlands continue their final preparations for Euro 2024 with an international friendly against Canada on Thursday evening at De Kuip. For the Dutch, it allows them to sort out their formation, tactical tweaks, and planning before the UEFA Euro 2024 group fixture begins this June. Ronald Koeman is preparing to take the Netherlands into another tournament as one of the dark horses in a bid to end their 36-year wait for another major trophy. The Dutch have enough quality players to perform very well on the pitch against a good Canadian side.

These two nations have gone three decades without crossing paths, with the Netherlands running out 3-0 victors when they met in Toronto in 1994. Canada should treat this friendly fixture against the Dutch and the following match against France as a chance to garner some momentum by delivering strong performances, leading into the Copa America 2024. This will be an exciting and mouthwatering fixture for sure as both teams have quality players and both will give each other a tough challenge.

Players to Watch
Denzel Dumfries (Netherlands)

He is extremely active down the right-hand side, constantly charging up and down the pitch. Dumfries attacking output is arguably his greatest asset, he utilizes his strength, pace and athleticism to dispossess opposing attackers. Due to his pace and athletic frame, he is able to quickly close down and block off the passing lanes, subsequently increasing his chances of successfully dispossessing the opposition player.

Jonathan David (Canada)

He is a versatile player who can play in multiple positions, he can play as a Centre forward as well as an attacking midfielder and is very well known for his goalscoring ability. He is really good at finding small gaps which he exploits very well to attack the opposition.

He is also able to play short passes and combine with his teammates to attack the goal area, Jonathan is able to play best with his tight control and sharp interplay between teammates. Due his physicality he is also able to shrug off the defenders challenges and is a threat for the opposition on counter attacks.
