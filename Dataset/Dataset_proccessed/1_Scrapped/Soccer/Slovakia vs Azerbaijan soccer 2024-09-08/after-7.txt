LOBOTKA FEATURES IN WIN OVER AZERBAIJAN

Stanislav Lobotka played the full 90 minutes as Slovakia ran out 2-0 winners over Azerbaijan on Sunday in their UEFA Nations League clash.

The midfielder’s national team currently sit on six points from six in League C, Group 1, joint top with Sweden.
