The national football team of Georgia will face Albania today

The national football team of Georgia will face the football team of Albania in the second round of "League of Nations" today. The match will be held in Tirana and will start at 22:45 Tbilisi time.

Let us remind you that in the first match of the group, the Georgian national team defeated the Czech football team with a score of 4:1.

The Albanian national team also finished the first round match against Ukraine with a victory.

After the first round, the football teams of Georgia and Albania are leading in the group with 3-3 points.

The public screening of the away match of the UEFA Nations League group stage of the Georgian national team will be held in Tbilisi, on the First Republic Square.
