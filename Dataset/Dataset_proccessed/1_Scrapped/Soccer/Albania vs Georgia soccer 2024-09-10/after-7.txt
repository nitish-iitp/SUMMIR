KVARATSKHELIA’S GEORGIA BEAT ALBANIA

Khvicha Kvaratskhelia helped Georgia defeat Albania in a UEFA Nations League game on Tuesday.

The Napoli forward came off in the 75th minute, shortly after Giorgi Kochorashvili scored what proved to be the winning goal.
