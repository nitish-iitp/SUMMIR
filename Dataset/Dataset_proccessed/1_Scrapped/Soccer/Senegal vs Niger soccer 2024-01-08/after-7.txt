Stade Me Abdoulaye Wade

The Stade Me Abdoulaye Wade is a soccer stadium located in Diamniadio, Senegal, has a capacity of 50,000 fans and has natural grass. Following the letter addressed by the President of the Republic of Senegal, Macky Sall, to former President Abdoulaye Wade, informing him of his decision to give his name to the new large national stadium built in Diamniadio, Senegal, the Stade Me Abdoulaye Wade was inaugurated.
