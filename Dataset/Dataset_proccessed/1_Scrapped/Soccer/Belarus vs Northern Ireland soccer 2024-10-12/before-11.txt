Highlights: Sussex Central Rushes Past Cape Henlopen, 41-27
By Tim Nolan Oct 12, 2024 Updated Oct 12, 2024 
Play Video

Facebook

LEWES, Del. -- Sussex Central scored on every single offensive drive en route to a 41-27 win over Cape Henlopen on Friday night on the road. 

Malik Bell and Will Harmon powered the Golden Knights with five rushing scores. Isaiah Wooters also returned an onside kick for a touchdown. Dell Richards, Amari Jackson and Jayden Messick all scored for the Vikings.
