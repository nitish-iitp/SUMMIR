Third round

The top two from each second-round group will head into the third round, where 12 nations will be split into three groups of four. From there, the top team from each section will secure their spot at FIFA World Cup 26™, while the best two runners-up will head into the FIFA Play-Off Tournament, where they will join four other nations.
