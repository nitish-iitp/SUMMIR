On Friday, June 21 at 8:00 PM ET, Chile and Peru square off in Group A at the 2024 Copa America, the first game for each squad.

For more about this contest, including where and how to watch on Fox Sports 1, keep reading.

How to Watch Chile vs. Peru
Match Day: Friday, June 21, 2024
Match Time: 8:00 PM ET
Venue: AT&T Stadium
Location: Arlington, Texas
TV Channel: Fox Sports 1 – Watch FREE on Fubo
Watch the Copa America on Fubo!
Chile vs. Peru Insights
Offensively, Chile racked up 22 goals (1.7 per match) the past two years. Defensively, Peru conceded 15 goals (1.1 per match).
Peru scored 10 times in 14 matches the past two years, and Chile allowed 12 goals in 13 matches.
Chile outscored its opponents 22-12 during that time frame.
Peru had a negative goal differential during that time span, at -5.
Chile Leaders
In 15 matches for Sheffield United in the Premier League this past season, Ben Brereton had six goals and one assist.
Diego Valdes had five goals and two assists in 21 games for CF America (Liga MX Clausura) last season.
With Inter Milan in Serie A, Alexis Sanchez had two goals and five assists in 33 matches.
Erick Pulgar’s final stats for CR Flamengo RJ (Brasileiro) last season, in 26 matches, were two goals and three assists.
