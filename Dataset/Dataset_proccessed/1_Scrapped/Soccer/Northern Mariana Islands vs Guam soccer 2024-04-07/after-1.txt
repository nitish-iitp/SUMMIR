City sniffing new rules for vape shops
Tom Joyce tjoyce@mtairynews.com Apr 7, 2024 

With the increased prevalence of youth vaping, Mount Airy officials issued new rules to restrict sales to youth in April. Media Detective classes at the Mount Airy, Elkin, and Pilot Mountain libraries will education young people on how advertisers are choosing to target younger audiences.

Tom Joyce | The News

Mount Airy officials are considering a new set of measures to better manage the growth of tobacco, vape and hemp stores in town.

“We don’t really have any regulations,” city Planning Director Andy Goodall said Thursday night of what is a relatively recent addition to the local business scene regarding the types of products such entities sell.
