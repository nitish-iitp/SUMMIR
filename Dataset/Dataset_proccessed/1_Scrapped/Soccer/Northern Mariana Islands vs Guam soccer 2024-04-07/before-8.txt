High school girls wrestling: Reno hardware returning to La Grande
By JOHN BRAESE EasternOregonSports.com Apr 7, 2024 Updated Apr 8, 2024  0
Facebook
Twitter
Email

The group representing La Grande's girls wrestling program display their medals upon completion of the Reno Worlds tournament on April 6, 2024.

RENO, Nevada — While many fans were taking in a current unbeaten La Grande softball game or the track and field team competing in Hermiston, others were in Reno, Nevada, watching six Tigers hang medals on their necks after taking part in the Reno Worlds.

The tournament features the best of female high school wrestling from 45 states meeting in Reno starting April 4 with three days of action. Over 9,000 wrestlers were present, covering all the age groups in attendance.
