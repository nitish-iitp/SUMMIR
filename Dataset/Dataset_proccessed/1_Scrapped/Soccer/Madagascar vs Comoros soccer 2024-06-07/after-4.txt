Watch Black Stars' first training at Baba Yara Stadium ahead of CAR game

Black Stars

The Black Stars held a recovery session at the Baba Yara Sports Stadium on Friday, June 7, 2024, to reshape after their win over Mali.

Ghana beat Mali 2-1 in Bamako to get back to winning ways and go tied with group leaders Comoros and Madagascar.

The technical team led by head coach Otto Addo led through the session that was opened to the public.

Players who had little or no minutes during the Mali game had an intense routine while the players who featured in the game had light and less demanding routines.

The team will train on Saturday and Sunday, as they continue preparations to face the Central African Republic on matchday 4 of the 2026 FIFA World Cup qualifiers.

Ghana sit third in the group, tied with Comoros and Madagascar at 6 points. Mali are fourth with 4 points, followed by the Central African Republic, and Chad with 3, and 0 points, respectively.
