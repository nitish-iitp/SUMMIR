CANADA
Bus carrying Canadian tourists crashes in Cuba leaving one dead, 26 injured

Air Transat said that one passenger on the bus died, and “out of our passengers, 26 were hospitalized, with only one remaining under preventative observation.”

Updated June 4, 2024 at 1:16 p.m.
June 3, 2024
1 min read
Save

The airline confirmed that a bus from one of their transport partners, crashed into a vehicle that lost control, causing the bus to overturn.

iStock/Getty Images
By Daniel RamosStaff Reporter

A bus carrying Canadian tourists was involved in a collision in Cuba near Santa Clara Airport on Sunday that left one dead and 26 injured, Air Transat said.

The airline confirmed that a car crashed into a bus from one of their transport partners, carrying 38 people who were to board flight TS715 to Montréal, causing the bus to overturn.
