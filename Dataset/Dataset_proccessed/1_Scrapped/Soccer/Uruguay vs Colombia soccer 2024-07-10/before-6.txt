Uruguay vs Colombia Predicted Lineups Preview

Uruguay and Colombia are set to face off in an exciting semi-final match at the Copa America 2024. The semi-final match between the two teams is scheduled for Wednesday night at the Bank of America Stadium in Charlotte, North Carolina.

Marcelo Bielsa’s team emerged victorious over Brazil in a thrilling quarter-final match, securing their spot in the semi-finals of the Copa America 2024. Meanwhile, Colombia dominated Panama with a resounding 5-0 victory.

Uruguay had an impressive performance in their four matches, scoring nine goals and keeping three clean sheets without needing a penalty shootout. They only allowed one goal through direct play. Meanwhile, Colombia has been quite impressive in their recent matches, scoring 11 goals and only conceding two.

Their defence has been solid, keeping two clean sheets. James Rodriguez has been in top form, contributing one goal and an impressive five assists in just four matches. Out of their 45 encounters in various competitions, Uruguay emerged as the stronger team against Colombia, securing 20 victories compared to Colombia’s 14 wins, with 11 matches resulting in draws.

Over their past 10 encounters, Uruguay emerged victorious in four matches, while Colombia secured three wins, with three games ending in a draw. Uruguay and Colombia have had an equal number of victories in major competitions like the FIFA World Cup and Copa America finals, with 6 wins each.

They have also played a draw against each other. In a thrilling match, Colombia emerged victorious over Uruguay in the quarterfinal of the Copa America 2023, securing their spot in the next round.
