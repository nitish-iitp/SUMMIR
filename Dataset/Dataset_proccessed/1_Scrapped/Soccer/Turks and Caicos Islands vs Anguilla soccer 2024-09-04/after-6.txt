The Westmoreland girls soccer team scored two goals over a 12-minute span late in the second half Wednesday to secure a 3-1 victory non-league over visiting Clinton. It was the first game of the 2024 season for both teams. Westmoreland is a member of the Center State Conference Division III while Clinton is in CSC Division I.
