Relatives and friends are advised of the passing of Esther Rose Benjamin Holloman on Feb. 14, 2024.

Esther Rose Benjamin Holloman affectionately known as “Rose” was born on Sept. 4, 1948, on the island of St. Kitts, to the late Eltruda Ryan, stepfather John Ryan and Eddie Williams. Esther was a great warrior and intercessor, who loved the Lord. She was preceded in death by her first husband, Eric A. Benjamin; second husband, Roy Holloman; sister, Almaria “Polly” Hendrickson; and nephew, Bernard Canning.
