STANFIELD — Karver Wilkins, Caden George and Cameron Proudfoot combined on a shutout Tuesday, March 26, as Heppner beat Umatilla 10-0 at the Stanfield Tournament.

“They threw a lot of strikeouts today, which was good, and they cut down on their errors,” Heppner coach Tim Wilkins said. “We played six innings and were at 60 pitches.”
