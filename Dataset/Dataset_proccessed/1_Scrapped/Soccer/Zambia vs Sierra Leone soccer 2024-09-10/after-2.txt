Sierra Leone’s Leone Stars suffered a narrow 3-2 defeat against Zambia in a gripping AFCON Cup Qualifier held on September 10, 2024, at the Levy Mwanawasa Stadium in Zambia.

The match was filled with intense action, as both teams battled hard for the crucial win.

Zambia opened the scoring early, with Kennedy Musonda finding the back of the net, giving the home side a 1-0 lead. Sierra Leone quickly responded, as Augustus Kargbo netted an equalizer, bringing the score to 1-1 before halftime.
