Portugal will meet the Czech Republic in the 2024 UEFA European Championship group stage, on June 18 at 3:00 PM ET. It’ll be the tournament opener for both teams.

See below as we dig deep into all of the info you need to know about this contest, including how to watch on FOX.

Match Time: 3:00 PM ET
Venue: Red Bull Arena
Location: Leipzig, Germany

Portugal vs. Czech Republic Insights
In the past two years of international play, Portugal notched 49 goals in 15 games, and the Czech Republic conceded 12 in 14 games.
The Czech Republic scored 30 times in 14 matches the past two years. On the flip side, Portugal conceded 10 goals in 15 international fixtures.
Portugal’s goal differential was +39 during that time span.
The Czech Republic outscored its opponents during that time span 30-12.
Portugal Leaders
In 34 Serie A matches this past season, Rafael Leao posted nine goals and 10 assists for AC Milan.
Bruno Fernandes, in 35 games for Manchester United (Premier League) last season, had 10 goals and eight assists.
With Premier League club Manchester City, Bernardo Silva posted six goals and nine assists in 35 appearances.
Diogo Jota’s final numbers for Liverpool FC (Premier League) last season, in 22 matches, were 10 goals and four assists.
