Qatar defeat Kyrgyzstan in AFC Asian Qualifiers - Road To 2026 third round match

Qatar and Kyrgyzstan took on each other at the Al Thumama Stadium on 10 October 2024, where they played the AFC Asian Qualifiers - Road To 2026 third round match.

Qatar won with a score of 3-1.

The first goal was scored for Qatar in the first half of the match by Almoez Ali in the 39th minute. The second goal was an own goal scored by Kygryz player Tamirlan Kozubaev in the 63rd minute. Ibrahim Al Hassan scored Qatar's third goal in the 81st minute.

Alimardon Shukurov scored Kyrgyzstan's only goal for the match in the 87th minute.
