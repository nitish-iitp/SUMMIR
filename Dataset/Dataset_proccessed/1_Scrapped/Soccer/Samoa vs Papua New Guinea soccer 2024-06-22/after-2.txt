Police release identity of bicyclist who died in hit-and-run accident
By STACY FAISON/The Lufkin Daily News Jun 22, 2024 Updated Jun 22, 2024 
Facebook

The Lufkin Police Department has released the identity of a bicyclist who was reportedly struck by two vehicles and killed late June 7 in a hit-and-run accident on North Timberland Drive.

Darryl W. Thorton, 58, died at the scene of the accident that occurred around 10:43 p.m. on an unlighted stretch of roadway, the report states. His address is not listed in the report.
