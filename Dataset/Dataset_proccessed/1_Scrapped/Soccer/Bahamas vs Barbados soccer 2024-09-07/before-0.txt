NASSAU, BAHAMAS – The Bahamas Football Association (BFA) is proud to announce the roster for the Bahamas Men’s National Soccer Team, known as the Junkanoo Boyz, as they prepare to compete in the upcoming Concacaf Nations League matches.

The team is fully focused on representing The Bahamas with determination and skill as they face their upcoming opponents.


Match Schedule:
September 4, 2024
Match: US Virgin Islands vs Bahamas
City, Country: Saint Croix, US Virgin Islands
Stadium: Bethlehem Soccer Complex
Kickoff Time: 6:00 PM EST
September 7, 2024
Match: Bahamas vs Barbados
City, Country: Saint Croix, US Virgin Islands
Stadium: Bethlehem Soccer Complex
Kickoff Time: 3:00 PM EST
Due to renovations at the Thomas A. Robinson National Stadium, the team will play its “home” game against Barbados in Saint Croix, US Virgin Islands. However, the Junkanoo Boyz are fully focused on delivering a strong performance, no matter the venue.

The Bahamas Football Association encourages all Bahamians to support our team as they compete in the Concacaf Nations League. Let’s rally behind the Junkanoo Boyz and show our national pride as they represent us on the international stage.
For match updates and more information, fans can visit www.concacaf.com/nations-league/schedule-results.
