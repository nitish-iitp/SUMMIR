Summit's Jasmine Pedersen wrestling her best before state
By BRIAN RATHBONE The Bulletin Feb 27, 2020 Updated Apr 3, 2021  0

Summit’s Jasmine Pedersen is the first female wrestler at the school to qualify for the state wrestling meet.

Ryan Brennecke/The Bulletin

The bad news, Friday and Saturday at the state championships in Portland, will be the end of Jasmine Pedersen’s wrestling career at Summit High School.

The good news is that Pedersen qualified for state in her final year and is wrestling the best that she ever has during her four years with the Storm.
