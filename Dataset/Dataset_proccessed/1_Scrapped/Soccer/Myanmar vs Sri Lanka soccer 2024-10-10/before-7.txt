Donald C. Stubbs, 89, of Woonsocket, passed away peacefully on Oct. 4, 2024, in St. Antoine Residence, North Smithfield.

He was the husband of Sarah "Sally" (Andrews) Stubbs of Woonsocket and the late Lorraine (Thivierge) Stubbs. Born in Providence, he was the son of the late Edward and Margaret (Clark) Stubbs.
