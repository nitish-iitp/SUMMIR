England women's squads set for April internationals

Written by:

Nicholas Veevers

SHARE

All of the details around games and squad announcements ahead of the women's international window in April
The England women’s teams are back in action in April, with three squads playing fixtures during the next international window.

Sarina Wiegman’s Lionesses kick-off their new UEFA Women’s EURO 2025 qualifying campaign when they host Sweden at Wembley Stadium on Friday 5 April before travelling to face Republic of Ireland in Dublin four days later.

There’s a home game for the WU23s who also host Sweden, while our WU19s head to Portugal for their final UEFA Women’s U19 EURO qualifying round.

Find out more details about all of the games and when the respective squads will be announced below.
