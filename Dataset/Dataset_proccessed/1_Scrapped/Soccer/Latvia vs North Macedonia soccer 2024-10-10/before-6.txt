NATIONAL TEAM | 8 GRASSHOPPERS GET A CALL UP

During the national team break, eighth Grasshoppers are travelling with their respective national teams.

The plan for the international Grasshoppers is as follows: 

Kristers Tobers | Latvian national team, Nations League

Thursday, 10 October 2024	Latvia - North Macedonia	Riga, Latvia
Sunday, 13 October 2024	Faroe Islands - Latvia	Tórshavn, Faroe Islands

Maksim Paskotši | Estonian national team, Nations League

Friday, 11 October 2024	Estonia - Azerbaijan	Tallinn, Estonia
Monday, 14 October 2024	Estonia - Sweden	Tallinn, Estonia

Mathieu Choinière | Canadian national team, Friendly games

Tuesday, 15 October 2024	Canada - Panama	Toronto, Canada

Amir Abrashi | Albanian national team, Nations League

Friday, 11 October 2024	Czech Republic - Albania	Prague, Czech Republic
Monday, 14 October 2024	Georgia - Albania	Tbilisi, Georgia

Michael Kempter | Philippine national team, King's Cup

Friday, 11 October 2024	Thailand - Philippines	Songkhla, Thailand
Monday, 14 October 2024	Philippines - Syira/Tajikistan	Songkhla, Thailand

Ayumu Seko | Japan national team, World Cup qualifiers

Thursday, 10 October 2024	Japan - Saudi Arabia	Jeddah, Saudi Arabia
Tuesday, 15 October 2024	Australia - Japan	Tokyo, Japan

Nikolas Muci | Swiss U21 national team, European Championship qualification

Friday, 11 October 2024	Switzerland - Finland	Lucerne, Switzerland
Tuesday, 15 October 2024	Romania - Switzerland	Bucharest, Romania

Tim Meyer | Swiss U20 national team, Friendly games

Friday, 11 October 2024	Switzerland - Ghana	Solothurn, Switzerland
Tuesday, 15 October 2024	Romania - Switzerland	Targoviste, Romania

Awer Mabil (Australia) is also on the standby list.
