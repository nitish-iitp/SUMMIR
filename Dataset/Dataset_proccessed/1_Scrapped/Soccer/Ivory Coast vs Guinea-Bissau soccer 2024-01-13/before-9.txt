All don set for di 34th edition of African Cup of Nations wey be di biggest football tournament for Africa wia 24 teams go compete wit each oda.

Na Cote D'ivoire go host dis Afcon tournament wey go begin for 13 January 2024 and plenti pipo still dey worry as to how dem go fit watch all di games.

For di tournament, na total of 52 matches dem go play wia 36 of di games go happun for group statges bifor di knockout stage go begin for 27 January.

Surprisngly, since Afcon begin in 1957, no newcomer dey for dis year edition. Dis mean say all di participating teams wey go face diasef na familiar kontris.

Till date, Egypt still remain di king of Afcon sake of say na only dem don win di trophy seven times, even though na since 2010 dem win di trophy last.

How to watch Afcon games for Nigeria?

Nigeria

NTA

Di Nigerian television Authority (NTA) on Wednesday announce say dem don sign new memorandum of understanding wit one Afro Sports wey go help dem transmit di 52 matches for di Afcon in Cote D'ivoire.

NTA na goment owned national media and na dem be di largest local TV network for NIgeria.

Wit di latest development, pipo wey get NTA channel fit enjoy all di Afcon games.

Multichoice, wey be owners of Digital Satellite Television (DStv), on Wednesday announce say SuperSports go show all 52 matches for di 2023 African Cup of Nations wey dey shelle for Cote d’Ivoire.

Multichoice bin tok last week say dem no go show di tournament “sake of say di channel bin no gbab di rights to broadcast di tournament.”

“However, Afcon 2023 games go dey available through our partner broadcast stations on Free to Air (FTA) channels."

How African kontris fit watch Afcon
Startimes

Since Supersport no dey show di games at Afcon, Startimes don confam say dem go show all di games for di tournament.

Startimes dey Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda and many oda African kontris. Dem get sports channel wey include Sports Premium, Sports Life and Beta Sports wia dem go broadcast live matches of di African Cup of Nations.

New World TV

CAF bin also sign a new deal wit New World Tv to make dem broadcast di Afcon game to sub-saharan Africa.

Di deal na di biggest investment wey CAF don sign so far to allow free to air and pay-TV rights across Africa. Na about $81 million wey go first last for three-year term.

Pipo wey get access to New World Tv fit watch all di Afcon games for di comfor of dia home.

beIN Sports

Doz wey dey northen Africa fit watch Afcon games ontop beIN Sports.

Pipo for Europe go fit watch di games ontop Sky Sports, doz for di United States go get di games ontop beIN Sports and doz for Canada go find di matches for FuboTV.

Even doz wey dey live for Asian kontris, Australia and New Zealand no go dey left behind as beIN Sports go still dey serve dem while pipo for India go fit watch ontop FanCode.

Meanwhile, some tori still dey fly say Supersports fit try renegitiate wit CAF so dem go fit broadcast di Afcon games.

Afcon 2023 schedule and kick-off times

Afta di opening match, dem go play at least two games each day during group stage wey go run until 24 January.

Di games go occur by 14:00, 17:00 and 20:00 (all times GMT) during di group stages, and di two last kick-off times dem go use am for di final round of group games and di knockout stages, wey go begin on Saturday, 27 January.
