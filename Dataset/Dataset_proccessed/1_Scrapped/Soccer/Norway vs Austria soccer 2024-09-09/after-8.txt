Norway vs Austria Player Ratings: Haaland Secures Late 2-1 Victory
Norway vs Austria
UEFA Nations League

Norway took on Austria at home in the Nations League tonight in an exciting game that finished 2-1 to Norway. Felix Horn Myhre gave Norway an early lead and Marcel Sabitzer equalised later in the first half for Austria. Erling Haaland then won it for Norway late in the game.
