Early goal propels Grant girls soccer past Wilsonville in matchup of two reigning state champions
Austin White Sep 10, 2024 Updated Sep 16, 2024

In a matchup of two state champions, it was Grant High School girls soccer coming out on top in a 2-0 win over Wilsonville on Tuesday, Sept. 10 at Delta Park.

The Generals, reigning 6A state champions, scored two minutes into the match to put the Wildcast, winners of three consecutive 5A state titles, on their heels from the get go.
