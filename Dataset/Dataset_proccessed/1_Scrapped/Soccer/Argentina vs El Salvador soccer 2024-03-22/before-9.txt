Violet “Vi” Barton, 93, passed away Saturday, March 16, 2024 at St. Francis Nursing Home, Breckenridge, Minnesota.

Visitation will be from 1-3 p.m. Friday, March 22, 2024, followed by the 3 p.m. funeral service, at United Church of Christ, Wahpeton, North Dakota. Burial will be at Fairview Cemetery, Wahpeton.

To plant a tree in memory of Violet Barton as a living tribute, please visit Tribute Store.
