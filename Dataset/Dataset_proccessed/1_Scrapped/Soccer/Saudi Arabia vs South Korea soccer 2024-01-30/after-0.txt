S. Korea beat Saudi Arabia in shootout to reach quarterfinals

South Korea's goalkeeper Jo Hyeon-woo rejoices after saving Saudi Arabia's fourth spot kick in an Asian Cup round of 16 football match at Education City Stadium in Al Rayyan, Qatar, on Jan. 30, 2024. South Korea won 4-2 in a penalty shootout to advance to the quarterfinals after the game finished 1-1. (Yonhap)
