Amazon returns now available at all Canadian Staples stores

Eligible Amazon items can be dropped off label-free, box-free at 298 stores.

Updated June 20, 2024 at 3:54 p.m.
June 20, 2024
1 min read
Save

Amazon purchases can now be returned through Staples Canada stores.

Paul Forsyth Metroland file photo
By Dominik KurekOnline Editor Insidehaltoncom

You can now return your Amazon purchases label-free, box-free at all Canadian Staples stores, Amazon and Staples Canada announced.

The service has been available at Staples stores in the U.S. for some time, but this is the first time it is available Canada-wide.
