RACHEL GAMBLE

 GAMBLE, Rachel Lauren, age 32, passed on June 7, 2024. She was preceded in death by her father, Jerry Gamble and her grandfather, Joe Myers. She is survived by her mother, Jo Ann Myers; grandmother, Yvonne Capley Myers; and a special uncle, cousins and friends. She will always be remembered and loved. No services are planned at this time.
