Wayne F Baillargeon of Gig Harbor, Washington, formerly of New Richmond, Wisconsin passed away March 21, 2024, after a short battle with cancer.  

Wayne was born in Amery, WI on December 11, 1950. Growing up the 6th of 9 siblings on a dairy farm, taughtWayne the value of family and hard work. He started school in a two-room schoolhouse in Wanderoos, WI, graduating from Amery high school in 1969. After graduating he enrolled in UW River Falls, WI, but went to serve his country in the Air Force enlisting in K-9 security traveling the world. His last base was Spokane, WA where he met the love of his life, his wife of 46 years Sonnie.
