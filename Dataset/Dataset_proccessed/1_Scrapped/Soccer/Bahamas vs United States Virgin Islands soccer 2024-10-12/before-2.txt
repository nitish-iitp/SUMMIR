Unicorns gallop past Chargers 49-34

Coming off a bye week, New Braunfels shook off any potential rust by defeating Boerne-Champion 49-34 at home Friday.

Both squads traded consecutive blows in the first half, with the Unicorns trailing 28-21, but their potent rushing attack and defensive adjustments earned them a victory.
