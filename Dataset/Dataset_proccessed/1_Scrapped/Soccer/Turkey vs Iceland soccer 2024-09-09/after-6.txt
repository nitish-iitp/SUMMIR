Türkiye 3-1 Iceland

Türkiye National Team rose on top of UEFA Nations League B Group 4 by defeating Iceland 3-1 in Matchday 2. The match was played at Gürsel Aksel Stadium in Izmir.

Turkish Football Federation President İbrahim Ethem Hacıosmanoğlu, Iceland Football Association President Thorvaldur Örlygsson and TFF Board Members followed the match from the stands.

In the other match of Group 4, Wales defeated Montenegro 2-1 away. At the end of Matchday 2, Türkiye National Team ranked up in 1st place in UEFA Nations League B Group 4. 

Stadium: Gürsel Aksel

Referees: Enea Jorgji, Denis Rexha, Ridiger Çokaj (Albania)

Türkiye: Mert Günok, Mert Müldür (73. min. - Zeki Çelik), Merih Demiral, Abdülkerim Bardakcı, Eren Elmalı, İrfan Can Kahveci (73. min. - Kenan Yıldız), Hakan Çalhanoğlu (46. min. - Orkun Kökçü), İsmail Yüksek (87. min. - Kaan Ayhan), Kerem Aktürkoğlu, Arda Güler, Umut Nayir (81. min. - Okay Yokuşlu)

Iceland: Valdimarsson, Palsson (59. min. - Fridriksson), Gretarsson, Hermannsson, Finnsson, Anderson (46. min. - Willumsson), Thordarson, Gudmundsson, Thorsteinsson (59. min. - Traustason), Gudjohnsen, Sigurdsson (59. min. - Oskarsson)

Goals: Kerem Aktürkoğlu (2., 52. and 88. mins.) (Türkiye), Palsson (37. min.) (Iceland)

Yellow Cards: Gretarsson (44. min.), Thordarson (73. min.) (Iceland), İrfan Can Kahveci (86. min.) (from the bench) (Türkiye)
