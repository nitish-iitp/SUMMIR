Watch the action from selected second round matches as AFC FIFA World Cup 26 qualifying heats up.

Stream and watch selected matches from AFC FIFA World Cup qualifying

Matches available to stream live for free across FIFA+ in selected territories

Click here to visit the streaming page

The race to qualify for FIFA World Cup 26™ is heating up across Asia, with a selection of matches from the second-round finale set to be streamed live and free on FIFA+.

With a host of qualification positions still up for grabs, supporters in selected territories across the globe can stream and watch for free, as the picture of who might travel to Canada, Mexico and the USA becomes clearer.
