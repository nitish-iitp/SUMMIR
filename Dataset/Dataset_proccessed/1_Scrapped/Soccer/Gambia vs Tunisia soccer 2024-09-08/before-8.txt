Match Statistics Report: Comoros 1-1 Gambia

Match Overview:
- Final Score: Comoros 1-1 Gambia
Gambia's Performance:
- Ball Possession: 65%
- Total Goal Attempts: 14
  - Shots on Goal: 4
  - Shots off Goal: 9
- Corner Kicks: 4
- Total Passes: 570
  - Completed Passes: 484
- Total Attacks: 115
Key Moment:
- Goal Scorer: Musa Barrow
  - Goal Timing: 45+1 minute (First Half)
Musa Barrow continued his excellent form by scoring in first-half stoppage time, to level the game  just before the break. Despite dominating possession with 65%, The Scorpions were unable to extend their lead, against Comoros. The Gambia’s 14 goal attempts highlighted their attacking intent, though they were left frustrated with just 4 shots on target.
With this draw, The Gambia remains focused on their next challenge, a crucial match against Tunisia on 8 September.
