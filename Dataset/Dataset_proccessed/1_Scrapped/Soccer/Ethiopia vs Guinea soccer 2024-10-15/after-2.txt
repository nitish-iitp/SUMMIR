Ethiopia

Ethiopia have been facing challenges in their recent games. They failed to win a single game in their last five games. They drew three games and lost two, conceding a total of seven goals. In their last matches, they conceded four goals, and they only managed to get a consolation goal after they conceded four. They sit 4th on the table with 1 point in 3 games, scoring 1 goal and conceding 6.


Guinea

Guinea played a 4-1 win in their last game against Ethiopia. Serhou Guirassy scored first, putting Guinea ahead as he converted a penalty in the early 18th minutes and scored again in the 37th and 45+2nd minutes, completing a hattrick. Seydouba Cisse added one to the lead to make it four goals, but Canaan Markneh scored a consolation goal in the 53rd minute for Ethiopia. Guinea have won three games and lost three in their last six games. They sit 3rd on the table with 3 points in 3 games, scoring five goals and conceding 4.


H2H

Both sides only met six times till date from 1976. Guinea won all the matches they played together, and the last match ended in 4-1.


Expert Betting Tips

Guinea have a great history playing against Ethiopia, so they are expected to win this game. However, both teams are expected to score in this game as they emerged with many goals in their last two matches.
