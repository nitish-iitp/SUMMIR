BLOG: Harambee Stars secure maiden win in 2025 AFCON qualifiers against Namibia

Kenya kicked off their campaign with a barren draw against Zimbabwe in a home match staged at the Nelson Mandela Stadium in Kampala, Uganda.

IMRAN OTIENO

email

Harambee Stars will be looking to secure their maiden win in the 2025 Africa Cup of Nations qualifiers when they take on Namibia at the Orlando Stadium in Johannesburg, South Africa.

The game which is slated for a 7 PM (EAT) kick-off on Tuesday 10 September is being staged down south with Namibia also sharing Kenya's situation of lacking a CAF-approved stadium.
