Davis High marks joyous graduation

The Davis Senior High School Class of 2024 held their graduation on Friday, June 7 at the Ron & Mary Brown Stadium at Halden Field. It was the high school's 97th commencement ceremony.

Processional music performed by combined orchestras directed by Thomas Slabaugh escorted the graduates to their seats. Shortly after, the ceremonial performance of The Star-Spangled Banner by the Jazz and Madrigal Choirs, directed by Natascha Bach, followed.
