VI & St Kitts to clash in CONCACAF Nations League Oct 9
October 6th, 2024 | Tags: Concacaf Nations League VI St. Kitts Warner Park football match
Email this Article
Virgin Islands' Luka Chalwell (right) presses forward with Ricardo Rivera of Puerto Rico in pursuit during the 2022 CONCACAF Nations League. Chalwell is expected to feature when the Virgin Islands (VI) Men's Senior National Team play St Kitts and Nevis on Wednesday, October 9, 2024. Photo: CONCACAF
The Virgin Islands have two tough matches in the coming days. Photo: Facebook
BASSETERRE, St Kitts & Nevis- The Virgin Islands (VI) Men's Senior National Team will be back in action for the CONCACAF Nations League on Wednesday, October 9, 2024.

The Virgin Islands will be clashing with St Kitts and Nevis from 3:30 PM (local time) at Warner Park Football Stadium, Basseterre, St Kitts & Nevis.

The two teams last met on September 10, 2024, at the Truman Bodden Sports Complex in the Cayman Islands. St Kitts and Nevis won 2-nil.

The VI will then play Cayman Islands at the same venue on October 12.
