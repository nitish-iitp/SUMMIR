AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™ trophy & mascot photo experience
Event Details:
Location: Different locations across Doha
Date: 4 January 2024 - 13 January 2024
Time: 04:00 pm - 10:00 pm
Price:
No tickets needed - It's free!
VISIT WEBSITE
GET DIRECTIONS HERE

The AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™ trophy and mascots are going on a tour around Doha!

Enjoy an opportunity to take a photo and make memories with the AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™ trophy and the mascot family which includes Saboog, Tmbki, Freha, Zkriti and Traeneh.

Learn more about the mascots here - Official mascots for the AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™ unveiled

Details:
Msheireb Downtown Doha: 4 January 2024 - 4 pm to 8 pm
The Pearl Island: 5 January 2024 - 4 pm to 8 pm
Souq Waqif: 6 January 2024 - 4 pm to 8 pm
Al Hazm Mall: 7 January 2024 - 6 pm to 10 pm
West Walk: 8 January 2024 - 4 pm to 8 pm
City Center Mall: 11 January 2024 - 4 pm to 8 pm
Doha Festival City: 12 January 2024 - 4 pm to 8 pm
Expo 2023 Doha: 13 January 2024 - 4 pm to 8 pm
