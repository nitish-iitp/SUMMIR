Chipolopolo chase second victory
 November 21, 2023
 1:56 pm

ROBINSON KUNDA

Ndola

ZAMBIA will tonight seek making it two wins in a row when they face Niger in a 2026 FIFA World Cup Group E qualifier in Marrakech, Morocco, with skipper Lubambo Musonda saying he has recovered from an injury that saw him miss the clash against Congo-Brazzaville.

Musonda said in an interview yesterday that he is ready to hit the ground running at Stade de Marrakech.

“I missed the first game but I have recovered and I am ready. Morale in camp is high and we are all looking forward to the game against Niger,” he said.
