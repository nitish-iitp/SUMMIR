Iran beats Qatar in the fourth round of the AFC Asian Qualifiers

Qatar played against Iran in the fourth round of the AFC Asian Qualifiers on 15 October 2024 at the Rashid Stadium in Dubai, UAE. The match kicked off at 7 pm Doha time.

Qatar's striker Almoez Ali scored an early goal in the 17th minute of the match, putting Qatar's lead at 0-1.

Shortly before halftime, Iran's Sardar Azmoun equalized the score with a goal in the 42nd minute, bringing the score to 1-1 by halftime.

Iran started the match's second half strong, with another goal from Sardar Azmoun in the 48th minute, taking Iran's lead to 2-1.

In the 65th minute, Iran's Mohammad Mohebi scored a goal, bringing the score to 3-1. Mohebi scored another goal in the 90+8th minute, securing the win at 4-1.

Iran won with a score of 4-1 at full-time.
