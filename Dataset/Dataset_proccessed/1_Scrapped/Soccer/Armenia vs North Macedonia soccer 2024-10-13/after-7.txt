Nations League: Armenia 0-2 North Macedonia
 Siranush Ghazanchyan 
Send an email
October 13, 2024, 22:06
 Less than a minute

Armenia weredefeated by North Macedonia 0-2 in Group G of the Nations League.

Bojan Miovski opened the score in the 72nd minute, Isnik Alimni made it 0-2 in the 85th minute.

Armenia currently sits second in Group G with 4 points, North Macedonia tops the group with 10 points.
