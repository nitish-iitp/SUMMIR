Morocco Arrive in Ivory Coast Ahead of Clash With Leone Stars
08 January, 2024
|

The Morocco National  Male Football Team has arrived in Ivory Coast ahead of their Friendly match encounter with Leone Stars on 11th January 2024 at the Laurent Pokou Stadium.

The Atlas Lion delegation Jet down in Abidjan with their National Carrier  Air Maroc. The said match  is a strategic move to enhance  Morocco’s Preparations for the upcoming  African Cup of Nations hosted in Ivory Coast.
