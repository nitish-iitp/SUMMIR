Morocco Thrash Leone Stars In Friendly Match
11 January, 2024
|

Morocco secured a 3-1 victory over Sierra Leone on Thursday at the San Pedro stadium in Cote d’Ivoire as the Atlas Lions concluded their preparations for the Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) set to start on 13 January.

Abu Komeh Musa, a local player for BO Rangers in Sierra Leone, scored the lone  goal against Morocco.

Sevilla striker Youssef En-Nesyri canceled Sierra Leone’s early lead by equalizing for the Atlas Lions in the 30th minute.Five minutes later, winger Sofiane Boufal doubled the lead for Morocco.
