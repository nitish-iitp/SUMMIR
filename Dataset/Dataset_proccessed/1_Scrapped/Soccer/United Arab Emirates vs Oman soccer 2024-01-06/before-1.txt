Charlestonians said goodbye to 2023 and hello to 2024 in different ways this holiday weekend in the Kanawha Valley.

At the Clay Center for the Arts and Sciences on Saturday, hundreds of children and their families saw science exhibits, popped balloons and drank apple juice at the stroke of noon as part of the center’s annual Noon Year’s Eve celebration.
