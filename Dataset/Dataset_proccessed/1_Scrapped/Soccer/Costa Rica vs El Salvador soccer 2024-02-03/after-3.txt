Opinion | Sidney Crosby supplied Canada’s iconic Olympic moment 14 years ago. Could Sid the Kid be an Olympian at 38?
Updated Feb. 5, 2024 at 6:35 p.m.
Feb. 3, 2024
4 min read
Save

Sidney Crosby is still the Pittsburgh Penguins’ best player, 19 years into his NHL career.

Gene J. Puskar / The Associated Press
By Rosie DiMannoStar Columnist

Rosie DiManno is a Toronto-based columnist covering sports and current affairs for the Star. Follow her on Twitter: @rdimanno.

Sid the perennial Kid will be 38 years old when the Milano-Cortina Games roll around with the National Hockey League back in the Olympic fold.

Will he be on that roster?
