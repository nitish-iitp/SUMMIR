How to Say the Countries of the World in French

World Geography and French in One Simple Language Lesson

PRINT 
STEEX / Getty Images
By ThoughtCo Team
Updated on September 03, 2024

Learning the French names for countries is relatively easy if you are already familiar with the names in English. In most instances, the translation is as simple as attaching something like -ique or -ie to the end. That means the list below should be an extremely easy French lesson that students of any level can master.

Les Pays en Français

Here is a list of virtually all the countries in the world, organized alphabetically in English and including the French pronunciation. You will need to know how to speak about various countries as you study geography in French.

Remember to use a definite article (the "the," such as le or la) for countries. Some countries do not have a definite article because they are islands. Articles are normally not used with islands.

You must also know the country's gender to use it in a preposition. Nearly all countries that end in -e are feminine, and the rest are masculine.

There are just a few exceptions:

le Belize
le Cambodge
le Mexique
le Mozambique
le Zaïre
le Zimbabwe

In the cases above, and for countries that use l' as the definitive article, the gender is indicated next to the name.
