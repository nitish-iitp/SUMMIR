Di group stage of di Afcon 2023 finally end on Wednesday night afta all di teams complete dia matches.

Di group games bin produce plenty surprises wey shock many pipo especially as kontris wey be favourites to win di tournament struggle wit lesser teams.

Afta all di games. Some kontris don comot while odas get luck to qualify to di next round sake of di new structure wey Caf introduce for di tournament.

Sake of dis, kontris like host, Cote d'Ivoire, Guinea, Mauritania, and Namibia wey carry third for dia groups eventually qualify.

All di four kontris qualify as best losers – Caf use points and one kain grading system wey automatically make di selection for di four 'best losers'.

Full Round of 16 Fixtures

Di Round of 16 fixtures for di Afcon 2023 go start on Saturday up to Tuesday - di first game of di round na between Angola vs Namibia .
