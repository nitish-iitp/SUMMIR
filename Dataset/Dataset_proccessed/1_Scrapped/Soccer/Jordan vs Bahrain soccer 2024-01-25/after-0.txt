Bahrain beats Jordan in Group E match of ﻿AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™, secures Round of 16 spot

Khalifa International Stadium witnessed a Group E match between Jordan and Bahrain on 25 January 2024, with the match kicking off at 2:30 pm.

Bahrain's Abdullah Yusuf scores the first and only goal of the match in the 34th minute, taking the score to 0-1.

The full-time score is 0-1. Bahrain collects 3 points from this win and secures their spot in the Round of 16.
