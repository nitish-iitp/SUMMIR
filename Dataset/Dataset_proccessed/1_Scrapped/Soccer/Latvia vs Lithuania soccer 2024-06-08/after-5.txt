MONT BELVIEU

In a showcase of some of the greater Houston area’s best high school football players, it was Clear Falls Knights senior quarterback Landon Vessel who shined brightest in leading the East team to a 62-21 blowout win over the West team in the 21st Bayou Bowl, played Saturday night at Barbers Hill’s Eagle Stadium.
