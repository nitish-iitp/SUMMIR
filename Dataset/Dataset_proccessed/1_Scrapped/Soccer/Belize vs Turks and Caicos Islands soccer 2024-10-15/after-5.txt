Baker's boys and girls soccer teams were in action Tuesday, Oct. 15, the start of a busy sports week for the Bulldogs.

The girls soccer team, 3-5-1 overall and 0-2-1 in the Greater Oregon League, played Pendleton/Weston-McEwen at the Baker Sports Complex.
