Sister, sister: Unique combo leading Lady Red soccer
Jake Nichols Sports Editor Jun 11, 2024 Updated Jun 11, 2024  0
1 of 3

Cocke County’s girls soccer program will feature a blood-related coaching combo this season, as head coach Mikayla Metzdorf announced that her sister, McKenzie Gregg, will be assisting in the 2024 campaign.

NEWPORT — Going into the 2024 season, Mikayla Metzdorf is working her Cocke County girls’ soccer team hard.

But she is not alone in doing so, as Metzdorf will be joined by her sister, former Chattanooga player McKenzie Gregg, this season.
