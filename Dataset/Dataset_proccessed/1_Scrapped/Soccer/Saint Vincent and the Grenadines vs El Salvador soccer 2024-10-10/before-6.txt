Monday, October 7, 2024 
Thursday, October 10, 2024
Chicago MART 2nd Floor WorkLife

KERAMIDA is excited to announce our attendance at the 2024 Allstate Business Diversity Summit! 

Join us Oct. 7-10, 2024, for this hybrid event focused on Stakeholder Collaboration: United to Drive Economic Equity and Sustainability. As a woman-owned global sustainability and EHS services firm, we’re excited to delve into impactful discussions and strategies for empowering diverse businesses and reducing emissions. See you there!

For more information on this event or to register, visit here.

Attendees:

Erin Ridout, Client Relations Representative, KERAMIDA Inc.
