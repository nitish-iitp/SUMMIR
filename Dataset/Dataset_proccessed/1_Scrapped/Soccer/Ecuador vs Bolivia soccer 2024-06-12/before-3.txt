Deborah Darlene Tallent passed away on June 4, 2024. A visitation for Deborah will be held Wednesday, June 12, 2024, from 5 p.m. to 8 p.m. at Earthman Baytown Funeral Home, 3919 Garth Road, Baytown, TX 77521. A graveside service will be held Thursday, June 13, 2024, from 11 a.m. to 12 p.m. at Kidd Cemetery, Conroe, TX.

Deborah Darlene Tallent was born October 24, 1952, in Conroe, TX and passed away June 4, 2024, in Houston, TX.
