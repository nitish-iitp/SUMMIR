Where to find Ecuador vs Bolivia on US TV: June 12, 2024

by World Soccer Talk June 12, 2024

Here are all of the details of where you can watch Ecuador vs Bolivia on US television and via legal streaming:
WHO	Ecuador vs Bolivia
WHAT	International Friendly
WHEN	8:30pm ET / 5:30pm PT • Wednesday, June 12, 2024
WHERE	PPV and Fanatiz
STREAM	WATCH NOW
With Fanatiz, you can watch Ecuador vs Bolivia. With the legal streaming service, you can watch the game on your computer, smartphone, tablet, Roku, Apple TV, Fire Stick or hook it up to your TV with Google Chromecast.
