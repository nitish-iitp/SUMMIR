AFCON 2023: Black Stars jersey numbers revealed
Date: 04 January 2024
By Vincent Ashitey
Category: Sports
115
Shares
The jersey numbers to be worn by the Black Stars of Ghana for the AFCON 2023 in Cote d'Ivoire have been revealed.

Ghana is currently training in Kumasi behind closed doors at the Dr Kwame Kyei Sports Stadium ahead of the tournament.

The Black Stars will wrap up preparations for the tournament with a friendly against Namibia on Monday, January 8, 2024.

The Black Stars are Group B alongside Cape Verde, giants Egypt and Mozambique.

The team will kick start their tournament against Cape Verde on Sunday, January 14, 2024 before taking on Egypt and Mozambique in the other group games.

The AFCON 2023 is scheduled for January 13 to February 11.
