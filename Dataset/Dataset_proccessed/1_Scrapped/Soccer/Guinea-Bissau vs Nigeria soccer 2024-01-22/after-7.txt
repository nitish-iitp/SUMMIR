Nigeria beat Guinea-Bissau 1-0 to reach di last 16 for di 2023 Africa Cup of Nations.

Di only goal for di match come wen Opa Sangate divert Moses Simon cross inside im own net seven minutes before di break.

Both sides bin score goals wey dem rule out as offside for second half, while Nigeria striker Victor Osimhen also get one effort wey dem cancel sake of handball.

Di result mean say Jose Peseiro side finish second for Group A behind Equatorial Guinea, wey beat hosts Ivory Coast 4-0.

Super Eagles go face di second-placed side from Group C, wey fit be Senegal, Guinea or Cameroon, for di last 16 on Saturday by 8pm West African Time.
