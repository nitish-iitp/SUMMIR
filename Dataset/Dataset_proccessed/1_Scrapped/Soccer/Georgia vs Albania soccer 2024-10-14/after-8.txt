The match between the football teams of Georgia and Albania ended with the victory of the guests

The match between the football teams of Georgia and Albania ended 0:1 in favor of the guests.

The fourth round match of "League of Nations" took place at the stadium named after Mikheil Meskhi.

As it is known, the opponents of the Georgian national team in the B1 group of the UEFA "League of Nations" are the national teams of the Czech Republic, Albania and Ukraine. At the moment, the Georgian football team has 6 points in the group
