HIGH SCHOOL
Adam Scott suffers dramatic loss in gold medal OFSAA AAA boys rugby game

Kitchener Collegiate Raiders defeated the Lions 2-1 in penalty kicks after teams were tied after regulation and overtime

Updated June 10, 2024 at 8:26 a.m.
June 8, 2024
3 min read
Save

Adam Scott Lions players react to their team’s loss to Kitchener Collegiate in the OFSAA AAA boys rugby gold medal game played at Nicholls Oval in Peterborough on Saturday, June 8, 2024.

Clifford Skarstedt Metroland
By Mike DaviesExaminer Sports Director

It’s a shame there had to be a loser in as dramatic a gold medal game at the OFSAA AAA boys rugby championships as one could expect.

The Kitchener Collegiate Raiders squeezed past the host Adam Scott Lions 2-1 in penalty kicks after the sides were tied 17-17 following overtime and 12-12 after regulation time at Nicholls Oval Saturday afternoon.
