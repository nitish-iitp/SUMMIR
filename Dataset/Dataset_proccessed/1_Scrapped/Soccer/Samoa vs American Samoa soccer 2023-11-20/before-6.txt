Pictorial for tennis.

Team PNG’s tennis squad will commence their 2023 Pacific Games campaign today in the team events competition.

The team is led by men’s top players Matthew Stubbings, Mark Gibbons, Eddie Mera and Abigail Tere-Apisah.

Polye talks about Tahiti bid

Sports Minister Don Polye says he is aware of Tahiti and the Pacific Games Bid but has put Papua New Guinea’s hand up to host the XVIII Pacific Games 2027.

Read more in your Post-Courier on Monday, November 20, 2023.

Basketball: PNG vs Tahiti

The first match between PNG and Tahiti is underway at the Friendship Hall in Honiara.

Tahiti are proving to be a strong side. Halftime now and the score stands at Tahiti 51 – PNG 19.

More updates to follow…

Tahiti takes the lead

Team PNG trying to get past Tahiti’s defense, which has proven strong so far with Tahiti still in the lead at 72 points to 24 as the fourth and final quarter resumes.

More updates to follow…

PNG High Commissioner to Solomon Islands wishes Team PNG

PNG High Commissioner to Solomon Islands, Moses Kaul wishes Team PNG good luck.

He also said “Let the spirit of good sportsmanship prevail, we are a Pacific, we are together in this, let’s play to unite.”

Volleyball: PNG vs Tuvalu 1st set

PNG Men’s Volleyball Team are now playing their opening match against Tuvalu at the Friendship Hall, Honiara.

The first set has begun with PNG in the lead with 17 sets while Tuvalu with 13 receiving.

Volleyball: PNG vs Tuvalu 2nd set

PNG has won the first set with 25 sets and 21 receiving for Tuvalu.

The second set has begun with PNG in the lead with 8 receiving and 3 sets from Tuvalu.

Volleyball PNG vs Tuvalu 3nd set

PNG has won the second set with 23 receiving to 18 setting by Tuvalu.

Game is in favor of PNG with winning two sets and taking the lead in the 3rd set with 12 setting to 6 receiving.

Cheers for PNG Women’s Soccer team

Cheering go PNG, go…from the rooftops as Pacific Games Gold medal defending queens, PNG Women’s football team pile on 9 goals to nil against American Samoa.
