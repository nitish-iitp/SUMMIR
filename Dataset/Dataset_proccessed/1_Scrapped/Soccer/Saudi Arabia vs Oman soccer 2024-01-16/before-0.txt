Both teams are underdogs to qualify for the AFC Asian Cup knockout stages from their group.

Thailand and Kyrgyz Republic will square off against each other in their opening fixture of the 2023 AFC Asian Cup at the Abdullah bin Khalifa Stadium in Doha, Qatar on 16 January 2024. The national teams find themselves in Group F, which also includes Saudi Arabia and Oman.

Thailand and Kyrgyz Republic are ranked lower than the other two teams in FIFA rankings and have lesser chances of securing one of the top two spots that ensure direct qualification into the knockout stages. This means that both teams will be directly competing for the third spot, which can ensure them a place in the knockouts as the best third-placed teams.

Winning this contest will be vital for both Thailand and Kyrgyz Republic to grab a ticket to the Round of 16. Three points in the first game of the tournament will fetch momentum and a confidence boost that may motivate either side to cause an upset later on. As the fixture nears, here is a look at what to expect:
