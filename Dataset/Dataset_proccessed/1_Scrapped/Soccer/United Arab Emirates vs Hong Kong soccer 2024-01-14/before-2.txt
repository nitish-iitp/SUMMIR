Iran have won the AFC Asian Cup three times.

In what promises to be a thrilling encounter at the AFC Asian Cup 2023, Iran are set to face Palestine in a Group C showdown that has fans eagerly awaiting the kick-off. Consequently, both teams prepare to showcase their skills on the continental stage. With both teams aiming to make a mark in the tournament, fans can expect a captivating contest that will set the tone for their respective campaigns in the competition.

Kick-Off:

Sunday, 14 January 2024 at 5:30 PM GMT

Location: Education City Stadium (Al Rayyan)

Form

Iran (in all competitions): W-W-D-W-W

Palestine (in all competitions): L-D-L-L-D

Players to Watch
Mehdi Taremi (Iran)
Mehdi Taremi was the highest scorer in the Portuguese League last season. (Courtesy: AFC)

Having clinched their initial three AFC Asian Cup titles, the latest triumph coming in 1976, Iran have since been unable to replicate that success, falling short of securing the continental championship on subsequent occasions.

Taremi gathered widespread recognition in the football community when he played a pivotal role in leading Portuguese club Porto to the league title last season, netting an impressive 22 goals, the highest in the league. He will be a key player for IR Iran in the upcoming contest.

Oday Dabbagh (Palestine)
Dabbagh will lead Palestine in the AFC Asian Cup 2023. (Courtesy: AFC)

In a span of eight years since making his professional debut with his hometown club Hilal Alquds, Oday Dabbagh has swiftly carved out a significant place for himself in discussions about Palestineâ€™s most exceptional footballing talent.

Dabbagh transitioned from finding the back of the net in the AFC Cup to competing in prominent European leagues, shouldering the aspirations of his nation as they look ahead to the AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023. He will be a huge threat to the opposition.

Match Facts
Palestine are set to participate in the AFC Asian Cup for the third consecutive edition, having initially entered the tournament during their debut appearance in 2015.
Palestine will be facing all three of their Group C opponents for the first time in the AFC Asian Cup.
Iran have successfully qualified for every edition of the AFC Asian Cup since 1968, marking an impressive streak that began with their inaugural participation when they secured the first of their three titles.
Injury & Team News

IR Iran will miss the services of Ali Gholizadeh, due to his injury issue. On the other hand, there are no injuries reported in the Palestine camp and they will have their full squad available for this clash.
