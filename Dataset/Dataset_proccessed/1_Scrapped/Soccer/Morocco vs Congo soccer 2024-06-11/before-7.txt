Ghana’s Referee Laryea handed international assignment

Next »

Referee Daniel Laryea

Daniel Nii Ayi Laryea will take charge of the FIFA World Cup 2026 Group E qualifier between Congo and Morocco next week.

He will be assisted by Kwasi Acheampong Brobbey (Assistant Referee I), Roland Nii Dodoo Addy (Assistant Referee II), and Charles Benle Bulu (Fourth Official), while Moussa Abakar Moussa will serve as the Match Commissioner.

The other officials include Evehe Divine Raphael from Cameroon as the Referee Assessor and his compatriot, Nduoa-Zie Christian, as the Security Officer.

The match is scheduled to take place at Stade des Martyrs in Kinshasa on Tuesday, 11 June 2024, at 17:00.
