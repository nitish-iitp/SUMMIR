Taylor Swift has recently confirmed a new way for fans to buy tickets for The Eras Tour at Wembley Stadium in London.

The 34-year-old hitmaker is currently in Scotland, with the singer gearing up to perform her final show in Edinburgh this evening after two sold-out shows left fans gobsmacked by the sensational show she puts on for them. But soon enough, Taylor will be back in the capital city, with London Swifties inching closer to the hitmaker arriving.

Travelling down the country a little after she wraps up her Scottish shows this evening, Taylor will take to the stage in Liverpool to perform at Anfield Stadium for three nights (June 13, 14, 15). Moving onto Wales a couple of nights later, Taylor will then perform one show on June 18 at the Principality Stadium.
