10/09/2024  AFC Cup

Sports





​Brunei Darussalam National Football squad continue the AFC Cup, Saudi Arabia 2027, campaign against Macau, 10th September night.

 

The second-leg match took place at Macau Olympic Stadium. Brunei Darussalam won 1-NIL against Macau. In the first-leg match, Brunei Darussalam won against Macau by 3-NIL.
