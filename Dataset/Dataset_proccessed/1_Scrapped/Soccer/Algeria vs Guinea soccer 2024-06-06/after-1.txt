Muse gives stirring speech for Best of Preps Banquet
Allen Worrell aworrell@thecarrollnews.com Jun 6, 2024 
1 of 4

Mike Muse, Director of Player Relations and Community Service for Wake Forest men’s basketball program, gave a stirring speech about GRACE - an acronym he preaches within the Demon Deacons’ hoops program, during the Best of Preps Banquet held May 21 at First Baptist Church in Mount Airy.

More than 130 high schoolers, coaches, relatives and friends gathered in Mount Airy on May 21 to celebrate some of the best prep athletic performances of the year from a five-county region covered by APG Publishing’s local community newspapers.

The annual Best of Preps gathering — put on by The Mount Airy News, Stokes News, Yadkin Ripple, Elkin Tribune, and The Carroll News in Virginia — recognizes the highest athletic achievements and performances of the past school year. Selected by sports writers from those papers, with input from area coaches and athletic directors, the Best of Preps highlights the best athletes in virtually all high school sports played in the five-county coverage area of those papers.
