Nike Tech Fleece Pants - Grey/Black

Offering maximum comfort in cold weather, these Tech Fleece jogging pants feature a slimmer cut, ideal for everyday wear. Slightly more fitted at the thighs, this tapered model tightens at the knees to give you a comfortable feeling without losing the clean, polished style you love so much. The long ribbed cuffs combine with a zipped pocket on the right leg to store your small items safely, while giving you the signature Tech Fleece look.

Tech Fleece
Our lightweight premium fleece fabric, smooth inside and out, gives you optimal warmth without adding bulk.
Iconic details
The taped tape around the zip pocket and at the seams is a signature detail of the Tech Fleece collection. The zipped pocket reveals an additional interior pocket that will keep your keys, cards and phone in place and within reach.
Contoured cut
The tapered cut from the knees to the ankles ensures a stylish look that highlights your sneakers.
The soft elastic waistband with drawstring allows you to adjust the fit.

Created in 1972 Nike is probably the most popular clothing brand in the world. Its name is inspired by the Greek goddess of victory: NIKE. Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman, the founders of the brand, wanted to “bring inspiration and innovation to all athletes around the world”, they created an authentic, innovative brand fundamentally focused on the world of sport. The numerous ranges of products created over the years make Nike a must in sports textiles: Football, Basketball, Running, Training, Nike pro Combat, Nike Sportswear... Always in search of innovation Nike draws on years of research and development to push its products to the cutting edge of technology combined with an innovative and unique design.
