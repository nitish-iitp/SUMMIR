WHY IS AUSTRALIA PLAYING?

Australia has been participating in the 2024 Asian Cup as a member of the Asian Football Confederation since June 2005. This decision was made due to the lack of clear direction in the Oceania Football Confederation (OFC) for the Socceroos, as well as the absence of a full World Cup quota allocated to the OFC.
Australia's official debut as a member of the Asian Confederation came in February 2006, when they participated in the qualifiers for the 2007 Asian Cup. Since then, Australia has been an integral part of Asian soccer, participating in competitions and contributing to the development of the sport in the region.
