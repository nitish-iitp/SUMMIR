CRUCIAL UEFA NATIONS LEAGUE SHOWDOWN BETWEEN MALTA AND MOLDOVA THIS SUNDAY

 Philippa Zammit
 October 10, 2024
 3:47 pm
 No Comments

On Sunday, 13 October 2024, at the National Stadium, Malta’s Senior National Team will face Moldova in a decisive UEFA Nations League match. Fans are invited to witness the battle for the top spot in Group 2 of League D, with kick-off at 18:00. Malta will aim to build on their recent 1-0 victory against Andorra, which reignited their push for promotion.

Head to the National Stadium to support Malta’s Senior National Team as they take on Moldova in a pivotal UEFA Nations League fixture. This match will see both teams battling for the top spot in Group 2 of League D.

Malta will be looking to build on the momentum from their impressive 1-0 victory against Andorra. That win has revitalised Malta’s push for promotion from League D, adding extra significance to this upcoming encounter.

This game offers excitement for all types of football fans, from casual viewers to die-hard supporters. The South End Core, Malta’s official fan group, will be in full force, generating an electric atmosphere and enhancing the matchday experience as they cheer on the National Team in this crucial contest.
