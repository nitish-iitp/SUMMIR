Jefferson’s Jake Kerkenbush scored three goals in an early first-half flurry to lead Jefferson’s boys soccer team past visiting Cambridge/Deerfield 3-1 in nonconference action on Thursday, Sept. 5.

Kerkenbush converted in the fourth and ninth minutes off assists from sophomore Noah Skoug before capping his hat trick by netting a 14th minute goal, which junior Gave Berge was credited with the assist on.George Schultz scored in the 31st minute for C/D United (1-1-1).
