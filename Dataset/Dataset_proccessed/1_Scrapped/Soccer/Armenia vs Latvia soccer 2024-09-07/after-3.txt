Armenia’s football chief presents national team jerseys to Olympic medalists
 Siranush Ghazanchyan 
Send an email
September 7, 2024, 21:06
 Less than a minute

Before the start of the Armenia-Latvia match, President of the Football Federation of Armenia Armen Melikbekyan presented jerseys of the Armenian national team to Olympic medalists Artur Aleksanyan, Malkhas Amoyan and Artur Davtyan.

Armenia hosts Latvia in the first Nations League match on September 7.
