LA GRANDE — The United States is more than 6,000 miles from Palau, an island nation in the South Pacific, but it is facing some of the same stubborn issues that Americans are facing, including high taxes and inflation.

Tommy Remengesau Jr., one of the most popular political leaders in Palau’s history, made this clear during a presentation at Eastern Oregon University on Thursday, May 30.
