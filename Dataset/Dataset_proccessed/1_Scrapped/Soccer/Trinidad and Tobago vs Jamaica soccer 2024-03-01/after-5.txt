The year has changed but there’s been no slow down in the winning by the Goose Creek Memorial boys’ soccer program.

The Patriots were known far and wide in the district about being the kingpins of soccer in this neck of the woods. Two-thirds of the way into the 2024 District 17-5A campaign and that reputation has only gotten stronger.
