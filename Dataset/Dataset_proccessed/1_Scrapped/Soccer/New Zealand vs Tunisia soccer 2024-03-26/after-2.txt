Zlatko Dalić eyes cup glory against Egypt

by

 KingFut Team
March 26, 2024

Croatia manager Zlatko Dalić has targeted winning the Egypt International Capital Cup against the Pharaohs on Tuesday.

Egypt are set to face Croatia in the Egypt International Capital Cup final after beating New Zealand 1-0, while Croatia overcame Tunisia on penalties.

The game will be played at the newly built New Administrative Capital Stadium, holding a capacity of over 93,940 seats on Tuesday at 22:00 CLT.

Speaking ahead of the game, Dalić said: “We aim to win the Capital Cup. When reaching the final, our goal is to clinch the title.

“We will gather our strengths again and give some players the chance against Egypt.

“We participated in this friendly tournament to prepare for the Euro 2024 finals. We will try to finish the next match without any injuries.

“I want everyone who joined the camp to have the opportunity to participate.”
