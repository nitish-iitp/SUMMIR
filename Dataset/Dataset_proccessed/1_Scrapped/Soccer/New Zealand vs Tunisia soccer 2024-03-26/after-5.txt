Sarpreet Singh is fouled by Nader Ghandri of Tunisia during the clash between the All Whites and Tunisia. Photosport

The All Whites and Tunisia clash in the play-off for third place at the Fifa Series 2024 Egypt.

- All Whites lose penalty shootout 4-2

- Ben Waine and Elijah Just miss attempts in shootout

- Sarpreet Singh had first half penalty saved in 0-0 draw

- Second penalty for New Zealand was over-ruled by VAR
