CHAMPION HILL TO SHOW ENGLAND MEN'S FIXTURES AT THE 2024 EUROPEAN CHAMPIONSHIPS
Kian Ward
 Club News
 13 June 2024
0 minutes read

We're delighted to announce that all 3 of the Three Lions Group Stage fixtures at the 2024 UEFA European Championships will be shown inside the Clubhouse and in our outside bar this summer! 

Want to secure your table in either our outside or inside bar? Then you can buy yours by following this link here.

You can also purchase game specific tickets by clicking the following links: 

England vs Serbia, 16/06/24, 20:00 - here. Bar opens 4.30pm
England vs Denmark, 20/06/24, 17:00 - here. Bar opens 4.30pm
England vs Slovenia, 25/06/24, 20:00 - here. Bar opens 4.30pm

Money raised from table bookings will go to Albrighton Food Bank. There will be standing places available on the night.
