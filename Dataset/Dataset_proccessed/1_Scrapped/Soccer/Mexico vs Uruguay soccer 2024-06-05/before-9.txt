MexTour Denver: Mexico National Football Team vs. Uruguay National Football Team
EVENTS
JUNE 1, 2024

With the soccer world’s eyes on North America ahead of the 2026 FIFA World Cup, Denver is one of five cities across the United States that has been selected to host the biggest Mexican Men’s National Team in the United States.

Head out to Empower Field to watch Mexico take on the global powerhouse Uruguay!

 

Date: June 5, 2024

Location: Empower Field at Mile High – Denver, CO

Tickets and more information: somoslocales.com
