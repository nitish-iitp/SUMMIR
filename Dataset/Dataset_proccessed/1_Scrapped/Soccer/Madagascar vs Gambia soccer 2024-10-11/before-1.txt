GET FUNKY: Funktafest returns to the Ritter Park Amphitheater on Saturday, Oct. 12, with the live music running from 11:30 a.m. until 10 p.m. The tickets for this family friendly music event are $40 for general admission, and catered VIP passes are $95 until Friday, Oct. 11, at midnight. Prices will then increase on the day of the show. Admission is free for children 12 and younger with a supervising adult. Pets on a leash are welcome, but please, no coolers or outside food and drink except for a single, sealed bottle of water per attendee. The lineup will include funky jams by Yam Yam, Rasta Rafiki, Dinosaur Burps, HellNaw, The M.F.B., Kitana, Megan Jean’s Secret Family, Quortz, Massing, DJ Charlie Blac, Randy and Blue Heron. Immediately after the fest is over in Ritter Park, the aftershow will take place at The Loud Music Venue and Beer garden beginning at 10:15 and going until 2 a.m.

GET SPICY: The 41st annual West Virginia State Chili Championship, aka ChiliFest, will happen on Saturday, Oct. 12, beginning at 11 a.m. Benefiting the Ronald McDonald House, the festival will take place in downtown Huntington on 3rd Avenue between 8th Street and 10th Street. More information on entering chili, eating chili, tickets, live music, pepper-eating contests and a full schedule can be found at chilifestwv.com.
