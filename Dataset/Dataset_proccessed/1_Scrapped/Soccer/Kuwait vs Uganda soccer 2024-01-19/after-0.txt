INTERNATIONAL FRIENDLY Debutants shine as Uganda eases past Kuwait
Allan Damba • 19:47 - 19.01.2024
Debutants Ronald Ssekiganda and Alex Kitata scored for Paul Joseph Put's charges against the Asian nation.

A goal apiece either side of the half guided the Uganda Cranes past Kuwait in an international friendly bout played in Egypt on Friday.

Debutants Ronald Ssekiganda and Alex Kitata scored for Paul Joseph Put's charges against the Asian nation.

Ronald Ssekiganda scored for Uganda against Kuwait|FUFA IMAGE

Ssekiganda, who plays for Uganda Premier League record holders SC Villa, needed just ten minutes into his debut to put the Cranes ahead.
