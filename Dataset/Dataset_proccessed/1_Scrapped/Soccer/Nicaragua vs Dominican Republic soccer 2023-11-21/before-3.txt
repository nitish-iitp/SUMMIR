Different Ways to Apply the Ford Fusion Vinyl Wrap

Customizing your Ford Fiesta involves the application of a specialized vinyl film onto your vehicle's exterior, effectively concealing the original paint. Not only does this process allow for stunning color transformation, but it also unlocks an array of texture and design possibilities. Full Wrap For Your Ford

A full wrap involves covering your entire Ford Fusion, from the front bumper to the rear bumper, with a sheet of vinyl film. This comprehensive approach opens up the opportunity to choose from a wide spectrum of colors, textures, and finishes, enabling a complete transformation of your car's exterior appearance.

It's important to note that fully wrapping a vehicle is a complex process best executed by skilled professionals. Their expertise ensures precise installation, the use of specialized tools, and thorough vehicle preparation, resulting in a flawless, high-quality finish. It's worth mentioning that a full wrap typically involves a higher cost due to the increased material and labor required.

Partial Wrap - Covering Selected Parts Of Your Ford

If you're inclined towards a more understated transformation or wish to highlight specific areas of your Ford Fusion, a partial wrap is a fantastic choice. This option allows you to cover select parts of the vehicle, such as the hood, roof, trunk, mirrors, or bumpers while leaving other areas untouched. Partial wraps can be a cost-effective way to introduce stylish accents that enhance your Ford Fusion's overall aesthetic.

It is important to consider color selection for partially wrapping your Ford. The shade of your wrap should either complement or be in contrast with the original color of your sedan. For example, if your Ford is in Ruby Red color, you can go for Super Glossy Piano Black vinyl wrap in contrast available at VinylFrog. Also, check the Carbon Fiber vinyl films that look excellent when used for selected coverage.

Preserving the Factory Finish

The primary advantage of applying a vinyl wrap is that it acts as a protective shield for your sedan's original paint. This means that, beneath the wrap, your Ford Fusion's factory finish remains untouched, pristine, and unharmed. When the time comes to sell your vehicle, you have the option to reveal the unblemished paint, potentially boosting its resale value.

Protection Against Everyday Hazards

The world outside can be harsh on your car's exterior. Vinyl wraps offer an additional layer of defense against common hazards like minor dings, scratches, road debris, tar, and even the occasional parking lot mishap. They also offer a shield against the onslaught of the harsh UV rays preserving the original paint of your Ford Fusion.

Easily Removable And Flexible

A significant advantage of vinyl wraps is their easy removal process. Unlike a permanent paint job, vinyl wraps can be removed without causing any harm to the original paint. This flexibility allows you to experiment with different looks or return your car to its factory appearance whenever you desire.
