Argentina Soccer: When & Who Do They Play This Week? | World Cup Qualifying

Sportsgrid-Staff

0 of 3 minutes, 22 secondsVolume 0%
 

Argentina stands among the elite teams in World Cup history, with three championship titles to their name, claimed in 1978, 1986, and most recently in 2022. Additionally, La Albiceleste has finished as the runner-up on three occasions: 1930, 1990, and 2014. Over the course of 18 World Cup appearances, the team has secured 47 wins out of 88 matches, cementing their legacy as one of the sport’s most formidable nations. Argentina has qualified for 13 straight World Cups, having last missed the tournament in 1970.

Does Argentina Play in the World Cup Qualifying This Week?

Yes, Argentina plays on Thursday, October 10, 2024, against Venezuela. This year, Argentina is 1-1 in World Cup qualifiers. They opened 2024 with a 3-0 shutout victory over Chile on September 5 and followed it up with a 3-1 loss to Colombia. Overall, they are 6-0-2 in 2026 World Cup qualifiers, which includes matches from 2023. La Albiceleste leads the CONMEBOL table with 18 points.
