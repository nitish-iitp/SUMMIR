This week’s highlight will be Indian football team’s vital clash against Uzbekistan in the AFC Asian Cup.

After an exciting second week of football events worldwide, which also featured the Indian men’s team’s match against Australia, we are now transitioning to the third week of January 2024.

The upcoming week will center around the encounter between India and Uzbekistan in the 2023 AFC Asian Cup. Concurrently, the Kalinga Super Cup and the Indian Women’s League will proceed with their scheduled fixtures.
