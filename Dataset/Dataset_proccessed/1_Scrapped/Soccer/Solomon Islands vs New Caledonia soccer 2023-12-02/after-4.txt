Vanuatu to host OFC Men's Nations Cup next year
By Vourie Molivakoro Dec 2, 2023  0

Vanuatu will be hosting the prestigious Oceania Football Confederation (OFC) Men’s Nations Cup in June next year, after successfully hosting the Men’s Champions League for the first time this year.

Vanuatu will host the competition from 15-30 June.
