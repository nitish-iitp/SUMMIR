JOAN MARIE ZEKAN of Charleston, WV, was born on June 4, 1992, and departed us suddenly on March 22, 2024, in Las Vegas, NV.

Joan was a member of St. Agnes Catholic Church and graduated from Charleston Catholic High School in 2010, where she was a standout student and a member of the drama club and cheerleading squad. She attended the College of Wooster where she majored in French with a minor in Communications. She was captivated by the French language and culture, studied abroad at the Sorbonne and the Catholic University of Paris, then later moved there for a time.
