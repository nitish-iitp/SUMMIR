SIERRA VISTA — Sierra Vista’s Gridiron Desert Elite flag football league concluded its season on Friday, March 29, crowning six champions during a "Friday Night Lights Championships" event at Keith Hampton Memorial Field.

The event was highlighted by the guest appearance of Michael Bankston, who is listed as one of the Arizona Cardinals' top 100 all-time players, and performance camp coach Mo Streety of the Los Angeles Rams.
