Eid Al Fitr 2024 events & activities

Eid Al Fitr 2024 is soon approaching, and it's time to start planning how you will be spending it!

To ease your planning, the ILoveQatar.net (ILQ) team brings to you a list of events taking place across Qatar for Eid.

Fireworks & other events will be added to this article as they get announced, so make sure to bookmark this link!

Book your private cabana for a cosy retreat, providing a comfortable spot to relax and unwind.
