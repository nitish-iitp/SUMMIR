Clayton County Board of Education Approves 2024-25 School Calendar
From staff reports Jan 17, 2024 Updated Jul 22, 2024  0
Facebook
Twitter
Email
Print
Copy article link
Save

JONESBORO — The Clayton County Board of Education has approved the proposed academic calendar for the 2024-25 school year.

The calendar, approved Jan. 8, has 180 days for students and 190 for teachers and includes new teacher orientation, four pre-planning days, various holidays and breaks, three staff development days, two extended learning beyond the class days, and three post-planning days.

A glance at some dates on the calendar:

July 19 to 25: New teacher orientation.

July 26: Pre-planning.

July 29 to 31: Pre-planning.

Aug. 1: The first day of school.

Sept. 7 to 11: Fall break.

Nov. 25 to 29: Thanksgiving break.

Dec. 23 to Jan. 3: Semester break.

Jan. 6: Staff development.

Feb. 14: Staff development.

March 14: Staff development.

April 7 to 11: Spring break.

May 23: Last day of school.

A general interest survey taken with two versions of the 2024-25 calendar was shared with all stakeholders.

The version approved by the school board was preferred by 72.8% of the respondents.

The 2024-25 calendar and the current 2023-24 calendars are available at https://www.clayton.k12.ga.us/about/calendars/school-year-calendars.
