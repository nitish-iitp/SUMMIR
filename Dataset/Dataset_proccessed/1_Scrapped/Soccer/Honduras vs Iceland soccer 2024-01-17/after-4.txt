Qatar beats Tajikistan in Group A match of the ﻿AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™

Qatar (Al Annabi) played against Tajikistan in a Group A match of the AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™ on 17 January 2024 at the Al Bayt Stadium. The match kicked off at 5:30 pm.


Qatar's Akram Afif scores the match's first and only goal in the 17th minute.

In the 81st minute, Tajikistan's Amadoni Kamolov was shown the red card for violent conduct and eliminated from the match, the first in the tournament.

The full-time score is 1-0 in favour of Qatar. Al Annabi leads Group A with 6 points, securing their spot in the Round of 16.
