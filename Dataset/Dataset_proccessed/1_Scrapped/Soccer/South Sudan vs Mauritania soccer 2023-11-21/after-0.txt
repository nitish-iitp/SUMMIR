World Cup 2026 (Q): Mauritania held by South Sudan

Mauritania are still without a win in these 2026 World Cup qualifiers.After their defeat in DR Congo, the Mourabitounes followed up with a draw against South Sudan (0-0). In a match relocated to Diamniadio in Senegal, Amir Abdou's men were never able to find a solution against the hard-fought South Sudanese, and were rewarded with a point. Mauritania have 1 point in Group B and are already seeing Senegal (4 points), Sudan (4 points) and the DRC (3 points) take the lead. Togo have 2 points, and Sudan 1.
