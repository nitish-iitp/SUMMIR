Boys soccer: Talladega Tigers have a Hispanic flavor
By Chris Fernsler, Daily Home Sports Writer, cfernsler@dailyhome.com Mar 24, 2024 

Talladega's Arturo Villanueva-Cruz celebrates after scoring a goal.

TALLADEGA — There is a definite and distinctive international vibe on the Talladega High boys soccer team.

The Tigers are loaded to the goal box with Hispanic players, who have the skills, the instincts and the total soccer upbringing. They live and breathe anything remotely resembling the game that they love.
