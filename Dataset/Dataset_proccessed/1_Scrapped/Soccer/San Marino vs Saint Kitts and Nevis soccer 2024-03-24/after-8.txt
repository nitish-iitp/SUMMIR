TH Girls Basketball Player of the Year: Cuba City's Olson always striving to improve
BY SHANNON MUMM shannon.mumm@thmedia.com Mar 24, 2024 Updated Apr 1, 2024 
1 of 4

Telegraph Herald Girls Basketball Player of the Year Olivia Olson led Cuba City High School to the Wisconsin Division 4 state championship game in March. The Cubans made their first trip to the state tournament since 2015 and reached a runner-up finish.

CUBA CITY, Wis. — The grind never stops for Cuba City’s Olivia Olson.

Not even 48 hours after playing in the Division 4 championship game, the junior guard was back in the gym.

olivia olson file

Season averages of 21.3 points, 7 rebounds, 5.1 assists, 3.7 steals per game … became the ninth player in Cuba City girls basketball history to join the 1,000-point club earlier this season … 1,525 career points after just three seasons ranks second in program history … set single-season records in total points (641), points in a game (37), free-throw percentage (81.8), made free throws (126) … Wisconsin Division 4 honorable mention all-state as a freshman … Division 4 first-team all-state last two seasons … three-time SWAL first-team all-conference … back-to-back SWAL player of the year
