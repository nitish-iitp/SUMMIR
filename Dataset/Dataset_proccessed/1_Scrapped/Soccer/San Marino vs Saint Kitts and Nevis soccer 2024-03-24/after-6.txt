High school boys track and field: Imbler set a number of personal records at Thurmond Memorial
By JOHN BRAESE EasternOregonSports.com Mar 24, 2024  0

Imbler freshman Isla Howard joined her teammates to win the 4-by-400 relay on March 21, 2024, at the annual Diana Thurmond Memorial in Harper.

HARPER — The Imbler track and field team joined 15 teams representing both Oregon and Idaho at the annual Diana Thurmond Memorial in Harper on Thursday, March 21. Team scores were not tabulated for the meet.

Panthers set 26 new personal records in both the track and field events with additional new seasonal records also seen as times and distances rolled in.
