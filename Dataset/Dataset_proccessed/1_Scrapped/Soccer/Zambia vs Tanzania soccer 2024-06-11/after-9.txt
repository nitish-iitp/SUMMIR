African World Cup qualifiers: Tanzania beats Zambia 1-0
Hespress EN
Tuesday 11 June 2024 - 20:51

Tanzania defeated Zambia 1-0, today at the Levy Mwanawasa Stadium, in the fourth round of African qualifiers for the 2026 FIFA World Cup.

Junior Waziri gave Tanzania the victory over Zambia in the 5th minute of the first half.

The Tanzanian team raised its points tally to 6, and is now in second place behind Morocco in Group F, while Zambia’s score froze at 3 points and is in fourth place.
