AFC Asian Cup 2023: When Is Indian Football Team's Next Match?
The Indian men's national football team finds themselves in a do-or-die situation in the upcoming game after suffering consecutive losses in the AFC Asian Cup 2023.
By : ABP News Bureau  | Updated at : 20 Jan 2024 02:28 PM (IST)
The Indian men's national football team finds themselves in a do-or-die situation in the upcoming game after suffering consecutive losses in the AFC Asian Cup 2023.
1/6
Despite enduring two consecutive defeats, Igor Stimac's men still hold a chance of qualifying for the Round of 16 (knockout stage) in the AFC Asian Cup 2023.
2/6
The Indian men's national football team currently occupies the bottom spot in 4-team Group B with zero points, after two back-to-back defeats against Australia and Uzbekistan.
3/6
India is set to face Syria in their next 2023 AFC Asian Cup fixture on Tuesday, January 23, 2024, at the Al Bayt Stadium in Al Khor, Qatar.
4/6
The Blue Tigers are under immense pressure, as a loss against Syria in their final group stage match will bring an end to their AFC Asian Cup campaign.
5/6
According to the AFC Asian Cup format, the top two teams from each of the six groups secure a place in the Round of 16 (knockout stage). Additionally, the top four out of the six third-placed teams from the groups also advance to the next round.
6/6
If India can secure a position among the top four third-placed teams across all groups, they will successfully qualify for the Round of 16, which is the knockout stage.
