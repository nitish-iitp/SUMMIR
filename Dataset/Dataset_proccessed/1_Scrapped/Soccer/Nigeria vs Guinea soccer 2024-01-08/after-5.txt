Nigeria vs Guinea: Peseiro releases Super Eagles XI [Full list]

Published

 

on

 January 8, 2024

By

 John Owen Nwachukwu

Super Eagles coach, Jose Peseiro has released his starting XI for their friendly game against Guinea.

The Syli Stars will take on the Super Eagles in their final friendly before the 2023 Africa Cup of Nations at the Bani Yas Stadium, Abu Dhabi on Monday (today).

The game will kick-off at 1pm Nigeria time.

Jose Peseiro’s side walloped a local side, Al Gharib 12-0 on Sunday.

The game against Guinea is expected to be a stern test for the Super Eagles.

It will also be an opportunity for Peseiro to assess his squad after close to a week of intensive training in Abu Dhabi.
