Tullahoma picks up more medals
RUSSELL SMYTHIA Sports Writer Sep 6, 2024 
Facebook

The Tullahoma High School and Middle School cross country teams competed in the Green Hill Invitational in Hermitage and had medalists.

Katie Grace Nichols and Brindley Duncan medaled for THS. Nichols finished eighth and Duncan right behind in ninth. There were 81 total girls running. Nichols finished in 21 minutes 51.49 seconds. Duncan had a time of 21:59.44. Ashleigh Johnson and Lincoln Bitton got medals for TMS. Johnson placed seventh with a time of 14:00.10. Bitton finished fourth with a time of 11:32.10.
