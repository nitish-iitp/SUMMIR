Bet of the day.

For today’s games, my bet of the day will be on Hammarby Women vs St. Polten Women in the women’s Champions league.

My Predictions.

My best bet is a straight win for Hammarby Women @1.28 while my value odd prediction is a combination of Hammarby Women win and over 2.5 goals @1.65 odd.

Why I chose this match.

Hammarby Women are competing in the first-ever European tournament after winning the 2023 Damallsvenskan title and are highly motivated to end this match on a positive note.

The Swedish women looked as if they will be eliminated so early after they lost 2-1 in their home leg against Benfica women but they showed great resilience to turn the moment in their favour with a 2-0 victory in the second leg.

This great feat made them the first team to overturn a first-leg defeat in Women’s Champions League qualifying match which is a morale booster to me.

The hone team will play without their attacker, Klara Folkesson, who is working hard to come back from an ACL injury

On the other hand, the visitors are appearing in the group stage for a third straight time in three seasons after winning the OFB Frauen Bundesliga title last season.

They also won their champions league qualification kick off match against Neftci Baki and Vllaznia in the first stage and will hope to cause an upset against Hammarby Women who are in a very decent form.

The away team’s defender Ella Touon has been sidelined after missing the last five matches due to injury.

Hammarby women put up an impressive performance to beat Benfica in the second qualifying round, and I strongly believe that they will be highly from that success to beat a St Polten side that have lost each of their last three away games in the Women’s Champions League.
