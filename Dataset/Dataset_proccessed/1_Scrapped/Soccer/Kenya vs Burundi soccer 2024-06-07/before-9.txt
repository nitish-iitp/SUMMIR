Should the truck driver who caused the deadly Humboldt crash be allowed to stay in Canada? His MP says yes — and is trying to rally Canadians to his side

MP speaks out on behalf of Jaskirat Singh Sidhu, who he says has atoned for his role in a crash that shook the country.

Updated June 7, 2024 at 4:10 p.m.
June 6, 2024
3 min read
Save

Jaskirat Singh Sidhu is shown at his home in Calgary on Dec. 20, 2022.

Todd Korol for the Toronto Star
By Alex BoydStaff Reporter

CALGARY — As the countdown to deportation begins for the driver who caused the devastating crash that killed 16 players and staff of the Humboldt Broncos, a Calgary member of Parliament is hoping to rally support in a last-ditch bid for forgiveness. 

George Chahal says he feels a moral obligation to speak out on behalf of Jaskirat Singh Sidhu, who he argues has atoned for his role in a crash that shook the country.
