Failed Start: The Lithuanian Team Surrendered Against Cyprus
 2024-09-06 20:49 ¬© lff.lt

The Lithuanian men's national football team had an unsuccessful start to their performance in the UEFA Nations League.

Edgaras Jankauskas' pupils lost 0:1 at home to Cyprus in a Division C match.

The decisive moment was the goal scored by I. Pittas in the 34th minute.
