Over 20. 8 runs. Bowler: Hardik Pandya. South Africa: 169/8(rr 8.45)

19.6

Hardik Pandya to Anrich Nortje. Off cutter length ball, off stump on the front foot Slog, mis-timed in the air uncontrolled to deep mid wicket for 1 run, fielded by Singh. A nail-biting thriller concluded with India winning their 2nd ICC Men's T20 World Cup. Although India picked up two early wickets, Quinton de Kock and Tristan Stubbs established a solid foundation for South Africa, and Heinrich Klaasen turned the game entirely in favour of the Proteas. However, Hardik Pandya's 2nd over changed the outcome of the match in India's favour. Suryakumar Yadav took a match-winning catch in the last over. Virat Kohli is the Player of the Match for his brilliant batting display. Jasprit Bumrah is the Player of the Tournament for his exceptional bowling performance.
