Brookwood Girls Soccer Powers Into Sweet Sixteen
From Staff Reports Apr 16, 2024  0

Brookwood goalkeeper Peyton Rhodes (1) smiles with her teammates during a first-round state playoff win over Colquitt County.

Dale Zanine

SNELLVILLE — The Region 4-AAAAAAA champion Brookwood girls soccer team opened the Class AAAAAAA state playoffs with a 12-0 rout of Colquitt County on Tuesday.

Hayden Barnett scored four goals and had three assists, and Skyler Schroder scored three goals along with an assist to lead the Broncos’ attack.
