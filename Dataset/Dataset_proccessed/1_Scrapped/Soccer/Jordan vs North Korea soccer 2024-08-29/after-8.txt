Reigning conference champions Helias and Hannibal face off in heavyweight matchup
By Quentin Corpuel and Chris Farbolin Aug 29, 2024 
Facebook
Twitter
Email

Helias running back Alex Marberry moves past the Capital City defensive line in the Class 5 District 5 championship game Nov. 10 at Helias in Jefferson City. Marberry and the Crusaders open the 2024 season at 7 p.m. Friday against Hannibal. Both teams are coming off double-digit-win seasons in their 2023 campaigns.

Evan Johnson/Missourian
Facebook
Twitter
Email
Print
Copy article link
Save

Helias and Hannibal are fresh off double-digit victory seasons that saw both teams win their respective conferences. The Crusaders and Pirates look to start the 2024 season strong with a statement win in Week 1.

Defending Central Missouri Activities Conference champion Helias opened the 2023 season with a 49-13 victory over Hannibal and has won four of its past five games against the Pirates.
