No deal yet for Air Canada pilots: Here’s what to know before a possible strike

“Informational pickets” by the pilots were held across Canada on Tuesday. The stage is now set for a possible Sept. 17 strike.

Updated Sept. 3, 2024 at 3:37 p.m.
Aug. 27, 2024
1 min read
Save

Air Canada pilots and the airline have so far failed to reach a contract agreement, resulting in the possibility of a strike on Sept. 17. 

Christopher Katsarov/The Canadian Press
By Jermaine WilsonStaff Reporter

The conciliation period has ended with Air Canada pilots and the airline unable to reach an agreement, setting the stage for a possible strike to begin in 21 days. 

On Tuesday, pilots across the country held “informational pickets” to show management that workers are “united to secure a contract.”
