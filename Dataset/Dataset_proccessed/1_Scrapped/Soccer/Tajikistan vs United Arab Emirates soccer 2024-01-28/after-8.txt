Doha, Qatar: Excitement returns to the Asian Cup 2023 as the Round of 16 kicks off. UAE and Tajikistan face each other with their sights set on victory, aiming to join Australia, who have already secured their spot in the quarter-finals.

Stay tuned as we bring you real-time match highlights and capture the vibrant football atmosphere at the Ahmad bin Ali Stadium!
