KIKOSI Tanzania Vs DR Congo, Results and Live Updates 15 October 2024

Tanzania are playing DR Congo at the Preliminary round of Africa Nations Cup on October 15. The match will kick off 16:00 your local time.

Tanzania national football team and DR Congo national football team (commonly referred to as Congo DR, D.R. Congo or Democratic Republic of the Congo) meet again 3 days after the Preliminary round match which DR Congo won 1-0. Tanzania national football team are aproaching the match after a defeat from DR Congo last Thursday but this time things might turn around differently for them since their defense has been giving solid performances in home games lately having 3 consecutive clean sheets playing as hosts.

DR Congo national football team, on the other hand, are approaching the match in a good mood securing victories in 4 straight games against Tanzania, Etheopia, Guinea and Togo extending their unbeaten streak to 5 matches. They have been flawless defensively having 4 consecutive clean sheets.
