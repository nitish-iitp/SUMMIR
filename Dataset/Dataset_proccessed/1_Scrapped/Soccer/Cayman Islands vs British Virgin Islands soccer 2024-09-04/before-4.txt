It is with heavy hearts that we announce the passing of our beloved 81 year old husband, father, grandfather, brother, uncle and friend, Reuben Aaron. Reuben was from the beautiful island of Trinidad and Tobago. He transitioned into eternal peace on Aug. 14, 2024.

He was preceded in death by his parents, Doris Lona Aaron and Ewath Gladstone Aaron; sisters, Luna and Stella. He is survived by his wife, Josefina Encarnacion- Aaron; daughters, Maribel Aaron-Tufino, Annibel Aaron, Mariana Aaron-Romero; son, Reuben Aaron Jr.; brothers, Alexander, Russel, Samuel and Kenny; granddaughter, Avani Romero; grandsons, Xiaó Romero and Elienai Romero. He is also survived by daughter-in-law, Roshanda Aaron; sons-in-law, Hector Romero and Xavier Tufino; nieces, Lynette Gillette, Michelle Batista and too numerous to mention; nephew, Richie Aaron, and too numerous to mention; special friends, Jeffery Gittens, Tonge, Thomas Samuel, Pilgrim, Brother Francis, Quinto, Esther Laboy, Martha De Jesus, and Eugenio Santiago, other family and friends too numerous to mention.
