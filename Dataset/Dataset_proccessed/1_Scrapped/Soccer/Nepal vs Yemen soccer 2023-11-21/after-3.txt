Nepali football team announced
NEPALNEWS
2023 NOV 21, 14:44,

Goalkeeper Kiran Kumar Limbu will lead the Nepali national football team that will play against Yemen today for World Cup and Asia Cup qualifiers. Alternate goalkeepers are Abhishek Baral and Deep Karki. 6 people including Rohit Chand and Anant Tamang have been selected as defenders of the team. Similarly, there are 5 people in the midfielder and 9 people in the forward.


 The match will be played tonight at Dasharath Stadium from 7 PM. All Nepal Football Association has arranged buses on different routes for the spectators to return home after the match.
