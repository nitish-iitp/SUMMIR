World Cup Qualifier: Nepal defeated by Yemen

TRN Online
 Tue, 21 November 2023
Share
Change Font Sizes:

Kathmandu, Nov 21: Nepal lost to Yemen in their second match of the second round of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers.     

Nepal lost to Yemen by 0-2 in today's Group A match held at the Dasharath Stadium in Tripureshwar. Omar Abdul Al Dahi and Mohammad Abdullah Al Dahi scored one goal each in the 72nd minute and 90th minute respectively of the game.     

This is Nepal's second defeat in a row. Earlier, in the first match of the second round of qualifiers, Nepal lost to the United Arab Emirates (UAE) by 0-4. (RSS)
