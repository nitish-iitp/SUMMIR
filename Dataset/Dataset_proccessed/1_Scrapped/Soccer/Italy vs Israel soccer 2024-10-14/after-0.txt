Nations League: Italy beat Israel 4-1
Di Lorenzo scored twice in fourth day of championship

ROME, 15 October 2024, 10:04

ANSA English Desk

Share

Link copiato

 - ALL RIGHTS RESERVED

Italy beat Israel 4-1 at home late on Monday in the fourth day of the Nations League championship with Luciano Spalletti's side staying at the top of the League A group two with 10 points ahead of France.
    Italy - whose defender Giovanni Di Lorenzo scored twice in the match - now need at least one point from their two remaining group matches to make it to the next round.
