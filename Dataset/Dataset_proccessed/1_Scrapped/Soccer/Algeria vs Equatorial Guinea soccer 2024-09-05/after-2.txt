WESTON, W.Va (WV News) — Lewis County hosted Robert C. Byrd for a Big Ten Conference soccer doubleheader Thursday. The RCB boys broke through late for a 1-0 victory and the Lewis County girls rebounded from down a goal to win 2-1.

In the opening game, the boys teams battled to a scoreless first half thanks in large part to the effort of Lewis County’s defensive backline.
