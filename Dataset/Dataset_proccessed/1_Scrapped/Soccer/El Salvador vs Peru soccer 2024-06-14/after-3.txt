A quick guide to this summer’s Copa América and Euro 2024

Two of the world’s biggest football tournaments are taking place over the next month in the United States and Germany.

Updated July 23, 2024 at 1:42 p.m.
June 14, 2024
2 min read
Save

Dreamstime

By Joe Callaghan Special to the Star

The summer of soccer is upon us. Two years before the beautiful game descends for an historic first-ever FIFA Men’s World Cup on Canadian soil — which is hosting along with Mexico and the United States — the next four weeks will set the table for the feast of football that awaits.

On either side of the Atlantic, two huge, historic tournaments will run pretty much side by side with the cream of three continents, and so many of the biggest names and faces in the game, vying for silverware.
