RRAHMANI SCORES IN KOSOVO WIN

Amir Rrahmani got on the scoresheet for Kosovo on Tuesday evening with the opening goal in their 3-0 victory over Cyprus in the UEFA Nations League. Rrahmani ended up being substituted on 77 minutes.
