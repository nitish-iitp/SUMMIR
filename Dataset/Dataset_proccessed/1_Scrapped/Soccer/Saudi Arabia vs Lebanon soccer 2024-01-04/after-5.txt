NT beats Lebanon in friendly in preparation for the AFC Asian Cup

2024-01-04
18:25:06

The Saudi Arabia national team defeated Lebanon 1-0, in a friendly match on Thursday evening at Al-WakrahClub Stadium.
The match came as part of the final stage of the preparatory program in preparation for the 2023 AFC Asian Cup, which begin on the 12th of January, in Qatar.
Firas Al-Braikan scored the Green's only goal in the match.
For his part, manager Roberto Mancini decided to play the match with two different formations, each of which participated in one half.
