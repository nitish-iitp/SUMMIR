Euro 2024: Full schedule, match dates, groups and format, squads, venues and live streaming info
European Championships 2024 Schedule: Here are the full match fixtures, dates, venues, squads and live streaming info of 2024 Euro to be played in Germany from June 14.
By: Sports Desk
June 11, 2024 08:08 IST
18 min read
Euro 2024: The 24-team tournament will be played across the 10 venues in 10 cities of Germany. (Reuters)

Euro 2024 all you need to know:¬†The 17th edition of the European Championships or Euro will be underway in Germany starting June 14 (June 15 in India).

The tournament will keep with the 24-team format, that has gained popularity, especially since the previous edition that saw the race for the playoffs going right down the wire on the final matchday in the group stages.

Italy walk into the competition as the defending champions, after having bested England at the Wembley Stadium in London to clinch the title in the penalty shootout.

ADVERTISEMENT

Here is a look at everything you need to know about Euro 2024:

Format and Groups

Six groups of four teams shall engage in a single-round robin format in the league stage. The top two teams from each group shall qualify for the Round of 16. The top-four third-ranked teams will make up for the remaining four teams for the first round of knockout matches to be followed by the quarter-finals, semifinals and the final.

The group for Euro 2024 are:

Group A ‚Äì Germany, Scotland, Hungary, Switzerland

Group B ‚Äì Spain, Croatia, Italy, Albania

Group C ‚Äì Slovenia, Denmark, Serbia, England

Group D ‚Äì Poland, Netherlands, Austria, France

Group E ‚Äì Belgium, Slovakia, Romania, Ukraine

Group F ‚Äì Turkey, Georgia, Portugal, Czech Republic

10 venues in Germany shall host the 51 matches throughout the competition. The host cities are Berlin, Dortmund, Munich, Cologne, Stuttgart, Hamburg, Leipzig, Frankfurt, Gelsenkirchen, and Dusseldorf.
