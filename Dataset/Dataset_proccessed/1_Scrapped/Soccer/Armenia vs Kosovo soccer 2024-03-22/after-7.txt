Football: Armenia to host Kosovo in a friendly
 Siranush Ghazanchyan 
Send an email
March 22, 2024, 13:32
 Less than a minute

Armenia will host Kosovo in a friendly today. The match will take place at Hanrapetakan (Republican) Stadium in Yerevan and will kick off at 8 pm local time.

Georgian referees will officiate the match. Irakli Kvirikashvili is appointed as the match Referee, he will be assisted by David Chigogidze and Irakli Kakabadze. Zaven Hovhannisyan has been appointed as the 4th official.
