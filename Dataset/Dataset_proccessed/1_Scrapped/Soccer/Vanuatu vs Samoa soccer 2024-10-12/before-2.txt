Columbia Housing Authority receives $3.5 million for housing project
Katie Grawitch Oct 10, 2024 
Facebook
Twitter
Email
Facebook
Twitter
Email
Print
Copy article link
Save

The Columbia Housing Authority recently received $3.5 million in federal low-income housing tax credits to redevelop the Providence Walkway Apartments.

The tax credits are the final financial component needed to start renovating 25 affordable housing units in Columbia, according to the housing authority's announcement Thursday.
