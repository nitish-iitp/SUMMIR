The 2024 Copa América is the on­go­ing 48th edi­tion of the Copa América, the qua­dren­nial in­ter­na­tional men's soc­cer cham­pi­onship or­ga­nized by South Amer­ica's foot­ball rul­ing body CON­MEBOL. The tour­na­ment is being held in the United States from June 20 to July 14, 2024, and is co-or­ga­nized by CON­CA­CAF. The United States is host­ing the tour­na­ment for the sec­ond time, hav­ing hosted the Copa América Cen­te­nario in 2016. Ar­gentina is the de­fend­ing cham­pion. The final will be played on July 14, 2024, at Hard Rock Sta­dium in Miami Gar­dens, Florida.
