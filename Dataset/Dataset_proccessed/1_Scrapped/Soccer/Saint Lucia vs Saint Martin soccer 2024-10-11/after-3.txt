Hundreds of Saint Lucia football fans are expected to gather at the Daren Sammy Cricket Ground this evening for the CONCACAF Nations League doubleheader starting at 5:00 p.m.

In the first game, Grenada will play against Curacao, while Saint Lucia will take on French Saint Martin at 8:00 p.m.

After securing two wins in Grenada last month against Curacao and host Grenada at the Kirani James National Stadium, Saint Lucia currently leads the table with six points in Group B of League B.
