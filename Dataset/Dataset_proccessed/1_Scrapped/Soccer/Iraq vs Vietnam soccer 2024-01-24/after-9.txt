Iraq beats Vietnam 3-2 in Group D match of AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™

Iraq and Vietnam faced each other on 24 January 2024 in the Group D AFC Asian Cup Qatar 2023™ match at Jassim Bin Hamad Stadium. The kick-off took place at 2:30 pm.

During the first half, in the 43rd minute, player Viet Anh Bui Hoang from Team Vietnam scored the first goal.

Red card for national team player Vietnam, Kuyt van Kang, in minute 4+45 of the first half.

Iraq scores the equalizer against Vietnam through player Rebi Laga in the 47th minute of the second half. Ayman Hussein scores the second goal in the 73rd minute.


A penalty kick is awarded to Iraq after returning to the VAR technology. Ayman Hussein scores the third goal from a penalty kick in the 90th + 11th minute.

The final score was 3-2 in favor of Iraq.
