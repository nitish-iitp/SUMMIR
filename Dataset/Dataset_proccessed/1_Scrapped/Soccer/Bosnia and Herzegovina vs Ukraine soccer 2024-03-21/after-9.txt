How to watch Euro 2024 Qualifiers: Bosnia-Herzegovina vs. Ukraine, time, TV channel, live stream
 share   tweet   email  
FTW Staff 
March 21, 2024 2:20 pm ET

The stakes couldn’t be higher as Bosnia-Herzegovina will host Ukraine in a Euro 2024 qualification playoff semifinal at Stadion Bilino Polje on Thursday.

The victors of this single-leg contest will secure a coveted spot in Group E alongside Belgium, Slovakia, and Romania in the Euro 2024 tournament. However, the journey doesn’t end there, as the winners will also advance to a playoff final to face either Israel or Iceland for a ticket to Germany.

This will be a great day for UEFA Euro 2024 Qualifying, here is everything you need to know to watch and stream the action:

Bosnia-Herzegovina vs. Ukraine
When: Thursday, March 21
Time: 3:45 p.m. ET
TV Channel: Fubo Sports exclusive
Live Stream: fuboTV (watch for free)

Bosnia-Herzegovina possible starting lineup:
Sehic; Gazibegovic, Ahmedhodzic, Hadzikadunic, Kolasinac; Cimirot, Pjanic, Krunic; Hamulic, Dzeko, Demirovic

Ukraine possible starting lineup:
