Nepal secures victory against Netherlands by 9 wickets

KATHMANDU, Feb 17: Nepal secured victory against the Netherlands in the ICC Cricket World Cup League, with half-centuries from Aasif Sekh and Anil Kumar Sah. 




Nepal convincingly defeated the Netherlands by 9 wickets in the match held at Kirtipur ground on Saturday. Chasing the target of 137 runs set by the Netherlands, Nepal reached the milestone in just 15.2 overs with the loss of only one wicket.
