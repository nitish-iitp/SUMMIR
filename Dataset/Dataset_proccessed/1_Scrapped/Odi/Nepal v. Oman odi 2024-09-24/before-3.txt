PREVIEW

The ‘down-and-out’ United Arab Emirates (UAE) will lock horns with the ‘number 3’ United States of America (USA) in the 33rd fixture of the Cricket World Cup League 2 2024. Wanderers Cricket Ground in Windhoek on September 24, 2024, 1:00 PM (IST). The game will be a must-win clash for both teams owing to their different positions in the tournament standings.

UAE are enduring a miserable run of form in their campaign, winning only one game while losing four in their five games so far. They only have two points to their name in the table. A win in this game will help them gain a spot in the table, surpassing Nepal. 

Speaking of the United States of America (USA), they have enjoyed an amazing campaign in the tournament. They have won five matches, alongside only two defeats in the seven games they have played. The Monank Patel-led side has 10 points to their name, and a win in this game will take them to the second spot in the table, surpassing the Netherlands.

As the team gears up for the upcoming clash, all eyes will be on the thrilling competition set to unfold on the cricket field. Ahead of the game, here is a look at the Dream11 predictions, the fantasy tips, the pitch report, the top player picks, and the best-predicted XIs for the fantasy contests of the match.
