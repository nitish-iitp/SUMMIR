Match Preview

In Match 31 of the ICC CWC League 2, Namibia faced the USA and lost by 7 wickets. Namibia batted first, scoring 286 runs after losing 9 wickets in their allotted 50 overs. Michael van Lingen led the innings with a brisk 67 runs off 44 balls. Jonathan Smit contributed a steady 60 runs from 84 balls, while Jan Nicol Loftie Eaton added a notable 49 runs from 47 balls. Despite setting a competitive target, Namibia struggled to defend their total.

In Match 33, the United Arab Emirates suffered a heavy defeat against the USA, losing by 136 runs. The USA posted an impressive 339 runs for 4 wickets in their 50 overs. Aayan Afzal Khan was the standout bowler for the UAE, taking 2 wickets for 49 runs in 10 overs. Ali Naseer and Junaid Siddique each claimed a wicket, finishing with figures of 1 for 57 and 1 for 69, respectively. Chasing a daunting target, the UAE faltered and was bowled out for just 203 runs in 36.2 overs. Rahul Chopra led the batting with 52 runs off 44 balls, supported by Asif-Khan Lala’s 51 and Basil Hameed’s quick 50. However, these efforts were not enough to secure a win.
