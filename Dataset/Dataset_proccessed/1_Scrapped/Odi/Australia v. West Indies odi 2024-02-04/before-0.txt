Australia vs West Indies ODI Series: Schedule, Squads, Venues, Live Streaming & Telecast Details
Australia and West Indies lock horns in the three-match ODI series starting from February 2 at the Melbourne Cricket Ground.
Sumit Channa Published: 31 Jan 2024 15:13:PM

New Delhi: After an exciting two-match Test series, Australia and West Indies lock horns in the ODI series starting from February 2, Friday. Australia registered a commanding 10-wicket win over the visitors in the first Test match at Adelaide while the West Indies bounced back terrifically in the second Test match registering an exceptional 8-run win in Brisbane.

After the commencement of the ODI leg of the white-ball series, both the sides take part in the T20I leg which will mark the culmination of the tour. After their outing in the longest format of the game, both the sides shift gears towards the one-day format where they will need to alter their way of playing. While the hosts would want to begin the series with a win, the visitors too would love to continue their dominance they displayed in the last Test match.

Australia vs West Indies ODI Squads 2024

Australia ODI Squad: Steve Smith (captain), Travis Head, Sean Abbott, Xavier Bartlett, Jake Fraser-McGurk, Cameron Green, Aaron Hardie, Josh Inglis (wicketkeeper), Marnus Labuschagne, Lance Morris, Matt Short, Adam Zampa, Will Sutherland.

West Indies ODI Squad: Shai Hope (captain/wicketkeeper), Alzarri Joseph, Alick Athanaze, Teddy Bishop, Keacy Carty, Roston Chase, Matthew Forde, Justin Greaves, Kavem Hodge, Tevin Imlach, Gudakesh Motie, Kjorn Ottley, Romario Shepherd, Oshane Thomas, Hayden Walsh Jr.

Australia vs West Indies ODI schedule- When to watch AUS vs WI ODI matches?

First ODI – Friday, February 2, 2024 at the MCG, Melbourne. Match starts at 9 AM IST.

Second ODI: Sunday, February 4 2024 at the SCG, Sydney. Match starts at 9 AM IST.

Third ODI: Tuesday, February 6 2024 at the Manuka Oval, Canberra. Match starts at 9 AM IST.

Live Streaming & Telecast Details of Australia vs West Indies ODI matches

The Australia vs West Indies ODI series will be broadcast live on the Star Sports Network in India. For the fans who won’t be able to travel to the stadium for the games, they can tune into Disney + Hotstar as the series will be live streamed on it.
