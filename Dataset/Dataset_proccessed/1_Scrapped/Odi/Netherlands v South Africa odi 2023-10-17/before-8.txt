SA vs NED Weather Report, World Cup 2023: Rain likely to play spoilsport in Dharamshala
By Tarkesh Jha  
October 16, 2023, 6:41:00 PM IST (Updated)
2 Min Read
South Africa will aim to continue their winning streak when they face Netherlands at the Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association (HPCA) Stadium in Dharamshala on October 17, Tuesday.



The Proteas have recorded two wins in as many games so far and have the perfect opportunity to further their impressive form against the Dutchmen, who are yet to open their account in the tournament.

Also Read: SA vs NED World Cup 2023 Preview: Predicted XI, key players, head to head, pitch report, where to watch

The first innings of the match between Australia and Sri Lanka at the  Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium in Lucknow on Monday was interrupted due to a brief shower. Hence, there is uncertainty over whether weather will play spoilsport in Dharamshala too.

Here is a detailed report of what to expect from a climatic perspective during the course of the game.

Dharamshala - Weather Report, October 17, 2023

According to accuweather.com, the temperature during afternoon, which is when the game is slated to start, will be around 16°C. The weather will be partly sunny with thunderstorms in certain parts of the city. Wind will be blowing towards south-southwest at 9 km/hr with the wind gusts blowing at 26 km/hr. Rain of around 8.2 mm is expected with 55% probability of precipitation and 79% cloud cover. Humidity will be on the higher side at 66%.

The temperature will cool down to 12°C by evening, with wind changing its direction to northeast at 9 km/hr. Cloud cover will cut down to 19% with a minimal 3% probability of precipitation. The wind gusts will blow at 22 km/hr and the dew point will be at 6°. Humidity will ease out, but only slightly, to 62%. Overall, there are chances of the first innings being affected by rains.

Predicted Playing XI 




South Africa: Temba Bavuma (c), Quinton de Kock (wk), Rassie van der Dussen, Heinrich Klaasen, Aiden Markram, Keshav Maharaj, David Miller, Marco Jansen, Kagiso Rabada, Lungi Ngidi, Tabraiz Shamsi




Netherlands: Colin Ackermann, Vikramjit Singh, Max ODowd, Scott Edwards (c & wk), Bas de Leede, Teja Nidamanuru, Ryan Klein, Sybrand Engelbrecht, Roelof van der Merwe, Aryan Dutt, Shariz Ahmad



Where to Watch

The match will begin at 2 pm IST and it will be telecast live on the Star Sports Network. It will be streamed digitally on the Disney+Hotstar application.
