1. Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad

The grandeur of Narendra Modi Stadium Ahmedabad simply cannot be justified in words. Being the largest cricket stadium in the world, it is often the venue for cricket tournaments. The stadium can accommodate 132,000 viewers in well-designed seats. If you are travelling with a personal vehicle, need not worry. The stadium has a capacity of 13,000 vehicles in the parking zone. 


Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad

Sardar Patel Stadium is now renovated and named Narendra Modi Stadium. Interestingly, the stadium has 76 executive suites and 4 spacious dressing rooms. Many high-profile matches are often held and you must simply not miss out on watching them from here!


Location: Stadium Rd, Parvati Nagar, Motera, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380005
