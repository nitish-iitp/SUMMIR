Match 23 of the ODI World Cup 2023 is set to be played at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai, as South Africa will lock horns with Bangladesh. With South Africa's recent rollercoaster performance in the tournament and Bangladesh's quest for redemption, this match promises to be a thriller.

The Proteas entered the tournament with mixed results, winning matches against Sri Lanka and Australia but stumbling against the Netherlands. However, they bounced back emphatically with a commanding 229-run victory over England, displaying their true potential. The absence of captain Temba Bavuma in their previous clash didn't deter them, with Aiden Markram leading the team effectively.

On the other side, Bangladesh have been on a rough journey, facing defeats against England, New Zealand, and India after a promising start with a win over Afghanistan. They've encountered a loss by 137 runs, 8 wickets, and 7 wickets in their last three games, necessitating a massive shift in momentum to challenge South Africa.

In the South African camp, key players to watch include the explosive Heinrich Klaasen, who smashed a breathtaking 109 runs against England. Aiden Markram, the stand-in skipper, has been in scintillating form, and Marco Jansen, the young all-rounder, is riding high on confidence after a stellar performance in the last match. Kagiso Rabada, the pace spearhead, adds depth to their formidable lineup.

For Bangladesh, Litton Das and Najmul Hossain Shanto will play crucial roles in setting up a challenging total. Shakib Al Hasan's experience and match-winning ability, even at less than 100% fitness, can't be underestimated. Shoriful Islam, the left-arm quick bowler, promises to bring life to the contest with his movement off the pitch and through the air.

The renowned batting-friendly pitches at the Wankhede Stadium are expected to provide an exciting contest, with opportunities for both batters and bowlers as the game progresses.Â
