Match Preview –

The ongoing ODI series between Afghanistan and South Africa in the UAE has captured the cricket world’s attention. The first match was a nail-biter, showcasing Afghanistan’s dominance across all departments. They made history by securing their first-ever win against South Africa. Their comprehensive performance in batting, bowling, and fielding highlighted their all-round strength. With this victory, Afghanistan leads the series 1-0.

As the teams gear up for the second match, South Africa will be eager to bounce back and level the series. On the other hand, Afghanistan will be determined to clinch the series with another win. Fans are eagerly awaiting what promises to be another thrilling encounter, as both sides are prepared to give their best. The stage is set for an exciting showdown in the UAE.

In the 1st ODI of Afghanistan’s tour of South Africa, held in the UAE, Afghanistan won by 6 wickets. South Africa, batting first, was dismissed for 106 runs in 33.3 overs. Wiaan Mulder top-scored for South Africa with 52 runs off 84 balls. Bjorn Fortuin added 16 runs from 34 balls, and Tony de Zorzi contributed 11 runs off 18 balls. Despite their efforts, South Africa struggled against a disciplined Afghan bowling attack.

Afghanistan’s bowlers delivered an exceptional performance. Fazalhaq Farooqi took 4 wickets for 35 runs in 7 overs, Allah Ghazanfar claimed 3 wickets for 20 runs in 10 overs, and Rashid Khan added 2 wickets for 30 runs in 8.3 overs. In their chase, Afghanistan reached 107 for 4 in 26 overs, with Gulbadin Naib remaining unbeaten on 34 runs from 27 balls. Azmatullah Omarzai scored 25 runs off 36 balls, and Hashmatullah Shahidi contributed 16 runs from 29 balls. South Africa’s bowlers, including Bjorn Fortuin with 2 wickets for 22 runs in 9 overs, Lungi Ngidi with 1 wicket for 6 runs in 4 overs, and Aiden Markram with 1 wicket for 21 runs in 3 overs, couldn’t stop Afghanistan from completing the chase comfortably.
