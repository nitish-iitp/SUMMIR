Match Preview

In the second T20I of the South Africa Women’s tour of Pakistan, Pakistan clinched a 13-run victory. They set a daunting target of 181 for 4 after their 20 overs. The innings saw standout performances from Muneeba Ali, who scored 45 off 34 balls, and captain Sana Fatima, who added 37 runs from 23 balls. Nida Dar also contributed with a brisk 29 from 21 deliveries. South Africa struggled to contain Pakistan’s total despite efforts from their bowlers: Tumi Sekhukhune claimed 2 wickets for 30 runs in 3 overs, Sune Luus took 1 for 10 in 2 overs, and Annerie Dercksen added 1 for 23 in 3 overs.

Chasing the target, South Africa Women ended at 168 for 4 in their allotted 20 overs. Sune Luus led the South African charge with a formidable 53 off 29 balls, supported by Laura Wolvaardt’s 36 from 25 deliveries and Chloe Tryon’s quickfire 30 off 16 balls. Despite these contributions, the South African batting lineup fell short of the required total.

Pakistan’s bowlers played a crucial role in securing the win. Nashra Sandhu was particularly effective, taking 2 wickets for 20 runs in 4 overs, while Sadia Iqbal also contributed with 2 wickets for 27 runs from 4 overs. Tuba Hassan, although not taking any wickets, delivered a solid performance, conceding 21 runs in her 2-over spell. Pakistan’s all-around performance with both bat and ball proved too strong for South Africa, securing a well-deserved victory in this closely contested match.
