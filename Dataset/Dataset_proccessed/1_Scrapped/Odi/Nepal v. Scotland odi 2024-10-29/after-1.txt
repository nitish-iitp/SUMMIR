KATHMANDU, Oct 29: Nepal are set to face Scotland today in the ICC Men's Cricket World Cup League-2 at the Grand Prairie Cricket Stadium, Dallas. The match is scheduled to begin at 9:15 PM (Nepal Time).




Nepal stand in a very weak position in the ODI format. In the first match, Nepal lost to the USA by three wickets. Therefore, the Nepali team wants to improve their score with the goal of winning today’s match.
