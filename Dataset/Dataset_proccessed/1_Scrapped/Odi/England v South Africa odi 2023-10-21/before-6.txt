South Africa vs England: Five most popular Springboks WAGS

Meet five popular Springboks WAGS ahead of the team’s Rugby World Cup semi-final match against England on Saturday 20 October.

By Alicia Naidoo
21-10-23 11:28
in Celeb News

The Springboks are set to play England in the Rugby World Cup semi-final on Saturday 21 October.

FIVE POPULAR SPRINGBOKS WAGS
1. RACHEL KOLISI – SPRINGBOKS FIRST LADY

The Springboks “first lady” is Rachel Kolisi, wife of Springboks captain Siya Kolisi. The pair, who met at a dinner party in Stellenbosch, Westerm Cape in 2012, got hitched in 2016. The loving couple have two children together — Nicholas (9) and Keziah (6).

Rachel has a whopping 374k followers on Instagram.

ALSO READ: Five years later! Rachel Kolisi shares timeline of love with Siya [photos]

2. ANLIA ETZEBETH

Eben Etzebeth married singer and actress Anlia at the La Paris estate in Franschhoek, Western Cape in February this year. Anlia and Eben met in Melkbosstrand, a coastal village north of Cape Town, in October 2021 – according to The Citizen.

Anlia has a total of 115k followers on Instagram.

4. MARISE POLLARD

Marise Pollard is the wife of fly-half Handre Pollard. The pair reportedly began dating in 2013 and tied the knot in 2017 at the Cavalli Estate, Cape Winelands.

Marise has 83.3k Instagram followers.

4. LAYLA KOLBE

Layla Kolbe is the wife of Cheslin Kolbe. The pair have been together since 2013 and met at the DHL Newlands stadium. The pair have three children. Layla has 81.3k followers on Instagram.

5. MINE DE KLERK

Faf de Klerk married his fiancée Miné van Niekerk at La Paris Estate in Franschhoek in October 2022. The pair have been together for a total of six years. 

The pair met through mutual friends back in 2015 and Miné reportedly invited him to a wedding thereafter. Their love grew over the years. Faf proposed to Miné in May 2022.

Miné has 23.3k followers on Instagram.
