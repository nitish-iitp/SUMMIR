Netherlands Hosts Tri-Series with Scotland and Papua New Guinea in August 2024

The Netherlands women’s team led by skipper Babette de Leede is hosting Papua New Guinea and Scotland for a tri-series. They will play a four-match One Day series and a two-match T20I series in August, later this year.

Netherlands Hosts Tri-Series with Scotland and Papua New Guinea in August 2024. Credit: KNCB

Scotland and the Netherlands announced their squads for the upcoming tri-series earlier this month. On the other hand, Papua New Guinea announced their squad for the One Day series, earlier in the day. The series will commence on 5th August 2024 and will conclude on 16th August 2024.

Scotland Squad for the tri-series in the Netherlands

Chloe Abel, Abbi Aitken-Drummond, Olivia Bell, Darcey Carter, Priyanaz Chatterji, Maryam Faisal, Gabriella Fontenla, Katherine Fraser, Saskia Horley, Lorna Jack, Abtaha Maqsood, Megan McColl, Niamh Muir, Nayma Sheikh, and Ellen Watson.

Netherlands Squad for the upcoming tri-series

Babette de Leede (wk) ©, Annemijn van Beuge, Annemijn Thomson, Carlijn van Koolwijk, Caroline de Lange, Eva Lynch, Frederique Overdijk, Hannah Landheer, Iris Zwilling, Merel Dekeling, Myrthe van den Raad, Phebe Molkenboer, Robin Schmidt, Robine Rijke, and Silver Siegers.

Papua New Guinea Squad for the upcoming tri-series in the Netherlands

BrendaTau ©, Sibona Jimmy, Tanya Ruma, Lakshmi Rajadurai, Pauke Siaka, Isabel Toua, Naoani Vare, Geua Tom, Kevau Frank, Henao Thomas, Vicky Ara’a, Buruka Vicky, Dika Lohia, and Mahuta Jayphert.
