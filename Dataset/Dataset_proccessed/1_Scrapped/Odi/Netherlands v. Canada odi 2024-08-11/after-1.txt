PREVIEW

Netherlands and Canada will face each other in the 19th match of the ongoing CWC League 2 2024. The game will be played at the Sportpark Duivesteijn in Voorburg. Both teams are placed among the top four teams in the tournament standings. So, it is going be a very crucial fixture for both teams to strengthen their position among the top four teams.

Canada are in the second spot of the points table with four successive wins in the four matches they have played. A win in this game will help them move to the top spot with an addition of 2 more points on their credit. Besides that, it will also be the fifth successive win for Saad Bin Zafar’s men.

On the other hand, the Netherlands have played four matches in the tournament. They are in the fourth spot of the points table, winning and losing two matches each. Scott Edwards-led side is coming with an 8-wicket win against Nepal in the last game they played earlier this year.
