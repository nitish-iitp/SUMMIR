Sri Lanka announced the 16-member squad for the upcoming three-match ODI series against the West Indies at home. Charith Asalanka will lead the Sri Lanka Cricket Team in the ODI series, and they would like to extend their winning streak against the Caribbean side at home.

The three-match Test series between Sri Lanka and the West Indies is all set to get underway on October 20 at the Pallekele International Cricket Stadium in Kandy. The Pallekele International Cricket Stadium will host all the matches of the ODI series against the West Indies.
