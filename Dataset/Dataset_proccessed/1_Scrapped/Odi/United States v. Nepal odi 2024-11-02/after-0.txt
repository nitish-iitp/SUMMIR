When and Where Will USA vs Nepal ICC Men's Cricket World Cup League 2 Match?

The USA vs Nepal ICC Men's Cricket World Cup League 2 match will be played at the Prairie View Cricket Complex, Houston. The USA vs Nepal CWC League 2 match has a scheduled time of 09:00 PM IST (Indian Standard Time).
