USA vs Nepal 42nd Match ICC CWCL2 2024 Highlights

Watch the highlights of USA vs Nepal 42nd Match ICC CWCL2 2024 - ICC Cricket World Cup League 2 - The 2023 - 2027 Cricket World Cup Qualification process of the 42nd ODI match played between Nepal and United States of America at Grand Prairie Stadium, Dallas in 02nd November 2024.



Saurabh Netravalkar captured four-for © USA Cricket / Twitter 


Monank Patel, Saiteja Mukkamalla and Harmeet Singh's fifties before Bowlers and Saurabh Netravalka's four-for helped to USA comprehensive 37-run victory over Nepal in the 42nd match of a ICC Cricket World Cup League 2.
 


USA scored 281-8 in 50 overs with top scorer by Saiteja Mukkamalla struck 75 off 87-balls including 4-fours & a six.


Monank Patel cracked a 67 off 81-balls including 5-fours & a six, Harmeet Singh hammered a 59 off 44-balls included 3-sixes & 3-fours with strike rate of 134.09, Aaron Jones hit 26 off 20-balls contained 5-fours with strike rate of 130 and Sanjay Krishnamurthi 15.


Nepal best bowler by Gulsan Jha picked up 3-wickets, Sandeep Lamichhane, Sompal Kami both took 2-wickets and one for Kushal Bhurtel.


Nepal scored 244 for all-out in 49.5 overs with top scorer by Anil Sah struck 52 off 80-balls including 4-fours & a six.


Sompal Kami hammered a 46 off 27-balls including 4-fours & 2-sixes with strike rate of 170.37, Kushal Malla scored 33 off 54-balls included one-six & a four.


Dipendra Singh Airee scored 31 off 38-balls including one-six & a four, Kushal Bhurtel scored 29 off 51-balls included 2-fours & a six and Aarif Sheikh blasted a 21 off 13-balls contained 2-sixes with strike rate of 161.53.


USA best bowler by Saurabh Netravalkar 4-47, Nosthush Kenjige, Shadley van Schalkwyk both took 2-wickets and one for Harmeet Singh - Jasdeep Singh.


Saurabh Netravalkar named Player of the match for his pace bowling performance to claimed a 4-wickets for 47-runs in 9.5-overs including a maiden.
