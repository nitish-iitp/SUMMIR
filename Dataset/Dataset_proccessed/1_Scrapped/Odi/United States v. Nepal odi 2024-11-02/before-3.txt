Hong Kong Sixes 2024 schedule
Posted by

The Hong Kong Sixes 2024 will be broadcast on the Star Sports Network in India.

Hong Kong Sixes is back on the 1st of November with South Africa taking on the hosts Hong Kong in the Inaugural Match. There are 10 matches scheduled per day. The Final Match of the competition will be played on the 3rd of November, 2024. In this article, we will give you full details about Hong Kong Sixes schedule and Hong Kong Sixes live streaming

The tournament will be played in a Five Overs per side format. Each team will be able to field only 6 players. The bowlers can bowl only 1 over per match. Hence, the captains will have to be smart in picking up the bowlers according to the match situation.

Wides and No Balls will be dealt with a 2 run penalty instead of 1 run. The tournament rules also state that all 6 players will have to dismissed for the opposition teams to effect an all out situation.

Where to watch Hong Kong sixes live

The Hong Kong Sixes 2024 will be broadcast on the Star Sports Network in India. It will be live streamed on the FanCode app and website in India. The coverage begins at 5:45am India time.
