PREVIEW

The 28th ODI of the CWC League 2 2024 will see the clash between Nepal and Oman at the Maple Leaf North-West Ground (A), King City, on Wednesday, 18 September 2024.

This match promises to be a thrilling encounter, with both teams having strong bowling units. Nepal’s recent dominance over Oman in head-to-head encounters gives them a slight edge. However, Oman’s experienced all-rounders could pose a significant challenge. 

As the team gears up for the upcoming clash, all eyes will be on the thrilling competition set to unfold on the cricket field. Ahead of the game, here is a look at the Dream11 predictions, the fantasy tips, the pitch report, the top player picks, and the best-predicted XIs for the fantasy contests of the match.

PITCH REPORT

The Wanderers Cricket Ground in Windhoek has a pitch known for its pace and bounce. Pace bowlers can expect favorable conditions with good bounce, while batting conditions are average, offering a balanced challenge. Spin bowlers will also find the pitch suitable, as it provides average conditions for spin as well. Overall, the pitch supports both pace and spin effectively.
