Match Preview

The sixth match of the ICC CWC League 2 between the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and Scotland was unfortunately abandoned. Due to unforeseen circumstances, neither team was able to take the field, leading to the match being called off. This means both teams will have to wait for their next opportunities to compete in the tournament. As a result, the standings remain unchanged for now, and UAE and Scotland will look to their upcoming fixtures to earn crucial points.

In Match 25 of the ICC CWC League 2, the United States of America defeated Namibia by 6 wickets. Namibia batted first and scored 199/9 in their 50 overs. The USA bowlers performed well, with Juanoy Drysdale taking 2 wickets for 16 runs in 6 overs, Milind Kumar picking up 2/18 in 5 overs, and Saurabh Netravalkar contributing 2/41 in 10 overs. Their effective bowling helped restrict Namibia to a manageable total.

The USA then chased down the target comfortably, reaching 200/4 in 41.3 overs. Captain Monank Patel led with a solid 72 from 82 balls. Saiteja Mukkamalla remained unbeaten on 59 from 71 balls, and Sushant Modani added 36 from 36 balls. The team’s strong batting performance ensured a win with more than eight overs to spare.
