WANDERERS CLUB LEADS IN BCA ELITE (3-DAY) DIVISION AS REVISED DATES FOR SERIES 7 MATCHES REVEALED

 

The Wanderers club are currently in first position on 82 points as the seventh (7th) series of matches in the BCA Elite (3-day) Division commence on November 2, 2024.

 

Due to the upcoming International Home Series matches (West Indies vs. England) at Kensington Oval on November 6, 9 and 10, 2024, the dates for the upcoming series of local matches have been revised.
