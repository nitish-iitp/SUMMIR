Match Preview

In Match 29 of the ICC CWC League 2, Namibia faced a narrow defeat against the United Arab Emirates by just one wicket. Batting first, Namibia scored 313 runs, losing all their wickets in 50 overs. Michael van Lingen led the team with an impressive 107 runs off 85 balls. Jean-Pierre Kotze added a solid 64 runs from 53 deliveries, while Jan Nicol Loftie Eaton contributed 54 runs from 63 balls. Despite their strong batting display, Namibia couldn’t secure the win. In response, the UAE chased down the target, finishing with 316 runs for 9 wickets in 49.3 overs. Jan Nicol Loftie Eaton excelled with the ball, taking 4 wickets for 79 runs in 8.3 overs.

In Match 27, the United States of America secured a dominant 10-wicket victory over the UAE. Batting first, the UAE struggled, managing only 106 runs before being bowled out in 31.2 overs. Jasdeep Singh stood out for the USA, claiming 4 wickets for just 18 runs in 5 overs. Milind Kumar and Nosthush Kenjige each took 2 wickets. Chasing a modest target, the USA reached 107 runs without losing a wicket in just 15.5 overs. Andries Gous led the charge with 66 runs, while Smit Patel added 38 runs.
