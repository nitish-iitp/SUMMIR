Netherlands vs Canada(NED vs CAN) Match 22 Match Preview

In Match 21 of the ICC CWC League 2, the Netherlands defeated the United States of America by 19 runs. The Netherlands batted first, scoring 237 runs for the loss of 7 wickets in 50 overs. During the USA’s innings, they were all out for 218 runs in 49.5 overs. Vivian Kingma was the standout bowler for the Netherlands, taking 3 wickets for 47 runs in 9 overs. Paul van Meekeren supported with 2 wickets for 32 runs in 9.5 overs, and Shariz Ahmad also took 2 wickets, conceding 35 runs in 7 overs. The Netherlands’ disciplined bowling ensured their victory in a competitive match.
