Wanindu Hasaranga ruled out from ODI series against India

India tour of Sri Lanka 2024

By Kanishka Sanjeewa -
03/08/2024
628

Sri Lankan wrist-spinner Wanindu Hasaranga has been ruled out from the ODI series against India.

Hasaranga picked up a hamstring injury while bowling in the first ODI against India yesterday (2nd August).

>>Wellalage makes an impact

Jeffrey Vandersay will replace Hasaranga in the squad for the remaining 2 ODIs in the series.

>>Sri Lanka Women’s squad announced for Ireland Tour

The second and third ODI of the series between Sri Lanka and India will be played on the 4th and 7th August 2024 at the R. Premadasa International Cricket Stadium in Colombo.
