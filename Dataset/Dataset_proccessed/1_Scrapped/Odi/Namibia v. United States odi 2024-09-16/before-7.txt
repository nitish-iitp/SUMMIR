Match Preview

In Match 18 of the ICC CWC League 2, Scotland defeated Namibia by 138 runs. Scotland set a strong total of 301/6 in their 50 overs. Namibia’s bowlers struggled, with Shaun Fouche taking 3 wickets for 60 runs in 10 overs. Tangeni Lungameni and Ben Shikongo each took 1 wicket, giving away 64 runs in 10 overs and 64 runs in 9 overs, respectively. In their innings, Namibia was all out for 163 in 40.1 overs. Gerhard Erasmus, the captain, scored 53 runs from 73 balls. Zane Green added 28 runs from 35 balls, and Malan Kruger contributed 27 runs from 42 balls. Despite their efforts, Namibia couldn’t chase down Scotland’s total and suffered a heavy defeat.

In Match 24, the Netherlands beat the United States by 27 runs. The Netherlands was bowled out for 206 in 49.1 overs. Harmeet Singh led the USA’s bowling attack with 2 wickets for 25 runs in 10 overs. Shadley van Schalkwyk and Nosthush Kenjige each took 2 wickets, conceding 32 runs in 8 overs and 33 runs in 8.1 overs, respectively. During the chase, the USA was all out for 179 in 45.5 overs. Monank Patel, the captain, top-scored with 66 runs off 79 balls. Harmeet Singh and Shayan Jahangir added 20 runs each. Despite their contributions, the USA fell short and lost by 27 runs.
