Word of the Day for School Assembly 3 November 2023: Modest

Here is the word of the day for 3 November  2023 along with its meaning, synonym, and antonyms.

Meaning, Example, Antonyms, and Synonyms for Word of the Day




Meaning

	

The word “Modest” refers to being humble, not drawing attention to yourself, and not seeking excessive praise or recognition. When a person is said to be modest, he/she is not arrogant but rather is down to earth. Modest is often associated with humility, simplicity, and lack of pretending something.




Synonyms

	

Humble, Moderate, Prudent, Quiet, Simple, Silent, Temperate




Antonyms

	

Brave, Complex, Complicated, Intricate, Arrogant, Assured, Bold, Conceited
