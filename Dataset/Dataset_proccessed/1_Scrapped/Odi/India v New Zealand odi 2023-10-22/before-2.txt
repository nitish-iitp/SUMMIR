India vs New Zealand Head to Head in ICC ODI World Cup and ODI

Head to Head in World Cups

In the world of cricket, rivalries often take center stage, and one such exciting yet friendly rivalry is between India and New Zealand. These two cricketing powerhouses have a long and thrilling history of clashes in various formats, with the most significant showdown occurring in the 2019 ICC World Cup. As they prepare to face each other once again in the 2023 Cricket World Cup, fans are eagerly anticipating an intense battle.

The forthcoming match is set to take place on October 22, 2023, at the Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association Stadium in Dharamshala. Both India and New Zealand are among the favorites to clinch the World Cup trophy, making this encounter even more intriguing.

When it comes to head-to-head statistics, India holds a slight edge over New Zealand in all three major formats of cricket: Test, ODI, and T20I. However, their ODI encounters in World Cup competitions have been particularly memorable.

Overall head to head

In the grand scope of their head-to-head encounters across various formats of cricket, India and New Zealand have shared a longstanding rivalry. Over the years, they’ve faced each other 116 times, making for a compelling cricketing history.

In this extensive cricketing saga that spans from 1975 to 2023, both teams have displayed their prowess and determination on 116 occasions. These encounters have led to numerous unforgettable moments, epic clashes, and nail-biting finishes.

When you delve into the statistics, India has emerged victorious in 58 of these matches, showcasing their remarkable cricketing abilities. On the other side, New Zealand has claimed victory in 50 encounters, proving their resilience and skill on the field. This balance of wins and losses reflects the competitive nature of their rivalry.

Head to Head in World Cups

The rivalry between India and New Zealand may not be as widely celebrated as some other cricket rivalries, but whenever these two teams face off, it leads to thrilling matches.
In the ICC Cricket World Cup, India and New Zealand have squared off on nine occasions.
New Zealand holds the upper hand with five victories over India. India has secured three wins, while one match ended without a result.
Notably, these two teams did not have any encounters in the World Cup between 2003 and 2019.

In ICC Cricket World Cup matches, India and New Zealand have crossed paths nine times. New Zealand holds a better record, having emerged victorious in five encounters, while India has triumphed in three matches. One match ended without a result.

Their most recent meeting took place during the 2019 World Cup, where New Zealand defeated India by 18 runs, sending shockwaves through the cricketing world. It was a heart-wrenching defeat for India, and fans have been eagerly awaiting their next clash.

This rich history of clashes between the two teams extends back to the 1975 Prudential Cup, where they faced each other for the first time. Over the years, they have produced thrilling matches and unforgettable moments.

As the two cricketing giants prepare to face off once again in the 2023 World Cup, all eyes will be on the likes of Rohit Sharma, who will lead India, and Tom Latham, who is expected to captain New Zealand in the absence of Kane Williamson.

Cricket enthusiasts and fans of both teams are eager to see how this epic rivalry unfolds in the upcoming match. With a legacy of captivating encounters, India vs. New Zealand is sure to be a contest worth watching, and cricket aficionados around the world are awaiting the showdown with bated breath.
