Dr. Nabil Abdel Jabbar
Professor
PhD, University of Michigan-Ann Arbor, United States

Dr. Nabil Abdel Jabbar's areas of specialization include process modeling, simulation, advanced control, process design, and optimization. He has diverse academic, industrial, and administrative work experiences at different institutions. He published numerous research articles in high-impact factor journals and conference proceedings. He held/attended many professional workshops. Dr. Abdel Jabbar received several scientific and research grants awards. He served as a guest co-editor and a member of the editorial board of international journals.
