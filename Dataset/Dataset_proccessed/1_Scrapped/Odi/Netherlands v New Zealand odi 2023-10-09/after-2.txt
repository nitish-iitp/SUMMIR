New Zealand vs Netherlands Highlights, World Cup 2023

New Zealand vs Netherlands Highlights, World Cup 2023: New Zealand made it two out of two in ODI 6 of the ongoing Cricket World Cup 2023, defeating Netherlands by 99 runs in Hyderabad, on Monday. Chasing a target of 323 runs, Netherlands were bowled out for 223 runs in 46.3 overs. Mitchell Santner was in fiery form, bagging a five-wicket haul for New Zealand.
