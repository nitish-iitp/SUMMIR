Bangladesh vs Sri Lanka 1st ODI Live Streaming: When and where to watch BAN vs SL live?
BAN vs SL 1st ODI Live Streaming: Here is you can watch the live streaming of the upcoming Bangladesh vs Sri Lanka ODIs in India.
By: Sports Desk
Updated: March 13, 2024 12:07 IST
2 min read
BAN vs SL Live Streaming, 1st ODI: Najmul Shanto will lead the hosts in the series. (AP)

Bangladesh will host Sri Lanka in the second leg of the bilateral series for three One-Day Internationals, starting Wednesday. The first ODI will be played in Chattogram, which will also be hosting the remaining games.

The teams are coming off an engaging three-match T20I series which the Lankans won by a 2-1 margin. While the visitors won the first ODI by 28 runs, Bangladesh clawed back with an eight-wicket win in the second. In the series decider, Sri Lanka edged out Bangladesh by a narrow three-run margin after scoring 206 with fifties from Sadeera Samarawickrama and Kusal Mendis.

The Sri Lanka-Bangladesh rivalry has intensified in recent years, despite the former‚Äôs commanding record over the Tigers. Sri Lanka have won 42 of the 54 games against Bangladesh with only 10 defeats.

When will Bangladesh vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI take place?

The Bangladesh vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI will take place on March 13, 2024, Wednesday, 2:00 PM IST.

Where will Bangladesh vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI take place?

The Bangladesh vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI will take place at the Zahur Ahmed Chowdhury Stadium in Chattogram.

Which TV Channel will broadcast the Bangladesh vs Sri Lanka, 1st ODI live?

The Sri Lanka vs Bangladesh ODIs will be live-streamed on FanCode app and website.
