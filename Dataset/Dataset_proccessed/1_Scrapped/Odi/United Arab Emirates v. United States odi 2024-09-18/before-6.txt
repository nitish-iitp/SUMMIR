Infibeam Avenues Limited completed the Spin-Off of Odigma Consultancy Solutions Private Limited.
September 17, 2024
 Share
Infibeam Avenues Limited agreed to Spin-Off 69.44% stake in Odigma Consultancy Solutions Private Limited on August 8, 2023. Infibeam Avenues Limited will transfer and vest its Global Top Level Domain in Odigma, As per the terms, each shareholder of Infibeam Avenues Limited will receive 1 share of odgima for every 89 shares they held in the company. Upon completion, the promoters and promoters group will hold 30.56% stake in Odigma. The turnover of the demerged division for the financial year ended March 31, 2023, was INR 19.469 million. The Scheme is subject to the receipt of requisite approvals from the Securities and Exchange Board of India (?SEBI?), National Company Law Tribunal (?NCLT?), BSE Limited (?BSE?), National Stock Exchange of India Limited (?NSE?) and other statutory and regulatory authorities, and the respective shareholders and creditors, under applicable law. The board has approved the transaction. As of August 30, 2024, National Company Law Tribunal has sanctioned the scheme. The record date to determine entitlement of shares to shareholders has been fixed at September 11, 2024.

Company Profile
Infibeam Avenues Limited is an India-based financial technology (Fintech) company. The Company offers digital payment solutions and enterprise software platform to large enterprises and government globally. It offers digital payment solutions under the brand name CCAvenue and enterprise software solutions under the brand name BuildaBazaar. Its segments include Payment Business, and E-Commerce Platform Business. Its Payment Business segment includes payment gateway business with CCAvenue business brand and payment infrastructure including CPGS towards banks, and credit and lending related business. Its E-Commerce Platform Business segment includes software framework and infrastructure to enable e-commerce for large enterprises and related services including domains and advertising. The Company provides its solutions to merchants, institutions, enterprises, corporations, merchants, and banks. The Company offers its solutions across the India, United Arab Emirates, and Oman.
