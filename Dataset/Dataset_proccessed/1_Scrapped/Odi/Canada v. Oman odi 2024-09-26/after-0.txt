Match Preview

In Match 32 of the ICC CWC League 2, Canada secured a 5-wicket win against Nepal. Nepal batted first and posted a total of 181 runs, being bowled out in 46.1 overs. Canada’s bowlers were impressive, with Kaleem Sana leading the attack. He took 3 wickets for 29 runs in his 10 overs. Harsh Thaker also performed well, taking 2 wickets for just 13 runs in his 4 overs. Dillon Heyliger contributed with 2 wickets for 26 runs in 9.1 overs, rounding out a solid bowling effort.

In response, Canada successfully chased down the target, scoring 186 runs for the loss of 5 wickets in 41.5 overs. Navneet Dhaliwal played a crucial role in the victory, scoring 87 runs from 113 balls. Pargat Singh supported him with an important knock of 64 runs off 83 balls. Harsh Thaker also chipped in with 13 runs off 24 balls. Canada’s top-order batting performance ensured they comfortably reached the target, highlighting their balanced approach throughout the match.

In Match 34 of the ICC CWC League 2, Nepal was set to take on Oman, but the match was abandoned. Fans were left disappointed as they had been eager to see the contest unfold. The abandonment not only halted the excitement but also affected the standings in the league.
