What to Know
The Celtics swept the Nuggets in Abu Dhabi and have won their last six games outside North America.
Jaylen Brown (21 points) and Jayson Tatum (17 points) led the C's in scoring.
Sam Hauser (15 points, 6-of-7 shooting) and Payton Pritchard (14 points, 5-of-7 shooting) made big contributions off the bench.
The Celtics will fly back to Boston for their next preseason game Saturday, Oct. 12 vs. the 76ers at TD Garden.

The Boston Celtics and Denver Nuggets, the last two NBA champions, played their second preseason game in Abu Dhabi on Sunday. The C's won the first matchup Friday, and they won again 130-104 on Sunday. Jaylen Brown led all scorers with 21 points. Jayson Tatum (17 points) and Sam Hauser (15 points) also played well for the C's. The Celtics shot 18-of-47 (38.3 percent) on 3-point shots.

How to watch Celtics vs. Nuggets preseason games in Abu Dhabi
By Darren Hartwell

The Celtics unveiled their new court at Reem Central Park in Abu Dhabi. Derrick White and Joe Mazzulla stopped by to surprise kids at a basketball clinic on the court.

Follow live updates on the Boston Celtics in Abu Dhabi¬†by checking out our live blog.

The Boston Celtics' quest to repeat will begin overseas.

The defending NBA champions landed in Abu Dhabi earlier this week to prepare for two preseason games against the 2023 champion Denver Nuggets. It's the third year in a row that the NBA has hosted exhibition contests in the capital city of the United Arab Emirates, but this is the first matchup between the league's last two champions.

The action begins Friday, Oct. 4 for Game 1 of Celtics-Nuggets at Etihad Arena in Abu Dhabi. Game 2 is set for Sunday, Oct. 6. Both games will air on NBC Sports Boston, which also will have exclusive pregame and postgame coverage.
