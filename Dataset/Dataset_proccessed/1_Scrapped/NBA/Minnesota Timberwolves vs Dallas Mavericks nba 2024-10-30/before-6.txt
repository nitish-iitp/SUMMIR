Los Angeles Lakers (3-1, third in the Western Conference) vs. Cleveland Cavaliers (4-0, first in the Eastern Conference)

Cleveland; Wednesday, 7 p.m. EDT

BOTTOM LINE: Cleveland and Los Angeles face off in non-conference action.

Cleveland went 48-34 overall with a 26-15 record at home during the 2023-24 season. The Cavaliers averaged 16.6 points off of turnovers, 13.6 second-chance points and 34.4 bench points last season.
