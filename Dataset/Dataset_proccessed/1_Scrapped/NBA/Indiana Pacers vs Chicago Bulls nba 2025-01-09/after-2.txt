Takeaways: Bulls fall to Pacers
WILL GOTTLIEB
JANUARY 9, 2025
Jan 8, 2025; Indianapolis, Indiana, USA; Chicago Bulls guard Zach LaVine (8) dribbles the ball while Indiana Pacers guard Bennedict Mathurin (00) defends in the second half at Gainbridge Fieldhouse. Mandatory Credit: Trevor Ruszkowski-Imagn Images

Leading into Wednesday's game 129-113 loss to the Indiana Pacers, the Chicago Bulls were 9-6 over their previous 15 with the 12th best defense in the NBA. They allowed opponents to score just 112.1 points per 100 possessions over that time.
While it may be a single bad outing on an otherwise upward trajectory, that trend did not hold. Without shooting particularly well (10-of-29) from three, the Pacers scored 129 against the Bulls with a 126.5 offensive rating.
