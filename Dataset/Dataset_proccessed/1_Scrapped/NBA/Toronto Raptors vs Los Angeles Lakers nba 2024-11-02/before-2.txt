Memphis Grizzlies (3-3, ninth in the Western Conference) vs. Philadelphia 76ers (1-3, 12th in the Eastern Conference)

Philadelphia; Saturday, 7:30 p.m. EDT

BOTTOM LINE: Philadelphia takes on Memphis in a non-conference matchup.

Philadelphia went 47-35 overall with a 25-16 record at home during the 2023-24 season. The 76ers averaged 8.5 steals, 6.0 blocks and 11.3 turnovers per game last season.
