RAPTORS
On a night reserved for a legend, sophomore Gradey Dick takes the next step for the Raptors

After struggling from the field, Gradey Dick grabbed a crucial steal and sank a key basket in Saturday night’s overtime win over the Kings.

Updated Nov. 3, 2024 at 5:14 p.m.
Nov. 3, 2024
3 min read
Save

Gradey Dick, with 22 points, and the Raptors celebrated a win over the Sacramento Kings on a special night at Scotiabank Arena.

Steve Russell Toronto Star
By Ilyas HusseinStaff Reporter

Gradey Dick is finding his groove for the Raptors.

The 2023 first-round draft pick set a career high in points against the Los Angeles Lakers on Friday with 31. His previous best was 30 against the Charlotte Hornets, in the game before that.
