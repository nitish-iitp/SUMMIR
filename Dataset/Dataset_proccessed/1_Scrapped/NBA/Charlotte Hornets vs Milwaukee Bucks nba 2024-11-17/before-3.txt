The Cleveland Cavaliers (14-0) will look to build on a 14-game winning streak when hosting the Charlotte Hornets (5-7) on Sunday, November 17, 2024 at Rocket Mortgage FieldHouse. It airs at 6:00 PM ET on FDSOH and FDSSE.

In their previous game, the Cavaliers topped the Bulls on Friday, 144-126. Their leading scorer was Donovan Mitchell with 37 points. In their previous game, the Hornets defeated the Bucks on Saturday, 115-114. Their high scorer was LaMelo Ball with 26 points.
