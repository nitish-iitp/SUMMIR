Warriors of the Week: Week 12 Jan. 7 - Jan. 10
Top Performers from Week 12 of the Warriors Season
Kalyb Champion
January 10, 20257:13 PM PST

*Updated Jan. 10*

Stay up to date with the Warriors' top performers from Week 12 of the 2024-25 regular season, which includes games against the Miami Heat, Detroit Pistons and Indiana Pacers.

Jan. 10, Pat Spencer: Warriors 96 - Pacers 108

PTS	REB	FG%
17	3	58.3

Pat Spencer set a career-high with 17 points on 7-for-12 shooting in the Warriors' 108-96 loss to the Pacers in Indiana on Friday. After opening with a 3-pointer, the rest of Spencer's six field goals came inside the arc. He was in control with the ball in his hands, getting to his spots for a dunk, floater, mid-range jumper and several layups. 

Jan. 9, Gui Santos: Warriors 107 - Pistons 104

PTS	STL	3PM
13	2	4

Gui Santos tied a career-high with 13 points in the Warriors' 107-104 win over the Pistons in Detroit on Thursday. Santos also set career-highs in steals (2) and made 3-pointers (4). Three of his five rebounds came on the offensive glass, contributing to the Warriors' 21-6 advantage in second-chance points.

Jan. 7, Stephen Curry: Warriors 98 - Heat 114

PTS	REB	3PM
31	7	8

Stephen Curry scored 20 of his 31 points in the first half of the Warriors' 114-98 loss to the Miami Heat at Chase Center on Tuesday. Curry also tied a season-high with eight made 3-pointers, six of which came in the first half.
