Nets vs. 76ers Betting Trends
The 76ers are 13-19-0 against the spread this season.
Against the spread, the Nets are 17-15-2 this year.
Games involving the 76ers have hit the over 16 times out of 34 chances this season.
Nets games this year have gone over the point total 50% of the time (17 out of 34 games with a set point total).
Philadelphia has covered the spread in a lower percentage of its home games than away games. It has covered four times in 14 games when playing at home, and it has covered nine times in 18 games when playing on the road.
The 76ers have exceeded the over/under less consistently at home, hitting the over in six of 14 home matchups (42.9%). In away games, they have hit the over in 10 of 18 games (55.6%).
Brooklyn has been better against the spread away (13-6-1) than at home (4-9-1) this year.
Both at home (seven of 14) and on the road (10 of 20), the Nets' games have finished over (in terms of the over/under) 50% of the time.

Nic Claxton's numbers on the season are 9.9 points, 7.6 boards and 2 assists per game. He is sinking 56.7% of his shots from the field.
