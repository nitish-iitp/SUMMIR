Injury Report: Zion Williamson, Jordan Hawkins remain questionable for Cavaliers game
Pelicans host Cavaliers at 7 p.m. in the Smoothie King Center
Pelicans.com
November 5, 20244:51 PM CST

The New Orleans Pelicans injury report will remain the remain the same as their previous contest for the Pelicans' game against the Cleveland Cavaliers on Wednesday, Nov. 6 (7 p.m., GCSEN TV, WWL radio) with four Pelicans out and two questionable for the final game of the Pelicans four-game home stand. Forward Zion Williamson (right thigh soreness) and guard Jordan Hawkins (low back tightness) are both questionable again after the pair missed the game against the Portland Trail Blazers with the same ailments on Monday, Nov. 4.

Guard/forward Herb Jones (right shoulder strain), guard CJ McCollum (right adductor strain), forward Trey Murphy (right hamstring strain) and guard Dejounte Murray (left hand fracture) will remain out for the Pelicans.
