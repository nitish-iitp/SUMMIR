Alvin Kamara Fantasy Stats
Kamara has 89.6 fantasy points (22.4 per game), first at his position and third in the league.
Kamara has averaged 24.2 fantasy points (accumulating 72.6 total) over his past three games. He has 65 carries for 279 yards and four touchdowns, plus 147 receiving yards on 12 catches (15 targets) and one TD.
Alvin Kamara accumulated 42 fantasy points — 20 carries, 115 yards, 3 TDs; 2 receptions, 65 yards, 1 TD — in Week 2 versus the Dallas Cowboys, his best game of the season.
Kamara accumulated 12.7 fantasy points — 26 carries, 87 yards; 3 receptions, 40 yards — in Week 3 against the Philadelphia Eagles, which was his worst game of the season.
