House of Villains is back for a second season on Wednesday. This reality TV show features the biggest villains in reality TV history.

Sometimes it is good to be bad. That is what every contestant on House of Villains has thought over the years as the primary villain on their respective reality TV series. Season two of House of Villains premieres Wednesday with an all new cast all trying to survive the battle royale style of competition series. Season one featured Tanisha Thomas from Bad Girls Club 2 winning and Johnny Bananas from The Real World: Key West as the runner-up. This season a new batch of villains featuring former cast members from The Real World: Austin, The Real Housewives of New Jersey, Big Brother 10, Survivor: Borneo, The Bachelor 25, 90 Day Fiance 6, RuPaul’s Drag Race 13, Bad Girls Club: Las Vegas, Flavor of Love and Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.
