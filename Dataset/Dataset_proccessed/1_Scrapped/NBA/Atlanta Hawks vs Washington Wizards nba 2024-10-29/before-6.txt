Houston Rockets (0-1, fourth in the Western Conference) vs. San Antonio Spurs (0-1, 12th in the Western Conference)

San Antonio; Saturday, 8:30 p.m. EDT

BOTTOM LINE: Western Conference foes San Antonio and Houston will play.

San Antonio finished 22-60 overall and 14-37 in Western Conference play a season ago. The Spurs averaged 7.1 steals, 6.3 blocks and 14.6 turnovers per game last season.
