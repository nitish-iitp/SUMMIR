5 things to know about the Pelicans on Nov. 14, 2024
Pelicans.com
November 14, 20248:55 AM CST

The New Orleans Pelicans fell to the Oklahoma City Thunder 106-88 on Wednesday, Nov. 13. The Pelicans kept the game close through the first two quarters with the game tied at 59-59 with 9:23 left to go in the third quarter, before the Thunder rattled off a 16-0 run and never looked back. Check out a recap of the game.

The loss drops the Pelicans to 3-9 on the season putting them three games back from them the 10 seed in the Western Conference. Check out the standings.

The Pelicans will look to rebound on a home back-to-back as the Pelicans take on the Denver Nuggets in the first leg on Friday, Nov. 15 and then take on the Lakers on Saturday, Nov. 16. Both games tip off at seven and will be broadcast on the Gulf Coast Sports & Entertainment Network. Get tickets to the game against the Nuggets and the game against the Lakers.

The Pelicans unveiled their NBA City Edition Uniforms inspired by New Orleans Night Life and Culture. Check out the latest on the Pelicans new jerseys and merchandise.

The team will release the injury report for the team's contest against the Denver Nuggets before the 5 p.m. deadline to submit the info to the NBA. Check back on Pelicans.com for the latest on the team's injuries.
