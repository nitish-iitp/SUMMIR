Philadelphia 76ers (2-9, 14th in the Eastern Conference) vs. Orlando Magic (7-6, third in the Eastern Conference)

Orlando, Florida; Friday, 7 p.m. EST

BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: 76ers -3.5; over/under is 224.5

BOTTOM LINE: Orlando seeks to keep its four-game win streak intact when the Magic take on Philadelphia.

Orlando went 47-35 overall, 32-20 in Eastern Conference play and 29-12 at home during the 2023-24 season. The Magic gave up 108.4 points per game while committing 19.7 fouls last season.

Philadelphia went 47-35 overall and 31-21 in Eastern Conference play during the 2023-24 season. The 76ers averaged 114.6 points per game last season, 19.5 on free throws and 36.3 from beyond the arc.

INJURIES: Magic: Paolo Banchero: out (abdomen), Wendell Carter Jr.: out (foot).

76ers: Tyrese Maxey: out (hamstring), Joel Embiid: day to day (knee), Paul George: day to day (knee).
