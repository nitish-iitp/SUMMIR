Golden State Warriors (7-2, third in the Western Conference) vs. Oklahoma City Thunder (8-1, second in the Western Conference)

Oklahoma City; Sunday, 7 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Oklahoma City hosts Golden State aiming to extend its four-game home winning streak.

Oklahoma City finished 57-25 overall, 36-16 in Western Conference action and 33-8 at home during the 2023-24 season. The Thunder averaged 20.5 points off of turnovers, 11.8 second-chance points and 34.6 bench points last season.
