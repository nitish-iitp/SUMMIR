What is House of Villains?

House of Villains is a reality competition show hosted by Joel McHale that invites notorious reality television villains to compete in a game reminiscent of Big Brother. The first season of the show was won by Tanisha Thomas, best known for her time on Bad Girls Club. Johnny Bananas was the runner up.
