Executive Transactions - November 14, 2024
11.14.2024
 
 
 
 
 
 

The UFL named Meta Global Marketing Lead/Sports, Entertainment & Innovation VINCE PANNOZZO SVP/Creative & Content and former longtime ESPN exec JASON BERNSTEIN VP/Partner Strategy & Solutions. The league also named NBA Dir/Brand Strategy JEN SAILER VP/Marketing & Brand Strategy and NBA Dir/Team Business & Partner Communications MIKE WADE SVP/Communications. Pannozzo and Sailer will report to UFL EVP/Marketing & Team Business Operations BROOKE CAMPBELL. Bernstein will report to EVP/Commercial Operations JOSH CELLA. Wade will report to President & CEO RUSS BRANDON (UFL).

Former Australian rugby player BRETT ROBINSON has been elected as the first non-European Chair of World Rugby, succeeding BILL BEAUMONT. Robinson beat ABDELATIF BENAZZI and ANDREA RINALDO in the voting (AP, 11/14).


NICK NOBLE is leaving his role as the Dir/Communications at CONCACAF for a new role as the Chief Communications and Marketing Officer at the ECA in Jan. (Leaders in Sport).

Notre Dame named PAT ROGERS Dir/Student-Athlete Management. He is the first to hold the position that is within the university’s burgeoning NIL division. Rogers, who spent four years as Dir/Basketball Operations, began the role on Nov. 1 (Notre Dame).

Valparaiso named New Mexico Senior Associate AD/Revenue Generation WILL PRICE Deputy AD/External Affairs. Price, who will assume the role on Dec. 2, will also serve as a liaison to department multimedia rights and sponsorship partner Van Wagner. Prior to New Mexico, Price served as Assistant AD/Marketing, Promotions & Branding at Memphis; Dir/Marketing & Promotions at Northern Arizona; Dir/Corporate Partnerships & Marketing for the MEAC; and Dir/Marketing & Promotions at New Mexico State (Valparaiso).

TMRW Sports promoted Business Development Coordinator SCARLETT EATON to Business Development Manager. Eaton joined TMRW Sports in 2023 after serving with Charlotte FC (SBJ).

The Predators promoted Guest Services Coordinator JOHN SULLIVAN to Senior Coordinator/Guest Services. Sullivan has been serving with the Predators since 2021 (SBJ).

The Suns named Sacramento Kings Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Intern OLUWATOFARATI SOMOTUN Phoenix Suns/Phoenix Mercury Foundation Coordinator (SBJ).

Sports betting network VSiN named MATT DEUTSCH Manager of Sports Programming. Deutsch is a longtime sports media exec who spent 16 years at SiriusXM and six years at WFAN Radio (SBJ).
