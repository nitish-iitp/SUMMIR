Dallas Mavericks (20-13, fourth in the Western Conference) vs. Houston Rockets (21-11, third in the Western Conference)

Houston; Wednesday, 8 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Alperen Sengun and the Houston Rockets host Luka Doncic and the Dallas Mavericks in Western Conference play.

The Rockets are 6-1 against opponents in the Southwest Division. Houston averages 113.0 points while outscoring opponents by 6.4 points per game.

The Mavericks are 14-11 against Western Conference opponents. Dallas scores 117.1 points while outscoring opponents by 5.6 points per game.

TOP PERFORMERS: Sengun is shooting 49.1% and averaging 18.9 points for the Rockets.

Doncic is averaging 28.1 points, 8.3 rebounds, 7.8 assists and two steals for the Mavericks.

LAST 10 GAMES: Rockets: 6-4, averaging 109.8 points, 48.8 rebounds, 22.0 assists, 7.2 steals and 5.7 blocks per game while shooting 44.8% from the field. Their opponents have averaged 105.8 points per game.
