Brian Robinson Jr. Fantasy Stats
Ranked 13th at his position and 38th overall in the NFL, Robinson has 82.4 fantasy points (13.7 per game).
In his past three games, Robinson has rushed for 190 yards on 40 carries, with four touchdowns. He has accumulated 44.2 fantasy points (14.7 per game).
Robinson has 73 carries for 356 yards and five touchdowns, plus 19 receiving yards on five catches (seven targets) in his past five games. He has accumulated 67.5 fantasy points (13.5 per game) in that span.
Brian Robinson Jr. picked up 17.3 fantasy points — 21 carries, 101 yards, 1 TD — in his best game of the season. That was in Week 4 against the Arizona Cardinals.
In his worst game of the season — Week 3 versus the Cincinnati Bengals — Robinson finished with 9.7 fantasy points. His stat line: 16 carries, 33 yards, 1 TD.
