Brian Thomas Jr. Fantasy Stats
Ranked 45th overall in the NFL and sixth at his position, Thomas has accumulated 78.8 fantasy points (11.3 per game) so far in 2024.
Thomas has averaged 12.7 fantasy points (38 total) over his past three games. He has 238 yards receiving, on 13 catches (19 targets), and two touchdowns.
In his past five games, Thomas has accumulated 58.7 fantasy points (11.7 per game). He has 372 receiving yards on 24 catches (37 targets) and three touchdowns.
In Week 5 against the Indianapolis Colts, Brian Thomas Jr. put up a season-high 18.2 fantasy points, thanks to these numbers: five receptions, 122 yards and one touchdown.
In his worst game of the season, Thomas finished with 2.7 fantasy points — three receptions, 27 yards, on six targets. That was in Week 6 versus the Chicago Bears.
