New Orleans Pelicans Weekly Show 11/12/2024
Pelicans.com
November 12, 20242:43 PM CST

On the New Orleans Pelicans Weekly Show podcast for Tuesday, Nov. 12, 2024, Gus Kattengell recaps the Pelicans' games against Cleveland, Orlando and Brooklyn. Hear how it sounded on the Pelicans Radio Network with highlights from the voice of the Pels Todd Graffagnini plus hear reactions from head coach Willie Green and his players. This week’s interview is Yves Missi who recorded his first career double-double on Monday against the Nets.
