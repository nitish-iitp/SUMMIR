RAPTORS
OG Anunoby and the Knicks remind the Raptors what they lost

The Raptors have lost three times in a month to the Knicks, roughly one year out from their blockbuster trade.

Jan. 9, 2025
2 min read
Save

Ex-Knick RJ Barrett guards ex-Raptor OG Anunoby in Wednesday night’s game at New York’s Madison Square Garden. 

Al Bello/Getty Images
By Gilbert NgaboSports Reporter

RJ Barrett and Immanuel Quickley were back in their old stomping grounds and played like it for most of the night, but it wasn’t enough for the Raptors.

That’s because OG Anunoby was also facing a familiar team and had his way at Madison Square Garden.
