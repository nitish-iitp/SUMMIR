Indiana Pacers (5-5, fifth in the Eastern Conference) vs. Orlando Magic (6-6, third in the Eastern Conference)

Orlando, Florida; Wednesday, 7 p.m. EST

BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: Pacers -2.5; over/under is 226.5

BOTTOM LINE: Orlando will try to keep its five-game home win streak alive when the Magic take on Indiana.

Orlando went 47-35 overall, 32-20 in Eastern Conference play and 29-12 at home during the 2023-24 season. The Magic averaged 110.5 points per game last season, 51.8 in the paint, 18.6 off of turnovers and 12.4 on fast breaks.

Indiana finished 47-35 overall and 32-20 in Eastern Conference play during the 2023-24 season. The Pacers allowed opponents to score 120.2 points per game and shoot 49.6% from the field last season.

INJURIES: Magic: Paolo Banchero: out (abdomen), Wendell Carter Jr.: out (foot).

Pacers: Aaron Nesmith: out (ankle), Andrew Nembhard: out (knee), Isaiah Jackson: out (calf), Obi Toppin: day to day (ankle), James Wiseman: out (calf).
