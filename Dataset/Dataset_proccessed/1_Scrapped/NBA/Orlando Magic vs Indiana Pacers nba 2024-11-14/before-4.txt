Zach Ertz Fantasy Stats
In 2024, Ertz has 46.1 fantasy points (4.6 per game) — 14th at his position, 184th in the league.
Looking at his past three games, Ertz has 12 receptions on 20 targets, for 113 yards, and a total of 11.3 fantasy points (3.8 per game).
In his past five games, Ertz has averaged 5.6 fantasy points (28.1 total). He has 221 receiving yards on 20 catches (30 targets) and one touchdown.
In Week 7 versus the Carolina Panthers, Zach Ertz put up a season-high 10 fantasy points, thanks to this stat line: four receptions, 40 yards and one touchdown.
In his worst game of the season — Week 9 against the New York Giants — Ertz finished with 0.5 fantasy points. His stat line: one catch, five yards, on one target.
