Games will be available in NBA Arena in Meta’s Horizons Worlds App and in the Xtadium App.

NBA.com Staff

October 24, 2024 2:35 AM

Games will be available in NBA Arena in Meta’s Horizons Worlds App on 10/25/24 and in the Xtadium App beginning 10/25/2024.
