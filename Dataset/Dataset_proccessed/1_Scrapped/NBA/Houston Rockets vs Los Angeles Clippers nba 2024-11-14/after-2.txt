The Houston Rockets (8-4) will be looking to build on a four-game home winning streak when hosting the Los Angeles Clippers (6-6) tonight at Toyota Center. It airs at 8:00 PM ET on SCHN and FDSSC.

Rockets vs. Clippers Predictions
Our prediction to win on the Moneyline in this game is the Rockets, by a final score of Rockets 112, Clippers 106.
Against the spread, we predict that the Rockets will cover. The spread is currently Rockets -4.
As for the total, we see this one going over the posted over/under of 215 points.

Rockets Betting Trends
The Rockets have won seven games against the spread this season, while failing to cover five times.
Houston has been favored by 4 points or more seven times this season, and covered the spread in four of those games.
Rockets games have hit the over four out of 12 times this season.
This season, the Rockets have won six out of the nine games in which they’ve been favored.
This season, Houston has won three of its five games when favored by at least -185 on the Moneyline.
Clippers Betting Trends
The Clippers have covered seven times in 12 matchups with a spread this year.
Los Angeles has been an underdog by 4 points or more eight times this year, and covered the spread in five of those matchups.
A total of four Clippers games this season have gone over the point total.
The Clippers have come away with three wins in the eight contests they have been listed as the underdogs in this season.
This year, Los Angeles has won three of seven games when listed as at least +154 or better on the Moneyline.
