Statistics: How Houston Rockets players fared in the 2024-25 NBA preseason
Ben Dubose, Rockets Wire
Fri, Oct 18, 2024·2 min read

The 2024-25 NBA preseason is in the books for the Houston Rockets, who finished it with a 3-1 record. They lost an opening game in Utah before winning at Oklahoma City and defeating both New Orleans and San Antonio at home.

Courtesy of RealGM, here is a statistical look at preseason production (on a per-game basis) for all of Houston's projected rotation players under head coach Ime Udoka. All players played in four games unless otherwise noted.

This list is sorted by average points per game, from the most to least:

Jalen Green: 20.5 points, 3.3 rebounds in 23.8 minutes; 43.9% FG, 43.2% on 3-pointers, 80.0% on free throws

Alperen Sengun: 16.8 points, 7.3 rebounds, 2.8 assists, 1.3 steals in 24.5 minutes; 66.7% FG, 33.3% on 3-pointers, 77.8% on free throws

Dillon Brooks (three games): 13.3 points, 4.3 rebounds, 2.0 assists in 23.9 minutes; 53.8% FG, 50.0% on 3-pointers

Amen Thompson: 12.5 points, 5.3 rebounds, 2.0 assists, 1.0 steals in 22.8 minutes; 55.0% FG, 40.0% on 3-pointers, 44.4% on free throws

Cam Whitmore: 9.5 points, 2.3 assists, 1.0 steals in 18.8 minutes; 37.1% FG, 18.8% on 3-pointers, 64.3% on free throws

Jabari Smith Jr.: 8.3 points, 6.5 rebounds, 2.0 assists in 23.8 minutes; 45.5% FG, 20.0% on 3-pointers

Fred VanVleet (three games): 7.3 points, 7.3 assists, 1.0 steals, 0.7 turnovers in 22.6 minutes; 26.1% FG, 23.5% on 3-pointers, 60.0% on free throws

Tari Eason (two games): 7.0 points, 5.0 rebounds, 1.5 assists in 14.4 minutes; 50.0% FG, 20.0% on 3-pointers

Reed Sheppard: 6.5 points, 3.0 rebounds, 2.5 assists, 1.0 steals, 1.0 blocks in 21.1 minutes; 44.4% FG, 18.2% on 3-pointers

Steven Adams (three games): 3.3 points, 7.3 rebounds, 1.7 assists in 14.7 minutes; 83.3% FG

Houston opens its 2024-25 regular season at home next Wednesday, Oct. 23, versus Charlotte. Tipoff from Toyota Center is at 7:00 p.m. Central.
