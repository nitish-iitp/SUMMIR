Detroit Pistons (5-8, 10th in the Eastern Conference) vs. Toronto Raptors (2-10, 15th in the Eastern Conference)

Toronto; Friday, 7 p.m. EST

BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: Raptors -2.5; over/under is 231

BOTTOM LINE: Toronto will try to break its five-game skid when the Raptors play Detroit.

The Raptors are 1-3 in Eastern Conference games. Toronto is 1-3 in games decided by 3 points or fewer.

The Pistons are 4-7 against conference opponents. Detroit is second in the Eastern Conference with 35.3 defensive rebounds per game led by Tobias Harris averaging 6.6.

The Raptors average 112.8 points per game, 1.0 more point than the 111.8 the Pistons allow. The Pistons are shooting 46.5% from the field, 1.3% lower than the 47.8% the Raptors' opponents have shot this season.

TOP PERFORMERS: Jakob Poeltl is averaging 13.9 points and 11.1 rebounds for the Raptors.

Cade Cunningham is scoring 23.8 points per game and averaging 7.2 rebounds for the Pistons.
