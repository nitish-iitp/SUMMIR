Chicago Bulls to win with handicap (+8.5)

While the Bulls have lost three in a row and the Wolves are clearly the better team, I expect this game to be closer than the bookies see it. First of all, the Bulls have played a few very good games this season and they have won just one game fewer than Minnesota.

More importantly, the Bulls have beaten the Timberwolves in their last four meetings in a row in the regular season, despite Minnesota being the better team, including last season. With that in mind and considering that the Bulls are hosting the game, I can see them coming under the spread, which is my first Bulls vs Timberwolves prediction.

Over 227 total points

Only one of the last eight H2H duels between Chicago Bulls and Minnesota Timberwolves has gone under 227 total points and that is enough of a reason for me to expect another high-scoring game tonight.

Meanwhile, these two teams have been on a similar levels offensively this season, with the Bulls averaging 113.00 and the Wolves averaging 111.70 points per game. So, they'll have to go a bit above those averages this evening, but I can see that happening, which is why over 227 total points is another of my Bulls vs Timberwolves picks.

Anthony Edwards to make 4+ threes

The last of my Bulls vs Timberwolves tips is for Anthony Edwards to make at least four three-pointers. Ant has been a great 3-point shooter last season too, but he's been taking a lot more threes since the start of this season, only failing to shoot double-digit threes in the last two games.

In the first five games of the season, Edwards has made at least 4 threes each time and overall he's averaging 5.1 made threes per game on .450% 3-point shooting. With that in mind and with the Bulls not really having a great defense, Edwards should be able to make four or more threes tonight.

The Wolves currently have a clean bill of health.
