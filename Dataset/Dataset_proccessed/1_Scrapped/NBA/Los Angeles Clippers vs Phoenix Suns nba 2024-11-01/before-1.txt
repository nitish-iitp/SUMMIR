Los Angeles Lakers (3-2, fifth in the Western Conference) vs. Toronto Raptors (1-4, 14th in the Eastern Conference)

Toronto; Friday, 7:30 p.m. EDT

BOTTOM LINE: Toronto and Los Angeles face off in non-conference action.

Toronto went 25-57 overall with a 14-27 record at home during the 2023-24 season. The Raptors shot 47.1% from the field and 34.7% from 3-point range last season.
