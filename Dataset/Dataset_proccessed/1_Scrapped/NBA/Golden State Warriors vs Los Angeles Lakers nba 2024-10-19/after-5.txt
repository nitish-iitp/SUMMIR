Warriors Waive Blake Hinson, Kevin Knox II & Jackson Rowe
October 19, 20242:10 PM PDT

The Golden State Warriors have waived guard Blake Hinson, forward Kevin Knox II and forward Jackson Rowe, it was announced today.

Hinson appeared in two preseason games with the Warriors, averaging 1.5 points in 5.3 minutes per game.

Knox II played in two preseason games with Golden State, averaging 3.0 points, 2.0 rebounds and 1.00 block in 7.4 minutes per game.

Rowe appeared in two preseason games with the Warriors, averaging 4.0 points, 3.0 rebounds and 1.0 assist in 6.3 minutes per game.

All three players originally signed with the Warriors on September 29. Golden State’s roster currently stands at 17 players.
