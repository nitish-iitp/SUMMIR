RAPTORS
Opinion | Should the Raptors have called a timeout to set up a play at the end of the Nuggets game? Of course not
Updated Oct. 29, 2024 at 4:52 p.m.
Oct. 29, 2024
2 min read
Save

Toronto Raptors guard RJ Barrett drives past Denver Nuggets centre Nikola Jokic Monday night in his first game back from injury.

Nathan Denette / CP photo
By Doug SmithSports Reporter

Doug Smith is a sports reporter based in Toronto. Follow him on Twitter: @smithraps.

Of course RJ Barrett should have gotten the ball and gone, forced the issue and tried to create an advantage. Even in the absence of some dramatic greatness, what he did Monday night was the right call.

Yes, there’s a debate over whether the Raptors — and by extension Barrett — should have called a timeout with 10 seconds left in overtime, down two, and a delightful game against the Denver Nuggets in the balance at Scotiabank Arena.
