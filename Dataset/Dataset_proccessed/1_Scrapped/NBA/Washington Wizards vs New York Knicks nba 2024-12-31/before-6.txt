Karl-Anthony Towns stats tonight: How did Knicks star fare against Wizards? (Dec. 30) (Image Source: Getty)

Karl-Anthony Towns and the New York Knicks won against the Washington Wizards for the second straight game in Capital One Arena, Washington D.C.

Knicks registered their season-best eighth consecutive win on Monday. The Knicks improved to 23-10 and are now 0.5 games behind the defending champion Boston Celtics for second place in the Eastern Conference.

While the game on Saturday went down to the wire, with the Knicks escaping with a 136-132 win, it is a much easier victory for New York on Monday, 126-106. Towns led all scorers in the game and registered another double-double performance. He finished with an efficient 32 points and 13 rebounds. He shot 68.4% from the field (13-for-19).

Trending

His numbers in the two games against the Wizards are nearly identical. On Saturday's overtime win, Towns finished with 30 points and 14 rebounds. He also made 13 of his 19 shots (68.4%) in the first game.
