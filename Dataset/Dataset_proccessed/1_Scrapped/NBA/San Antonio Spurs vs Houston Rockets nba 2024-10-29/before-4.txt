Injury Report: Four Pelicans out for Tuesday game at Golden State, including G League assignments
Steph Curry is also out for Golden State
Jim Eichenhofer
October 28, 20246:46 PM CDT

New Orleans submitted an official injury list Monday afternoon to the NBA that consisted of four players who will be out for Tuesday’s game at Golden State (9 p.m., TNT, WWL radio). Trey Murphy (right hamstring strain) and Dejounte Murray (left hand fracture) are joined by two Pelicans rookies on G League assignment, center Karlo Matkovic and guard Antonio Reeves.

For Golden State, Steph Curry (left peroneal strain) and De’Anthony Melton (right facet inflammation) are out. Andrew Wiggins (low back strain) is questionable.
