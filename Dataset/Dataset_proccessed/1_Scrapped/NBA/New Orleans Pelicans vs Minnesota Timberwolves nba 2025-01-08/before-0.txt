Pelicans-Timberwolves Connections
Timberwolves Head Coach Chris Finch served as a Pelicans Assistant Coach from 2017-20, including being the Associate Head Coach during the 2019-20 season.
Timberwolves Quality Control Coach Nathan Bubes served as an assistant video coordinator for the Pelicans during the 2019 season.
Pelicans Player Development Coach Corey Brewer was drafted by the Minnesota Timberwolves 7th overall in the 2007 NBA Draft and played a total of six seasons with Minnesota.
Timberwolves G Nickeil Alexander-Walker played 3 seasons with the Pelicans where he appeared in 143 regular season games and averaged 9.9 points, 2.7 rebounds, and 2.3 assists.
Timberwolves President of Basketball Operations Tim Connelly was the Assistant General Manager for New Orleans for three years (2010-13) prior to moving to Denver.
Timberwolves F Julius Randle was a member of the Pelicans during the 2018-19 season.
Randle appeared in 73 games, averaging 21.4 points on .524 shooting from the floor, 8.7 rebounds, and 3.1 assists.
Additionally, Randle and Pelicans F Brandon Ingram and Assistant/Player Development Coach Corey Brewer were teammates on the Los Angeles Lakers during the 2017-18 season.
