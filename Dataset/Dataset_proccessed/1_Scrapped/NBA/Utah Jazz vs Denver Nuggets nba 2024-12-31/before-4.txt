Denver Nuggets (17-13, seventh in the Western Conference) vs. Utah Jazz (7-23, 14th in the Western Conference)

Salt Lake City; Monday, 9 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Utah heads into the matchup against Denver after losing three straight games.

The Jazz are 3-20 in Western Conference games. Utah is eighth in the NBA with 44.9 rebounds led by Walker Kessler averaging 10.9.

The Nuggets are 3-3 against the rest of their division. Denver averages 120.1 points and has outscored opponents by 2.7 points per game.

TOP PERFORMERS: Lauri Markkanen is averaging 19.7 points and 6.5 rebounds for the Jazz.
