Injury Report: Seven Pelicans out for Wednesday game at OKC
Playoff opponents square off on ESPN
Jim Eichenhofer
November 12, 20244:41 PM CST

A total of seven New Orleans players are officially listed as out on Tuesday’s injury report, ahead of a Wednesday national TV game at Oklahoma City (7 p.m., ESPN, WWL radio). Jose Alvarado (left hamstring strain), Jordan Hawkins (low back strain), Herb Jones (right shoulder strain), CJ McCollum (right adductor strain), Dejounte Murray (left hand fracture), Zion Williamson (left hamstring strain) and Karlo Matkovic (G League on assignment) are all out.

For Oklahoma City, Isaiah Hartenstein (hand), Chet Holmgren (hip), Nikola Topic (knee) and Jaylin Williams (hamstring) have all been sidelined for recent games due to injury. Hartenstein, Topic and Williams have not played in the regular season.
