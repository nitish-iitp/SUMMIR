Phoenix Suns (9-3, third in the Western Conference) vs. Oklahoma City Thunder (10-2, first in the Western Conference)

Oklahoma City; Friday, 8 p.m. EST

BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: Thunder -6.5; over/under is 232.5

BOTTOM LINE: Devin Booker and the Phoenix Suns visit Shai Gilgeous-Alexander and the Oklahoma City Thunder in Western Conference play.

The Thunder have gone 7-2 against Western Conference opponents. Oklahoma City is 0-1 in games decided by 3 points or fewer.

The Suns have gone 7-3 against Western Conference opponents. Phoenix ranks sixth in the NBA with 34.5 defensive rebounds per game led by Jusuf Nurkic averaging 8.5.

The Thunder are shooting 46.5% from the field this season, the same percentage the Suns allow to opponents. The Suns average 15.4 made 3-pointers per game this season, 2.7 more made shots on average than the 12.7 per game the Thunder allow.

TOP PERFORMERS: Gilgeous-Alexander is averaging 27.9 points, 5.5 rebounds, 6.3 assists and 1.8 steals for the Thunder.

Booker is averaging 23.3 points and 6.8 assists for the Suns.
