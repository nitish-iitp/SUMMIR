Toronto Raptors (2-5, 13th in the Eastern Conference) vs. Sacramento Kings (4-3, ninth in the Western Conference)

Sacramento, California; Wednesday, 10 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: The Sacramento Kings host the Toronto Raptors in non-conference play.

Sacramento went 46-36 overall a season ago while going 24-17 at home. The Kings allowed opponents to score 114.8 points per game and shoot 48.0% from the field last season.
