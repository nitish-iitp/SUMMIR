Published Oct. 10, 2024, 9:20 a.m. ET
Knicks guard Cameron Payne
NBAE via Getty Images

Across both conferences and all four rounds, Knicks-Sixers was the scrappiest of all playoff series in 2024.

The Joel Embiid stuff generated the headlines because it included Mitchell Robinson being reinjured after getting dragged to the court, prompting New Yorkers to adopt the series soundtrack, “F–k Embiid.” Yankees fans even chanted it after a Game 1 playoff victory over the Royals in this year’s MLB playoffs.
