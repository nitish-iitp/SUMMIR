Ray-Ray McCloud Fantasy Stats
Ranked 219th overall in the NFL and 76th at his position, McCloud has picked up 30 fantasy points (3.8 per game) so far in 2024.
McCloud has picked up 48 yards receiving, on eight catches (11 targets), with zero touchdowns and an average of 1.6 fantasy points (4.8 total) in his past three games.
In his past five games, McCloud has averaged 3.3 fantasy points (16.6 total). He has 166 receiving yards on 20 catches (27 targets) and zero touchdowns.
In his best game of the season, Ray-Ray McCloud finished with 6.6 fantasy points — six receptions, 66 yards. That was in Week 5 against the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.
In Week 7 versus the Seattle Seahawks, McCloud posted a season-low 0.9 fantasy points, with this stat line: two receptions, nine yards, on four targets.
