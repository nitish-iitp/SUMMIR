BOTTOM LINE: The New York Mets host the Boston Red Sox looking to extend a three-game home winning streak.

New York has a 37-33 record in home games and a 75-64 record overall. The Mets have a 38-13 record in games when they did not give up a home run.

Boston has a 70-69 record overall and a 39-31 record in road games. The Red Sox have the ninth-ranked team ERA in the AL at 4.15.

Wednesday's game is the third time these teams square off this season.

TOP PERFORMERS: Francisco Lindor has 30 home runs, 50 walks and 84 RBI while hitting .273 for the Mets. Mark Vientos is 12-for-41 with a double, two home runs and seven RBI over the past 10 games.
