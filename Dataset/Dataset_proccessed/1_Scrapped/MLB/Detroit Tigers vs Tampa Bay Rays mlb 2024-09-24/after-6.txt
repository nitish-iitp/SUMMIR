Tarik Skubal shines as Tigers beat Rays, take another step to playoffs
Updated: Sep. 24, 2024, 5:26 p.m.|Published: Sep. 24, 2024, 4:00 p.m.

Detroit Tigers fans celebrate a Detroit Tigers pitcher Tarik Skubal strikeout against the Tampa Bay Rays in the seventh inning of a baseball game, Tuesday, Sept. 24, 2024, in Detroit. (AP Photo/Paul Sancya)AP

By Evan Woodbery | ewoodber@mlive.com

DETROIT -- Tarik Skubal threw seven shutout innings in a playoff-worthy performance as the Detroit Tigers beat the Tampa Bay Rays 2-1 on Tuesday afternoon at Comerica Park.

The Tigers (83-74) are now in sole possession of the second American League wild-card spot with five games left in the season, including two more against the Rays (78-79).
