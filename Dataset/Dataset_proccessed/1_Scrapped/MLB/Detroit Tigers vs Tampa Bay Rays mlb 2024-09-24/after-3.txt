BOTTOM LINE: The Tampa Bay Rays will attempt to keep their four-game win streak going when they visit the Detroit Tigers.

Detroit is 39-36 at home and 82-74 overall. The Tigers have the third-best team ERA in baseball at 3.64.

Tampa Bay has a 78-78 record overall and a 36-39 record in road games. Rays pitchers have a collective 3.80 ERA, which ranks sixth in the AL.

The teams meet Tuesday for the fourth time this season.

TOP PERFORMERS: Riley Greene has 25 doubles, six triples and 24 home runs while hitting .262 for the Tigers. Kerry Carpenter is 10-for-27 with two doubles, a triple and three home runs over the past 10 games.
