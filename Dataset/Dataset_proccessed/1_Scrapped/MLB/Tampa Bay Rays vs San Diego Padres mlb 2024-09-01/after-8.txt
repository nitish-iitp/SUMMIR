No need for fancy math. If Rays don’t win this week, the season is over
John Romano | If the Rays don’t sweep the Twins this week, you can probably start debating offseason moves.
Rays centerfielder Jose Siri could not get high enough to pull back Jackson Merrill's fourth-inning home run in San Diego's 4-3 victory against the Rays on Sunday. [ LUIS SANTANA | Times ]
By
John RomanoTimes staff
Published Sept. 1, 2024

ST. PETERSBURG — Give them one more game. Put your common sense aside and offer the Rays that much.

They’re not really in a pennant race, and their odds of reaching the postseason alternate between ridiculous and preposterous. But, despite your better judgment, give them Monday night’s game against the Twins before closing the book on 2024.

It’s bonkers, I know.
