DAVANTE ADAMS RECEIVING STATS
Adams’ 150 receiving yards is the best mark on the Raiders. He has been targeted 17 times, and has 12 catches plus one touchdown (75.0 yards per game).
Adams has been the target of 34.0% (17 total) of his team’s 50 passing attempts this season.
He has accounted for 33.3% of his team’s three offensive touchdowns this season, with one total.
AGAINST PITTSBURGH
In his two matchups against the Steelers, Adams’ 39.5 receiving yards average is 35.0 less than his over/under for Sunday’s game (74.5).
Adams has never had over 74.5 receiving yards against the Steelers.
Adams, in two matchups, did not have a touchdown catch.
LAST SEASON STATS: RAIDERS VS. STEELERS
Last year, the Raiders put up just 2.8 more points per game (23.2) than the Steelers surrendered (20.4).
The Raiders racked up 352.5 yards per game last season, 22.1 more yards than the 330.4 the Steelers gave up per contest.
The Raiders passed for 231.4 yards per game last season, just 9.1 more yards than the 222.3 the Steelers allowed per contest.
Last season the Raiders racked up 121.1 yards per game on the ground, 13 more than the Steelers allowed per contest (108.1).
