San Diego Padres vs Seattle Mariners Prediction, 9/11/2024 MLB Picks, Best Bets & Odds
by Tony Sink - 9/10/2024



Game: San Diego Padres vs Seattle Mariners

Date: Wednesday, September 11, 2024

Location: T-Mobile Park in Seattle, WA

TV: Padres.TV

Odds/Point Spread: San Diego (EVEN) Seattle (-120)

T-Mobile Park is the site where the Seattle Mariners (73-71) will try to beat the San Diego Padres (81-64) on Wednesday. The moneyline on this game has the Padres at EVEN and the Mariners are at -120. The betting total comes in at 7. The expected starting pitchers will be Michael King and Bryan Woo.

The Padres have recorded 219 two-baggers as a squad and have knocked 167 baseballs out of the park. San Diego has a slugging rate of .417 and have been rung up 944 times, while being walked on 416 occasions. As a unit, the San Diego Padres are putting up 4.8 runs per outing, which has them sitting at 8th in MLB. They have 659 runs batted in as well as 1,308 hits for the season, while their team batting average is .265. They have earned a total of 690 runs while having a team OBP of .327.

They have a strikeout to walk ratio of 3.14 and their pitching staff has earned a collective WHIP of 1.24. Padres pitchers have surrendered 156 home runs in addition to 620 runs in total (13th in baseball). San Diego has yielded 1,184 hits (8.3 per 9 innings) and 575 earned runs. The Padres are sitting with a team earned run average of 4.02 so far this season (15th in MLB), and their staff has struck out 1,311 batters. They have walked 418 opposing hitters and their FIP is 3.82 as a team on the season.

Padres relief pitchers have entered the game with runners on base 145 times as well as having 129 appearances in high leverage situations. The relievers have racked up 68 holds this year (24th in baseball). The Padres relief pitchers have earned a save rate of 63.2% and has stepped onto the mound in 127 save situations. They have compiled 36 saves on the campaign and have blown 21 out of their 57 save opportunities. The relievers have inherited 221 runners on the campaign with 36.2% of those players crossed home plate. The Padres have dispatched 482 relief pitchers to the mound this year.

The Padres have turned 69.2% of baseballs in play into outs in their 11,586 innings, which ranks them 20th in pro baseball. The San Diego Padres have earned a total of 3,862 putouts at this point in the season, as well as 1,140 assists and 64 errors. Their fielding percentage is currently at .987 which has them sitting 4th in professional baseball, and have earned 105 double plays.

King (25-25 win-loss mark in his career) sits with a FIP of 3.23 and he has faced 1,692 batters in the majors. He has allowed 344 base hits (7.7 hits per nine innings) and has had 141 free passes. His ERA is 3.28 (147 earned runs allowed) and his WHIP is 1.202. King has thrown 403 innings and has 467 strikeouts so far in his pro baseball career.

The Mariners have a team slugging percentage of .369 and they tally 4.08 runs per contest (25th in baseball). They have recorded 200 doubles, while taking a walk 505 times and earning 588 runs. Seattle has recorded 161 home runs this season as well as 558 runs batted in. They have been rung up on 1,461 instances (1st in MLB) and have a total of 1,032 base hits. The Seattle Mariners have accounted for an OBP of .306 and a team batting average of .219 on the campaign.

The Mariners hold a team WHIP of 1.083 with a FIP of 3.74 as a team over the course of the season. They are currently ranked 1st in the league as a staff in total hits conceded with 1,047. The Seattle pitching staff have surrendered 542 runs this season while holding an ERA of 3.51 (495 earned runs allowed). Their K/BB ratio sits at 8.90 (1,251 strikeouts vs 328 free passes). They have given up 161 home runs and they yield 3.84 runs per 9 innings (2nd in the league).

Sitting with 123 save situations, the Mariners have accrued 72 holds and also 19 blown saves. Seattle has called on relievers to step onto the mound in 49 save chances and they have converted 30 saves. Their bullpen pitchers have come in 160 times in high leverage situations and on 131 occasions with base runners. Seattle relief pitchers have an inherited score percentage of 39.2% of 181 inherited runners. They are currently ranked 21st in baseball holding a save percentage of 61.2%, and they have dispatched 459 bullpen pitchers onto the field on the year.

The Seattle Mariners have turned 87 double plays and have a fielding percentage of .985 (13th in professional baseball). The Mariners have accrued 1,148 assists, 73 errors and have accounted for 3,811 putouts on the season. Out of their 11,433 innings played, the Mariners are sitting with a defensive efficiency of 72.2% (1st in MLB).

Woo (11-7 career win-loss record) is the owner of a 3.23 earned run average while surrendering 7.2 hits per nine innings. His strikeout to walk ratio is 4.25 and he has faced 752 batters so far in his pro baseball career. He has allowed 67 earned runs while holding a WHIP of 1.017 and having a FIP of 3.2. In his MLB career, Woo has allowed 150 base hits while accumulating 170 punch outs in 187 frames.
