BOTTOM LINE: The Tampa Bay Rays play the Toronto Blue Jays with a 1-0 series lead.

Tampa Bay has a 76-78 record overall and a 40-39 record in home games. The Rays have a 22-8 record in games when they hit two or more home runs.

Toronto is 35-44 in road games and 73-81 overall. The Blue Jays have a 57-11 record in games when they scored five or more runs.

Saturday's game is the 12th time these teams meet this season. The Rays are ahead 7-4 in the season series.

TOP PERFORMERS: Christopher Morel has 11 doubles, three triples, 21 home runs and 57 RBI while hitting .197 for the Rays. Yandy Diaz is 11-for-32 with three doubles over the last 10 games.
