A win for Taj Bradley, and Rays move into 3rd place in AL East
The second-year right-hander gets his first W since winning at Toronto in late July; the team is alone in 3rd place for 1st time since late May.
Starter Taj Bradley picks up his first victory since late July, as the Rays top the Blue Jays on Saturday at Tropicana Field. [ STEVE NESIUS | AP ]

Marc TopkinTimes staff
