CLEMENTE WEEKEND COMMUNITY EVENTS
ROBERTO CLEMENTE 3000 EXHIBIT
TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 12 – SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 17

The Pirates are partnering with GFR Media in Puerto Rico to bring a unique and one-of-it’s-kind exhibit to Pittsburgh featuring a previously unpublished selection of images related to the historic life and legacy of The Great One, Roberto Clemente. Fans can visit the exhibit at PNC Park, starting Tuesday, September 12 through the conclusion of Roberto Clemente Weekend on September 17.

ROBERTO CLEMENTE GALA
THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 14

The annual Roberto Clemente Foundation Gala will take place on Thursday, September 14, 2023 from 6:30 - 11:59 p.m. at the Senator John Heinz History Center and Western Pennsylvania Sports Museum, Pittsburgh, PA.

CLEMENTE DAY OF SERVICE
FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 15

The Pittsburgh Pirates celebrate the legacy of Roberto Clemente with their annual Roberto Clemente Day of Service which includes participation from players, executive leadership and Pirates employees. This year the Day of Service includes stops at locations all around Pittsburgh representing the Pirates dedication to community service.

Moderated by Pittsburgh Pirates In-Game Host Joe Klimchak, panelists Roberto Clemente Jr. and Luis Clemente will discuss Clemente's impact on and off the field and share about his remarkable career and humanitarian efforts. CLEMENTE FAMILY WALK
SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 17

Join Clemente fans in walking the North Shore to commemorate the life and legacy of Roberto Clemente. The walk will begin at the historic statue of The Great One and travel along the outfield Riverwalk of PNC Park. All paid participants will receive a limited edition cap, a ticket voucher for an upcoming Pirates game and a commemorative race bib. Proceeds benefit The Roberto Clemente Foundation to help underserved children and families all around the world.
