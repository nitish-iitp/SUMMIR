2023 Topps Tier One Baseball retains its premium format and high-end styles with an all-hit lineup.

Collectors can count on two autographs and one relic in each Hobby box.

Although Topps.com box preorders ($199.99) were scheduled to begin on August 22, the presale was postponed until September 19 at 2 PM (Eastern).

The release day pricing was bumped to $249.99 per box.

See also: 2022 Topps Tier One Baseball Cards

2023 Topps Tier One Baseball Autographs

The 2023 Topps Tier One Baseball checklist is built for hits. This means a selection of autographs with most adding Bronze Ink (#/25), Silver Ink (#/10) and Gold Ink (1/1) parallels.

Young stars again appear in the Breakout Out Autographs series.

The debut Advanced Stats Stars Autographs set highlights statistical standouts with on-card autographs.

As usual, the returning Tier One Autographs line acts as a case hit.

More choices are available in Dual Autographs (#/25 or less), Triple Autographs (#/10 or less), Clear Ones Autographs (#/10 or less), Dual Clear Ones Autographs (#/10 or less), and Cut Signatures (1/1).

2023 Topps Tier One Baseball Autograph Relics

Another key part of the 2023 Topps Tier One Baseball checklist is geared to memorabilia with most lines returning from past sets, such as Autograph Tier One Relics (#/99 or less), Dual Autograph Tier One Relic Book (#/10), Gripping Autographed Relics (#/10 or less), with grip tape pieces, Perfect Contact Autographed Bat Relics (#/5), with bat slices, and Prodigious Patches Autographs (#/10), with jumbo uniform patches.

The new Tier One Sluggers Triple Autograph Relics cards identify some of the best hitters in history.

In addition, fans can chase one-of-one rarities with Signature Tools Autograph Relics, Tier One Autographed Bat Knobs, Tier One Autographed Limited Lumber, and Cut Signatures Relics.

2023 Topps Tier One Baseball Relics

Among the relic-only lines, 2023 Topps Tier One Baseball features Tier One Relics (#/399 or less), Tier One Legends Relics (#/199 or less) and Gripping Relics (#/10 or less).

Prodigious Patches (#/10) is again home to oversized swatches.

Fans should also be on the lookout for some very rare relic sets, including Tier One All-Star Patches (1/1), Tier One Bat Knobs (1/1), Tier One Limited Lumber (1/1), and Tier One Uniform Button Relics (#/5).

Release Date: October 25, 2023
Hobby Configuration: 3 cards per pack, 1 pack per box
