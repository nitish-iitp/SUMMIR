MLB: Milwaukee Brewers lose to St. Louis Cardinals but clinch NL Central title (15 images)
By Wade Sheridan
The Milwaukee Brewers clinched the National League Central title after losing to the St. Louis Cardinals 4-1 at American Family Field in Milwaukee on Tuesday. The Brewers won their third NL Central title in six seasons after a Chicago Cubs fielding error led to an Atlanta Braves victory.



The Milwaukee Brewers celebrate after winning the National League Central Division championship at American Family Field in Milwaukee on September 26, 2023. The Brewers, who lost earlier to the St. Louis Cardinals 4-1, won the championship when the Chicago Cubs lost to the Atlanta Braves. Photo by Tannen Maury/UPI
