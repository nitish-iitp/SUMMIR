Guardians starter: Ben Lively
The Guardians will call on Lively (11-9) to make his 27th start of the season. He is 11-9 with a 4.01 ERA and 110 Ks through 137 2/3 innings pitched.
The right-hander’s last time out came on Tuesday versus the Chicago White Sox, as he tossed two scoreless innings while allowing just one hit.
The 32-year-old has posted a 4.01 ERA and 7.2 Ks per nine innings across 26 games this season, while allowing a batting average of .243 to opposing batters.
Lively is trying to register his 10th quality start in this matchup.
