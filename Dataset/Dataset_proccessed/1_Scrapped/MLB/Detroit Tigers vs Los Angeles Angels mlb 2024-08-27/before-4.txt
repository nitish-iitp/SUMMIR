Their fielding percentage is holding at .985 which ranks 19th in professional baseball, and they have turned 115 double plays.

Cueto has taken the mound for 2,250 innings and is sitting with 1,852 strikeouts in his career. His ERA is 3.51 (877 earned runs allowed) and he has a career WHIP of 1.210. Detroit has recorded 134 home runs on the year to go along with 535 runs batted in. They have recorded 200 doubles, while taking a walk 368 times as well as scoring 556 runs. They sit at 13th in MLB as a staff in total hits given up with 1,048. They have allowed 131 dingers and they concede 4.23 runs per 9 innings (11th in MLB). The Detroit pitching staff have given up 546 runs over the course of the season while having a team ERA of 3.85 (497 earned runs yielded).

They are currently ranked 24th in the league holding a save rate of 58.3%, and they have dispatched 401 relievers to the hill for the year. Their relief pitchers have stepped onto the hill 124 times in high leverage situations in addition to 142 occasions with runners on base. Detroit relief pitchers hold an inherited score rate of 25.4% of 197 inherited base runners. With 107 save situations, the Tigers have earned 58 holds in addition to 20 blown saves.

The Detroit Tigers have gotten 103 double plays and own a fielding percentage of .986 (13th in pro baseball).
