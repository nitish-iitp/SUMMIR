BOTTOM LINE: The Philadelphia Phillies host the Tampa Bay Rays to open a three-game series.

Philadelphia has an 85-58 record overall and a 47-25 record in home games. The Phillies have a 60-11 record in games when they scored five or more runs.

Tampa Bay has a 71-72 record overall and a 34-34 record in road games. The Rays are 48-9 in games when they out-hit their opponents.

Monday's game is the first meeting between these teams this season.

TOP PERFORMERS: Nicholas Castellanos has 27 doubles, three triples, 19 home runs and 78 RBI for the Phillies. Kyle Schwarber is 14-for-42 with three doubles and five home runs over the last 10 games.
