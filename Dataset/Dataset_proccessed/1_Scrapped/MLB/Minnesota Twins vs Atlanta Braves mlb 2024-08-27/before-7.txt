BOTTOM LINE: The Chicago White Sox host the Detroit Tigers looking to stop their five-game home skid.

Chicago has a 31-100 record overall and an 18-47 record at home. The White Sox have a 9-26 record in games decided by one run.

Detroit has a 34-34 record on the road and a 65-66 record overall. The Tigers have the fifth-ranked team ERA in the AL at 3.85.

Monday's game is the 10th time these teams match up this season. The Tigers are ahead 8-1 in the season series.

TOP PERFORMERS: Andrew Vaughn has 28 doubles and 15 home runs for the White Sox. Korey Lee is 9-for-36 with three doubles, a home run and four RBI over the past 10 games.
