Cleveland Guardians, Minnesota Twins starting lineups for Sept. 17, 2024: Game 152
Published: Sep. 17, 2024, 2:55 p.m.

Gavin Williams and the Guardians face the Twins at Progressive Field.AP

By Joe Noga, cleveland.com

CLEVELAND, Ohio — Gavin Williams and the Guardians face the Twins at Progressive Field..

Here are the starting lineups for Tuesday’s game between the Guardians and Twins.

Where: Progressive Field, 6:40 p.m. ET.

TV/radio: Bally Sports Great Lakes, WTAM 1100 AM, WMMS 100.7 FM, WARF 1350 AM (Spanish) and the Guardians Radio Network will carry the game.

Teams: Guardians (87-64) vs. Twins (79-71).

Starting pitchers: RHP Gavin Williams (3-9, 5.23) vs. RHP Zebby Matthews (1-3, 7.11).
