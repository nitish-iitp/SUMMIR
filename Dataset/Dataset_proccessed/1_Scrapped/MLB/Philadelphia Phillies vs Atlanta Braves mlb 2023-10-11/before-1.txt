2023 Topps Update release date & release format

The release date for 2023 Topps Update is scheduled for October 11, 2023 (subject to change).

Topps Update comes in a variety of different configurations, including both hobby and retail offerings.

Hobby Boxes contain 24 packs of 14 cards each. There are 12 hobby boxes per hobby case. Each hobby box contains 1 autograph or relic card. Hobby Boxes are pre-selling for $80.

Hobby Jumbo Boxes contain 10 packs of 46 cards each. There are 6 hobby jumbo boxes per case. Each hobby jumbo box contains one autograph and two relic cards. Hobby Jumbo boxes are pre-selling for $150.

Retail Boxes contain 20 packs of 14 cards each. There are 12 retail boxes per case. Retail boxes do not offer guaranteed hits, and autographs will be fairly tough in all retail products. Prices for Retail boxes are not yet known.

Retail Blasters contain 7 packs of 12 cards each. There are 40 blaster boxes per case. These contain 3 exclusive retail parallels per Blaster. Blaster boxes are pre-selling for $25.

Retail Hanger Boxes contain 64 cards per hanger. Prices for Hanger Boxes have not yet been released.
