Cleveland Guardians, New York Yankees ALCS Game 4 starting lineups for Oct. 18, 2024
Updated: Oct. 18, 2024, 5:54 p.m.|Published: Oct. 18, 2024, 4:34 p.m.

Gavin Williams, Cleveland Guardians.Getty Images

By Joe Noga, cleveland.com

CLEVELAND, Ohio — Riding the euphoria of a 7-5 comeback win in extra innings on Thursday, the Guardians look to extend the American League Championship series and guarantee a return to New York when they face the Yankees in Game 4.

Here are the starting lineups for Friday’s game between the Guardians and Yankees.

Where: Progressive Field, 8:08 p.m. ET.

TV/radio: TBS, truTV, Max, WTAM 1100 AM, WMMS 100.7 FM, WARF 1350 AM (Spanish) and the Guardians Radio Network will carry the game.

Teams: Guardians (4-4) vs. Yankees (5-2).

Starting pitchers: RHP Gavin Williams (0-0, 0.00) vs. RHP Luis Gil (0-0, 0.00).
