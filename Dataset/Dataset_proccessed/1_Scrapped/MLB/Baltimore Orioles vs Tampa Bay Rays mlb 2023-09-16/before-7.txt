Detained Haitians slated for deportation stand in a police truck on a border bridge between Dajabón, Dominican Republic, and Haiti. The president of the Dominican Republic announced Thursday that he would close all borders with neighbouring Haiti starting Friday in response to the construction of a supposed canal on Haitian soil that targets waters from the Massacre River, which runs along the border shared by both countries.
