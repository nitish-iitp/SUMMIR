The Mexican national team will take on Colombia in the group play of the U-20 FIFA Women’s World Cup tonight. This will be the third game and last game of the group stage for both of these teams tonight. Mexico is currently second in Group A after taking on Cameroon in the team’s first game ending in a draw and beating Australia in its second game. Mexico’s draw ended 2-2 with eight total shots and just three on target. The two goals in the draw for Mexico came from forwards Paola Garcia and Montserrat Saldivar. In its win over Australia, Fatima Servin and Alejandra Lomeli had the two goals in the 2-0 shutout. With a total of four points, they need a win to secure advancement.
