Houston Astros Birthdays

RHP Dylan Coleman (28) has pitched one inning this season for Houston.

OF Robbie Grossman (35) hit .240 with 11 homers in 190 games for Houston from 2013 through 2015.

RHP Chad Harville (48) pitched 91 1⁄3 innings for Houston between 2004 and 2005, going 3-4 with a 5.28 FIP and a 93 ERA+.

IF Hector Torres (79) played 193 games for the Astros between 1968 and 1970, then returned in 1973 for 38 more. He slashed .235/.281/.314.
