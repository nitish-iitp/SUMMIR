Jacob deGrom goes up against the M’s

Texas Rangers lineup for September 20, 2024 against the Seattle Mariners: starting pitchers are Jacob deGrom for the Rangers and George Kirby for the M’s.

Texas starts their final home series of the season tonight, and are doing so in fine fashion, what with Jacob deGrom starting. Josh Jung is out of the lineup, with Jonathan Ornelas taking his place at third base.

The lineup:

Semien — 2B

Smith — SS

Langford — LF

Garcia — RF

Lowe — 1B

Heim — C

Duran — DH

Taveras — CF

Ornelas — 3B

7:05 p.m. Central start time. Rangers are -120 favorites.
