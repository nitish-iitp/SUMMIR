Rays make a mess in opening loss to Rangers, must win to stay alive
Four errors plus a run-scoring wild pitch, six walks by their pitchers and just one hit with a runner in scoring position make for a bad day.
Rays centerfielder Jose Siri (22) is unable to make a catch on a fly ball hit by Rangers shortstop Corey Seager (5) during the fifth inning in Game 1 of the Wild Card Series Tuesday. [ IVY CEBALLO | Times ]
By
Marc TopkinTimes staff
Published Oct. 3, 2023|Updated Oct. 4, 2023

ST. PETERSBURG — All the momentum, all the good vibes, all the optimism the Rays had going into the Wild Card Series opener against Texas disappeared quickly Tuesday when they played a miserably sloppy game in all facets, losing 4-0.

“We didn’t play a good, clean baseball game by any stretch,” manager Kevin Cash said.
