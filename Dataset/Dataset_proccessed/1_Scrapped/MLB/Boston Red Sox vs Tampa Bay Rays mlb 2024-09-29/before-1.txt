How do Rays improve for 2025? A first look at what they need to do
Improving the offense needs to be Job 1 and may take several additions, as well as improvement from those the team has.
One area the Rays know they have to improve for next year is at the plate. Here, Jose Siri walks off after striking out. [ LUIS SANTANA | Times ]
By
Marc TopkinTimes staff
Published Sept. 28, 2024|Updated Sept. 29, 2024

BOSTON — At this time next year, the Rays are hoping they’ll have a lot more to do, such as prepping intensely for their first playoff opponent.

To get there from here — heading into the final weekend in fourth place in the American League East with a 79-81 record and playing their first meaningless-to-them games since September 2018 — will take some work.

Most critically, they need to bolster their offense.
