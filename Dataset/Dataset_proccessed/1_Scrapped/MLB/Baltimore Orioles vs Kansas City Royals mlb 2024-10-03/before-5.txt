The following is the MLB postseason schedule, including dates, start times (Eastern time zone), TV networks and scheduled announcers.

Friday, October 25, 2024

[AL #1] New York Yankees at [NL #1] Los Angeles Dodgers — World Series Game 1 (Fox, 8:08 p.m. ET/5:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
Los Angeles hosts New York in Game 1 of the 2024 World Series. The former crosstown rivals contest the Fall Classic for a record 12th time. The Yankees, who boast an MLB-leading 27 league titles, have won eight of those 11 Series against the Dodgers.

Saturday, October 26, 2024

[AL #1] New York Yankees at [NL #1] Los Angeles Dodgers — World Series Game 2 (Fox, 8:08 p.m. ET/5:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
The Dodgers look to extend their advantage over the Yankees, up 1-0 in Game 2 of the World Series. Freddie Freeman provided the storybook ending by hitting a grand slam in the bottom of the 10th inning to give Los Angeles a 6-3 win in Game 1.

Monday, October 28, 2024

[NL #1] Los Angeles Dodgers at [AL #1] New York Yankees — World Series Game 3 (Fox, 8:08 p.m. ET/5:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
The Yankees host the Dodgers for Game 3 of the World Series. Los Angeles earned a 2-0 series advantage after hanging on to beat New York 4-2 in Game 2. LA homered three times in the win, but Shohei Ohtani suffered a shoulder injury and left the game.

Tuesday, October 29, 2024

[NL #1] Los Angeles Dodgers at [AL #1] New York Yankees — World Series Game 4 (Fox, 8:08 p.m. ET/5:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
The Yankees host the Dodgers for Game 4 of the World Series. Los Angeles has a chance for an eighth MLB title after cruising to a 4-2 win in Game 3. Freddie Freeman (LAD) became the second in history to homer in five straight Fall Classic contests.

Wednesday, October 30, 2024

[NL #1] Los Angeles Dodgers at [AL #1] New York Yankees — World Series Game 5 (Fox, 8:08 p.m. ET/5:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
Los Angeles visits New York in Game 5 of the World Series, up 3-1. The Yankees lived to fight another day after their bats woke up for an 11-4 victory in Game 4. Freddie Freeman (LAD) became the first in MLB history to homer in six straight WS games.

Friday, November 1, 2024

[AL #1] New York Yankees at [NL #1] Los Angeles Dodgers — World Series Game 6 (Fox, 8:08 p.m. ET/5:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci

Saturday, November 2, 2024

[AL #1] New York Yankees at [NL #1] Los Angeles Dodgers — World Series Game 7 (Fox, 8:08 p.m. ET/5:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci

PRIOR GAMES

Tuesday, October 1, 2024

[#6] Detroit Tigers at [#3] Houston Astros — American League Wild Card Game 1 (ABC, 2:32 p.m. ET/1:32 p.m. CT)
Announcers: Michael Kay, Todd Frazier, Tim Kurkjian, Alden Gonzalez
AL Cy Young favorite Tarik Skubal and the Tigers face the Astros in Game 1 of an AL wild-card set. Detroit is making its first postseason appearance since 2014, while Houston seeks an eighth straight trip to the American League Championship Series.

Houston won the AL West for the fourth consecutive season and finished the regular season with an 88-73 record; Detroit was 17-8 in September and reached the playoffs for the first time in 10 years with an 86-76 record in the AL Central.

[#5] Kansas City Royals at [#4] Baltimore Orioles — American League Wild Card Game 1 (ESPN2, 4:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Sean McDonough, Jessica Mendoza, Ben McDonald, Jeff Passan
The Orioles host the Royals in Game 1 of an American League wild-card series. Kansas City swept Baltimore in the 2014 ALCS. Bobby Witt Jr. became the first Royal to win the AL batting title since the legendary George Brett in 1990. Cole Ragans (KC) and Corbin Burnes (BAL) start.

Baltimore reached the postseason in consecutive years for the first time since 1997. Kansas City is in the playoffs for the first time since it won the World Series in 2015.

[#6] New York Mets at [#3] Milwaukee Brewers — National League Wild Card Game 1 (ESPN, 5:32 p.m. ET/4:32 p.m. CT)
Announcers: Jon Sciambi, Doug Glanville, Jesse Rogers
The Brewers host the Mets for Game 1 of this NL wild-card series. New York punched its ticket with a wild 8-7 victory in the first half of a doubleheader with Atlanta on Sept. 30. Milwaukee wears the NL Central crown for the third time in four years.

Milwaukee won the National League Central for the third time in four years after going 93-69 behind Willy Adames, who was tied for fourth in the MLB with 112 RBI.

[#5] Atlanta Braves at [#4] San Diego Padres — National League Wild Card Game 1 (ESPN, 8:38 p.m. ET/5:38 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Karl Ravech, David Cone, Eduardo Pérez, Buster Olney
The Padres host the weary Braves for Game 1 of an NL best-of-three wild-card series. Atlanta traveled to San Diego after earning a playoff berth with a doubleheader split against the New York Mets on Sept. 30. The Padres went 43-20 after the break.

San Diego is playoff bound for the third time in five years after finishing the regular season 93-69 behind NL batting champion Luis Arraez and Manny Machado.

Wednesday, October 2, 2024

[#6] Detroit Tigers at [#3] Houston Astros — American League Wild Card Game 2 (ABC, 2:32 p.m. ET/1:32 p.m. CT)
Announcers: Michael Kay, Todd Frazier, Tim Kurkjian, Alden Gonzalez
The Tigers try to bounce the Astros in Game 2 of their AL wild-card series. Tarik Skubal threw six shutout innings and earned the win as Detroit rolled to a 3-1 win over Houston in Game 1. Catcher Jake Rogers (DET) was 2-for-4 with an RBI and a run.

[#5] Kansas City Royals at [#4] Baltimore Orioles — American League Wild Card Game 2 (ESPN, 4:38 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Sean McDonough, Jessica Mendoza, Ben McDonald, Jeff Passan
The Orioles host the Royals for Game 2 of their American League wild-card series. Kansas City superstar Bobby Witt Jr. drove home Game 1’s only run to give the Royals a 1-0 win and series lead. Baltimore has lost its past nine postseason contests.

[#6] New York Mets at [#3] Milwaukee Brewers — National League Wild Card Game 2 (ESPN, 7:38 p.m. ET/6:38 p.m. CT)
Announcers: Jon Sciambi, Doug Glanville, Jesse Rogers
The Mets try to put away the Brewers in Game 2 of this National League wild-card series. New York’s five-run fifth inning powered an 8-4 double up in Game 1. New York’s Matt Vientos, Jesse Winker and J.D. Martinez each drove in two runs in the win.

[#5] Atlanta Braves at [#4] San Diego Padres — National League Wild Card Game 2 (ESPN2, 8:38 p.m. ET/5:38 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Karl Ravech, David Cone, Eduardo Pérez, Buster Olney
The Padres aim to eliminate the Braves in Game 2 of their NL Wild Card Series. San Diego got a two-run home run from Fernando Tatís Jr. off Atlanta opener A.J. Smith-Shawver and Michael King struck out 12 Atlanta batters in a 4-0 Game 1 victory.

 

Thursday, October 3, 2024

 

[#6] New York Mets at [#3] Milwaukee Brewers — National League Wild Card Game 3 (ESPN, 7:06 p.m. ET/6:06 p.m. CT)
Announcers: Jon Sciambi, Doug Glanville, Jesse Rogers
Rookie slugger Jackson Chourio and the Brewers meet the Mets in the deciding Game 3 of their NL wild-card series. Chourio belted two solo home runs in Milwaukee’s 5-3 victory in Game 2. Fellow rookie Tobias Myers takes the ball for the Brewers.

 

Saturday, October 5, 2024

 

[#6] Detroit Tigers at [#2] Cleveland Guardians — American League Division Series Game 1 (TBS/truTV, 1:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Lauren Jbara
Central Division rivals in the Tigers and Guardians contest Game 1 of their best-of-five American League Division Series. Detroit stunned Houston with a two-game sweep in the wild-card round. AL Central champion Cleveland claimed the season set 7-6.

[#6] New York Mets at [#2] Philadelphia Phillies — National League Division Series Game 1 (Fox, 4:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Adam Amin, AJ Pierzynski, Adam Wainwright, Tom Verducci
The Phillies host the Mets for Game 1 of their NL Division Series. Pete Alonso’s three-run shot in the ninth inning lifted New York to a 4-2 victory at Milwaukee in the deciding game of their wild-card match. Philly won seven of 13 meetings in 2024.

[#5] Kansas City Royals at [#1] New York Yankees — American League Division Series Game 1 (TBS, 6:38 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Bob Costas, Ron Darling, J.P. Morosi
The Royals take on the Yankees in Game 1 of their American League Division Series. Kansas City and New York did battle four times in a five-season stretch from 1976-80 for the right to go to the World Series. The Yankees won three of those clashes.

[#5] San Diego Padres at [#1] Los Angeles Dodgers — National League Division Series Game 1 (FS1, 8:38 p.m. ET/5:38 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal
Bitter rivals meet again in the playoffs as the Dodgers host the Padres for Game 1 of their NL Division Series. Los Angeles swept San Diego in this round en route to its 2020 World Series title, but the Padres struck back for a 3-1 NLDS win in 2022.

 

Sunday, October 6, 2024

 

[#6] New York Mets at [#2] Philadelphia Phillies — National League Division Series Game 2 (FS1, 4:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Adam Amin, AJ Pierzynski, Adam Wainwright, Tom Verducci
The Phillies try to tie their NL Division Series against the Mets, down 1-0 entering Game 2. After falling behind 1-0 early, New York pieced together a five-run eighth inning for an eventual 6-2 Game 1 victory. Brandon Nimmo (NYM) had two RBIs.

[#5] San Diego Padres at [#1] Los Angeles Dodgers — National League Division Series Game 2 (FS1, 8:03 p.m. ET/5:03 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal
The Dodgers and Padres resume their NL Division Series with Game 2. Los Angeles took Game 1 with a 7-5 win over San Diego. Shohei Ohtani homered in his first postseason game, while the Dodgers bullpen allowed just two hits over six scoreless innings.

 

Monday, October 7, 2024

 

[#6] Detroit Tigers at [#2] Cleveland Guardians — American League Division Series Game 2 (TBS/truTV, 4:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Lauren Jbara
The Guardians host the Tigers for Game 2 of their ALDS. In Game 1, Cleveland faced little difficulty in winning 7-0. Lane Thomas’ three-run homer capped a five-run first inning for Cleveland in the victory. David Fry (CLE) went 2-for-3 with two RBIs.

[#5] Kansas City Royals at [#1] New York Yankees — American League Division Series Game 2 (TBS/truTV, 7:38 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Bob Costas, Ron Darling, J.P. Morosi
New York hosts Kansas City for Game 2 of their AL Division Series. The Yankees won a back-and-forth affair 6-5 in Game 1. New York’s Gleyber Torres and Austin Wells each produced two RBIs. Cole Ragans (KC) and Carlos Rodón (NYY) start on the mound.

 

Tuesday, October 8, 2024

 

[#2] Philadelphia Phillies at [#6] New York Mets — National League Division Series Game 3 (FS1, 5:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Adam Amin, AJ Pierzynski, Adam Wainwright, Tom Verducci
The Mets host the Phillies for Game 3 of their tied NL Division Series. Philadelphia erased multiple deficits in Game 2 for a 7-6 win. Nick Castellanos was 3-for-5, hit a home run, and delivered a walk-off single in the ninth inning to even the set.

[#1] Los Angeles Dodgers at [#5] San Diego Padres — National League Division Series Game 3 (FS1, 9:03 p.m. ET/6:03 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal
The NL Division Series shifts south as the Padres host the Dodgers for Game 3 of their tied set. Fernando Tatís Jr. homered twice as San Diego earned a split in Los Angeles with a 10-2 triumph in Game 2. Walker Buehler will start for the Dodgers.

 

Wednesday, October 9, 2024

 

[#2] Cleveland Guardians at [#6] Detroit Tigers — American League Division Series Game 3 (TBS/truTV, 3:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Lauren Jbara
The Tigers and Guardians play Game 3 of their AL Division Series. Detroit won 3-0 in Game 2. Emmanuel Clase (CLE), who yielded only five earned runs in 75 previous appearances in 2024, allowed a three-run shot to Kerry Carpenter to decide the game.

[#2] Philadelphia Phillies at [#6] New York Mets — National League Division Series Game 4 (FS1, 5:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Adam Amin, AJ Pierzynski, Adam Wainwright, Tom Verducci
The Mets host the Phillies for Game 4 of their NLDS. New York is one win away from playing for the pennant after beating Philadelphia in Game 3. New York teammates Pete Alonso and Jesse Winker each hit a solo home run to jump start a 7-2 victory.

[#1] New York Yankees at [#5] Kansas City Royals — American League Division Series Game 3 (TBS/truTV, 7:08 p.m. ET/6:03 p.m. CT)
Announcers: Bob Costas, Ron Darling, J.P. Morosi
The Royals seek an edge over the Yankees as they host Game 3 of their AL Division Series. Kansas City rode a four-run fourth inning to a 4-2 decision over New York in Game 2 in the Bronx, tying the series 1-1. Clarke Schmidt starts for the Yankees.

[#1] Los Angeles Dodgers at [#5] San Diego Padres — National League Division Series Game 4 (FS1, 9:03 p.m. ET/6:03 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal
The Dodgers have their backs against the wall in Game 4 of the NL Division Series against the Padres. San Diego put up a six-run second inning in Game 3 and made it hold up for a 6-5 victory over Los Angeles to take a 2-1 advantage in the set.

 

Thursday, October 10, 2024

 

[#2] Cleveland Guardians at [#6] Detroit Tigers — American League Division Series Game 4 (TNT/UniMás, 6:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers:
TNT: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Lauren Jbara
UniMás: Antonio de Valdés, Enrique Burak, Daniel Nohra, Nelson Cruz

The upstart Tigers try to take out the Guardians in Game 4 of their AL Division Series. Detroit is one win away from reaching its first AL Championship Series since 2013 following a 3-0 victory in Game 3. Cleveland gives the ball to ace Tanner Bibee. Los emergentes Tigers intentarán eliminar a los Guardians en el cuarto partido de la Serie Divisional de la Liga Americana. Detroit está a una victoria de alcanzar su primera Serie de Campeonato LA desde 2013.

[#1] New York Yankees at [#5] Kansas City Royals — American League Division Series Game 4 (TBS/truTV/UniMás, 8:08 p.m. ET/7:03 p.m. CT)
Announcers:
TBS/truTV: Bob Costas, Ron Darling, J.P. Morosi
UniMás: Antonio de Valdés, Enrique Burak, Daniel Nohra, Nelson Cruz

The Yankees look to put away the Royals in Game 4 of their AL Division Series. Giancarlo Stanton broke a 2-2 tie with an eighth-inning homer off Kris Bubic to give New York a 3-2 victory over Kansas City in Game 3. It was his second RBI of the game. Los Yankees y los Royals buscan evitar extender al quinto juego de la Serie Divisional de la Liga Americana. La última vez que se enfrentaron en postemporada fue en 1980 donde Kansas City barrió Nueva York en 3 juegos.

 

Friday, October 11, 2024

 

[#5] San Diego Padres at [#1] Los Angeles Dodgers — National League Division Series Game 5 (Fox, 8:03 p.m. ET/5:03 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal
The Dodgers and Padres meet in Hollywood for the decisive Game 5 of their NL Division Series. Los Angeles emphatically extended its season with an 8-0 win in San Diego in Game 4. The winner will face the New York Mets for a berth in the World Series. Los Dodgers y los Padres se enfrentan en Hollywood en el decisivo quinto partido de la Serie Divisional de la Liga Nacional. El equipo ganador se enfrentará a los New York Mets por un puesto en la Serie Mundial.

 

Saturday, October 12, 2024

 

[#6] Detroit Tigers at [#2] Cleveland Guardians — American League Division Series Game 5 (TBS/UniMás, 1:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers:
TBS: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Lauren Jbara
UniMás: Antonio de Valdés, Enrique Burak, Daniel Nohra

The Guardians and Tigers duel in a winner-take-all Game 5 of their AL Division Series. Cleveland brought the set back to the Lake Erie shores with a dramatic 5-4 Game 4 win over Detroit. David Fry put the Guardians up with a pinch-hit two-run homer.

 

Sunday, October 13, 2024

 

[#6] New York Mets at [#1] Los Angeles Dodgers — National League Championship Series Game 1 (Fox, 8:15 p.m. ET/5:15 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
The Dodgers host the Mets for Game 1 of the NLCS. New York was 11 games below .500 after losing to Los Angeles on May 29, but have since gone 72-42 for an improbable NLCS run. Enrique Hernández’s 14th career playoff homer helped LA bounce San Diego.

Los Dodgers reciben a los Mets en el primer partido de la Serie de Campeonato de la Liga Nacional. Nueva York estaba 11 partidos por debajo de .500 tras caer contra Los Ángeles el 29 de mayo, pero han ido 72-42 para una carrera NLCS improbable.

The Dodgers and Mets are meeting in the National League Championship Series for the first time since 1988, and it is the fourth meeting in the postseason.

 

Monday, October 14, 2024

 

[#6] New York Mets at [#1] Los Angeles Dodgers — National League Championship Series Game 2 (Fox/FS1, 4:08 p.m. ET/1:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
Los Angeles hosts New York for Game 2 of the National League Championship Series. Each Dodgers starter reached base at least once in a 9-0 runaway in Game 1. LA stars Shohei Ohtani, Mookie Betts and Freddie Freeman combined for five RBIs in the win.

[#2] Cleveland Guardians at [#1] New York Yankees — American League Championship Series Game 1 (TBS/truTV/UniMás, 7:38 p.m. ET)
Announcers:
TBS: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Ron Darling, Lauren Jbara
truTV: JP Morosi, Dexter Fowler, Mike Petriello
UniMás: Antonio de Valdés, Enrique Burak, Nelson Cruz

The Yankees host the Guardians for Game 1 of the American League Championship Series. New York took Cleveland out in the 2022 ALDS, and won the season set 4-2. Aaron Judge hit five home runs in those games, but is hitting just .154 this postseason.

 

Tuesday, October 15, 2024

 

[#2] Cleveland Guardians at [#1] New York Yankees — American League Championship Series Game 2 (TBS/truTV/UniMás, 7:38 p.m. ET)
Announcers:
TBS: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Ron Darling, Lauren Jbara
truTV: JP Morosi, Dexter Fowler, Mike Petriello
UniMás: Antonio de Valdés, Enrique Burak, Nelson Cruz
The Yankees and Guardians throw down their aces in Game 2 of the ALCS. New York’s Gerrit Cole opposes Tanner Bibee. The Yankees prevailed 5-2 in Game 1 behind a strong outing by Carlos Rodón, and solo home runs from Juan Soto and Giancarlo Stanton.

Segundo juego de la Serie de Campeonato de la Liga Americana. New York Yankees reciben a Cleveland Guardians en el Yankee Stadium. Jugando en el Bronx, los Yankees intentan avanzar a la final de las Grandes Ligas, necesitando un resultado en casa.

 

Wednesday, October 16, 2024

 

[#1] Los Angeles Dodgers at [#6] New York Mets — National League Championship Series Game 3 (FS1, 8:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
The Dodgers and Mets cross the country to play Game 3 of the NLCS. In Game 2, Mark Vientos hit a grand slam to help New York race to a 6-0 lead, and disrupt Los Angeles’ planned bullpen day. The Mets eventually prevailed 7-3, evening the series 1-1.

 

Thursday, October 17, 2024

 

[#1] New York Yankees at [#2] Cleveland Guardians — American League Championship Series Game 3 (TBS/truTV/UniMás, 5:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers:
TBS: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Ron Darling, Lauren Jbara
truTV: JP Morosi, Dexter Fowler, Mike Petriello
UniMás: Antonio de Valdés, Enrique Burak, Daniel Nohra, Nelson Cruz
The Guardians host the Yankees in Game 3 of the American League Championship Series. Aaron Judge swatted a two-run shot in the seventh inning of Game 2, his first homer of the postseason, to secure a 6-3 win and 2-0 series lead for New York.

Guardians reciben a Yankees en el tercer partido de la Serie de Campeonato de la Liga Americana. Aaron Judge bateó un cuadrangular de 2 carreras en la séptima entrada y aseguró la victoria, además de la ventaja de 2-0 en la serie para Nueva York.

[#1] Los Angeles Dodgers at [#6] New York Mets — National League Championship Series Game 4 (FS1, 8:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
The Mets try to rebound in the NL Championship Series, down 2-1 entering Game 4 versus the Dodgers. Los Angeles shut out New York 8-0 in Game 3. Five Dodgers, led by Walker Buehler, combined on a four-hitter while Shohei Ohtani hit a three-run homer.

 

Friday, October 18, 2024

 

[#1] Los Angeles Dodgers at [#6] New York Mets — National League Championship Series Game 5 (FS1, 5:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
The Dodgers aim to eliminate the Mets in Game 5 of their NL Championship Series. Los Angeles pushed New York to the brink with a decisive 10-2 victory in Game 4 to take a 3-1 lead. Mookie Betts was 4-for-6 with four RBIs, while Shohei Ohtani homered.

[#1] New York Yankees at [#2] Cleveland Guardians — American League Championship Series Game 4 (TBS/truTV/UniMás, 8:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers:
TBS: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Ron Darling, Lauren Jbara
truTV: JP Morosi, Dexter Fowler, Mike Petriello
UniMás: Antonio de Valdés, Enrique Burak, Daniel Nohra, Nelson Cruz
The Guardians try for the equalizer against the Yankees in Game 4 of the American League Championship Series. Cleveland trails two games to one after winning a Game 3 for the ages 7-5 on David Fry’s two-run walk-off homer in the 10th inning.

 

Saturday, October 19, 2024

 

[#1] New York Yankees at [#2] Cleveland Guardians — American League Championship Series Game 5 (TBS/truTV/UniMás, 8:08 p.m. ET)
Announcers:
TBS: Brian Anderson, Jeff Francouer, Ron Darling, Lauren Jbara
truTV: JP Morosi, Dexter Fowler, Mike Petriello
UniMás: Antonio de Valdés, Enrique Burak, Daniel Nohra, Nelson Cruz
The Guardians try to stave off elimination in Game 5 of the ALCS against the Yankees. New York is one step away from its first AL pennant in 15 years after winning a wild Game 4. Juan Soto, Giancarlo Stanton and Austin Wells homered in the 8-6 win.

 

Sunday, October 20, 2024

 

[#6] New York Mets at [#1] Los Angeles Dodgers — National League Championship Series Game 6 (FS1, 8:08 p.m. ET/5:08 p.m. PT)
Announcers: Joe Davis, John Smoltz, Ken Rosenthal, Tom Verducci
The NL Championship Series returns to Chavez Ravine for Game 6, with the Dodgers up 3-2 on the Mets. New York staved off elimination by doubling up Los Angeles 12-6 in Game 5. Mark Vientos (NYM) leads the postseason with four home runs and 12 RBIs.
