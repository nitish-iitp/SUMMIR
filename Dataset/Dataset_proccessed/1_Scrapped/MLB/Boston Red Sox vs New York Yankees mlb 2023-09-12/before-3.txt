Game Recap: Men's Soccer | 9/9/2023 9:15:00 PM

Holy Cross falls at Dartmouth
Next Game:

at Rhode Island

9/12/2023 | 7:00 PM

ESPN+

RECAP
LIVE STATS
WATCH LIVE
FULL SCHEDULE
ROSTER
HANOVER, N.H. – Junior forward Evan Jones scored his first goal of the season, but the Holy Cross men's soccer team was defeated by Dartmouth 3-1 at Burnham Field. The Crusaders fall to 1-3-1 on the year, while the Big Green improve to 1-2-0 overall.

HOW IT HAPPENED:
• Dartmouth opened the scoring in the ninth minute on a goal by Oskar Magnusson.
• Ben Jenkins added a goal in the 34th minute to put the Big Green up 2-0 at halftime.
• Douglas Arveskar scored in the 56th minute to make the Dartmouth lead 3-0.
• Jones put the Crusaders on the board in the 59th minute, when he took a pass from junior midfielder Ivor Teagle at the top of the penalty box before scoring from 16 yards out.

INSIDE THE NUMBERS:
• The Big Green finished the game with a 15-4 advantage in shots and an 8-1 edge in corner kicks.
• Junior goalkeeper Tristan Stephani made a career-high seven saves on the night.
• In 42 career games played, Jones now has 14 goals and nine assists for 37 points.

NEXT UP:
• Holy Cross returns to action on Tuesday, Sept. 12, when it travels to Rhode Island for a 7 p.m. game.
