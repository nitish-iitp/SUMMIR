BOTTOM LINE: The Texas Rangers host the Toronto Blue Jays to start a three-game series.

Texas has a 41-34 record at home and a 71-79 record overall. The Rangers have a 42-11 record in games when they scored at least five runs.

Toronto is 72-78 overall and 34-41 on the road. Blue Jays hitters have a collective .314 on-base percentage, the sixth-ranked percentage in the AL.

The matchup Tuesday is the fourth time these teams meet this season.
