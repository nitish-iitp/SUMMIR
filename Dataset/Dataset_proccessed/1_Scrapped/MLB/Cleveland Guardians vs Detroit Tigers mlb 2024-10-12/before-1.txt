Guardians starter: Matthew Boyd
The Guardians will call on Boyd (2-2) to make his ninth start of the season. He is 2-2 with a 2.72 ERA and 46 Ks in 39 2/3 innings pitched.
When he last took the hill on Monday versus the Detroit Tigers, the lefty tossed 4 2/3 scoreless innings while surrendering four hits.
The 33-year-old has amassed an ERA of 2.72, with 10.4 strikeouts per nine innings in eight contests this year. Opposing hitters have a .213 batting average against him.
Boyd is trying to notch his fourth quality start of the season.
