Powerful Yankees once again shatter another October dream for Guardians — Paul Hoynes
Updated: Oct. 20, 2024, 4:18 p.m.|Published: Oct. 20, 2024, 2:42 p.m.
47

Cleveland Guardians vs. New York Yankees in game 5 of the ALCS, Ocotber 19, 2024john kuntz
By Paul Hoynes, cleveland.com

CLEVELAND, Ohio — The Yankees have made a habit of sending Cleveland’s baseball team home in October.

They did it again Saturday night in Game 5 of the American League Championship Series. It was a series that left manager Stephen Vogt and Tanner Bibee close to tears and a fan base wondering if the Guardians will ever intentionally walk Giancarlo Stanton.
