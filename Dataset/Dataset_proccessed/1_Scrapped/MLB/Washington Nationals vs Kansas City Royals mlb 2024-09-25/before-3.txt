The Washington Nationals will kick of their final home stand of the season with a series against the Kansas City Royals at Nationals Park

It's the Final Countdown

The Washington Nationals open their final homestand of the 2024 season on tonight, looking to build momentum ahead of their season-ending series against the Philadelphia Phillies.

Following their penultimate series against the Kansas City Royals, the Nationals will face the Phillies this weekend in a pivotal matchup that could shape their offseason plans.

Washington has significantly improved at home this season, with 36 wins at Nationals Park (36-39, .480). This marks their highest home win total since the 2019 season (50-31, .617).

Since June 7, the Nationals have posted a solid 26-22 record (.542) at home, contrasting their 10-17 (.370) start from Opening Day to June 6.
