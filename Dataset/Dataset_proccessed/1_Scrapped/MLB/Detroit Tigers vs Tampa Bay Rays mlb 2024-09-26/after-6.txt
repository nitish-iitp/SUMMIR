BOTTOM LINE: The Detroit Tigers, on a three-game home winning streak, host the Tampa Bay Rays.

Detroit has gone 41-36 in home games and 84-74 overall. The Tigers have a 36-5 record in games when they hit at least two home runs.

Tampa Bay has a 78-80 record overall and a 36-41 record in road games. The Rays have a 52-11 record in games when they out-hit their opponents.

Thursday's game is the sixth meeting between these teams this season. The Tigers are up 4-1 in the season series.

TOP PERFORMERS: Riley Greene has a .264 batting average to lead the Tigers, and has 26 doubles, six triples and 24 home runs. Parker Meadows is 10-for-41 with two home runs and three RBI over the past 10 games.
