Dónovan Solano prop bet insights
In 54.3% of his games this year (69 of 127), Solano has recorded a hit, and in 27 of those games (21.3%) he’s recorded at least two hits.
He has hit a home run in 3.9% of his games in 2023 (five of 127), 1.2% of his trips to the dish.
Solano has touched home plate at least one time in 24.4% of his games this season (31 of 127), with two or more runs scored in six of those contests (4.7%).
He has plated at least one run 24 times this season in 127 tries (18.9%), including plating multiple runs in 7.1% of games (nine times) and producing three or more of his team’s runs on four occasions..
Solano has gone down on strikes in 55.1% of his 127 games this season, with more than one strikeout in 17.3% of those games (22).

Athletics starter: Paul Blackburn
Blackburn (4-6) takes the hill for the Athletics in his 20th start of the season. He has produced a 4.29 ERA in 100 2/3 innings pitched, with 101 Ks.
His most recent outing was on Wednesday versus the Seattle Mariners, when the righty went five innings, allowing two earned runs while giving up four hits.
