MLB: Yankees down Orioles to claim 21st division title
Agence France-Presse / 03:32 PM September 27, 2024

The New York Yankees, including Aaron Judge, center top, celebrate after clinching the American League East title in an MLB against the Baltimore Orioles, Thursday, Sept. 26, 2024, in New York. (AP Photo/Noah K. Murray)

New York star Aaron Judge belted his g 58th home run and Giancarlo Stanton homered and drove in four runs as the Yankees clinched their 21st division title with a 10-1 victory over the Baltimore Orioles in the MLB on Thursday.

Gerrit Cole delivered a strong performance on the mound for the Yankees, who won the American League East crown for the second time in three years to secure a first-round bye and home-field advantage in the AL Division Series.
