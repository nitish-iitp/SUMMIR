BOTTOM LINE: The St. Louis Cardinals begin a three-game series at home against the Seattle Mariners on Friday.

St. Louis is 36-32 at home and 71-69 overall. The Cardinals have a 53-28 record in games when they record eight or more hits.

Seattle has gone 30-42 on the road and 71-70 overall. Mariners pitchers have a collective 3.53 ERA, which leads MLB.

The matchup Friday is the first meeting this season between the two teams.

TOP PERFORMERS: Masyn Winn leads the Cardinals with a .277 batting average, and has 26 doubles, four triples, 12 home runs, 37 walks and 49 RBI. Paul Goldschmidt is 20-for-41 with two home runs and nine RBI over the last 10 games.
