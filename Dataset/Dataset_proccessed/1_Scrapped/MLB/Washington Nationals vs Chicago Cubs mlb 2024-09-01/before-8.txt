BOTTOM LINE: The Baltimore Orioles lead 1-0 in a three-game series against the Colorado Rockies.

Colorado has a 50-86 record overall and a 31-36 record in home games. Rockies hitters are batting a collective .245, which ranks eighth in the NL.

Baltimore is 78-58 overall and 39-28 in road games. The Orioles have the top team slugging percentage in the majors at .442.

Saturday's game is the second time these teams square off this season.

TOP PERFORMERS: Brenton Doyle has 23 doubles, four triples, 22 home runs and 66 RBI for the Rockies. Brendan Rodgers is 11-for-41 with a double, three home runs and five RBI over the past 10 games.
