MLB Playoffs clinching scenarios: DBacks, Astros, Marlins, Rangers, Blue Jays get berths
Jeremy Cluff, Arizona Republic
Sat, Sep 30, 2023·2 min read
9
The Houston Astros and Arizona Diamondbacks are still looking to clinch berths in the 2023 MLB Playoffs.

Update: The final five postseason berths in the 2023 MLB Playoffs were secured Saturday as the Toronto Blue Jays, Miami Marlins, Texas Rangers, Houston Astros and Arizona Diamondbacks all earned their spots in the MLB postseason.

The Texas Rangers (90-71) and Houston Astros (89-72) are still battling to see which team will win the AL West and which team will be a wild card team.

Original story: The final five postseason berths in the 2023 MLB Playoffs could be secured on Saturday, if things fall into place for those five teams, with the Toronto Blue Jays, Miami Marlins, Texas Rangers, Houston Astros and Arizona Diamondbacks close to guaranteeing their spots in the MLB postseason.

What needs to happen for each team to clinch their postseason bid?

Take a look at the MLB clinching scenarios for Saturday, with seven postseason berths already locked up (Baltimore Orioles, Minnesota Twins, Tampa Bay Rays, Atlanta Braves, Los Angeles Dodgers, Milwaukee Brewers and Philadelphia Phillies).
