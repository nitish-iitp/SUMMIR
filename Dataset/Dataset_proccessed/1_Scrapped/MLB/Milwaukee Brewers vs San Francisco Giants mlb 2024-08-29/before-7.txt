San Francisco Giants vs Milwaukee Brewers Prediction, 8/29/2024 MLB Picks, Best Bets & Odds
by Guy Bruhn - 8/28/2024



Game: San Francisco Giants vs Milwaukee Brewers

Date: Thursday, August 29, 2024

Location: American Family Field in Milwaukee, WI

TV: Bally Sports Wisconsin

Odds/Point Spread: San Francisco (+140) Milwaukee (-168)

American Family Field is where the Milwaukee Brewers (75-56) will try to beat the San Francisco Giants (67-66) on Thursday. The moneyline on this matchup has the Giants at +140 while the Brewers are priced at -168. The total is 8. The pitchers expected to start will be Hayden Birdsong and Aaron Civale.

They have relinquished 163 homers and they yield 4.03 runs per 9 innings (4th in MLB). The Brewers have a team WHIP of 1.242 and have a FIP of 4.32 as a unit for the season. Milwaukee has had relief pitchers take the mound in 60 save opportunities and they have tallied 42 saves. Their relief pitchers have taken the field 158 times in high leverage situations and on 118 occasions with runners on. Milwaukee relief pitchers hold an inherited score percentage of 28.3% out of 173 inherited base runners. With 126 save situations, the Brewers have amassed 66 holds and also 18 blown saves.
