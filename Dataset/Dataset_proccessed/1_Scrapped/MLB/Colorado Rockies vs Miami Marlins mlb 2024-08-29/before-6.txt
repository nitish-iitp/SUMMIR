BOTTOM LINE: The Chicago White Sox take on the Texas Rangers as losers of four straight games.

Chicago is 31-101 overall and 18-48 at home. The White Sox have a 9-26 record in games decided by one run.

Texas has gone 26-40 on the road and 60-71 overall. The Rangers are 27-14 in games when they hit two or more home runs.

The teams match up Wednesday for the fifth time this season. The Rangers are ahead 4-0 in the season series.
