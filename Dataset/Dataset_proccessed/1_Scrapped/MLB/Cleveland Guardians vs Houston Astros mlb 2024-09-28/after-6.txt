Guardians can’t overcome Ben Lively’s early wildness in 4-3 loss to Astros

1/26
Cleveland Guardians vs. Houston Astros, September 28, 2024
By Paul Hoynes, cleveland.com

CLEVELAND, Ohio -- It was a pitching matchup of extremes.

Houston sent 41-year-old Justin Verlander to the mound in his 57th career start against Cleveland. The Guardians sent Ben Lively, 32, to the center of the diamond to make his first start against the Astros.
