BOTTOM LINE: Both the Texas Rangers and the New York Yankees are looking for a series win with a victory on Wednesday.

Texas has a 66-73 record overall and a 37-33 record at home. The Rangers have a 38-11 record in games when they scored at least five runs.

New York has a 43-28 record on the road and an 80-59 record overall. The Yankees have the fifth-ranked team ERA in the AL at 3.85.

The teams square off Wednesday for the sixth time this season. The Yankees lead the season series 3-2.

TOP PERFORMERS: Marcus Semien has 25 doubles, two triples, 18 home runs and 64 RBI while hitting .234 for the Rangers. Corey Seager is 15-for-43 with five doubles and two home runs over the past 10 games.
