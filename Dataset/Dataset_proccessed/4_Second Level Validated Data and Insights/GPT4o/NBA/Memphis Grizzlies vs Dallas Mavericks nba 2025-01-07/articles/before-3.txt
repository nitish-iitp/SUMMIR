Dallas Mavericks (20-15, sixth in the Western Conference) vs. Memphis Grizzlies (23-13, third in the Western Conference)

Memphis, Tennessee; Monday, 8 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Dallas comes into the matchup with Memphis after losing four games in a row.

The Grizzlies are 2-2 against Southwest Division teams. Memphis ranks second in the NBA with 30.5 assists per game led by Desmond Bane averaging 5.2.

The Mavericks are 4-2 against the rest of the division. Dallas ranks fourth in the Western Conference shooting 37.3% from 3-point range.

TOP PERFORMERS: Jaren Jackson Jr. is averaging 22.3 points, 6.2 rebounds and 1.6 blocks for the Grizzlies.

Kyrie Irving is averaging 24.3 points and 4.9 assists for the Mavericks.
