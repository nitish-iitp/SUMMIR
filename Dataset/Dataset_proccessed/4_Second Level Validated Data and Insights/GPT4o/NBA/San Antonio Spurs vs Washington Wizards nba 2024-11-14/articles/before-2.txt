Washington Wizards (2-7, 13th in the Eastern Conference) vs. San Antonio Spurs (5-6, 12th in the Western Conference)

San Antonio; Wednesday, 8 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Washington travels to San Antonio looking to stop its three-game road losing streak.

San Antonio went 22-60 overall with a 12-29 record at home during the 2023-24 season. The Spurs gave up 118.6 points per game while committing 17.2 fouls last season.
