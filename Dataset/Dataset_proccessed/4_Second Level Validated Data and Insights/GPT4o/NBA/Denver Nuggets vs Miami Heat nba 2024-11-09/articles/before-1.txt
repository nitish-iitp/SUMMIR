Miami Heat (3-4, seventh in the Eastern Conference) vs. Denver Nuggets (5-3, seventh in the Western Conference)

Denver; Friday, 9 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Denver heads into a matchup against Miami as winners of three straight games.

Denver finished 57-25 overall last season while going 33-8 at home. The Nuggets averaged 114.9 points per game last season, 15.1 on free throws and 35.1 from beyond the arc.
