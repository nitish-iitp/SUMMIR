Dallas Mavericks (5-5, 11th in the Western Conference) vs. Utah Jazz (2-8, 15th in the Western Conference)

Salt Lake City; Thursday, 9 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Dallas plays the Utah Jazz after Kyrie Irving scored 43 points in the Mavericks' 122-120 loss to the Denver Nuggets.
