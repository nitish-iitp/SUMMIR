Chicago Bulls (14-18, ninth in the Eastern Conference) vs. Charlotte Hornets (7-24, 13th in the Eastern Conference)

Charlotte, North Carolina; Monday, 7 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Charlotte enters the matchup against Chicago after losing seven straight games.

The Hornets have gone 6-21 against Eastern Conference teams. Charlotte is fifth in the league with 45.5 rebounds led by Moussa Diabate averaging 7.0.
