Brooklyn Nets (4-4, fourth in the Eastern Conference) vs. Boston Celtics (7-2, second in the Eastern Conference)

Boston; Friday, 7:30 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Brooklyn faces Boston in Eastern Conference action Friday.

Boston went 64-18 overall, 41-11 in Eastern Conference games and 37-4 at home during the 2023-24 season. The Celtics averaged 120.6 points per game last season, 16.3 from the free-throw line and 49.5 from deep.
