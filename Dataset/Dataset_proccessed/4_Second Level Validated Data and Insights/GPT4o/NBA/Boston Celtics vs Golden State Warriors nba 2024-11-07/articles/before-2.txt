Golden State Warriors (6-1, third in the Western Conference) vs. Boston Celtics (7-1, second in the Eastern Conference)

Boston; Wednesday, 7:30 p.m. EST

BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: Celtics -7.5; over/under is 229

BOTTOM LINE: The Boston Celtics host the Golden State Warriors in a non-conference matchup.

Boston finished 64-18 overall last season while going 37-4 at home. The Celtics averaged 120.6 points per game while shooting 48.7% from the field and 38.8% from deep last season.

Golden State finished 46-36 overall with a 25-16 record on the road last season. The Warriors gave up 115.2 points per game while committing 19.5 fouls last season.

INJURIES: Celtics: Kristaps Porzingis: out (foot), Jaylen Brown: day to day (hip).
