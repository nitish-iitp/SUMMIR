NBA: Nikola Jokic strikes again as Nuggets dispose of Hawks
Reuters / 03:23 PM January 02, 2025

Denver Nuggets center Nikola Jokic (15) and guard Jamal Murray (27) talk in the third quarter against the Atlanta Hawks of an NBA game at Ball Arena. Mandatory Credit: Ron Chenoy-Imagn Images

Nikola Jokic had 23 points, 17 rebounds and 15 assists for his 14th triple-double of the season and third in four games, and the host Denver Nuggets beat the Atlanta Hawks 139-120 in the NBA on Wednesday night.

The 15 assists were one shy of his season high of 16, which he has done twice.
