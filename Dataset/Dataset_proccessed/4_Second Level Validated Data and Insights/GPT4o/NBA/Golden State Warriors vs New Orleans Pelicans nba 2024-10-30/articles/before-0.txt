5 things to know about the Pelicans on Oct. 30, 2024
Jim Eichenhofer
October 30, 20249:31 AM CDT

New Orleans (2-2) dropped to .500 on Tuesday by virtue of a 124-106 loss at Golden State (3-1).

Watch Zion Williamson (31 points) and Brandon Ingram (30 points) highlights.

Watch Ingram’s postgame interview from the locker room, as well as that of Willie Green.

The Pelicans play Golden State again Wednesday at 9 p.m. TV coverage on GCSEN begins at 8:30, as is the case for pregame on the Pelicans radio network.

On Western Conference Wednesday, New Orleans is one of six West teams sporting a 2-2 record, essentially filling spots No. 7 through 12 in the standings. Last week’s team to watch was Phoenix, which started the season 3-1, splitting a pair of games vs. the Lakers, while beating the Clippers and Dallas. Listen to every Wednesday episode of the Pelicans Podcast for our mid-week look around the conference (today’s stock up: Clippers; stock down: Denver; team to watch over the next seven days: San Antonio).
