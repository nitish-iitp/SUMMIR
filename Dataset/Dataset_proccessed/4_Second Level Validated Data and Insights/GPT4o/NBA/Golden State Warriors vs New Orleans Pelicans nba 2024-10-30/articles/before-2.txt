New Orleans Pelicans (2-1, eighth in the Western Conference) vs. Golden State Warriors (2-1, sixth in the Western Conference)

San Francisco; Wednesday, 10 p.m. EDT

BOTTOM LINE: Golden State faces New Orleans in a matchup of Western Conference teams.

Golden State went 46-36 overall and 26-26 in Western Conference play last season. The Warriors averaged 117.8 points per game last season, 48.6 in the paint, 16.2 off of turnovers and 12.4 on fast breaks.
