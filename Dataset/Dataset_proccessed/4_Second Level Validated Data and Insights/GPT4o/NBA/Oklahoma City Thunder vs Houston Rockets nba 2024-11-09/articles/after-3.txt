Photos: Best images from Thunder's 126-107 win over Rockets
Clemente Almanza, Okc Thunder Wire
Sat, Nov 9, 2024·1 min read
Nov 8, 2024; Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, USA; Oklahoma City Thunder forward Chet Holmgren (7) moves the ball around Houston Rockets center Steven Adams (12) during the second half at Paycom Center. Mandatory Credit: Alonzo Adams-Imagn Images
More

The Oklahoma City Thunder cruised to a 126-107 win over the Houston Rockets on Friday. The lopsided win improved OKC's record to 8-1 on the season. All eight wins have been by double-digit points.

Shai Gilgeous-Alexander and Chet Holmgren scored 29 points apiece. Jalen Williams pitched in with 14 points. A 44-point second quarter blew this game open for the Thunder.

Meanwhile, the Rockets struggled. Jalen Green was limited to 14 points on 14 shot attempts. Alperen Sengun had 11 points, nine rebounds and six assists.
