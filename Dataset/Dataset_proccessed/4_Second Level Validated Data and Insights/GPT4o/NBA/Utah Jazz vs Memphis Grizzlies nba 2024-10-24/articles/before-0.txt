Memphis Grizzlies vs. Utah Jazz

Utah went 31-51 overall, 16-36 in Western Conference play and 21-20 at home during the 2023-24 season. The Jazz allowed opponents to score 120.5 points per game and shoot 48.7% from the field last season.
