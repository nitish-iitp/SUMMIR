NBA: Norman Powell Helps Los Angeles Clippers Thump Atlanta Hawks 131-105

Norman Powell scored 20 points, Kawhi Leonard added 12 in his first game of the season and the Los Angeles Clippers rolled to a 131-105 victory over the Atlanta Hawks in the NBA on Sunday (January 5, 2025). Leonard, who missed the Clippers’ first 34 games with what the team described as right knee injury recovery, played 19 minutes. He was 4 of 11 from the floor, including three 3-pointers, along with three rebounds and one assist. Ivica Zubac had 18 points and 18 rebounds as the Clippers snapped a two-game losing streak. Amir Coffey had 17 points. Trae Young led Atlanta with 20 points and 14 assists.
