Indiana Pacers (16-18, eighth in the Eastern Conference) vs. Miami Heat (17-14, sixth in the Eastern Conference)

Miami; Thursday, 7:30 p.m. EST

BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: Heat -2; over/under is 224.5

The Heat are 11-10 in Eastern Conference games. Miami ranks fifth in the Eastern Conference with 33.6 defensive rebounds per game led by Bam Adebayo averaging 7.5.

The Pacers are 9-14 in conference matchups. Indiana is fourth in the Eastern Conference scoring 50.6 points per game in the paint led by Pascal Siakam averaging 9.0.

The Heat score 111.3 points per game, 5.6 fewer points than the 116.9 the Pacers allow. The Pacers average 12.7 made 3-pointers per game this season, 0.6 fewer makes per game than the Heat allow.

TOP PERFORMERS: Herro is averaging 24.3 points, 5.6 rebounds and 5.2 assists for the Heat.

Myles Turner is scoring 14.9 points per game and averaging 7.2 rebounds for the Pacers.
