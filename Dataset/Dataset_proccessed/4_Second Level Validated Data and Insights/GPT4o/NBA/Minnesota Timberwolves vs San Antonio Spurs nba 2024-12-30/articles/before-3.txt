San Antonio Spurs (16-15, ninth in the Western Conference) vs. Minnesota Timberwolves (16-14, eighth in the Western Conference)

Minneapolis; Sunday, 8 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Anthony Edwards and the Minnesota Timberwolves host Victor Wembanyama and the San Antonio Spurs in Western Conference action.

The Timberwolves are 13-9 in Western Conference games. Minnesota scores 109.2 points and has outscored opponents by 2.1 points per game.

The Spurs are 13-12 in conference games. San Antonio is sixth in the Western Conference with 33.9 defensive rebounds per game led by Wembanyama averaging 8.3.

TOP PERFORMERS: Edwards is averaging 25.3 points, 5.5 rebounds and four assists for the Timberwolves.

Chris Paul is averaging 9.6 points and 8.2 assists for the Spurs.
