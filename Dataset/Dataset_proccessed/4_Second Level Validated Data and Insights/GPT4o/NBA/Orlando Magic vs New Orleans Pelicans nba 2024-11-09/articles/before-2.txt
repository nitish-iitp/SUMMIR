New Orleans Pelicans (3-6, 14th in the Western Conference) vs. Orlando Magic (3-6, 10th in the Eastern Conference)

Orlando, Florida; Friday, 7 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: New Orleans travels to Orlando looking to break its three-game road skid.

Orlando finished 47-35 overall a season ago while going 29-12 at home. The Magic averaged 110.5 points per game last season, 51.8 in the paint, 18.6 off of turnovers and 12.4 on fast breaks.
