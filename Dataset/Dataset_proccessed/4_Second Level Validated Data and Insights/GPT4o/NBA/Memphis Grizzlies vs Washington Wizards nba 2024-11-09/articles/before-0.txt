Washington Wizards (2-4, 11th in the Eastern Conference) vs. Memphis Grizzlies (5-4, ninth in the Western Conference)

Memphis, Tennessee; Friday, 8 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Memphis and Washington meet in non-conference action.

Memphis went 27-55 overall with a 9-32 record at home during the 2023-24 season. The Grizzlies averaged 17.4 points off of turnovers, 13.4 second-chance points and 33.0 bench points last season.
