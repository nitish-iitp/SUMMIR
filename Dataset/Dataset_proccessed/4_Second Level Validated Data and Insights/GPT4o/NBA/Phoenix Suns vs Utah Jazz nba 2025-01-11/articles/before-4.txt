Suns vs. Jazz Betting Trends
The Suns have covered the spread 13 times this season (13-23-0).
The Jazz are 18-17-1 against the spread this year.
Suns games have gone over the total 17 times this season.
The Jazz have gone over the point total 52.8% of the time this year (19 of 36 games with a set point total).
When playing at home, Phoenix owns a worse record against the spread (6-13-0) compared to its ATS record in away games (7-10-0).
The Suns have eclipsed the over/under in seven of 19 home games (36.8%). They've fared better in away games, going over the total in 10 of 17 matchups (58.8%).
Utah's winning percentage against the spread at home is .375 (6-9-1). Away, it is .600 (12-8-0).
In 2024-25 a lower percentage of the Jazz's games have finished above the over/under at home (37.5%, six of 16) than on the road (65%, 13 of 20).

Jazz Leaders

Per game, Walker Kessler gets the Jazz 10.7 points, 11.4 boards and 1.6 assists. He also averages 0.5 steals and 2.5 blocks (second in NBA).
The Jazz are getting 20 points, 6.1 rebounds and 1.9 assists per game from Lauri Markkanen.
Per game, John Collins gets the Jazz 17.9 points, 8.3 boards and 2.5 assists, plus 1.1 steals and 0.9 blocks.
