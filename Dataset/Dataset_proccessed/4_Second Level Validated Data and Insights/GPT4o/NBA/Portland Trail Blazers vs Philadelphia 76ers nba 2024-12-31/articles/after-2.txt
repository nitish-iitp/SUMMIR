Abysmal 3-point shooting sinks Blazers in 125-103 loss to Sixers
By AP staff (AP)
PORTLAND, Ore. Dec. 31, 2024 3:28 p.m.

Portland guard Anfernee Simons, left, drives on Philadelphia forward Caleb Martin on Monday, Dec. 30, 2024, in Portland, Ore. Simons led the Trail Blazers with 25 points.

Howard Lao / AP

Joel Embiid had 37 points and nine rebounds as the Philadelphia 76ers defeated the Portland Trail Blazers 125-103 on Monday night.
