IND vs SA 2022 T20I World Cup Highlights: Proteas defeat India by 5 wickets
7 min read . Updated: 30 Oct 2022, 09:58 PM IST
Livemint
2022 T20I World Cup Highlights: Following the initial fall of wickets, India manages to score 133 runs after by losing 9 wickets. In return, SA's Miller and Markram set the momentum and led the Proteas to a 5-wickets victory.
South Africa's David Miller, right, is congratulated by teammate Wayne Parnell after reaching 50 runs during the T20 World Cup cricket match between the India and South Africa in Perth, Australia, Sunday, Oct. 30, 2022. (AP Photo/Gary Day) (AP)

The Proteas snatched away victory from India's hand in the 2022 T20I World Cup match at Perth, as both Miller and Markram hit half centuries. India's Arshdeep took 2 wickets, but could not save the game. India had made 133 after losing 9 wickets as it chose to bat first. Apart from Suryakumar Yadav no batter could score more than 20 runs.

Proteas defeat India by 5 wickets

South African experienced player David Miller led his team to a 5 wicket victory against India in the 2022 T20I World Cup match at Perth.

After initial fall of wickets, Miller along with Aiden Markram (52), brought the Proteas into the limelight again.

Though Arshdeep Singh, Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Mohammed Shami bowled well and clicked few wickets, India could not managed to win the match.
