India vs Pakistan T20 World Cup 2024 Date, Venue, Time, Live Streaming & More

India vs Pakistan T20 World Cup 2024. The cricketing giants clash on June 9th in New York. Find out the match time, and venue, how to watch on TV, stream online, and score tickets before they sell out!

AMIT DIWAN
EDITORIAL
UPDATED - 2024-06-07, 15:42 IST

Brace yourselves, cricket fans, as the much-awaited clash between arch-rivals India and Pakistan is set to electrify the cricketing world during the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024. This high-octane encounter promises to be a spectacle like no other, with the two powerhouses battling it out for supremacy on the grandest stage.

India Vs Pakistan ICC T20 World Cup Match Date and Time

According to the ICC, the highly anticipated India vs Pakistan T20 World Cup 2024 match is scheduled to take place on June 9th, 2024. The excitement will commence at 8 PM IST, as the two teams step onto the field to showcase their cricketing shots.

India Vs Pakistan ICC T20 World Cup Match Venue

The iconic Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in New York will play host to this colossal clash.

How To Watch India Vs Pakistan ICC T20 World Cup On TV

Cricket enthusiasts can tune in to the Star Sports Network channels to witness every moment of the India vs Pakistan T20 World Cup 2024 match live on their television screens.

How To Watch India Vs Pakistan ICC T20 World Cup Online

For those preferring the convenience of online streaming, Disney+ Hotstar will provide a seamless viewing experience, allowing fans to catch all the action on their preferred digital devices.

India Vs Pakistan ICC T20 World Cup Ticket Price

To be part of this historic event, fans have a range of ticket options to choose from, catering to various budgets and preferences. According to the reports, ticket prices start from a budget-friendly $175, with standard and premium seats currently priced around $300 and $400, respectively.

How To Book India Vs Pakistan ICC T20 World Cup Ticket
Visit the official ICC ticketing website: icc-cricket.com
Navigate to the ticketing section and click on "buy tickets"
Complete the captcha to proceed to the events page
Select "Nassau County International Cricket Stadium" in New York from the dropdown box
Apply the filter for India vs Pakistan
Choose your desired seats and add them to your basket
Proceed to make the payment and receive confirmation
Download your ticket and get ready for the Ultimate clash!
