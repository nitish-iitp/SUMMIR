T20 World Cup 2024 Match Time In India, Date & Where To Watch Online

Gear up for the T20 World Cup 2024! Catch India's crucial warm-up match against Bangladesh on Star Sports. Live stream all practice games on Disney+Hotstar.

AMIT DIWAN
EDITORIAL
UPDATED - 2024-06-01, 17:57 IST

The cricketing world is abuzz with anticipation as the highly-awaited T20 World Cup 2024 commences on June 2nd in the United States and West Indies. Leading the charge for India is the indomitable Rohit Sharma, who has already arrived in New York with his formidable squad, nearly a week before their opening clash against Ireland.

India T20 World Cup Match List

Placed in the competitive Group A, alongside the USA, Canada, Pakistan, and Ireland, the Men in Blue will need to bring their A-game to secure a spot in the semi-finals, where only the top two teams from each group will advance. India's journey kicks off with a high-octane encounter against Ireland on June 5th at the iconic Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in New York.

June 19-24, 2024: Super Eight

The top two teams from each of the four groups will advance to the Super Eight stage. These eight teams will be divided into two groups of four teams each.

June 26 and 27, 2024: Semi-finals

The winner and runner-up from each Super Eight group will face each other in the semi-finals.

June 29, 2024: Final

The winners of the two semi-finals will compete in the final match to determine the T20 World Cup 2024 champion.

Where To Watch The T20 World Cup 2024 In India?

Cricket aficionados in India can catch every ball, every wicket, and every moment of the T20 World Cup extravaganza live on the Star Sports network. For those who prefer to stream the matches in real-time, the Disney+ Hotstar app and website will be your go-to destination.
