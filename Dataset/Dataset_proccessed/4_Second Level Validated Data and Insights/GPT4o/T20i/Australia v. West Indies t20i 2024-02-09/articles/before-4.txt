How to watch Australia vs. West indies T20 series: TV and live streaming details

KIERAN FRANCIS

08 February, 2024
•
10:27 pm UTC

To finish off their tour of Australia, the West Indies will take on the host nation in three T20 internationals in February.

The Windies journey to Australia will be forever remembered for their first Test win in the region for 27 years in Brisbane.

Can the tourists cause problems for the green and gold in the shortest form of the game?

MORE: Australia sinks to historic loss against West Indies but Steve Smith's return to form a welcome boost

The Sporting News has all the details on the Australia vs West Indies T20 series below.

When is the Australia vs West Indies T20 series?

After a two-match Test series and three-match ODI series, the West Indies will complete their tour of Australia with three T20 internationals.

These matches will occur at three Australian venues not used during the ODI series, with those matches in Melbourne, Sydney and Canberra.

What time do the Australia vs West Indies T20 matches start?

For Australians in the AEDT timezone, all three T20 matches will start at 7:00pm.

The first T20 in Hobart is in the AEDT timezone, but the second T20 is held in ACDT (6:30pm start) and the third in AWST (4pm start).

How to watch or live stream Australia vs West Indies T20 series

Every match of Australia's summer of cricket - including Tests, ODIs and T20Is - will be broadcast live on Fox Cricket and streamed on Kayo Sports.
