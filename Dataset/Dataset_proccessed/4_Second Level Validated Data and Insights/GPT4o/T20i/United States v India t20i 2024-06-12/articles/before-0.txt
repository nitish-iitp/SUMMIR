USA vs. India T20 Cricket World Cup match in NY: Cheapest prices on last-minute tickets
Updated: Jun. 11, 2024, 2:02 p.m.|Published: Jun. 11, 2024, 2:01 p.m.

United States' captain Monank Patel plays a shot during the ICC Men's T20 World Cup cricket match between United States and Pakistan at the Grand Prairie Stadium in Grand Prairie, Texas, Thursday, June 6, 2024.AP Photo/Tony Gutierrez

By Madison Kemeny | MKemeny@syracuse.com

The United States will face off against India in Match 25 of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup at the Nassau County International Cricket Stadium in East Meadow, NY, on Wednesday, June 12 (6/12/24) at 10:30 a.m. ET.

This will be the United States’ third group stage match after overcoming the odds in a historic upset against Pakistan on Thursday, June 6.

The match is completely sold out, but it’s still possible for fans to get tickets via the resale market.

You can view the complete ICC Men’s T20 World Cup schedule here.

How much are USA-India ICC T20 World Cup tickets?

Limited tickets are available through resale platforms like Vivid Seats, SeatGeek and StubHub.

Here’s a look at the best prices available:

Vivid Seats: Starting at $293
SeatGeek: Starting at $325
StubHub: Starting at $270

Prices and availability are noted at the time of publishing and are subject to change.

How can I stream the ICC T20 World Cup online?

Cricket fans will need a subscription to Willow TV, which includes every game in the ICC T20 Men’s World Cup and is available as an add-on via Sling (half off first month) and fuboTV (free trial).

Willow is the top broadcaster of cricket in the USA and Canada, airing numerous cricket Boards, including the ICC, Cricket Australia, England Cricket Board, Indian Premier League and others.

fuboTV subscribers can add Willow TV with the International Sports Plus Pack ($6.99 per month), which includes Willow TV, FOX Deportes, Golf TV and FOX Soccer Plus.

Sling subscribers can add Willow TV with the Dakshin Flex Hindi entertainment pack ($10 per month), which includes Asianet, Suvarana, Simply South and more networks.
