India vs England: What is India's record against England in T20Is ahead of T20 World Cup 2024 semifinal
Saino Zachariah
26-06-2024
•
3 min read
Getty Images

India are all set to take on one of their biggest rivals and defending champions, England in the second semi-final clash of the T20 World Cup 2024 at Guyana, on Thursday, June 27.

Rohit Sharma and Co. have remained unbeaten in the tournament so far, having defeated the likes of Australia and Pakistan en route. On the other hand, England have managed to secure qualification despite losing against teams like South Africa and Australia.

The two teams had met in the semifinals of the last edition of the T20 World Cup as well, where England thrashed India by 10 wickets. Ahead of their clash at Guyana, The Sporting News takes a look at the record between the two sides in T20 internationals.

What is Team India's record against England in T20Is?

India and England have faced each other 23 times in T20 internationals, in which, the Men in Blue have had a marginally better record, winning 12 matches compared to England's 11 wins.

How has India performed against England in Cricket World Cups?

Team India has faced off against England in the World Cup 13 times since 1975 and won on 6 occasions.

India vs England in ODI World Cups:

The two teams have faced each other nine times in ODI World Cups where both teams have won 4 matches each, with one match in 2011 ending in a tie.

The most recent match between the two teams came during the 2023 World Cup in Lucknow, where India beat England by 100 runs.

India vs England in T20 World Cups:

The two teams have only faced each other 4 times in T20 World Cups, with both teams winning 2 matches each.

The last T20 World Cup match between the two teams came during the 2022 World Cup in Australia, where England beat India by 10 wickets.

When will India play England at the T20 World Cup 2024 Semi-Finals?

India will play England in second semi-final of the T20 World Cup 2024 on June 27, 2024, which will be hosted at the Providence Stadium in Guyana. The match is scheduled to start at 8 PM IST.

Fans in the subcontinent can catch the action live on Star Sports Network and Disney+ Hotstar app. The toss will take place at 7:30 PM IST.
