Ind vs Aus T20 World Cup 2024 June 24 Match: Can Rohit Sharma & co overcome November 19 heartbreak? What history tells us
Historically, India has often been the favorite in many tournaments, but has struggled to overcome formidable teams like Australia.
Written by Shubham Chhabra
June 21, 2024 16:07 IST
Follow Us
India’s batting lineup has faced significant challenges. Both Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli have struggled to find form, (PTI)

The anticipation is palpable as India gears up to face Australia in a high-stakes clash on June 24. Historically, India has often been the favorite in many tournaments, but has struggled to overcome formidable teams like Australia. The 2003 World Cup final is a haunting reminder of this challenge, making every India vs Australia match a thrilling encounter. In their last 50-over World Cup clash, India fell short against Australia, adding an extra layer of tension to this upcoming match.

Australia, with power-packed batsmen like Travis Head and David Warner, has consistently put pressure on bowlers from the start. The question on everyone’s mind is whether India’s bowling attack can turn the tide or if Australia will once again dominate.

India’s poor opening performance

India’s batting lineup has faced significant challenges. Both Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli have struggled to find form, and Shivam Dube, despite his stellar IPL performance, has failed to impress on the international stage. Surprisingly, players who shone in the IPL have not been able to replicate their success.

On the other hand, Australia’s match against Bangladesh highlighted their formidable strength. Captain Pat Cummins secured the first hat trick of the T20 World Cup 2024, while Adam Zampa has been consistently breaking crucial partnerships. With all-rounders like Marcus Stoinis and Glenn Maxwell, Australia appears to have an edge. However, India is not far behind.

India’s key players

Rishabh Pant has been the shining star for India, scoring consistently in all matches and taking charge with innovative shots. Suryakumar Yadav delivered a standout performance against Afghanistan, and Hardik Pandya, despite a lackluster IPL season, has been hitting crucial runs.

Axar Patel’s inclusion, batting at number eight, has significantly bolstered India’s batting depth, potentially serving as their X-factor.

As both teams prepare for this epic showdown, the stakes couldn’t be higher. Will India’s bowlers rise to the occasion and turn the odds in their favor, or will Australia’s formidable lineup once again prove too strong to overcome? Only time will tell, but one thing is certain: cricket fans around the world are in for a thrilling match.
