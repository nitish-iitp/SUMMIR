T20 World Cup 2024 Group D: Bangladesh vs Netherlands - Who will win today’s match?
Bangladesh have a 3-1 record against the Netherlands in the T20I format.

Preview

Bangladesh will play against the Netherlands in the Group D match of the T20 World Cup 2024 at the Arnos Vale Ground in Kingstown, St Vincent. In a potential decider for the second Super 8 spot from the group stages, Bangladesh and the Netherlands will be putting it all on the line in the Caribbean. After winning their opening match in the T20 World Cup 2024 in Dallas, these two teams have lost to South Africa in New York recently.

Pitch Report

In the couple of T20I internationals at the Arno Vale Ground in Kingstown, the spin bowlers were the prime wicket-takers, while the batters and fast bowlers did have their say on the proceedings too. With that, the average first innings of 144 at this venue in its short T20I history, might not be a bad total to aim initially for either Bangladesh or the Netherlands in their upcoming T20 World Cup 2024 match.

Team Analysis

Bangladesh

The batting lineup of Bangladesh has found it tough in the spicy conditions in Dallas and New York, but still, the likes of Towhid Hridoy and Mahmudullah Riyad found a way to score valuable runs for the team against Sri Lanka and South Africa. In the bowling side of things, Bangladesh have been delivered the goods with players like Tanzim Hasan Sakib, Rishad Hossain, Taskin Ahmed, and Mustafizur Rahman finding themselves amongst the wickets.

Bangladesh squad for T20 World Cup 2024

Najmul Hossain Shanto (c), Taskin Ahmed, Litton Das, Soumya Sarkar, Tanzid Hasan Tamim, Shakib Al Hasan, Towhid Hridoy, Mahmudullah Riyad, Jaker Ali Anik, Tanvir Islam, Shak Mahedi Hasan, Rishad Hossain, Mustafizur Rahman, Shoriful Islam, Tanzim Hasan Sakib

Netherlands

Max O’Dowd and Sybrand Engelbrecht played two exceptional innings in the first two matches for the Netherlands in the T20 World Cup 2024, ensuring that they competed well against Nepal and South Africa respectively. The bowling attack has been the strong point for the Dutch in the tournament thus far with the likes of Logan van Beek, Vivian Kingma, Paul van Meekeren, Tim Pringle, and Bas de Leede picking up key wickets at important times.

Netherlands squad for T20 World Cup 2024

Scott Edwards (c), Aryan Dutt, Bas de Leede, Kyle Klein, Logan van Beek, Max O’Dowd, Michael Levitt, Paul van Meekeren, Ryan Klein, Saqib Zulfiqar, Sybrand Engelbrecht, Teja Nidamanuru, ⁠⁠Tim Pringle, Vikram Singh, Viv Kingma, Wesley Barresi

Match Prediction




Conclusion

Bangladesh and the Netherlands are expected to play a humdinger at the Arnos Vale Stadium, but still, the South Asian country has a slightly better chance of winning their T20 World Cup 2024 match, owing to the possibility of their spinners being the difference maker.
