South Africa vs Bangladesh Highlights, T20 World Cup 2022: Rossouw, Nortje star as SA defeat BAN by 104 runs in Sydney
By 
HT Sports Desk
Oct 27, 2022 12:20 PM IST
T20 World Cup 2022 Highlights, South Africa vs Bangladesh Today's Match: South Africa defeated Bangladesh in their Super 12 stage fixture at the SCG in Sydney, on Thursday. Follow here HIGHLIGHTS of SA vs BAN match, straight from Sydney here.
SA vs BAN Highlights: South Africa defeated Bangladesh in their T20 World Cup 2022 fixture, in Sydney.

South Africa vs Bangladesh, T20 World Cup 2022, Highlights: South Africa defeated Bangladesh by 104 runs in Match 22 of the T20 World Cup 2022 Super 12 Stage, at the SCG in Sydney, on Thursday. Defending a target of 206, South Africa bowled out Bangladesh for 101 in 16.3 overs with Anrich Nortje taking a four-wicket haul. Initially, South Africa raced to 205 for five in 20 overs, courtesy of a century by Rilee Rossouw. Rossouw smacked 109 runs off 56 balls, packed with seven fours and eight sixes. Meanwhile, Quinton de Kock hammered 63 runs off 38 balls. On the other hand, Shakib Al Hasan took two wickets for Bangladesh.
