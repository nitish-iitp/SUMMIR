The pitch at this Stadium is expected to be similar to traditional Gwalior pitches, which are typically batting-friendly, offering true bounce and consistent pace thanks to the red soil in the region. This makes the surface ideal for high-scoring matches.

The weather for Sunday, October 6, will be sunny with a high of 33°C and a low of 25°C. Wind speeds will be light at 15 km/h, and there’s no expected precipitation, keeping the day dry. Humidity will be around 62%, with atmospheric pressure at 1010 mb.
