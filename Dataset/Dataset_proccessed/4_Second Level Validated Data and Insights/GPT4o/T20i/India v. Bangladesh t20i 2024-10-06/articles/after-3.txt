India vs Bangladesh Live Score, 1st T20I: Gwalior fun facts

Gwalior holds a unique distinction in Indian cricket history. It is the only venue in the country where the host team has played matches on consecutive days. In 1993, India hosted England in two One-Day Internationals on March 4th and 5th. Additionally, Captain Roop Singh Stadium in Gwalior witnessed a historic moment in 2010 when Sachin Tendulkar became the first batsman to score a double century in an ODI match. This achievement solidified Gwalior's place in cricket annals.

Captain Roop Singh Stadium, named after the legendary hockey player and brother of Dhyan Chand, holds a special place in cricket history. It was here that the legendary Sachin Tendulkar achieved a historic milestone, scoring the first-ever ODI double century against South Africa in 2010.
