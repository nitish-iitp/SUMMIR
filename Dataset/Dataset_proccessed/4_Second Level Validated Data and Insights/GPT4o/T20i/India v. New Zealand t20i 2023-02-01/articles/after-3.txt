Ind vs NZ, 3rd T20 | Gill hits maiden T20I ton as India beat NZ with biggest run margin
Shubman Gill continued his fine form and showcased his array of shots, hitting 126 not out off just 63 balls balls with the help of 12 fours and seven sixes.

Updated - February 02, 2023 06:54 pm IST - Ahmedabad

PTI
READ LATER
PRINT

Shubhman Gill raises his bat and helmet to celebrate scoring a century during the third T20 international cricket match between India and New Zealand in Ahmedabad on February 1, 2023. | Photo Credit: AP

Shubman Gill smashed a whirlwind unbeaten maiden century in the shortest format as India thrashed New Zealand by 168 runs, their biggest-ever win in terms of runs, in the third and final T20 International to pocket the hard-fought series 2-1 here on Wednesday.

India's second highest margin of victory was 143-run win over Ireland in Dublin in 2018.
