PAK vs IRE T20: Schedule, Squad, Live streaming details of Pakistan vs Ireland series
Posted by
Payal Tomar
Thursday, May 9, 2024 6:49 pm
PAK vs IRE T20: Pakistan cricket team and Ireland cricket team will play 3-match T20I series ahead of the T20 World Cup 2024.

Pak vs Ire T20 match: Teams are gearing up for the prestigious T20 World Cup 2024, with cricketers honing their skills in the Indian Premier League (IPL). However, Pakistan, lacking access to the IPL platform, is engaged in T20 series to prepare for the upcoming tournament. Following a drawn series with New Zealand, the Pakistan Cricket Team is set to face Ireland at home in a three-match T20I series.

The first match of the series will begin on May 10 at the Clontarf Cricket Club in Dublin.

Where can you watch PAK vs IRE T20 live streaming in India?

Indian fans can watch Pakistan vs Ireland T20 series live streaming on Fancode app. However, it is not free as the user have to give Rs 69 to purchase their tour pass subscription.

Where to watch IRE vs PAK T20 Series 2024 live telecast in India?

Unfortunately, the Pak vs Ire T20 match will not be available on television in India.

When will PAK vs IRE T20 series start?

Pakistan will lock horns with Ireland in the opening match which will begin on May10.

What is the venue of PAK vs IRE T20 series?

All the three matches of the Pakistan vs Ireland T20 series will be played in Dublin at the Clontarf Cricket Club.

Pakistan vs Ireland T20I series schedule

1st T20I: Friday, 10 May 2024 – Dublin – 7 PM Local Time (PKT) / 7:30 PM IST

2nd T20I: Sunday, 12 May 2024 – Dublin – 7 PM Local Time (PKT) / 7:30 PM IST

3rd T20I: Tuesday, 14 May 2024 – Dublin – 7 PM Local Time (PKT) / 7:30 PM IST

Pakistan T20I squad

Babar Azam (captain), Abrar Ahmed, Azam Khan, Fakhar Zaman, Haris Rauf, Hasan Ali, Iftikhar Ahmed, Imad Wasim, Abbas Afridi, Mohammad Amir, Mohammad Rizwan, Irfan Khan, Naseem Shah, Saim Ayub, Salman Agha, Shadab Khan, Shaheen Afridi, Usman Khan.

Ireland T20I squad

Paul Stirling (captain), Mark Adair, Ross Adair, Andrew Balbirnie, Curtis Campher, Gareth Delany, George Dockrell, Graham Hume, Barry McCarthy, Neil Rock, Harry Tector, Lorcan Tucker, Ben White, Craig Young.
