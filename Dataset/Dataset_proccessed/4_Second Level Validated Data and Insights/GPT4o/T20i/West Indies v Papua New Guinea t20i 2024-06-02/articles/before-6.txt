West Indies vs Papua New Guinea T20 World Cup 2024: Expected lineups, head-to-head, toss, predictions and betting odds

DIVY DUBEY

02 June, 2024
•
2:30 am UTC

The T20 World Cup 2024 will see West Indies face off against Papua New Guinea on Sunday, 2nd June 2024, at the Providence Stadium in Guyana. The West Indies team, with a rich history in T20 cricket, is set to face Papua New Guinea, a team eager to prove itself on the big stage. Both teams are geared up for a competitive encounter under the lights.

As we build up the excitement for the tournament, here are the predicted line-ups, live toss, match prediction, and betting odds for the Twenty20 World Cup match between West Indies and Papua New Guinea.
