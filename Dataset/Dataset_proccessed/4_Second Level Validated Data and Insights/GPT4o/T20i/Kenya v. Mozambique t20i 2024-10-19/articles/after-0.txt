The ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Africa Sub-regional Qualifier B 2024 will start on October 19 – here’s everything you need to know about the schedule, squads and where to watch competition details.

Hosts Kenya, Rwanda, Seychelles, Gambia, Zimbabwe and Mozambique will participate in the tournament, set to be held between October 19 and 24. Two grounds – Gymkhana Club Ground and Ruaraka Sports Club Ground – in Kenya’s capital Nairobi will host all 15 matches.

Each side will play the other five sides in a round-robin format. The top two sides will join the other four sides from sub-regional qualifiers A and C for the final. The winners and the runners-up will then seal a spot for the 2026 T20 World Cup.

Namibia and Uganda have already qualified for the regional final qualifier after they participated in the 2024 T20 World Cup.

Zimbabwe, a Full Member of ICC, are participating in the tournament after missing out on direct qualification for the 2026 T20 World Cup. To find the reason, click here.
