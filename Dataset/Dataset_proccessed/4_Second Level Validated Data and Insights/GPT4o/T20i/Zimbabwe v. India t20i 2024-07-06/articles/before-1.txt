Zimbabwe vs India, 1st T20I: Probable XI, Match Prediction, Pitch Report, Weather Forecast, and Live Streaming Details

India are touring Zimbabwe for a five-game T20I series, starting from Saturday, July 6. The Harare Sports Club will be hosting all games of this series. The tour comes to an end on Sunday, July 14.

India recently won the ICC Menâ€™s T20 World Cup 2024. They lifted their second title after winning the inaugural edition way back in 2007. It is to be noted that India have sent a second-string squad to Zimbabwe for the T20I series and it looks to be a solid one.

Shubman Gill has been handed the responsibility of leading the Indian side. The likes of Gill, Ruturaj Gaikwad, Abhishek Sharma, Rinku Singh, and Riyan Parag form a solid batting line-up. The bowling attack comprises Avesh Khan, Khaleel Ahmed, Mukesh Kumar, and Ravi Bishnoi.

Trending

The Indian players who had done well in the Indian Premier League 2024 have been given an opportunity to represent their nation. It remains to be seen how this young Indian side goes about against Zimbabwe in the five-match T20I series.

Zimbabwe, on the other hand, will be led by Sikandar Raza. Their last international assignment came back in May when they faced Bangladesh in a five-match T20I series. It was a closely fought series which they lost by a 4-1 margin.

Zimbabwe have announced a well-balanced squad to face India. The likes of Raza, Milton Shumba, Wessley Madhevere, Luke Jongwe, and Blessing Muzarabani have a good amount of experience and will play a crucial role for the hosts in the five-match T20I series.

Zimbabwe vs India Match Details:

Match: Zimbabwe vs India, 1st T20I, India tour of Zimbabwe, 2024

Date and Time: July 6, 2024, 4:30 PM IST

Venue: Harare Sports Club, Harare

Zimbabwe vs India Head-to-Head

Zimbabwe and India have faced each other eight times in the shortest format. India have won six of those meetings whereas Zimbabwe have emerged victorious on two occasions.

Matches played: 8

Zimbabwe vs India Pitch Report

The pitch at the Harare Sports Club is generally a good one to bat on. The new ball comes nicely onto the bat and the batters can hit through the line right from the start of their innings. It will be hard work for the bowlers. The spinners may find some turn with the old ball.

Zimbabwe vs India Weather Forecast

Clear skies will greet the two sides on Saturday. The temperature is expected to range between 12 and 26 degrees Celsius.
