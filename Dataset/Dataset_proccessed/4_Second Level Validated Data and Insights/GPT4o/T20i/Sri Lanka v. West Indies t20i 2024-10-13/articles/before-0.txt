Sri Lanka vs West Indies(SL vs WI) 1st T20I Match Preview

The excitement of white-ball cricket is back. Sri Lanka and West Indies are preparing to clash in a three-match T20I series. After recently hosting India for a T20I series, Sri Lanka is ready to welcome another challenge on home soil. Meanwhile, the West Indies will travel to the island for this series. Fans are eagerly looking forward to an exhilarating contest filled with thrilling moments and fierce competition.

Before the upcoming T20I series, Sri Lanka hosted India in a three-match T20I series. That T20I series was a heartbreaker for the home side as they faced a whitewash on their home condition. In front of the Indian side, they struggled in both, batting and bowling. After facing two consecutive defeats in the first two matches, Sri Lanka was looking forward to securing a victory in the last match. But the 3rd T20I match witnessed a thrilling super over and India secured a victory. As the new series is approaching, Sri Lanka is hoping for a positive start. 

On the flip side, West Indies faced South Africa in their previous T20IO series. As the West Indies side hosted the series, they thrived in the home condition. After giving a dominating start to that series, West Indies carried their exceptional form in the entire series. With batting and bowling, they executed their plans and dominated the opponent. As a result of this, they whitewashed the South African side. As the new series is commencing, they are aiming to have another successful outing.
