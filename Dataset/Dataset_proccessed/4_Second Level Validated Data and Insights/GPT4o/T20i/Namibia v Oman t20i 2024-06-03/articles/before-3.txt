Namibia and Oman face off in the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 at Kensington Oval, Barbados. Key players and fantasy team selections are crucial for this exciting match.
Namibia team (ANI Photo)
NEW DELHI: Namibia (NAM) and Oman (OMN) are gearing up to face off in the third T20 International of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024. Scheduled for Monday, June 3rd, 2024, at 6:00 AM IST, the match will be held at the historic Kensington Oval in Barbados, West Indies.
Head to head stats between Namibia and Oman:
In their previous encounters, Namibia has dominated with four wins out of six matches against Oman, who have secured victories in two games.




Pitch Report:
Kensington Oval is renowned for its pitch, which offers an ideal balance for both batsmen and bowlers. It particularly favours fast bowlers, allowing them to achieve high scores. The outfield is lush and meticulously maintained, further enhancing the stadium's reputation as a premier venue for national and international cricket matches.
Ground history:
Established in 1871 and open to the public in 1872, Kensington Oval is located in Bridgetown, the capital city of Barbados. Known for its lush and well-maintained outfield, the stadium has a seating capacity of 28,000.

The T20I record at Kensington Oval reflects the pitch's favourability for the team batting first, with 16 wins compared to 7 for the team bowling first. The average first innings score is 156, while the average second innings score is 145. The highest total recorded at the ground is 224/5 by West Indies against England, while the lowest is 80/10 by Afghanistan against South Africa.
Fantasy value from pitch report:
Considering the pitch conditions, selecting top-order batsmen and quality all-rounders could prove to be beneficial for fantasy cricket enthusiasts for this match.


Key Players:
1. Gerhard Erasmus: The captain of Namibia, provides crucial batting stability essential for the team's success in challenging matches. His leadership and experience are invaluable assets.
2. David Wiese: He is a game-changer for Namibia, possessing the ability to influence matches with both bat and ball. His capacity to bowl crucial overs and deliver powerful performances strengthens Namibia's lineup.
3. Aqib Ilyas Sulheri: He is a dependable all-rounder for Oman, known for his impactful contributions with both bat and ball. His versatility makes him a key player for his team, capable of turning the tide of a game.
