Namibia comes into the match with strong recent form, while Oman will be eager to prove their capabilities on the big stage.

Let's take a look at the predicted line-ups, live toss, match prediction, and betting odds for the Twenty20 World Cup match between Namibia and Oman.

Namibia vs Oman, T20 World Cup 2024: Prediction

Namibia seems poised for victory against Oman in this match. They earned their place in the 2024 T20 World Cup with a flawless performance in the Africa Regional Qualifiers, winning all six matches. Key players like J.P. Kotze and Gerhard Erasmus have shown excellent form with the bat, while Erasmus and Bernard Scholtz have been impressive with the ball. This combination of strong batting and effective bowling depth gives Namibia an edge over Oman.
