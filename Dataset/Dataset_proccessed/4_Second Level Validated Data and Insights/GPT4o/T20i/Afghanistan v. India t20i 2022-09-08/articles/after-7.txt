Asia Cup 2022 Super 4: Ind vs Afg | Virat Kohli ends long wait for a hundred as India signs off campaign with big win
Virat Kohli reached the milestone after nearly three years, his 71st international century.

Updated - September 08, 2022 11:02 pm IST - Dubai:

PTI
READ LATER
PRINT

Virat Kohli celebrates his century during the Asia Cup 2022 super 4 match against Afghanistan in Dubai on September 8, 2022. | Photo Credit: Getty Images

Virat Kohli got the most-awaited hundred in international cricket as he reached the elusive three-figure mark after 1020 days to set up India’s massive 101-run win over Afghanistan and lighten up the dead rubber in the Asia Cup in Dubai on Thursday.

Kohli struck a high-quality 122 not out off 61 balls for his 71st international hundred to take India to 212 for two after Afghanistan put India in to bat.
