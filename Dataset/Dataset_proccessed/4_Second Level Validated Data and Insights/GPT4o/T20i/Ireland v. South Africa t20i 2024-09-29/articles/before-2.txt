South Korea’s innings featured significant contributions from players like Balage Ishan Dilruksha and Raja Shoaib, who both scored 23 runs, with Dilruksha facing 29 balls and Shoaib facing 30. Alam Nakash added 14 runs off 18 deliveries, but the team struggled to build a larger total. On the bowling side, An Hyobeom was particularly impressive, finishing with remarkable figures of 3 wickets for just 8 runs in 3 overs.
