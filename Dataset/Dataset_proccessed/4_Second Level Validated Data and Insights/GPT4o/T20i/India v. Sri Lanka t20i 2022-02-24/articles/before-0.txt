Table of Contents

Rishabh Pant Wishes Isha Negi on Her Birthday: Indian wicketkeeper-batsman Rishabh Pant in his official Instagram account posted a birthday wishes message to his rumoured girlfriend Isha Negi.

Rishabh Pant Wishes Isha Negi on Her Birthday

Pant made a heart emoji and two Queen emojis to show her love for Isha. Here’s how he wished her:

Rishabh Pant wishes Isha Negi(Image Source – Rishabh Pant Instagram Account)

The first time the world got to know that these two were dating each other was when Pant shared a photo from a vacation at the start of 2020.

Though Pant and Isha have not made it official yet but these stories do tell a story.

Who is Isha Negi?

Isha belongs to Uttarakhand and is an Interior Designer by profession. She has many followers on Instagram and needless to say, she is a big hit on the internet.

Rishabh Pant Misses T20I vs Sri Lanka 

Rishabh has been given a rest from the third and last T20 against West Indies along with Virat Kohli. In the recently concluded India vs West Indies, 3 matches T20I series Pant scored 8 and 52* in the first 2 T20I matches.

Earlier to the T20I triumph, India whitewashed the visitors by 3-0 in the 3 match ODI series where Pant scored 11, 18, and 56 runs respectively.

He will also miss the T20s against Sri Lanka and BCCI selectors want to give him an extended break. Pant will return to play 2 Tests at home against Lankans.
