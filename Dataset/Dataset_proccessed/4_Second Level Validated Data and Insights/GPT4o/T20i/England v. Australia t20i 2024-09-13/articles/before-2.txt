ENG vs AUS Head-to-Head Records: Key Statistics

If we take a detailed glance at ENG vs AUS head-to-head records, both sides have played 25 matches. England have won 11 while Australia have won 12. Two games ended with no result.

ENG vs AUS Head-to-Head Records: Recent 5 Encounters

In the recent 5 clashes between both sides, the hosts have won 2 games while the visitors Aussies have won 2 matches. 1 encounter witnessed no result. Out of these 5 games, one took place in the ongoing series.

ENG vs AUS Head-to-Head Records: Key moments and stand-out performances

If we analyze England vs Australia head-to-head records in detail, we get to know about some players with standout performances. For instance, Aaron Finch has skammed most 619 runs for the Aussies vs England.

He has also shattered the top knock of 156. Cameron White has the best average of 50.83 from 8 games. Shane Watson has the best bowling show; with his 4/15 vs ENG in 2011.

Talking about England, Jos Buttler is the leading run-scorer vs Australia with 584 runs. Alex Hales holds the record for the top knock (94). Craig Kieswetter played just 1 game and scored 63 runs. He has the best batting average.

In bowling, Chris Jordan and Adil Rashid have the most wickets to their names (13). Adil Rashid has the best economy rate at 8.02 in 12 games.

ENG vs AUS Head-to-Head Records: Trends and Patterns

The Englishmen have been quite powerful at home in England vs Australia encounters. Out of 10 T20I games, England has won 6 and lost only 3.

One match ended with no result. England also won the only game played at Cardiff against Australia in the past in 2015.

Conclusion

Australia and England have had a tough battle with each other in the shortest format and have almost won an equal number of games. Aussies do have a challenge in the ongoing series due to the lack of some key members and England's home advantage.

However, Australia have stunned many foreign teams on numerous occasions and it will be interesting to see if the new contingent led by March can shock the hosts. Both teams will wrestle at Cardiff for the second time in England vs Australia 2nd T20I 2024.
