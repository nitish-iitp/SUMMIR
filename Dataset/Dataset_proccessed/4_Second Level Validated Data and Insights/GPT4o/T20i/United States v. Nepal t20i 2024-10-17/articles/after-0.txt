The bi-lateral T20I Series between Nepal and the United States of America(USA) is set to begin on 17th October 2024 in Grand Prairie Stadium, Texas. The two teams will be participating in this exciting series: Nepal and USA. The opening match will feature Nepal facing the home team, USA in the Grand Prairie Stadium, Texas, the same venue which hosted the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2024. The game will start at 5.45 AM Nepali time. While the time difference of Nepal and America makes the game start in Nepal on 18th October 2024. Fans from the both countries are eagerly waiting for this thrilling series to kick off in USA.

Match Information:

Match: Nepal vs USA

Time: 5.45 AM Nepali time

Venue: Grand Prairie Stadium, Texas

Head to Head

Nepal and USA have not faced each other in a T20I match. This will be the first meeting between them. The first match is expected to be exciting for fans of the both nations.
