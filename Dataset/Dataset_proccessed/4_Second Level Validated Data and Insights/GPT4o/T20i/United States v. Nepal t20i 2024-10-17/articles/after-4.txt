USA vs Nepal T20I Series 2024: Full schedule, squads, match timings, and live-streaming details

Nepal are set to tour the United States of America (USA) for a limited overs series, comprising three T20Is. The series will begin on October 18, Friday, and will end on Monday, October 21.

Nepal last participated in the T20I tri-series in Canada where they won two games and lost as many from four outings. They beat Oman twice but lost to Canada on two occasions.

On the other hand, hosts USA also recently participated in a T20I tri-series in Namibia. They had a successful outing, winning three out of their four games, beating Namibia twice and UAE once.

All three T20Is between Nepal and the USA will be played at the Grand Prairie Cricket Stadium in Grand Prairie, Texas, Dallas. The first T20I begins on Friday, October 18, at 5:30 AM IST. The second T20I will be played on Sunday, October 20, starting at 5:30 AM IST while the final T20I will take place on Monday, October 21, also starting at 5:30 AM IST.

USA vs Nepal T20I Series 2024: Telecast & Live-Streaming Details

There will be no television telecast of the series. However, fans can catch all the action as there will be live-streaming on the Fancode app and website.
