Ugandan Match officials appointed for Djibouti Vs Ethiopia World Cup qualifier
Last updated: June 4, 2024 1:10 pm
By
admin
Share
1 Min Read
SHARE

David GOMBE

The Confederation of African Football (CAF) has appointed four Ugandan matches to handle the FIFA World Cup qualifier preliminary round group A game between Djibouti and Ethiopia.

Ali Sabilla Chelengat will be the center referee for the contest slated for 9th June, 2024 at El Jadida Stadium in Morocco.

He will be assisted by Robald Katenya and Hakim Mulindwa as the first and second assistants while William Oloya will be the fourth official.

Nigeria’s Shantal Shuaibu Amiru is the referee assessor while Mohamed Khalid Abdallah from Tanzania is the match commissioner.

- Advertisement -

Match Officials for Djibouti Vs Ethiopia

Referee: Ali Sabilla Chelengat (Uganda)
Assistant Referee 1: Ronald Katenya (Uganda)
Assistant Referee 2: Hakim Mulindwa (Uganda)
Fourth official: William Oloya (Uganda)
Referee Assessor: Shantal Shuaibu Amiru (Nigeria)
Match commissioner: Mohamed Khalid Abdallah (Tanzania)
