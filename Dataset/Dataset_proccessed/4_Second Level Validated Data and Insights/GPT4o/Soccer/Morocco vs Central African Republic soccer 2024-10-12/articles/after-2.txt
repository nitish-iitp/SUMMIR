Morocco beats Central African Republic 5-0 in AFCON qualifier
Hespress EN
Saturday 12 October 2024 - 21:51

The Moroccan national football team beat the Central African Republic team 5-0 in an AFCON qualifier game tonight in Oujda.

The national team dominated first half was with four goals.

Abdessamad Ezzalzouli opened the scoring for the Lions in the 18th minute, followed by Azzedine Ounahi’s second goal in the 38th minute.

Achraf Hakimi scored a third goal in the 45th minute from a well-coordinated attack.

Azzedine Onahi ended the first half with a fourth goal in the 47th minute.

In the second half, the national team continued to dominate the Central African field, in order to add more goals.

In the 69th minute, Abdelzouli was awarded a penalty kick, which Sofiane Rahimi translated into a fifth goal.

It is worth noting that the Moroccan national team leads Group B of the African qualifiers with 9 points, followed by Gabon with 4 points, Central Africa (3 points), and Lesotho (1n).
