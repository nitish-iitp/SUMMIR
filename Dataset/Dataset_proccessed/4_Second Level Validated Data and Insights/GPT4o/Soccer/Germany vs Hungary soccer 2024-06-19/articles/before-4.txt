In the second round of Group A games at the 2024 UEFA European Championship, Germany (coming off a 5-1 win over Scotland) meets Hungary (3-1 defeat by Switzerland) at 12:00 PM ET on Wednesday, June 19.

We dig deep into all of the info you need before this contest starts in this article, including how to watch on Fox Sports 1.

How to Watch Germany vs. Hungary
Match Day: Wednesday, June 19, 2024
Match Time: 12:00 PM ET
Venue: Mercedes-Benz Arena
Location: Stuttgart, Germany
TV Channel: Fox Sports 1 – Watch FREE on Fubo
