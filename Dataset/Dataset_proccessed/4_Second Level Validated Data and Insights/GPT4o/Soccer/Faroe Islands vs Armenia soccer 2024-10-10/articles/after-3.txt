Nations League: Eduard Spertsyan to miss Armenia’s match against Faroe Islands
 Siranush Ghazanchyan 
Send an email
October 10, 2024, 15:09
 Less than a minute

Eduard Spertsyan, midfielder of the Armenian national team, will miss today’s Faroe Islands-Armenia match due to a minor injury, the Armenian Football Federation informs.

Armenia will take on Faroe Islands in an away Nations League match tonight.
