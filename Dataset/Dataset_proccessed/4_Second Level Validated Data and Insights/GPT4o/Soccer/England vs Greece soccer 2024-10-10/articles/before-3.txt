Where to watch England men's teams in October

Written by:

England Communications

Share

 Copy page link
 Share on Facebook
 Share on Twitter
 Share on Whatsapp
Find out where you can watch of stream live coverage of England's games in the October international window

England fans will be able to watch live coverage of the men's senior and men's under-21s during the October international window.

The Three Lions take on Greece at Wembley before travelling to Finland for their second game of the month in the UEFA Nations League and both games will be shown live by ITV for viewers in the UK.

And supporters will also be able to watch our MU21s live in both of their games this month on the official England YouTube channel, as they take on Ukraine in a crucial UEFA U21 EURO qualifier at AFC Bournemouth before facing Azerbaijan at Bristol City's Ashton Gate.

There may be live streams available for our other men's development teams in action in October, which are still to be confirmed but will be listed here if they are to be made available for viewers in the UK.

Where to watch England men's Senior

England v Greece
UEFA Nations League
7.45pm BST, Thursday 10 October 2024
Wembley Stadium
Live on ITV1 and ITVX - coverage to start at 7pm BST

Finland v England
UEFA Nations League
5pm BST, Sunday 13 October 2024
Helsingin Olympiastadion, Helsinki
Live on ITV1 and ITVX - coverage to start at 4.15pm BST

Where to watch England men's under-21s

England v Ukraine
UEFA U21 EURO qualifier
7.45pm BST, Friday 11 October
Vitality Stadium, AFC Bournemouth
Live on England YouTube

England v Azerbaijan
UEFA U21 EURO qualifier
7.30pm BST, Tuesday 15 October
Live on England YouTube

ENGLAND OFFICIAL YOUTUBE
