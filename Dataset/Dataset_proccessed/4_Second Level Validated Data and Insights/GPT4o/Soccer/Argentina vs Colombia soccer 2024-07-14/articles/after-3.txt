Copa América final as it happened: Argentina vs. Colombia
ESPN
Jul 15, 2024, 01:00 AM ET

We've reached the 2024 Copa América final! The tournament gave us jam-packed action, thrilling storylines and plenty to talk about and now Argentina and Colombia face off in Sunday's final.

Will Lionel Messi & Co. win back-to-back titles at the tournament, or will a Colombia side supercharged by James Rodríguez give the country a second Copa América? We bring you all the highlights, updates and analysis in real time as the game unfolds.
