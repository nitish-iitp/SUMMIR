We said: Madagascar 1-1 Gambia
Madagascar and Gambia have stumbled into the new qualifying campaign and will head into the first of their two consecutive October clashes seeking their first win in Group A. However, both sides are evenly matched on paper and we predict that they will cancel out each other's efforts at the Stade Larbi Zaouli. Read more.
