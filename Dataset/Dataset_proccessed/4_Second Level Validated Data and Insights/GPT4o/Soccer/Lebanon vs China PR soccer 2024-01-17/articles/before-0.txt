Both teams will be hoping to record their first win of the AFC Asian Cup 2023.

Lebanon and China will face each other in their next Group A fixture of the 2023 AFC Asian Cup on 17 January 2024. The sides need a win and must secure all three points to create a chance of making it into the knockout stages.

Lebanon fought bravely but eventually came up short against a strong Qatar side in a 3-0 defeat. On the other hand, China played a gritty goalless draw with Tajikistan and sit with just a point. As both teams are direct competitors for the second qualifying spot that offers direct qualification into the Round of 16, winning the upcoming game becomes essential for them.

Here is a look at what to expect from the AFC Asian Cup fixture between Lebanon and China from Group A in a high-stakes contest:
