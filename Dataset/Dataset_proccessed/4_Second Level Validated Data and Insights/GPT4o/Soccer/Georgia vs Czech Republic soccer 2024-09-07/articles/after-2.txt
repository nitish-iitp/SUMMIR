The Georgian national team defeated the Czech Republic with a score of 4:1

The match between Georgia and the Czech Republic ended with the victory of the Georgian national team. In the meeting, the Georgian national team defeated the Czech Republic with a score of 4:1. In the first half of the match, Khvicha Kvaratskhelia opened the scoring with a penalty. And, in the second half of the match, the players of the Georgian national team scored three goals. Giorgi Chakvetadze scored the second goal of the match, Giorgi Mikautadze gave the lead to the Georgian national team with the third goal, and Giorgi Kochorashvili scored the fourth goal.

For information, the match was held at the stadium named after Mikheil Meskhi in Tbilisi. In addition, a suitable space was arranged on the Republic square, where the match could be watched on a big screen.
