Morocco beat Congo Brazzaville 6-0 in WC African Qualifiers
Hespress EN
Tuesday 11 June 2024 - 21:55

Morocco has beat Congo-Brazzaville 6-0 today in Agadir, gaining 3 points during the ongoing World Cup African Qualifiers.

The goals of the match were scored by Azeddine Ounahi (8′), Chadi Ryad (16′), Ayoub El Kaabi (20′, 39′, 53′), Soufiane Rahimi (62′)

Victory takes Morocco to the top of Pool E as the Lions lead Pool E with 9 points, the other 6 having been earned away against Tanzania (0-2) last November and at home against Zambia (2-1) last Friday.

Earlier in the evening, in the same group, Tanzania overcame Zambia (1-0), taking their tally to 6 points and second place behind Morocco.
