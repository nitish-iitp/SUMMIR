Nicolas Jackson has picked an injury ahead of Senegal's 2026 FIFA World Cup qualifier against neighbours Mauritania
The Chelsea striker has been ruled out for the game on Sunday after scans detected a sprained ankle problem on his right foot
The former Villareal forward adds to Senegal's long injury list for the World Cup qualifiers in the month of June
