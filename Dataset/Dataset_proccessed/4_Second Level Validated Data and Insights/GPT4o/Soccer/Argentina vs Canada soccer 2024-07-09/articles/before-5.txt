Argentina vs. Canada: Copa America Semifinal Preview
Updated: Jul. 09, 2024, 1:00 p.m.|Published: Jul. 08, 2024, 10:15 a.m.

Argentina's Lionel Messi walks with the ball to the penalty spot during a penalty shootout against Ecuador in a Copa America quarterfinal soccer match in Houston, Thursday, July 4, 2024.AP

The Copa America semifinal between Argentina and Canada promises to be an exciting matchup, pitting the tournament’s defending champions against a surprise contender. Scheduled for Tuesday, July 9, 2024, at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, N.J., this game is set to kick off at 8 p.m. ET and will be broadcast live on FS1 and streamed on Fubo.

How to Watch
Date: Tuesday, July 9
Time: 8 p.m. ET
Location: MetLife Stadium, East Rutherford, N.J.
TV: FS1
Live Stream: Fubo
Odds: Argentina -310; Draw +390; Canada +850
Match Preview

Argentina: Argentina enters the semifinals with high expectations as they seek to secure back-to-back Copa America titles. Led by Lionel Messi, who has taken on a more supportive role in this tournament, Argentina boasts a formidable attacking lineup. Lautaro Martinez has emerged as the tournament’s leading scorer with four goals, while Alexis Mac Allister has contributed two assists. Despite a somewhat shaky performance against Ecuador, where they needed a penalty shootout to advance, Argentina remains the heavy favorite.

Canada celebrates their win over Venezuela in a Copa America quarterfinal soccer match in Arlington, Texas, Friday, July 5, 2024.AP

Canada: Canada has been the surprise package of this Copa America. Under the guidance of new head coach Jesse Marsch, they have exceeded expectations, making it to the semifinals for the first time. Despite losing 2-0 to Argentina in the tournament’s opening match, Canada has shown resilience and growth, particularly in their defensive organization. Their Cinderella run, marked by a dramatic penalty shootout win over Venezuela, has captivated fans and set the stage for a historic semifinal.

Key Players and Lineups

Argentina:

Goalkeeper: Emiliano Martinez
Defenders: Nahuel Molina, Cristian Romero, Lisandro Martinez, Nicolas Tagliafico
Midfielders: Rodrigo De Paul, Alexis Mac Allister, Enzo Fernandez
Forwards: Nicolas Gonzalez, Lionel Messi, Lautaro Martinez

Canada:

Goalkeeper: Maxime Crepeau
Defenders: Alistair Johnston, Moise Bombito, Alphonso Davies
Midfielders: Jonathan Osorio, Stephen Eustaquio, Richie Laryea
Forwards: Jonathan David, Jacob Shaffelburg, Cyle Larin
Predictions

Argentina’s depth and experience make them the favorites in this semifinal clash. While Canada’s journey has been impressive, they will face a stern test against an Argentine side determined to reach the final. Expect an entertaining match, but Argentina’s quality should see them through.

Prediction: Argentina 3, Canada 1
