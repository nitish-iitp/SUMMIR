Last-minute tickets to see Lionel Messi play at NRG Stadium in Houston: Copa America, Argentina vs. Ecuador
Updated: Jul. 02, 2024, 9:58 a.m.|Published: Jul. 02, 2024, 9:30 a.m.

Argentina's Lionel Messi dribbles during a Copa America Group A soccer match against Chile in East Rutherford, N.J., Tuesday, June 25, 2024.AP Photo/Julia Nikhinson

By Madison Kemeny | MKemeny@syracuse.com

Lionel Messi and Argentina will play Ecuador in the Quarter-Finals of the 2024 Copa America Final Stage on Thursday, July 4 (7/4/2024) at the NRG Stadium in Houston, TX. The match starts at 9:00 p.m. ET.

After sustaining a right groin/adductor injury (via USA Today) during the match against Argentina and Chile at the MetLife Stadium on June 15, Messi sat out during Saturday’s (June 29) match against Peru, where Argentina came out on top regardless, 2-0.

According to ESPN, Messi is back training ahead of the Quarter-Finals against Ecuador, so fans can hopefully get the chance to see him in action.

Ecuador most recently faced off against Mexico in Matchday 3 on June 30, which ended in a draw 0-0.

Where to buy Copa America, Argentina vs. Ecuador tickets

Tickets to the match are completely sold out, but fans can score resale tickets at reasonable prices through Vivid Seats, SeatGeek, StubHub or Ticketmaster.

At the time of publishing, the cheapest tickets start at $138 before fees on SeatGeek.

Prices and availability are noted at the time of publishing and are subject to change.

Here’s a look at the best prices available (before fees):

Vivid Seats: Starting at $148
SeatGeek: Starting at $138
StubHub: Starting at $144
Ticketmaster: Starting at $175

Can’t make it to the match? Stream Argentina vs. Chile on DirecTV Stream, fuboTV (free trial), Sling (half off first month), or Hulu + Live TV.
