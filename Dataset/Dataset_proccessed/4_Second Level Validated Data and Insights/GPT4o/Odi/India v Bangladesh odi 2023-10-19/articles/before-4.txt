India vs Bangladesh match in ODI Cricket World Cup 2023: TV channel, telecast and live stream details

SOHAM MUKHERJEE

18 October, 2023
•
6:50 pm UTC

India are set to face Bangladesh in their fourth match of the ICC Cricket World Cup 2023, at the Maharashtra Cricket Association Stadium in Pune on Thursday. 

The Men in Blue have kicked off the tournament with strong performances winning their first three games quite convincingly. This successful start has positioned them as one of the top contenders for the World Cup.

MORE: Full Cricket World Cup 2023 fixtures & results | Bet on CWC 2023 matches here!

Skipper Rohit Sharma has been in sensational form and is leading the batting order, whereas Jasprit Bumrah and Kuldeep Yadav have been the key bowlers.  Riding on their excellent performances they have beaten Australia, Afghanistan, and arch-rivals Pakistan, further enhancing their reputation as a formidable cricketing force.

Bangladesh, on the other hand, began their campaign with an easy win against Afghanistan but has encountered setbacks, including losses to New Zealand and Pakistan. As India faces their neighbours, the cohesiveness and form of the Indian team will present a significant challenge for Bangladesh in the upcoming match.

When is IND vs BAN CWC 2023 match?

The ICC World Cup 2023 game between India and Bangladesh will be played on Thursday, October 19, 2023. 

Where is IND vs BAN being played?

The match between India and Bangladesh will be played at the Maharashtra Cricket Association Stadium in Pune. 

What time does IND vs BAN start?

The game between India and Bangladesh is scheduled to begin at 14:00 (IST) (+5:30 GMT).

Which TV channel in India will telecast IND vs BAN?

Star Sports Network has acquired the rights to broadcast the ICC ODI World Cup 2023. The India and Bangladesh match of the ODI World Cup 2023 will be broadcast live in multiple languages (English, Hindi, and Vernacular) across the Star Sports Network.
