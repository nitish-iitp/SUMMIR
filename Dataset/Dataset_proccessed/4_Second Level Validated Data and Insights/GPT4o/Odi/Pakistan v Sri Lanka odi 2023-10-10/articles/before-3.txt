Pakistan vs Sri Lanka match in ODI Cricket World Cup 2023: TV channel, telecast and live stream details

RAHUL IYER

09 October, 2023
•
11:00 pm UTC

The second round of Cricket World Cup matches will continue on Monday, 10 October as the second match of the day sees Pakistan taking on Sri Lanka in Hyderabad.

Pakistan won their first match, at the same venue on Friday, beating Netherlands by 81 runs after posting a competitive 286 thanks to fifties from Mohammad Rizwan (68) and Saud Shakeel (68).

MORE: Full Cricket World Cup 2023 fixtures & results | Bet on CWC 2023 matches here!

Sri Lanka were handed a brutal defeat in Delhi on Saturday as South Africa smashed the highest-ever World Cup total, 428/5, before bowling the Lankans out for 326. Kusal Mendis' burst of eight sixes in the Powerplay was the only bright spot for them.

When is PAK vs SL CWC 2023 match?

The ICC World Cup 2023 game between Pakistan and Sri Lanka will be played on Tuesday, October 10, 2023. 

Where is PAK vs SL being played?

The match between Pakistan and Sri Lanka will be played at the Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium in Hyderabad. The venue has already hosted two matches so far in the competition.

What time does PAK vs SL start?

The game between New Zealand vs Netherlands is scheduled to kickstart at 14:00 (IST) (+5:30 GMT).

Which TV channel in India will telecast PAK vs SL?

Star Sports Network has acquired the rights to broadcast the ICC ODI World Cup 2023. The New Zealand vs Netherlands match of the ODI World Cup 2023 will be broadcasted live in multiple languages (English, Hindi, and Vernacular) across the Star Sports Network.

Where will PAK vs SL be live streamed?

The live streaming of the match between New Zealand vs Netherlands will be on Disney+ Hotstar. All the mobile users would be able to view the World Cup opener for free of cost on the Disney+ Hotstar mobile app.
