India vs New Zealand semifinal match in ODI Cricket World Cup 2023: TV channel, telecast and live stream details

MOHIT KHAKHAR

13 November, 2023
•
6:50 pm UTC

After an enthralling end to the league stages, it is now time for the knockout matches of the ODI World Cup. Hosts India will play New Zealand in the first semifinal of the World Cup 2023. The fixture will be a replay of the 2019 World Cup last-four clash.

In the last edition, the Kiwis got the better of the Men in Blue, as they broke a billion hearts and made it to the final.

MORE: Full Cricket World Cup 2023 fixtures & results | Bet on CWC 2023 matches here!

Fast forward to 2023 and India finished the group stage as the table toppers, winning all of their nine games in the tournament so far. The hosts are the only unbeaten team in the competition. 

New Zealand have had a topsy-turvy tournament. After going unbeaten in their first four games, they suffered defeats in their next four. However, the Blackcaps managed to seal a semifinal berth after beating Sri Lanka in their final match of the league stage.

When is IND vs NZ CWC 2023 semifinal match?

The ICC World Cup 2023 semifinal game between India and New Zealand will be played on Wednesday, November 15, 2023. 

Where is IND vs NZ semifinal being played?

The semifinal match between India and New Zealand will be played at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai.

What time does IND vs NZ semifinal start?

The ICC ODI World Cup 2023 semifinal between India and New Zealand is scheduled to start at 2:00 p.m. IST. (5:30 a.m. GMT)

Which TV channel in India will telecast IND vs NZ semifinal?

Star Sports Network has acquired the rights to broadcast the ICC ODI World Cup 2023. The World Cup Semifinal between India and New Zealand will be broadcasted live in multiple languages (English, Hindi, and Vernacular) across the Star Sports Network.

Where will IND vs NZ semifinal be live streamed?

The live streaming of the ODI World Cup Semifinal between India and New Zealand will be on Disney+ Hotstar. Mobile users will be able to view the World Cup clash involving the hosts for free on the Disney+ Hotstar mobile app.
