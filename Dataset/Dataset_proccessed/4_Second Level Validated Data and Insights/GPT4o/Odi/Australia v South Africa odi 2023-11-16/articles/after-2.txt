AUS vs SA | Australia overcomes middle-order stutter, makes final
David Miller’s fighting century lifts South Africa to a modest total before Cummins’ men, riding on opener Head’s brisk half-century, complete the job after surviving some anxious moments

Updated - November 17, 2023 07:24 am IST - Kolkata

ASHWIN ACHAL
1
READ LATER
PRINT

Australia’s Pat Cummins and Mitchell Starc celebrate progressing to the ICC Cricket World Cup final after winning the match against South Africa on November 16, 2023. | Photo Credit: Reuters

For a few brief moments, South Africa believed that it could finally get it right in a ICC World Cup semifinal match. But it was not to be, as Australia withstood some painful blows to emerge on top by three wickets.

The Australians, never ones to back down from a fight, will take on the Indians in what should be a classic final on Sunday.
