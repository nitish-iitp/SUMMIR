Australia vs South Africa match in ODI Cricket World Cup 2023: TV channel, telecast and live stream details

MOHIT KHAKHAR

15 November, 2023
•
9:40 pm UTC

The penultimate clash of the cricket festival is here! Heavyweights South Africa will face five-time champions, Australia in the second semi-final. The match will determine India's opponent in the elusive final.

South Africa and Australia finished level on points at the end of the group stages. However, the Proteas have played an aggressive brand of cricket throughout, earning them a higher Net Run Rate over Australia. 

MORE: Full Cricket World Cup 2023 fixtures & results | Bet on CWC 2023 matches here!

Australia entered the semis on a seven-match unbeaten streak. After a shocker of a start, the Aussies pulled their way back into the tournament. However, South Africa have had their number over Australia in the group stages early on in the tournament. 

South Africa were schooled by India later on in the group stages but came back stronger in their final league game against Afghanistan. Can South Africa make it to their first-ever World Cup final, or will the Aussies show they're the kings of the competition?

When is AUS vs SA CWC 2023 semifinal match?

The ICC World Cup 2023 semifinal game between Australia and South Africa will be played on Thursday, November 16, 2023. 

Where is AUS vs SA semifinal being played?

The semifinal match between Australia and South Africa will be played at the Eden Gardens in Kolkata.

What time does AUS vs SA semifinal start?

The ICC ODI World Cup 2023 semifinal between Australia and South Africa is scheduled to start at 2:00 p.m. IST. (+5:30 GMT)

Which TV channel in India will telecast AUS vs SA semifinal?

Star Sports Network has acquired the rights to broadcast the ICC ODI World Cup 2023. The World Cup Semifinal between Australia and South Africa will be broadcasted live in multiple languages (English, Hindi, and Vernacular) across the Star Sports Network.

Where will AUS vs SA semifinal be live streamed?

The live streaming of the ODI World Cup Semifinal between Australia and South Africa will be on Disney+ Hotstar. Mobile users will be able to view the World Cup clash involving the hosts for free on the Disney+ Hotstar mobile app.
