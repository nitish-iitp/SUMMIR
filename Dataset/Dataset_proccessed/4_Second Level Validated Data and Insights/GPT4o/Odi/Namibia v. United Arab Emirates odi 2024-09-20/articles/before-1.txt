Match Preview

In Match 25 of the ICC CWC League 2, the United States of America triumphed over Namibia by 6 wickets. Namibia batted first, setting a target of 200 runs. They managed 199/9 in their 50 overs. Jan Frylinck led with a solid 70 off 88 balls, Jonathan Smit added 49 from 75 balls, and captain Gerhard Erasmus contributed 27 off 49 balls. Despite their efforts, Namibia’s total proved insufficient. In response, the USA chased down the target with 200/4 in 41.3 overs. Namibia’s top bowlers included Gerhard Erasmus with 2 wickets for 34 runs in 8 overs, and Tangeni Lungameni with 1 wicket for 34 runs in 7 overs. Jan Nicol Loftie Eaton went wicketless, conceding 23 runs in 4 overs.

In Match 27, the USA defeated the United Arab Emirates by 10 wickets. UAE batted first and struggled, scoring only 106 runs before being dismissed in 31.2 overs. Rahul Chopra was the top scorer with 32 runs off 64 balls, Aryansh Sharma contributed 31 runs from 38 balls, and Ali Naseer added 23 runs off 28 balls. The USA chased the target effortlessly, reaching 107/0 in just 15.5 overs. UAE’s bowlers, including Aayan Afzal Khan, Muhammad Jawad Ullah, and Ali Naseer, failed to take wickets, with Khan conceding 18 runs in 4 overs, Ullah giving away 19 runs in 3 overs, and Naseer allowing 20 runs in 2 overs.
