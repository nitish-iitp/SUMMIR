Australia vs Netherlands match in ODI Cricket World Cup 2023: TV channel, telecast and live stream details

RITABRATA BANERJEE

24 October, 2023
•
9:56 pm UTC

Australia and Netherlands will play their fifth fixture of the 2023 World Cup when they clash at the Arun Jaitley Stadium in New Delhi on Wednesday.

MORE: Full Cricket World Cup 2023 fixtures & results | Bet on CWC 2023 matches here!

The Aussies have had an up-and-down tournament so far. After losing their first two matches of the World Cup against India and South Africa, the five-time world champions came back strongly to beat Sri Lanka in back-to-back outings.

Netherlands, on the other hand, have won one out of their first four games with their only win coming against South Africa.

When is AUS vs NED CWC 2023 match?

The ICC World Cup 2023 game between Australia and Netherlands will be played on Wednesday, October 25, 2023. 

Where is AUS vs NED being played?

The match between Australia and Netherlands will be played at the Arun Jaitley Stadium in New Delhi.

What time does AUS vs NED?

The ICC ODI World Cup 2023 game between Australia and Netherlands is scheduled to start at 14:00 (IST) (+5:30 GMT).

Which TV channel in India will telecast AUS vs NED?

Star Sports Network has acquired the rights to broadcast the ICC ODI World Cup 2023. The Australia vs Netherlands match of the ODI World Cup 2023 will be broadcasted live in multiple languages (English, Hindi, and Vernacular) across the Star Sports Network.

Where will AUS vs NED be live streamed?

The live streaming of the game between Australia and Netherlands will be on Disney+ Hotstar. All the mobile users would be able to view the World Cup clash involving the hosts for free of cost on the Disney+ Hotstar mobile app.
