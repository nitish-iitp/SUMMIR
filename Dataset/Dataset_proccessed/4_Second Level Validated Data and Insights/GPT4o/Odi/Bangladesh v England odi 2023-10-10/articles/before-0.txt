England vs Bangladesh match in ODI Cricket World Cup 2023: TV channel, telecast and live stream details

MOHIT KHAKHAR

09 October, 2023
•
8:00 pm UTC

The second round of Cricket World Cup matches will continue on Tuesday, 10 October as we are set to witness a double-header. The first match of the day will be played between England and Bangladesh.

The defending champions faced a disappointing loss at the hands of New Zealand in the opening encounter of the ODI World Cup 2023.

MORE: Full Cricket World Cup 2023 fixtures & results | Bet on CWC 2023 matches here!

They would be looking to bounce back in their encounter against Bangladesh.

Bangladesh had a dream start to the competition. The Bangla Tigers beat Afghanistan in their opening encounter courtesy of Mehidy Hasan Miraz and Nazmul Hossain Shanto's half-centuries. They secured a six-wicket win over Afghanistan. However, they face a much bigger test this time around against England.

When is ENG vs BAN CWC 2023 match?

The ICC World Cup 2023 game between England and Bangladesh will be played on Tuesday, October 10, 2023. 

Where is ENG vs BAN being played?

The match between England and Bangladesh will be played at the Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association (HPCA) Stadium in Dharamshala. This will be the second match at the venue so far at the ICC ODI World Cup.

What time does ENG vs BAN start?

The game between England vs Bangladesh is scheduled to kickstart at 10:30 (IST) (+5:30 GMT).

Which TV channel in India will telecast ENG vs BAN?

Star Sports Network has acquired the rights to broadcast the ICC ODI World Cup 2023. The England vs Bangladesh match of the ODI World Cup 2023 will be broadcasted live in multiple languages (English, Hindi, and Vernacular) across the Star Sports Network.

Where will ENG vs BAN be live streamed?

The live streaming of the match between England and Bangladesh will be on Disney+ Hotstar. All the mobile users would be able to view the World Cup opener for free of cost on the Disney+ Hotstar mobile app.
