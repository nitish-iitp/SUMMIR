1 min read . Updated: 13 Oct 2023, 11:15 PM IST
Livemint, Edited By Sounak Mukhopadhyay, Devesh Kumar
NZ vs BAN Score Highlights: New Zealand skipper Kane Williamson, Daryl Mitchell and Devon Conway led Kiwis to their 3rd consecutive win the ICC World Cup 2023 and the team stands strong on the top of the points table
LIVE NZ vs BAN Score Updates: New Zealand's captain Kane Williamson, left, celebrates his fifty runs with New Zealand's Daryl Mitchell during the ICC Men's Cricket World Cup match between New Zealand and Bangladesh in Chennai , India, Friday, Oct. 13, 2023. (AP Photo/Eranga Jayawardena) (AP)

NZ vs BAN Score Highlights: Kane Williamson and Daryl Mitchell led New Zealand to 8-wicket victory against Bangladesh and the Kiwis maintain their dominance in the ICC World Cup 2023. This is third consecutive win of New Zealand and the team maintain its hold over the points table. 

Bangladesh skipper Shakib Al Hasan dismissed in form Devon Conway and ended the strong partnership. Daryl Mitchell is on the crease and he started with a massive SIX. Bangladesh looking for more wickets to increase the pressure. 

New Zealand's run chase began on a shaky note as Mustafizur Rahman dismissed opener Rachin Ravindra early to put batters under pressure. Devon Conway and skipper Kane Williamson are on the crease treading a cautious path. 

Bangladesh batter Mahmudullah provided his team with some crucial runs in the end as New Zealand pacers restrict Bangladesh to 245/9 after 50 overs. Lockie Ferguson clinched 3 wickets, Trent Boult and Matt Henry got two wickets each while Glenn Phillips and Mitchell Santner have one-one wicket to their name.

After a good partnership between Shakib Al Hasan and Mushfiqur Rahim, Bangladesh is again reeling under pressure as New Zealand bowlers continue to grab wickets. Lockie Fergusan dismissed Shakib Al Hasan, Matt Henry took out Mushfiqur Rahim and Trent Bould departed Towhid Hridoy

New Zealand seem to be in complete control of the game as Bangladesh batters are collapsing under pressure. Lockie Ferguson and Glenn Phillips clinched more wickets to derail Bangladesh batting. Shakib Al Hasan and Mushfiqur Rahim on crease. 

New Zealand is off to a good start against Bangladesh as Trent Boult and Lockie Ferguson clinched one wicket each. Currently, Mehidy Hasan and Najmul Hossain Shanto are trying to stabilise the innings.
