Bangladesh and South Africa are all set to go head-to-head in their fifth match of the 2023 World Cup, at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai on 24 October 2023.

Bangladesh began their campaign in style, bowling Afghanistan out for 156 before chaisng down the score in quick time. However, they then slipped to consecutive defeats against England and New Zealand before losing their most recent fixture to India in Pune.

South Africa also started strongly, beating Sri Lanka by 102 runs after scoring a World Cup-record 428/5. They then thrashed Australia before suffering a shock loss to Netherlands. The Proteas got back to winning ways in their latest match, beating defending champions England by 229 runs in Mumbai.
