Andrew Vaughn and the Chicago White Sox take the field on Wednesday at Guaranteed Rate Field against Matthew Boyd, who will start for the Cleveland Guardians. First pitch is set for 2:10 p.m. ET for the final game of a three-game series.

The Guardians pitching staff is 10th in the majors with a collective 8.8 strikeouts per nine innings. The White Sox have pitched to a 4.86 ERA this season, which ranks 29th in baseball.
