Lane Thomas gets a lot out of a little in the Guardians’ 6-4 win over the White Sox
Updated: Sep. 12, 2024, 2:22 a.m.|Published: Sep. 11, 2024, 5:14 p.m.
14
1/14
Cleveland Guardians vs. Chicago White Sox, Sept. 11, 2024
By Paul Hoynes, cleveland.com

CHICAGO — Center fielder Lane Thomas squeezed the most out of the least on Wednesday afternoon, and the Guardians are going home with a sweep over the White Sox because of it.

The bullpen helped out as well as the Guardians finished this nine-game trip through Kansas City, Los Angeles and Chicago with a 6-3 record.
