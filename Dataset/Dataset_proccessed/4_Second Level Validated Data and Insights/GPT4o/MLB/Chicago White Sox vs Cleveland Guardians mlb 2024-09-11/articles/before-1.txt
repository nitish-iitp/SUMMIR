Chicago has recorded 115 HRs this year as well as 428 RBIs. They have tallied 209 two-baggers, while taking a walk 351 times as well as recording 447 runs. The Chicago White Sox are sitting with an on-base percentage of .278 in addition to a batting average of .221 on the year. The White Sox have a slugging percentage of .340 and they score 3.08 runs per contest (30th in the league). The White Sox hold a team WHIP of 1.453 and are the owners of a FIP of 4.67 as a pitching staff so far this year. Their strikeout to walk ratio sits at 8.60 (1,207 strikeouts against 575 bases on balls). They sit at 26th in the league as a staff in total hits allowed with 1,265.

Chicago relievers have an inherited score rate of 34.0% of 247 inherited base runners. Their relief pitchers have taken the field 142 times in high leverage situations in addition to 157 occasions with runners on. Sitting with 108 save situations, the White Sox have 57 holds in addition to 31 blown saves. They rank 30th in MLB with a save rate of 36.7%, and they have sent 475 bullpen pitchers to the mound for the season. Chicago has had relievers enter the contest in 49 save chances and they have come away with 18 saves.

The Chicago White Sox have turned 99 double plays and have a fielding percentage of .983 (26th in the majors). The White Sox have accrued 1,149 assists, 86 errors and have amassed 3,800 putouts this season.
