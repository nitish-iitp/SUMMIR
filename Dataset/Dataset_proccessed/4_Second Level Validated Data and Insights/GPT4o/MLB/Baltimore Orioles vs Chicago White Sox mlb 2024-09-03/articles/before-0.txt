Chicago White Sox vs Baltimore Orioles Prediction, 9/3/2024 MLB Picks, Best Bets & Odds

Location: Oriole Park at Camden Yards in Baltimore, MD

TV: NBC Sports Chicago

The Chicago White Sox (31-107) are headed to Oriole Park at Camden Yards on Tuesday where they will try to beat the Baltimore Orioles (79-59). The starting pitchers are Nick Nastrini and Cade Povich.

The White Sox have converted 68.5% of balls in play into outs in their 10,869 innings, which has them ranked 26th in MLB. The Chicago White Sox have amassed 3,623 putouts on the campaign, in addition to 1,092 assists and 81 errors. Their fielding percentage is currently at .983 which is 25th in the majors, and have accumulated 93 double plays.

Nastrini has thrown 30 innings and has tallied 22 punch outs so far in his MLB career. His ERA is 7.15 (24 earned runs allowed) and he has a WHIP of 1.755. He has conceded 27 base knocks (8.0 hits per 9 innings) and has had 26 walks. Nastrini (0-6 career mark) has a FIP of 7.05 and he has faced 144 opposing hitters in the majors.

Baltimore has accumulated 206 home runs on the year and 660 runs batted in. They have 228 doubles, while taking a walk 407 times as well as scoring 684 runs. The Baltimore Orioles have tallied an OBP of .315 as well as a batting average of .251 this year. The Orioles have a team slugging percentage of .442 and they have an average of 4.96 runs per outing (4th in the league). They have been rung up on 1,137 instances (19th in baseball) and have recorded 1,187 hits.

The Baltimore pitching staff have surrendered 597 runs for the year while having an ERA of 3.94 (538 earned runs conceded). They have allowed 144 dingers and they allow 4.37 runs per 9 innings (13th in the league). The Orioles have a team WHIP of 1.236 with a FIP of 3.91 as a squad on the campaign. They currently have a strikeout to walk ratio of 8.50 (1,167 strikeouts against 408 free passes). They are currently ranked 15th in the league as a pitching staff in total hits given up with 1,110.

Baltimore bullpen pitchers have an inherited score percentage of 30.8% of 208 inherited base runners. Their bullpen pitchers have come in 140 times in high leverage situations in addition to 145 occasions with runners on. With 159 save situations, the Orioles have earned 98 holds and also 19 blown saves. They are currently ranked 5th in the league holding a save percentage of 68.3%, and they have dispatched 460 relief pitchers onto the field over the course of the season. Baltimore has called on relievers to enter the contest in 60 save chances and they have walked away with 41 saves.

Out of their 11,058 innings on the diamond, the Orioles have earned a defensive efficiency of 70.4% (12th in MLB). The Baltimore Orioles have turned 78 double plays and have a fielding percentage of .986 (9th in baseball). The Orioles have accrued 1,149 assists, 68 errors and have 3,686 putouts so far this season.

So far in his MLB career, Povich has allowed 64 hits while earning 37 K's in 52 innings pitched. He has surrendered 38 earned runs while holding a WHIP of 1.731 and a FIP of 6.5. He has a strikeout to walk ratio of 1.42 and he has gone up against 245 opposing batters in his pro baseball career. Povich (1-7 career record) is the owner of a 6.58 earned run average and has conceded 11.1 hits per nine innings.
