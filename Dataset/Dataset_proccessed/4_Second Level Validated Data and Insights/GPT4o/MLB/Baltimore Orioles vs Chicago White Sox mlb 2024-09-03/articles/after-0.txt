BOTTOM LINE: The Chicago White Sox head into the matchup with the Baltimore Orioles after losing 11 in a row.

Baltimore has an 80-59 record overall and a 40-30 record in home games. Orioles hitters have a collective .444 slugging percentage to lead MLB.

Chicago has a 31-108 record overall and a 13-54 record on the road. The White Sox have a 17-84 record in games when they have allowed a home run.

Tuesday's game is the sixth time these teams square off this season. The Orioles have a 5-0 advantage in the season series.

TOP PERFORMERS: Anthony Santander has 22 doubles, two triples, 39 home runs and 89 RBI while hitting .240 for the Orioles. Colton Cowser is 9-for-39 with two doubles and two home runs over the last 10 games.
