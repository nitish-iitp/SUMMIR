Cleveland Guardians vs. Tampa Bay Rays, Sept. 15, 2024
By Joe Noga, cleveland.com

CLEVELAND, Ohio — Ben Lively and the Guardians take on the Rays at Progressive Field. .

Here are the starting lineups for Sunday’s game between the Guardians and Rays.
