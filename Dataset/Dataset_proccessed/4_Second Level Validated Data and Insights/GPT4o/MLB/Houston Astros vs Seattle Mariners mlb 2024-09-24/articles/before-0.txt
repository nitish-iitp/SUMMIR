Mariners Coming off a Win

The Mariners are averaging 4.13 runs while their pitching is giving up 3.78 runs per game. The offense is ranked 22nd in the league while the pitching is ranked 1st. Astros Offense Looks to Be Better

The Astros scored one run on Monday and that led to the defeat. Jason Heyward hit a home run but that was the only run scored.
