BLUE JAYS
Spencer vs. Spencer is all Blue Jays in Atlanta

Jays rookie Spencer Horwitz finished with four hits, including two homers, and 11 total bases in Saturday night’s win over the Braves.

Updated Sept. 7, 2024 at 11:10 p.m.
Sept. 7, 2024
1 min read
Save

Blue Jay Spencer Horwitz went deep twice in Saturday night’s win in Atlanta.

Mike Stewart AP
By Mike WilnerBaseball Columnist

The battle of the Spencers was no contest at all.

Blue Jays rookie Spencer Horwitz homered twice and doubled off the right-field wall against Braves rookie Spencer Schwellenbach, powering the Jays to a 9-5 win at Truist Park in Atlanta that snapped a four-game losing skid Saturday night.
