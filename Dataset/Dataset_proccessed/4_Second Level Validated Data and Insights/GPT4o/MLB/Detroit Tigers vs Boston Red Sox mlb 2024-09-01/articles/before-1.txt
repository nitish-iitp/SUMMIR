The Tigers have a WHIP of 1.193 and have a FIP of 3.74 as a pitching staff for the season. They rank 10th in the league as a staff in total hits surrendered with 1,072. The Detroit pitching staff have surrendered 556 runs this year and come in with a team ERA of 3.78 (504 earned runs yielded). They have earned a strikeout to walk ratio of 8.60 (1,140 strikeouts against 358 walks). They have given up 134 long balls and they surrender 4.17 runs per 9 innings (9th in the league).

Detroit has called on bullpen pitchers to enter the contest in 51 save opportunities and they have recorded 31 saves. They sit at 22nd in the league with a save rate of 60.8%, and they have sent 413 relief pitchers onto the diamond over the course of the season. Their relief pitchers have stepped onto the mound 125 times in high leverage situations and on 145 occasions with base runners. Detroit bullpen pitchers hold an inherited score percentage of 24.9% out of 201 inherited base runners. Sitting with 112 save situations, the Tigers have recorded 60 holds and 20 blown saves.

In their 10,791 innings played, the Tigers have earned a defensive efficiency of 70.2% (14th in professional baseball). The Detroit Tigers have gotten 106 double plays and have a fielding rate of .985 (14th in MLB). The Tigers have accrued 1,144 assists, 70 errors and are sitting with 3,597 putouts so far this year.
