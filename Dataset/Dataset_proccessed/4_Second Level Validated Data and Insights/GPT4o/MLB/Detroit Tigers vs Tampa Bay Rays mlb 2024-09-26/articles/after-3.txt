See photos as Detroit Tigers sweep Rays, lower playoff magic number
Updated: Sep. 27, 2024, 7:57 a.m.|Published: Sep. 27, 2024, 7:05 a.m.

Detroit Tigers host Tampa Bay Rays on Sept. 26Josh Boland
By Josh Boland | jboland@mlive.com

DETROIT, MI - The Detroit Tigers won the third and final game of the series with the Tampa Bay Rays, 4-3, on Thursday, Sept. 26 at Comerica Park.

The Rays led 3-0 until the bottom of the 6th inning when the Tigers scored two runs. The Tigers then scored two in the bottom of the 8th inning to secure the win.

Kayla Williams, Charmin Smith Comment on Williams' Performance

Pitcher Jason Foley sealed the Tigers win with a strikeout.

The Tigers’ magic number is now one. This means the Tigers just need to win one more game, or have the Minnesota Twins lose one more, to reach the postseason.
