Red-hot Tigers rally to beat Rays, sweep series
Updated: Sep. 26, 2024, 5:38 p.m.|Published: Sep. 26, 2024, 3:41 p.m.

Detroit Tigers' Colt Keith slides for a RBI triple during the sixth inning of a baseball game against the Tampa Bay Rays, Thursday, Sept. 26, 2024, in Detroit. (AP Photo/Carlos Osorio)AP

By Evan Woodbery | ewoodber@mlive.com

DETROIT -- With the 2024 Detroit Tigers, the magical is starting to feel almost routine.

The Detroit Tigers erased a three-run deficit to defeat the Tampa Bay Rays 4-3 on Thursday afternoon at Comerica Park, moving one step closer to their first playoff appearance since 2014.
