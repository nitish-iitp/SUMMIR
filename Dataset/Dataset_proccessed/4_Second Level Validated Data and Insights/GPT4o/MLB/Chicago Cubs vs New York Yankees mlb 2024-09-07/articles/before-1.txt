The New York Yankees will send a hot-hitting Jazz Chisholm to the plate against the Chicago Cubs and Dansby Swanson, who has been on a tear in recent games, when the teams meet on Saturday at 2:20 p.m. ET.

The New York Yankees average 1.5 home runs per game and have hit a league-leading 213 home runs in total. The Cubs’ 149 home runs rank 19th in Major League Baseball.
