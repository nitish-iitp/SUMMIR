Photos: New York Yankees 3, Chicago Cubs 0 at Wrigley Field
By TERRENCE ANTONIO JAMES | tjames@chicagotribune.com | Chicago Tribune
UPDATED: September 6, 2024 at 7:43 PM CDT

Photos from the Chicago Cubs’ 3-0 loss to the New York Yankees on Sept. 6, 2024, at Wrigley Field. The Cubs recorded only one hit — a Cody Bellinger double in the fourth inning — in the shutout.

Yankees starter Luis Gil delivers in the first inning against the Cubs on Sept. 6, 2024, at Wrigley Field. Gil allowed one hit in six innings in the Yankees’ 3-0 win. (Terrence Antonio James/Chicago Tribune)
