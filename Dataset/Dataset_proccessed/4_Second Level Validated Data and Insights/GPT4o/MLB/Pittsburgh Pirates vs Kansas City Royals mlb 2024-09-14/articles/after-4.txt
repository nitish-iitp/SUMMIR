BOTTOM LINE: The Kansas City Royals lead 1-0 in a three-game series against the Pittsburgh Pirates.

The Pirates are 70-77 overall and 37-39 in home games. The Pirates have gone 33-60 in games when they have allowed at least one home run.

The Royals have an 81-67 record overall and a 36-37 record on the road. The Royals have the sixth-ranked team ERA in the AL at 3.87.

Saturday's game is the second time these teams square off this season.

TOP PERFORMERS: Bryan Reynolds leads the Pirates with a .281 batting average, and has 28 doubles, three triples, 22 home runs, 51 walks and 84 RBI. Nick Gonzales is 11-for-34 with four RBI over the last 10 games.
