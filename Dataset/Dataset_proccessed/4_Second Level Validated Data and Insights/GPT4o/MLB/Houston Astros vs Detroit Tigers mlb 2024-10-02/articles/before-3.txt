The Houston Astros will host the Detroit Tigers in the second game of the AL Wild Card Series on Wednesday at 2:32 p.m. ET, live on ABC from Minute Maid Park. The Astros will look to extend the series after losing in Game 1. Hunter Brown is starting for the Astros and Tyler Holton is set to start for the Tigers.

The pitching staff for the Astros has a collective 9.3 K/9, the third-best in the majors. The Tigers have an 8.4 K/9 this season as a pitching staff, which ranks 21st in the majors.
