5 things to know about the Pelicans on Dec. 31, 2024
Jim Eichenhofer
December 31, 20249:23 AM CST

New Orleans (5-28) closed out the 2024 calendar year with a 116-113 defeat to the Los Angeles Clippers on Monday, also wrapping up a five-game homestand.

Watch postgame reaction from Willie Green, CJ McCollum and Dejounte Murray.

Check out action photos from Clips-Pels.

Listen to Monday’s Pelicans Podcast, featuring TV studio analyst Wes Johnson. The show will return on Monday, Jan. 6, after a holiday break.

New Orleans will try to end a 10-game losing streak Wednesday at Miami. Tip-off is 6:30 p.m., with pregame coverage on TV and radio at 6. The end of Week 11 will consist of two consecutive games vs. Washington.
