NBA: CJ McCollum scores 50, Pelicans end 11-game slide
Reuters / 04:19 PM January 04, 2025

New Orleans Pelicans guard CJ McCollum (3) celebrates after a 3-point basket that was later ruled a 2-point basket during the fourth quarter of an NBA basketball game against the Washington Wizards in New Orleans, Friday, Jan. 3, 2025. (AP Photo/Matthew Hinton)

CJ McCollum tied a career-high with 50 points and the host New Orleans Pelicans ended an 11-game losing streak by defeating the Washington Wizards 132-120 in the NBA on Friday night.

McCollum made 18 of 27 field-goal attempts, including 10 3-pointers on 16 attempts, as the Pelicans won for just the second time in 22 games. Trey Murphy III added 17 points, Brandon Boston scored 13 off the bench and Yves Missi had 12 points and 11 rebounds as six Pelicans scored in double figures.
