BOTTOM LINE: Tyrese Maxey and the Philadelphia 76ers host Yves Missi and the New Orleans Pelicans in out-of-conference play.

The 76ers have gone 6-10 in home games. Philadelphia has a 6-13 record in games decided by 10 points or more.
