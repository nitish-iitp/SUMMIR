New Orleans Pelicans sign Keion Brooks Jr. to two-way contract
Pelicans.com
January 9, 20256:30 PM CST

The New Orleans Pelicans announced today that the team has signed Keion Brooks Jr., to a two-way contract. Per team policy, terms of the deal were not disclosed.

Brooks Jr., 6-7, 210, has appeared in 20 games this season for the Birmingham Squadron, averaging 18.0 points, 6.8 rebounds and 2.7 assists in 37.5 minutes per game.

In a corresponding move, New Orleans has waived Trey Jemison III.
