Pelicans-Trail Blazers Connections
Pelicans Head Coach Willie Green was teammates with Trail Blazers Head Coach Chauncey Billups on the LA Clippers during the 2012-13 season.
Pelicans Coaching Advisor Mike Dâ€™Antoni acted as an assistant coach for the Portland Trail Blazers during the 2000-01 season.
Pelicans Assistant Coach Jarron Collins played for the Trail Blazers in his final season as an active player during the 2010-11 season.
Pelicans G CJ McCollum was drafted by the Trail Blazers with the 10th overall pick in the 2013 NBA Draft.
McCollum (2013-2022) spent nine seasons with the team where he appeared in 564 games and averaged 19.0 points, 3.4 rebounds, and 3.4 assists per game.
Pelicans Player Development Coach Corey Brewer played alongside Trail Blazers F Jerami Grant on the 2017-18 Oklahoma City Thunder.
Blazers C/F Robert Williams III is a native of Shreveport, LA, and played high school basketball for North Caddo in Vivian, LA.
