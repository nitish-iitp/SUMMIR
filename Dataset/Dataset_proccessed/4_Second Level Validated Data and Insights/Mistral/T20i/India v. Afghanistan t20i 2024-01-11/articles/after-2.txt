India vs Afghanistan Highlights, 1st T20I: Despite Rohit Sharma's Horrible Run Out, Shivam Dube Inspires India vs AFG
IND vs AFG Highlights: Chasing a target of 159, India defeated Afghanistan by six wickets in Mohali in the first T20I on Thursday.
NDTV Sports Desk
Updated:January 11, 2024 10:17 pm IST
Read Time: 1 min
IND vs AFG, 1st T20I: Shivam Dube shines on return as India crush Afghanistan.
© BCCI

India vs Afghanistan 1st T20 Highlights: Shivam Dube hit an unbeaten 60 as India defeated Afghanistan by six wickets in the first T20I in Mohali on Thursday. A run-chase that got underway with a horrible mix-up, resulting in Rohit Sharma's run out, India scampered home with 15 balls to spare. Earlier, Axar Patel and Mukesh Kumar bagged two wickets each to help India restrict Afghanistan to 158/5 in 20 overs. Mohammad Nabi hits quickfire 42 for Afghanistan, while Azmatullah Omarzai played a rapid cameo of 29. The pair added 68 runs for the fourth wicket before Mukesh dismissed both in the same over. (Scorecard)
