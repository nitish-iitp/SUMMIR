ICC T20 World Cup 2024: India matches – Dates, time, venue – FULL Schedule
India's official tournament starts on June 5, and fans are eagerly awaiting their favorite players in action.
Written by Shubham Chhabra
June 1, 2024 18:07 IST
Follow Us
Team India, one of the most formidable teams in international cricket, will begin their World Cup journey with a warm-up match against Bangladesh.

The International Cricket Council (ICC) is all set to host the ninth edition of its men’s T20 World Cup from June 1 in the Caribbean and the United States. 

Team India, one of the most formidable teams in international cricket, will begin their World Cup journey with a warm-up match against Bangladesh today at 8 PM IST. This match is crucial for the team to fine-tune their strategies. India’s official tournament starts on June 5, and fans are eagerly awaiting their favorite players in action.

This edition of the T20 World Cup is historic, expanding to feature 20 teams, up from the previous 16. The teams will be divided into four groups, with the top two from each group advancing to the Super 8 round. The Super 8 stage will further intensify the competition, splitting into two groups, with the top two from each group proceeding to the semi-finals.

The tournament will feature 55 matches, offering a cricketing feast for fans. The United States will host matches across three iconic venues: Florida, New York, and Texas. 

Meanwhile, six nations within the West Indies will also serve as hosts: Antigua & Barbuda, Barbados, Guyana, Saint Lucia, St. Vincent and the Grenadines, and Trinidad & Tobago.

England, the reigning champions, enter the tournament with high expectations after their victory in the 2022 edition held in Australia, where they triumphed over Pakistan in a thrilling final

Here are all the details of India Matches

Date: June 5, 2024

Match 8: India vs Ireland

Venue: New York

Time: 8 PM IST

Team India will kick off their World Cup campaign against Ireland in New York. This match is expected to set the tone for India’s performance in the tournament.

Date: June 9, 2024

Match 19: India vs Pakistan

Venue: New York

Time: 8 PM IST

One of the most anticipated clashes in cricket, India will face arch-rivals Pakistan in New York. This match promises to be a thrilling encounter, drawing huge viewership from around the globe.

Date: June 12, 2024

Match 25: USA vs India

Venue: New York

Time: 8 PM IST

Continuing their journey, India will play against the USA in New York. This match is crucial for India to secure a strong position in their group.

Date: June 15, 2024

Match 33: Canada vs India

Venue: Florida

Time: 8 PM IST

India will face Canada in Florida. This match is expected to showcase India’s cricketing prowess and strategy as they aim to progress further in the tournament.

Super Eight Stage (if India qualifies)

Date: June 19-24, 2024

If India finishes as one of the top two teams in their group, they will advance to the Super Eight stage. Matches will be held during this period, with the schedule to be determined based on the group stage outcomes.

Semi-finals (if India qualifies)

Date: June 26 and 27, 2024

The top two teams from each group of the Super Eight will battle it out in the semi-finals. Fans are hopeful that India will be one of the contenders.

Final (if India qualifies)

Date: June 29, 2024

The winners of the semi-finals will compete in the final in Barbados

The ICC T20 World Cup 2024 promises to be an unforgettable event, celebrating the spirit of cricket and showcasing the best talent from around the globe.
