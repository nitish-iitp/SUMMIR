Croatia vs Poland Player Ratings; Luka Modric’s brilliance separates the two teams
Croatia vs Poland
UEFA Nations League

Croatia grabbed all three points against Poland at home at Opus Arena in the Group A clash of the UEFA Nations League match week 2nd. A dominant display of powers by the hosts, as Poland struggled to prevent Croatia from scoring following the wave of attacking chances bombarded the Polish defence. Real Madrid’s midfield maestro Luka Modric’s beautiful free-kick goal of the match in the second half, stood as the factor to differentiate the teams on 1-0 scoreline on Croatian soil.

Croatia National Football Team Player Ratings vs Poland

Borna Sosa – 7/10: Brilliant on wide channels contributing to attacks through flanks.
