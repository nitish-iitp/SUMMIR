Republican Stadium Vazgen Sargsyan

The Republican Stadium Vazgen Sargsyan also called: Republican Stadium Hanrapetakan, is a multipurpose stadium in the city of Yerevan, capital of Armenia. The stadium was opened in 1938 and is usually used to host soccer matches, as the Armenian national team plays its home matches at the stadium. Since its opening, the stadium has undergone several renovations. The first renovations were made in 1995, but lack of financial resources delayed the process until 1999, when more than US$3 million was spent on infrastructure development. At the end of 2000, the stadium was completely renovated. In 2008, the stadium underwent a large-scale development in order to modernize the playing surface and create a high standard with VIP sections and other facilities that could comply with UEFA regulations.
