ICC T20 World Cup 2024: Check Full schedule Along With Dates And Venues
The ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 promises thrilling cricket action across six Caribbean islands and the US. Scheduled from June 2 to June 29, the tournament features 20 teams battling across four groups. Highlights include the opening clash between USA and Canada, and the India vs. Pakistan showdown in New York. Top teams advance to the Super Eight stage, leading to the semi-finals and grand finale in Barbados. Follow the excitement as cricket's best vie for glory in this highly anticipated event.
By MOHAMMAD JAZIB
MAY 29, 2024, 13:50 IST
ICC World Cup 2024 Schedule: Dates, Matches, Group, And Venues

The schedule for the T20 World Cup was unveiled in January, with the competition slated to unfold across three venues in the US and six in the West Indies. The first match of the T20 World Cup 2024 will feature Canada against the USA on June 1 in Dallas, while the grand finale is scheduled for June 29 in Barbados. The highly anticipated India vs Pakistan encounter, as per the T20 World Cup schedule, is slated for June 9 in New York.

The tournament's opening match is scheduled for June 2, with co-hosts USA facing arch-rival Canada. On the second day of group play, fellow hosts the West Indies will compete against Papua New Guinea in Guyana. The tournament structure involves four groups, each consisting of five teams. The top two teams from each group will progress to the Super Eight phase, paving the way for the knockout semi-finals and the ultimate final.

ICC T20 Worldcup 2024 Groups And Format

The 20 participating teams have been divided into four groups. From each group, the top two teams will progress to the Super 8s stage. During this phase, the qualifying teams will be divided into two groups of four. Subsequently, the top two teams from each group will secure berths in the semifinals, leading to the culminating final match.
