Match Preview

In Match 18 of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Asia Sub-Regional Qualifier A, Malaysia edged out Hong Kong by 3 runs. Malaysia, batting first, scored 124 runs for 10 wickets in 18.4 overs. Syed Aziz led with 40 runs from 30 balls, while Muhammad Amir added 29 runs from 25 balls. Sharvin Muniandy contributed 24 runs off 25 balls. Despite losing wickets, their total proved tough for Hong Kong to chase. Syed Aziz excelled with the ball, taking 4 wickets for 9 runs in 3 overs. Vijay Unni took 2 wickets for 18 runs in 4 overs, and Rizwan Haider also claimed 2 wickets, conceding 33 runs in 4 overs. Hong Kong ended their innings at 121 runs for 9 wickets in 20 overs, falling short by 3 runs.

In Match 17, the Maldives won against Mongolia by 117 runs. The Maldives set a total of 178 runs for 6 wickets in 20 overs. Luvsanzundui Erdenebulgan was Mongolia’s best bowler with 3 wickets for 29 runs in 4 overs. Mohan Vivekanandan took 2 wickets for 39 runs in 4 overs, and Zoljavkhlan Shurentsetseg had 1 wicket for 23 runs in 3 overs. Mongolia struggled in their chase, getting all out for 61 runs in 19.1 overs. Od Lutbayar was their top scorer with 18 runs from 17 balls. Mohan Vivekanandan added 11 runs off 20 balls, and Temuulen Amarmend scored 9 runs from 13 balls. The Maldives’ bowlers dominated, leading to Mongolia’s defeat.
