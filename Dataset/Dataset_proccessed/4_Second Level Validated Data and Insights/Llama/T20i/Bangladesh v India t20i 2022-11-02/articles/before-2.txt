India vs Bangladesh Head-to-Head Records, IND Head-to-Head Record Against BAN– ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022, Super-12, 34th Match.

India will compete with Bangladesh in their next match of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022. The IND vs BAN clash takes place on November 2 (Wednesday) at Adelaide Oval in Adelaide.

India has played 3 matches so far in the ICC T20 World Cup 2022, winning 2 and losing 1. With 4 points, they take the 2nd spot( Super-12, Group 2 ) in the points table. Bangladesh has played 3 matches so far, winning 2 and losing 1. With 4 points, Bangladesh is placed in the 3rd spot( Super-12, Group 2 ).

In their earlier encounter in the Bangladesh tour of India 2019, India defeated Bangladesh by 30 runs. Batting first in the match, India posted 174 runs on the scoreboard. Later, Bangladesh couldn’t reach the target and lost the match.

India suffered a loss against South Africa in their previous game of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022. In the match, India batted first and piled up 133-9. Suryakumar Yadav slammed 68 runs while Rohit Sharma 14 runs for the India team.

For South Africa, the chase was quite easy. With David Miller’s 59 and Aiden Markram’s 52, SA reached the target in the 19.4th over itself with 5 wickets in their kitty. Lungi Ngidi was awarded man of the match for picking 4 wickets in the match. South Africa won by 5wickets (with 2 balls remaining) in the match.

Bangladesh defeated Zimbabwe in their previous game of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022. In the match, Bangladesh batted first and piled up 150-7. In the reply, Zimbabwe couldn’t reach the target with a loss of 8 wickets. Sean Williams slammed 64 runs while Ryan Burl 27 runs for the Zimbabwe team.

For Bangladesh, Najmul Hossain Shanto and Afif Hossain hit 71 and 29 respectively. Taskin Ahmed was awarded man of the match for picking 3 wickets in the match. Bangladesh won by 3 runs in the match.
