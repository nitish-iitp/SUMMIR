NZ vs AUS Live Streaming, 1st T20I: When and where to watch New Zealand vs Australia?
New Zealand vs Australia 1st T20 Live Streaming in India: Mitchell Santner's team will host Mitchell Marsh-led Australia in three T20Is for the Chappell-Hadlee Trophy at home.
By: Sports Desk
Updated: February 21, 2024 12:11 IST
3 min read
NZ vs AUS Live Telecast in India: New Zealand will face Australia in the first T20I on Wednesday.

New Zealand and Australia will lock horns in a three-match T20I series for the Chappell-Hadlee Trophy, set to be played for the first-time in the shortest format.

The first T20I between the sides will take place at the Sky Stadium in Wellington on Wednesday.

Check full schedule of NZ vs AUS series

Mitchell Marsh will lead Australia despite the return of seamer Pat Cummins, while Mitchell Santner will captain New Zealand. Regular captain Kane Williamson will miss the series expecting the birth of his third child. New Zealand will also miss all-rounder Daryl Mitchell owing to an injury.
