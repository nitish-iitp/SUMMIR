The ICC Men’s T20 World Cup enters the Super Eight stage with an exciting clash between the USA and South Africa.

The Americans earned a ticket to the knockout stages after finishing in Group A’s second spot, with five points, two behind leaders India. Their opponents South Africa won all their matches to top Group C with eight points, two more than the second-placed Bangladesh.

The USA will try to keep making history in the tournament, as it’s the first time they make it to the event’s quarterfinals. Due to their achievement of progressing to the Super Eight, they will be able to feature in the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup in 2026.

In the Super Eight, the best eight teams are split into two pools of four and will battle in a round-robin format, where the American side will try to take one of the first two spots to advance to the semifinals.

What time does USA vs South Africa start?
