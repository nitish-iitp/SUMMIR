India vs Sri Lanka 2nd T20I 2023 Highlights
by
CricHighlightsVidz
Published on :
1/05/2023 10:55:00 PM
Watch the highlights of India vs Sri Lanka 2nd T20I 2023 - Sri Lanka tour of India 3-match Twenty20 international series of the 2nd T20I match played between Sri Lanka and India at Maharashtra Cricket Association Stadium, Pune in 05th January 2023.



Charith Asalanka and Dasun Shanaka helped take Sri Lanka past 200 © BCCI


Captain Dasun Shanaka's record-breaking 20-ball fastest fifty and Kusal Mendis' blazing half-century, Charith Asalanka's rapid 37 and a first-over double-wicket from Kasun Rajitha sets up to Sri Lanka comprehensive 16-run victory over India, despite a fighting half-centuries from Suryakumar Yadav - Axar Patel and level the series with 1-1 in a high-scoring game of the second T20I.


Match Stats : 


Kusal Mendis hammered a 27-ball his third fastest fifty for Sri Lanka in T20 international and the sixth fastest fifty by an Sri Lankan batsman against India in T20Is.
80 : Pathum Nissanka and Kusal Mendis set up the second highest first-wicket partnership for Sri Lanka against India in T20 international and the Sri Lanka'a fifth highest any-wicket stand against India in T20Is.
68* : Dasun Shanaka and Chamika Karunaratne set up the record seventh-wicket partnership for Sri Lanka against India in T20 international, previously held by the unbeaten 40-run stand between Charith Asalanka and Dushmantha Chameera at Lucknow in the last year and the Sri Lanka's second highest seventh-wicket stand in T20Is.
Sri Lanka's 206 was their second highest score against India in T20 international after their 215 at Nagpur in 2009 and It is their joint seventh highest total in T20Is.
Sri Lanka's 206 was the highest-score by any team at Pune in T20Is, previous highest was 201 by India against Sri Lanka in 2020.
6 : Dasun Shanaka equalled a joint second most sixes hit in an innings for Sri Lanka in T20 international, jointly with Thisara Perera, Isuru Udana and Bhanuka Rajapaksa and the second most-sixes hit by an Sri Lankan batsman against India in T20Is after Kusal Perera's 7-sixes at Indore in 2017.
Dasun Shanaka hammered a 20-ball fastest fifty by an Sri Lankan batsman in T20 international cricket history, surpassing the previous record of 21-ball half-century held by Mahela Jayawardene against Kenya at Johannesburg in the inaugural 2007 World Twenty20 and also the fastest fifty by an Sri Lankan batsman against India in T20Is, surpassing the previous record of 21-ball half-century held by Kumar Sangakkara at Nagpur in 2009.
Axar Patel equalled a 20-ball joint fifth fastest fifty for India in T20 international and the joint second fastest fifty by an Indian batsman against Sri Lanka in T20Is, jointly with Yuvraj Singh.
6 : Axar Patel equalled a joint ninth most sixes hit in an innings for India in T20 international and the joint third most-sixes hit by an Indian batsman against Sri Lanka in T20Is.
91 : Suryakumar Yadav and Axar Patel set up the record sixth-wicket partnership for India against Sri Lanka in T20 international, previously held by the 70-run stand between Virat Kohli and Hardik Pandya against England at Ahmedabad in 2021 and the India's seventh highest any-wicket stand against Sri Lanka in T20Is.
India's 190 was their sixth highest score against Sri Lanka in T20 internationals.


Sri Lanka scored 206-6 in 20 overs with top scorer by Dasun Shanaka hammered a unbeaten 56 off 22-balls.


Kusal Mendis blasted a 52 off 31-balls including 4-sixes & 3-fours with strike rate of 167.74, Charith Asalanka hammered a 37 off 19-balls included 4-sixes with strike-rate of 194.73 and Pathum Nissanka struck 33 off 35-balls including 4-fours.


India best bowler by Umran Malik picked up 3-wickets, Axar Patel took 2-wickets and one for Yuzvendra Chahal.


India were 57-5 to scored 190-8 in 20 overs with top scorer by Axar Patel hammered a 65 off 31-balls including 6-sixes & 3-fours with strike rate of 209.67.


Suryakumar Yadav blasted a 51 off 36-balls including 3-sixes & 3-fours with strike rate of 141.66, Shivam Mavi hammered a 26 off 15-balls included 2-sixes & 2-fours with strike rate of 173.33.


Sri Lanka best bowler by Dasun Shanaka, Kasun Rajitha, Dilshan Madushanka each took 2-wickets and one for Chamika Karunaratne - Wanindu Hasaranga.


Dasun Shanaka named Player of the match for his match-winning all-round performance to took 2-wickets in the final over of the match and after smashing a power-hitting unbeaten 56-runs knocked off facing 22-balls including 6-sixes and 2-fours with strike rate of 254.54.
