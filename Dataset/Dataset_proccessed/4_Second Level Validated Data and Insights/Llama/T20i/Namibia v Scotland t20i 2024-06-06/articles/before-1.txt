T20 World Cup 2024: List of venues for ICC T20 WC in USA, West Indies; Stadiums, List of matches
Here are the venues being utilised for the 2024 ICC T20 World Cup and the list of matches that will be played in each stadium.

REPRESENTATIVE IMAGE: The ICC men’s Twenty20 World Cup 2024 will be played in the United States and the Caribbean from June 1 to June 29. | Photo Credit: AFP

The 2024 edition of the ICC T20 World Cup is set to begin in the West Indies and USA from June 1, 2024 with host United States taking on Canada at Dallas, Texas.

The Group Stage fixtures will shuttle between the West Indies and USA before the caravan moves solely to the Windies for the Super Eight fixtures and beyond.

Here are the venues being utilised for the 2024 ICC T20 World Cup:
