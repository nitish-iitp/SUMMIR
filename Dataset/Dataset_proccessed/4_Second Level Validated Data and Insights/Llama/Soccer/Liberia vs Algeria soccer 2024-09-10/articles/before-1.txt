Breaking!
Ryan Ayte Nori and Mohamed Amin Amoura are out of the Algeria squad to face Liberia on September 10, 2024 in the AFCON qualifiers.
The two Algerian professional footballers pick-up straight injuries today and the team's medical staff has decided to allow the players to stay and recover without risking their health. 
As a result, Nori and Amoura will return to their respective clubs starting on Saturday. The rest of the team will continue their preparations at the Sidi Musa National Technical Center before departing for Liberia on Sunday at 14:30.
