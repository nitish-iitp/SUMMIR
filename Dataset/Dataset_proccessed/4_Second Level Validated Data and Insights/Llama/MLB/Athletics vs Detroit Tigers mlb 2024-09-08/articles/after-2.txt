Tigers rout Athletics in series finale, gain ground in playoff chase
Updated: Sep. 08, 2024, 7:49 p.m.|Published: Sep. 08, 2024, 7:19 p.m.

Detroit Tigers' Trey Sweeney celebrates after hitting a home run during the third inning of a baseball game against the Oakland Athletics in Oakland, Calif., Sunday, Sept. 8, 2024. (AP Photo/Jed Jacobsohn)AP

By Evan Woodbery | ewoodber@mlive.com

After four consecutive one-run games, the Detroit Tigers ensured that Sunday’s series finale in Oakland wasn’t a nail-biter.

The Tigers erupted for six runs in the eighth inning to beat the Athletics 9-1 at Oakland Coliseum.
