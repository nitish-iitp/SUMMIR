Tigers get 2 big blasts, take series from Red Sox
Updated: Sep. 02, 2024, 10:03 a.m.|Published: Sep. 01, 2024, 4:03 p.m.

Detroit Tigers' Spencer Torkelson celebrates his two-run home run against the Boston Red Sox in the fifth inning of a baseball game, Sunday, Sept. 1, 2024, in Detroit. (AP Photo/Paul Sancya)AP

By Evan Woodbery | ewoodber@mlive.com

DETROIT -- Spencer Torkelson and Riley Greene hit two-run home runs in consecutive innings to lead the Detroit Tigers to a 4-1 win over the Boston Red Sox on Sunday afternoon at Comerica Park.

The Tigers (70-68) took two of three from the Red Sox (70-67) and finished their homestand 4-2.
