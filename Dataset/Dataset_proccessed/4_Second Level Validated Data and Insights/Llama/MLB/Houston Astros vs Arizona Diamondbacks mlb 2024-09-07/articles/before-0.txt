BOTTOM LINE: The Houston Astros open a three-game series at home against the Arizona Diamondbacks on Friday.

Houston is 39-29 at home and 75-65 overall. The Astros have the third-ranked team on-base percentage in the AL at .320.

Arizona has gone 40-31 on the road and 79-62 overall. The Diamondbacks are 41-15 in games when they hit at least two home runs.

The teams meet Friday for the first time this season.

TOP PERFORMERS: Yordan Alvarez has 30 home runs, 62 walks and 73 RBI while hitting .309 for the Astros. Mauricio Dubon is 10-for-28 with three RBI over the last 10 games.
