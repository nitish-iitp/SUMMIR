Cleveland Guardains vs. Kansas City Royals, Sept. 3, 2024
By Joe Noga, cleveland.com

KANSAS CITY, Mo. — Ben Lively and the Guardians face the Royals for the final time in the 2024 regular season.

Here are the starting lineups for Wednesday’s game between the Guardians and Kansas City.

Where: Kauffman Stadium, 7:40 p.m. ET.

TV/radio: Bally Sports Great Lakes, WTAM 1100 AM, WMMS 100.7 FM, and the Guardians Radio Network will carry the game.

Teams: Guardians (80-59) vs. Royals (75-65).

Starting pitchers: RHP Ben Lively (11-8, 3.92) vs. RHP Seth Lugo (14-8, 3.12).

Guardians Magic Number: 20.
