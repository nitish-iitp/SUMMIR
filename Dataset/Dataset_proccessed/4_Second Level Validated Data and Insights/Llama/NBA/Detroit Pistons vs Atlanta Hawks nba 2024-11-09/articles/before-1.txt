Atlanta Hawks (4-5, fifth in the Eastern Conference) vs. Detroit Pistons (3-6, 12th in the Eastern Conference)

Detroit; Friday, 7 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Eastern Conference foes Detroit and Atlanta will play.

Detroit finished 14-68 overall, 10-41 in Eastern Conference play and 7-33 at home during the 2023-24 season. The Pistons averaged 109.9 points per game while shooting 46.3% from the field and 34.8% from behind the arc last season.
