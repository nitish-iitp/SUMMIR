Utah Jazz (0-5, 15th in the Western Conference) vs. Denver Nuggets (2-2, 10th in the Western Conference)

Denver; Saturday, 10 p.m. EDT

BOTTOM LINE: Western Conference foes Denver and Utah will play on Saturday.

Denver went 57-25 overall, 10-6 in Northwest Division play and 33-8 at home a season ago. The Nuggets averaged 7.1 steals, 5.6 blocks and 11.8 turnovers per game last season.
