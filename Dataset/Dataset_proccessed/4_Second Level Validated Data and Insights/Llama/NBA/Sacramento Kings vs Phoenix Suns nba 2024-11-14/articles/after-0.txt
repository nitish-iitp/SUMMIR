What's wrong with Devin Booker, and how concerned should Suns be over his slow start?
GERALD BOURGUET
NOVEMBER 14, 2024
November 13, 2024; Sacramento, California, USA; Phoenix Suns guard Devin Booker (1) shoots the basketball against Sacramento Kings forward Keegan Murray (13) during the third quarter at Golden 1 Center. Mandatory Credit: Kyle Terada-Imagn Images

It feels strange to nitpick a team that's 9-3 after such its opening-month gauntlet, but there's no denying that Devin Booker hasn't looked like himself so far this season.
Whether it's a few memorable clunkers, perpetual angst over whether or not he's wearing a headband, or simply the fanbase's repeated glances over their shoulders at what other shooting guards like Donovan Mitchell and Anthony Edwards are doing, Book's start to the 2024-25 campaign has underwhelmed.
