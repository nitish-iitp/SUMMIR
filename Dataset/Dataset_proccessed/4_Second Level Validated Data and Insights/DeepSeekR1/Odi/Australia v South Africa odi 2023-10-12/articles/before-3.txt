Australia vs South Africa match in ODI Cricket World Cup 2023: TV channel, telecast and live stream details

RAHUL IYER

11 October, 2023
•
8:57 pm UTC

Australia will take on South Africa in their second match of the 2023 Cricket World Cup in Lucknow on Thursday.

Australia were defeated in their first game of the tournament by hosts India in Chennai last Sunday. After being bowled out for 199, they put India on the back foot, reducing them to 2/3 before Virat Kohli and KL Rahul played superbly to chase down the target in 41.2 overs.

MORE: Full Cricket World Cup 2023 fixtures & results | Bet on CWC 2023 matches here!

South Africa put in a stunning batting performance against Sri Lanka in their first match, smashing 428/5 in Delhi thanks to centuries from Quinton de Kock, Rassie van der Dussen and Aiden Markram before securing a 102-run win.

When is AUS vs SA CWC 2023 match?

The ICC World Cup 2023 game between Australia and South Africa will be played on Thursday, October 12, 2023. 

Where is AUS vs SA being played?

The match between Australia and South Africa will be played at the Arun Jaitley Stadium in Delhi. This will be the second match at the venue so far at the ICC ODI World Cup.

What time does AUS vs SA start?

The game between Australia and South Africa is scheduled to kickstart at 14:00 (IST) (+5:30 GMT).

Which TV channel in India will telecast AUS vs SA?

Star Sports Network has acquired the rights to broadcast the ICC ODI World Cup 2023. The Australia vs South Africa match of the ODI World Cup 2023 will be broadcasted live in multiple languages (English, Hindi, and Vernacular) across the Star Sports Network. 

Country	TV channels
India	Star Sports 1, Star Sports 1 HD, Star Sports 2, Star Sports 2 HD, Star Sports 1 Hindi HD, Star Sports 1 Kannada HD, Star Sports 1 Tamil HD, Star Sports 1 Telugu HD, Star Sports Select 2 HD (English)

Where will AUS vs SA be live streamed?

The live streaming of the match between Australia and South Africa will be on Disney+ Hotstar. All the mobile users will be able to view the World Cup clash involving the hosts for free of cost on the Disney+ Hotstar mobile app.

Country	Live stream
India	Disney+ Hotstar
