Pakistan Vs South Africa 2021-1st T20: Pak Defeat SA
10/04/2021
	

Match Basics 1st T20:  

Date: 10th April, 2021 – Venue: The Wanderers Stadium, Johannesburg, South Africa

Field Umpires: Adrian Holdstock and Allahudien Paleker

Toss won by: South Africa, decided to bat

Result: Pakistan Won By 4 Wickets – ODI Series Score: PAK 1 – 0 SA

South Africa Innings:  188 – 6 Wickets (20 Overs, RR: 9.40)


Performers: 

Batting: Aiden Markram 51; Heinrich Klaasen 50

Bowling: Mohammad Nawaz 2 Wickets in 21 Runs; Hasan Ali 2 Wickets in 28 Runs.

Target: 189 Runs from 20 Overs.

Pakistan Innings: 189 – 6 Wickets (19.5 Overs, RR: 9.52)

Performers: 

Batting: Muhammad Rizwan 74 Not Out.

Bowling: Beuran Hendricks 3 Wickets in 32 Runs; Tabraiz Shamsi 2 Wickets in 29 Runs
