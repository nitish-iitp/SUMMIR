Washington Sundar joins MS Dhoni in special T20I club

India lost the first T20I against New Zealand by 21 runs in Ranchi despite Washington Sundar's all-round show.

Sundar grabbed two wickets and then followed it up with a lightning 50 off 28 to storm into MS Dhoni's elite club of T20Is.

Let's have a look at the list of Indian batters who have scored 50-plus runs batting at no.6 or below in T20Is.

India's star all-rounder Axar Patel clobbered 65 off 31 against Sri Lanka in 2023.

India's veteran batter Dinesh Karthik cracked 55 off 27 against South Africa in 2022.

The legendary MS Dhoni whacked 52 off 28 against South Africa in 2018.

India's star batter Manish Pandey smashed 50 off 36 against New Zealand in 2020.

India's star all-rounder Washington Sundar clobbered 50 off 28 against New Zealand in the first T20I on January 27, 2023
