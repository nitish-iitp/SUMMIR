India vs New Zealand, 3rd T20I, New Zealand tour of India, 2023 - India Cricket team will play 3 T20 matches against New Zealand Cricket Team, where the 3rd T20 will be played on Wednesday, February 01, 2023. The IND vs NZ, 3rd T20I match will be played at Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad, and will get started at 07:00 PM LOCAL.

The toss of the match will be done 30 minutes before the start of the match at 06:30 PM. The Indian team will be captained by Hardik Pandya in the match and the New Zealand team will be captained by Mitchell Santner.

Here are the full details of the India vs New Zealand 3rd T20 Match 2023 Timing, Captain, Squad or Players List.
