AFC Asian Cup 2023: Hong Kong vs Iran: Predicted lineup, injury news, head-to-head, telecast

January 17, 2024 at 3:59 PM
Modified at :

Samannay Sen

0
0
Iran are determined to secure their place in the AFC Asian Cup 2023 knockouts.

In a highly anticipated match at the AFC Asian Cup 2023, Hong Kong, China are set to take on football giants Iran on Thursday, January 19, 2024, in the second Group C clash. The stakes will be high for Hong Kong, China as they will try to fight back against a much superior opposition – Iran. On the contrary, Iran will look to seal their RO16 qualifications with an impressive win over Hong Kong, China. As both teams gear for this fixture, excitement rises among both players as well as the fans.

Kick-Off:

Friday, 19 January 2024 at 11:30 GMT

Location: Education City Stadium (Al Rayyan)

Form

Hong Kong, China (in all competitions): L-L-D-L-L

Iran (in all competitions): W-D-W-W-W

Players to Watch
Mehdi Taremi (Iran)
Hong Kong should tightly mark Taremi else he might cause mayhem. (Courtesy: AFC)

Taremi gained significant acclaim within the football community for his instrumental role in guiding Portuguese club Porto to last season’s league title, where he scored an impressive 22 goals – the highest in the league. His goal-scoring ability and his attacking mindset are ones to look out for. His outstanding performance has positioned him as a crucial player for Iran in the upcoming AFC Asian Cup clash.

Everton Camargo (Hong Kong, China)
Everton is carrying the hopes of the Hong Kong fans on his shoulder. (Courtesy: AFC)

The forward, originally from Brazil, has made a mere four appearances for his recently joined national side. However, in this brief period, the 32-year-old has introduced an element of speed and strength to the right flank of the lineup.

Known for his robust build, the winger began garnering attention for his quick pace and direct running, consistently challenging opposition defenses in the Hong Kong, China Premier League. He will look to score for his team in their upcoming clash against Iran.

Match Facts
Hong Kong, China haven’t earned a win in their last six games.
Iran emerged victorious in the two sides’ previous meeting.
Injury and Team News

There is no news of injury in the Hong Kong, China squad whereas Iran will still miss the likes of Ali Gholizadeh who is injured.

Head-to-Head

Total Matches – 3

Hong Kong, China wins – 0

Iran wins – 3

Draws – 0

Predicted Lineup
Hong Kong (4-3-3):

Yapp (GK), Law, Gerbig, Li, Sun, Lam, Chan, Tan, Camargo, Orr, Udebuluzor

Iran (4-4-2):

H Hosseini (GK), Rezeaian, Pouraliganji, Hosseini, Mohammadi, Gholizadeh, Nourollahi, Ezatollahi, Hajsafi, Azmoun, Taremi

Match Prediction for Hong Kong vs Iran

With a 90% likelihood of securing victory, Iran enter this match as the superior and more experienced side who will look to grab their second victory of the AFC Asian Cup 2023 campaign.

Prediction: Hong Kong, China 0-3 Iran
