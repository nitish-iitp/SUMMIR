The Three Lions will face Greece in their third fixture of the UEFA Nations League on 10 October 2024. England vs Greece tickets are currently on sale on the official Wembley website.

If you support the Three Lions, this post provides all the information you need ahead of the England vs Greece matchup. It will be the second England game at Wembley in the 2024 competition, so we expect massive attendance in support of Lee Carsley's men.

Lee Carsley is the new head coach for the Three Lions. The first major assignment for the Irishman will be in the UEFA Nations League—photo by Icon Sport.
England vs Greece tickets – sale dates

Contents [show]

The first phase of ticket sales for this match began on 13 August 2024 and will conclude on 21 August, targeting the England Supporters Travel Club. You are advised to join the supporters' club for priority ticketing for home and away England matches. You can join the supporters club by visiting their official website, where you will be required to register and pay requisite fees.

Moreover, members of My England Football are also eligible for priority ticketing. Noting that the previous membership for the England Supporters Club had ended as the Three Lions prepare for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, fans are advised to apply for admission into a new cycle.

Membership approval takes up to 24 hours. Most importantly, those rejoining the England Supporters Travel Club in the new membership cycle will get a discounted admission fee valid until 12 September 2024.

The second phase of the sales for England vs Greece tickets will commence on 22 August and run through to 27 August 2024, targeting My England Football Club members.

Finally, the general public sale for tickets to England vs Greece is scheduled for 28th August 2024, starting at noon.

England vs Greece tickets – prices

Ticket prices for England vs Greece vary depending on the seating category and age. The highest price in the general category is £80, while the cheapest seat is £35.

The prices for adults and children aged 16 and below in the family encloser are £25 and £12.50, respectively. Moreover, students, OAPSs and U16s are eligible for a £10 discount.

There are only a limited number of England vs Greece tickets in the level two category, with prices ranging between £95 and £120. Note that members of the England Supporters Travel Club and My England Football pay a special price of £25 for the clash with Greece.

Moreover, travel club members who have booked seats in Wembley's other areas ahead of this clash with Greece will receive £5 off for seniors and £2.50 for juniors. Note that the offer is valid through the first and second ticket sale windows.

Ticket information for away supporters

Greece supporters travelling to England to watch this clash with the Three Lions on 10 October 2024 can visit the official Wembley site for directions on how to book seats. Alternatively, visit epo.gr for a more comprehensive booking guide. Note that the tickets for England vs Greece are only available online.

England vs Greece – travel guide

If you are travelling to Wembley for the match between England and Greece, please note that the venue is most conveniently accessed via public transport. You can use the train, bus, tube, rail, or pedestrian routes.

Wembley Stadium is served by three stations: Wembley Park Station, Wembley Stadium Station, and National Express. For directions to the respective parking stations, please visit their websites.

If you need live travel support on matchday, please call the Wembley Stadium Travel Information Center at 0845 6000 555. Note that standard local rates apply for the calls.

If driving to Wembley on October 10, 2024, please use the stadium parking lot. Make sure to reserve your parking space via wembleyofficialparking.com. Please note that Brent Council will fine illegally parked vehicles on matchday and, in the worst-case scenario, tow them away.
