Coby White Calls Out ‘Embarrassing’ Effort After Loss to Rockets
WILL GOTTLIEB
NOVEMBER 18, 2024
Nov 17, 2024; Chicago, Illinois, USA; Houston Rockets forward Dillon Brooks (9) drives tot he basket against Chicago Bulls guard Coby White (0) and guard Zach LaVine (8) during the first half at United Center. Mandatory Credit: Matt Marton-Imagn Images

Coby White did not mince words following the Chicago Bulls 143-107 loss to the upstart Houston Rockets:
"It's a compete thing. We need to compete at a higher level. We need to be more physical. We need to take a stance. Giving up 140-plus in back-to-back games is unacceptable. It's embarrassing. It's a disservice to the organization, a disservice to the fanbase."
"The fans come to watch us play, they support us," he went on. "Obviously it's a historic organization. You've got to compete. Giving up 143 points, any player in the NBA in any organization would say it's a disservice to their organization and fanbase."
