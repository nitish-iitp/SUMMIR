NBA: Second-quarter burst helps Rockets past Mavericks
Reuters / 03:16 PM January 02, 2025

Houston Rockets forward Cam Whitmore (7) and center Alperen Sengun (28) attempt to steal the ball from Dallas Mavericks guard Kyrie Irving (11) during the second quarter of an NBA game at Toyota Center. Mandatory Credit: Troy Taormina-Imagn Images

Cam Whitmore scored 18 points off the bench and teamed with Alperen Sengun to lead a decisive second-quarter run that helped the Houston Rockets to a 110-99 victory over the visiting Dallas Mavericks in the NBA on Wednesday.

Sengun tallied 23 points, six rebounds, four assists and five steals as Houston rebounded after dropping the first two games of a five-game homestand. Momentum flipped in the second quarter when Whitmore and Sengun combined to score 20 points during a 23-4 run that turned a 10-point deficit into a 53-44 lead.
