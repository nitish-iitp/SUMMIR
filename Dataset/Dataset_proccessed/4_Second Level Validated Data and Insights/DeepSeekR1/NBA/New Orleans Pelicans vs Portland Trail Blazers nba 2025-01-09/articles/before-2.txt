Injury Report: Zion Williamson out for back-to-back vs. Portland
Trey Murphy (ankle) will also be sidelined Wednesday against the Trail Blazers
Jim Eichenhofer
January 8, 20251:02 PM CST

After making his return from a hamstring injury Tuesday against Minnesota and scoring 22 points in just 28 minutes, Zion Williamson is listed as out for Wednesday’s second game of a back-to-back against Portland (7 p.m., GCSEN on TV, WWL on radio) due to return to competition reconditioning. Williamson is joined by Brandon Ingram (left ankle sprain) and Trey Murphy (left ankle sprain) as out for the mid-week Western Conference matchup.

Dejounte Murray (right elbow soreness/right shin contusion) is questionable for the Pelicans.

For Portland, Jerami Grant (face contusion) and Matisse Thybulle (right ankle) are out. Deni Avdija (right wrist sprain) and Donovan Clingan (rest) are questionable.
