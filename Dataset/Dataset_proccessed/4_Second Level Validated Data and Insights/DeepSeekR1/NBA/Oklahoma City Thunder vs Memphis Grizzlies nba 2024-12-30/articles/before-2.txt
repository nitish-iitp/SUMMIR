Memphis Grizzlies (22-10, second in the Western Conference) vs. Oklahoma City Thunder (25-5, first in the Western Conference)

Oklahoma City; Sunday, 7 p.m. EST

BETMGM SPORTSBOOK LINE: Thunder -7.5; over/under is 228

BOTTOM LINE: Oklahoma City is looking to continue its five-game win streak with a victory against Memphis.

The Thunder are 16-5 in conference play. Oklahoma City ranks seventh in the Western Conference with 33.8 defensive rebounds per game led by Isaiah Hartenstein averaging 9.9.

The Grizzlies are 9-7 in Western Conference play. Memphis ranks sixth in the NBA with 12.7 offensive rebounds per game led by Zach Edey averaging 3.7.

The Thunder make 46.4% of their shots from the field this season, which is 2.5 percentage points higher than the Grizzlies have allowed to their opponents (43.9%). The Grizzlies average 14.2 made 3-pointers per game this season, 1.6 more made shots on average than the 12.6 per game the Thunder give up.

TOP PERFORMERS: Shai Gilgeous-Alexander is averaging 30.8 points, 5.6 rebounds, 6.1 assists and two steals for the Thunder.

Santi Aldama is averaging 13 points and 7.3 rebounds for the Grizzlies.
