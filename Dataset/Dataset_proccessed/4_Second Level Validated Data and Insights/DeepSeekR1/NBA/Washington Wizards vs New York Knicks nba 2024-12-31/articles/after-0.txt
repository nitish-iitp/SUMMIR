New York Knicks Beat Washington Wizards 126-106 To Claim Eighth NBA Consecutive Victory

The New York Knicks continued their strong form with a 126-106 victory over the Washington Wizards on Monday. Josh Hart recorded a triple-double, and Karl-Anthony Towns added 32 points and 13 rebounds. A key 13-5 spurt in the third quarter helped push the Knicks' lead to 97-88, and then Towns' 11-point effort in the fourth quarter sealed the win. This marks the eighth consecutive victory for New York.
