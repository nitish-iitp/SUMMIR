Suns - Jazz Series Notes
The Suns won the first two matchups of the season with the Jazz, both coming in Utah on Nov. 12 and Dec. 13
The Suns have won eight-straight games over the Jazz, Phoenix's second-longest winning streak over Utah in series history (trails only an 11-game winning streak over Utah spanning Nov. 6, 1979- March 29, 1981)
The Suns swept Utah last season, the first time doing so since a 3-0 record against Utah during the 2020-21 season
The Suns have won 15 of the last 17 meetings and lead the all-time series 107-95
On Feb. 24, 2020, the Suns won by 20 to snap an 8-game losing streak in the series
On March 25, 2019, Devin Booker set the now Delta Arena scoring record with 59 points in Utah
Suns - Jazz Key Connections
Suns G Grayson Allen played his rookie season with the Jazz in 2018-19 after Utah selected him with the 21st overall pick in the 2018 NBA Draft
Jazz F Lauri Markkanen played his college basketball at the University of Arizona

Notes
The Suns improved to 17-19 on the season following a 123-115 win over the Hawks Thursday night in Phoenix
With the win on Jan. 6 in Philadelphia, Mike Budenholzer won his 500th career regular season game as an NBA head coach, becoming the 38th coach in league history to the milestone
Coming off the bench for the first time since March 4, 2016, Bradley Beal notched 25 points on 10-of-15 shooting and 5 assists on Jan. 6, 2025 at Philadelphia
Devin Booker has recorded a double-double in each of the last three games, tying his career-long streak of consecutive double-doubles at 3
Devin Booker (34 points, 8 assists) and Kevin Durant (30 points, 8 assists) became the first Suns duo since the 1991-92 season (Jeff Hornacek & Dan Majerle) to record 30+ points and 8+ assists in the same game on Dec.13, 2024 at Utah
With his first made basket early in the first quarter on Dec. 3 against the Spurs, Devin Booker became the 2nd player in franchise history to score 15,000 career points in a Suns jersey (Walter Davis) and became the 9th-youngest player in NBA history to reach the milestone
Bradley Beal and Grayson Allen each scored 20+ points off the bench on Jan. 9 vs. ATL, the first set of Suns teammates to each score 20+ points off the bench in the same game since Jan. 16, 2022
The Suns have made 505 three-pointers in the first 35 games of this season, the most in franchise history in the first 35 games of a season
With his 21st point of the night on Oct. 26 against the Mavericks, Kevin Durant became the eighth player in NBA history to reach 29,000 career points
Devin Booker needs 381 more points to surpass Walter Davis as the Suns all-time leading scorer
The Suns enter this season having reached the playoffs in each of the last four seasons, matching their longest such stretch (four-straight from 2005-2008) since doing so in 13-straight from 1989-2001
Since the start of the 2020-21 season, the Suns hold a combined regular season record 226-128, the second-best record in the NBA over the last five seasons (trails Boston)
Last Game Recap

PHOENIX– The Suns (17-19) topped the Hawks (19-19) on Jan. 10, 123-115. Tied 85-85 with 3:48 left in the third quarter, the Suns used an 11-3 run to go up eight (96-88) with 1:10 to go in the quarter. Phoenix never trailed the rest of the way and extended their lead up to as many as 15 in the fourth. The Suns shot 57.6% in the win, while limiting the Hawks to a 48.8% mark. The Suns have now won 14-straight games when shooting 57+% from the field and holding their opponent to under 50% shooting and improved to 166-6 all-time in such games. Bradley Beal posted a game-high 25 points on 11-of-16 shooting (2-of-5 3FG), 7 rebounds and 3 steals. Kevin Durant finished with 23 points on 9-of-15 shooting from the field (3-for-4 FT), to go along with 7 assists and 3 blocks. Devin Booker tallied 20 points and matched his season high for assists with a game-high 12. Recording a double-double, the game marked his sixth double-double of the season, his 60th all-time and his third-straight. Grayson Allen had a season-high 23-points on 7-of-8 shooting (5-of-6 3FG) in 27 minutes off the bench. With Booker, Durant, Beal and Allen each scoring 20+ points, the game marked the first time since March 14, 2024 that at least four Suns players scored 20+ points in the same game.
