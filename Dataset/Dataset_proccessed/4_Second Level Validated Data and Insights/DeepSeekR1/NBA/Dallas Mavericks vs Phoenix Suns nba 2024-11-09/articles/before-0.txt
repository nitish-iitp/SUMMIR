Phoenix Suns (7-1, first in the Western Conference) vs. Dallas Mavericks (5-3, fifth in the Western Conference)

Dallas; Friday, 7:30 p.m. EST

BOTTOM LINE: Phoenix is looking to keep its six-game win streak intact when the Suns take on Dallas.

Dallas went 50-32 overall and 31-21 in Western Conference play last season. The Mavericks averaged 117.9 points per game while shooting 48.1% from the field and 36.9% from 3-point range last season.
