The Chicago Cubs and Washington Nationals will meet on Sunday at Nationals Park, at 1:35 p.m. ET, with Nico Hoerner and Luis Garcia among those expected to deliver at the plate.

The Cubs have the ninth-ranked team ERA among all MLB pitching staffs (3.82). The Nationals rank 29th in Major League Baseball with just 114 home runs as a team.
