Date: Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Location: American Family Field in Milwaukee, WI

TV: Bally Sports Wisconsin

Odds/Point Spread: San Francisco (-108) Milwaukee (-112)

American Family Field is the location where the Milwaukee Brewers (75-55) will attempt to defeat the San Francisco Giants (66-66) on Wednesday. The moneyline on this contest has San Francisco at -108 and Milwaukee is priced at -112. The total is set at O8.0. The pitchers taking the mound will be Kyle Harrison and Freddy Peralta.
