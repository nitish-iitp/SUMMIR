Sanchez takes the hill for the Phillies in his 32nd of the season. He is 11-9 with a 3.32 ERA and 153 Ks through 181 2/3 innings pitched.
When he last took the hill on Wednesday, Sept. 25 versus the Chicago Cubs, the lefty tossed 4 2/3 innings, giving up three earned runs while surrendering five hits.
Among qualifying hurlers this year, the 27-year-old ranks 18th in ERA (3.32), 42nd in WHIP (1.244), and 45th in K/9 rate (7.6).
