Match Preview

In a thrilling encounter, the United Arab Emirates edged out Namibia by one wicket in Match 29 of the ICC CWC League 2. Namibia batted first, posting a challenging total of 313 runs in 50 overs. Ali Naseer stood out with the ball for the UAE, taking 3 wickets for 57 runs in his 10 overs. Vishnu Sukumaran and Muhammad Jawad Ullah contributed by claiming one wicket each. The UAE responded fiercely, chasing down the target with just three balls remaining, finishing at 316/9 in 49.3 overs. Vishnu Sukumaran led the way with a brilliant 97 runs off 90 balls. Basil Hameed added a quick 71 from 47 deliveries, while Ali Naseer finished the match with an unbeaten 38 off 21 balls, securing a memorable win for his team.

In Match 31, the United States defeated Namibia convincingly by 7 wickets. Namibia set a target of 287 runs, scoring 286/9 in their allotted 50 overs. Shadley van Schalkwyk was the standout bowler for the USA, taking 4 wickets for 59 runs. In response, the USA chased down the target in 45 overs, ending at 287/3. Andries Gous anchored the innings with 81 runs, while Saiteja Mukkamalla and Milind Kumar contributed with 79 and 74 runs, respectively. Their strong performances ensured a dominant victory, showcasing the team’s batting depth and effective bowling.
