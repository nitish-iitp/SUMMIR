NZ vs BAN Dream11 Prediction 11th Match World Cup 2023 | New Zealand vs Bangladesh Dream11 Team, Head To Head Records, Playing XI, Pitch Report: On 13 October 2023, New Zealand and Bangladesh are going to play the 11th match of the ODI World Cup 2023 at MA Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai. The New Zealand vs Bangladesh match will start at 2:00 PM (IST). 

New Zealand has played wonderful cricket in this tournament so far, and they are expected to do so in the upcoming matches as well. The all-round performance of the New Zealand players led their team to a strong position. They are present in first position in the latest World Cup 2023 Points Table.

Bangladesh needs to play consistent cricket to get a better position in the ICC Men's ODI World Cup 2023. The Key players are not performing well in this tournament, which might put them in trouble in the upcoming matches.

The Pitch of MA Chidambaram Stadium is good for bowlers, whereas batters can also play good innings at this venue. Generally, Spinners dominate the game in Chepauk, especially in the second inning when the pitch gets drier. We can expect a low-scoring match between both the teams.
