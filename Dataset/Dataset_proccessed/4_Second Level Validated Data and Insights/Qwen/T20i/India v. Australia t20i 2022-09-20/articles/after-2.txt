India vs Australia 1st T20I 2022 Highlights
by
CricHighlightsVidz
Published on :
9/20/2022 10:44:00 PM
Watch the highlights of India vs Australia 1st T20I 2022 - Australia tour of India 3-match Twenty20 international series of the 1st T20I match played between Australia and India at Punjab Cricket Association IS Bindra Stadium, Mohali in 20th September 2022.



Cameron Green uses the sweep to counter India's spinners © Getty Images


Nathan Ellis and Josh Hazlewood's combined 5-wickets before An blazing maiden half-century from Cameron Green and Matthew Wade's quick-fire 21-ball 45* steers to Australia's record four-wicket victory over India and take a 1-0 lead, despite a half-centuries from Hardik Pandya - KL Rahul in a high-scoring game of the first T20I.


Match Stats : 


Australia chased 209 was their second highest successful run-chase in T20 international and It is their highest chase against India in T20Is, previous highest chase was 191 at Bengaluru in 2019.
India's 208 was their highest score against Australia in T20 international, previous highest was 202 at Rajkot in 2013.
India's 208 was their seventh highest score at their home venue in T20 international and It is their second highest total at Mohali in T20Is after their 211 against Sri Lanka in 2009.
Hardik Pandya hammered a 25-ball his fastest fifty for India in T20 international and the joint second fastest fifty by an Indian batsman against Australia in T20Is, jointly with Yuvraj Singh.
68 : KL Rahul and Suryakumar Yadav set up the third highest third-wicket partnership for India against Australia in T20 Internationals.
70 : Cameron Green and Steve Smith set up the record second-wicket partnership for Australia against India in T20 international, previously held by the 65-run stand between Matthew Wade and Steve Smith at Sydney in 2020.
Cameron Green hammered a 26-ball second fastest fifty by an Australian batsman against India in T20Is after Matthew Wade's 25-ball half-century at Sydney in 2020.
India's 13-sixes was their joint fourth most sixes in an innings in T20 international and their joint most sixes in an innings against Australia in T20Is, jointly their 13-sixes at Bengaluru in 2019.


India scored 208-7 in 20 overs with top scorer by Hardik Pandya hammered a unbeaten 71 off 30-balls including 7-fours & 5-sixes with strike rate of 236.66.


KL Rahul blasted a 54 off 35-balls including 4-fours & 3-sixes with strike rate of 157.14, Suryakumar Yadav hammered a 46 off 25-balls included 4-sixes & 2-fours with strike rate of 184.


Australia best bowler by Nathan Ellis picked up 3-wickets for 30-runs in 4-overs, Josh Hazlewood took 2-wickets and one for Cameron Green.




Australia chased 211-6 in 19.2 overs with top scorer by Cameron Green hammered a career-best 61 off 30-balls.


Matthew Wade blasted a unbeaten 45 off 21-balls including 6-fours & 2-sixes with strike rate of 214.28, Steve Smith struck 35 off 24-balls included 3-fours & a six with strike rate of 145.83.


Aaron Finch hit 22 off 13-balls including 3-fours & a six with strike rate of 169.23, Tim David 18 and Josh Inglis 17.


India best bowler by Axar Patel picked up 3-wickets for 17-runs in 4-overs, Umesh Yadav took 2-wickets and one for Yuzvendra Chahal.


Cameron Green named Player of the match for his match-winning career-best blazing 61-runs knocked off facing 30-balls including 8-fours & 4-sixes with strike rate of 203.33.
