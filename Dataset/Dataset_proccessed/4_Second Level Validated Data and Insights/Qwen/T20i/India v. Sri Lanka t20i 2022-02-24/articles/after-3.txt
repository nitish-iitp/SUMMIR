India vs Sri Lanka 1st T20I Highlights: Ishan Kishan, Shreyas Iyer Fifties Set Up 62-Run Win For India
IND vs SL 2022 1st T20I, Highlights: India beat Sri Lanka by 62 runs in the first T20I at the Ekana Sports City in Lucknow. With this win, the host take a 1-0 lead in the series.
NDTV Sports Desk
Updated:February 24, 2022 10:23 pm IST
Read Time: 1 min
1st T20I: India beat Sri Lanka by 62 runs to take a 1-0 lead in the series.
© BCCI

IND vs SL 2022 1st T20I, Highlights: India beat Sri Lanka by 62 runs in the first T20I at the Ekana Sports City in Lucknow. With this win, the host take a 1-0 lead in the series. For India, Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Venkatesh Iyer have taken two wickets each while Yuzvendra Chahal and Ravindra Jadeja have also scalped a wicket each. Chahal also went past Jasprit Bumrah to become India's leading wicket-taker in T20Is. Earlier, Ishan Kishan and Shreyas Iyer smashed fifties as India managed to post a mammoth total of 199 for two against Sri Lanka. Ishan led the charge with a brisk 89 before Shreyas powered India's innings with an unbeaten 57 off just 28 balls. Ishan and Rohit Sharma had added 111 runs for the opening wicket before the latter was dismissed on 44. Ishan and Shreyas then added 44 for the second wicket before the latter and Jadeja added 44 to help India's cause. Earlier, Sri Lanka won the toss and skipper Dasun Shanaka elected to bowl. (SCORECARD)
