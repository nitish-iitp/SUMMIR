USA CRICKET ANNOUNCES MEMBER EXCLUSIVE TICKETING BONANZA FOR ICC MEN’S T20 WORLD CUP 2024 HOME MATCHES
By USA Cricket MediaMay 30, 2024

May 29, 2024

USA Cricket is proud to unveil an exclusive ticketing offer for its members in anticipation of the highly anticipated ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2024, co-hosted by the United States of America and West Indies from June 1 to June 29, 2024.

In a bid to rally support and enthusiasm for Team USA’s maiden outing on home soil, USA Cricket members across the nation are invited to take advantage of a special limited-time offer. Fans can now secure tickets for three home matches at a remarkable 25% discount, fostering an atmosphere of unity and fervor in backing the national team’s historic journey in the tournament.

The featured home matches, scheduled to take place at two premier venues, are as follows:

USA vs. Canada: June 1, 2024, in Dallas, TX
USA vs. Pakistan: June 6, 2024, in Dallas, TX
USA vs. Ireland: June 14, 2024, in Lauderhill, FL

This initiative aims to encourage supporters to rally behind Team USA as they embark on their inaugural campaign at the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup, bolstering the spirit of cricket within the country and beyond.

How to Purchase?

Visit the official ticketing page.
Select the desired match.
Choose the number of tickets (up to 6 per match per account).
Enjoy a 25% discount upon checkout.

Join us in celebrating this historic moment for cricket in the United States and seize the opportunity to witness world-class cricketing action live from the stands.

For more information and to secure your tickets, please visit this website.

Let’s unite in support of Team USA’s quest for glory at the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2024!

ABOUT USA CRICKET

USA Cricket is the National Federation for cricket in the United States as recognized by the International Cricket Council. USA Cricket’s mission is to govern, regulate, develop and promote all forms of cricket at all levels in the United States, and to enable U.S. cricketers to achieve sustained competitive excellence and successfully compete in national and international competition. Through a diverse Board of Directors that has representation from most cricket stakeholder groups, USA Cricket will lead the sport to new heights by creating and administering value added programs to grow and develop the sport.
