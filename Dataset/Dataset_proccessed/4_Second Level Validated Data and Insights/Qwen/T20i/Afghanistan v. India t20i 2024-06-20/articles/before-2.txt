T20 World Cup 2024 Super 8 India Schedule: Dates, Venues, Timings, Live Streaming

Get ready for the Super Eight phase of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024! Find out India's schedule, venues, timings, and live streaming details for this exciting stage of the tournament.

AMIT DIWAN
EDITORIAL
UPDATED - 2024-06-17, 16:14 IST

As the group stage of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 comes to a close, the stage is set for the highly anticipated Super Eight phase. According to ICC reports, the top teams from Groups A, B, C, and D will battle it out for the coveted trophy in this crucial round.

T20 World Cup 2024 Super 8 India Schedule

The eight qualified teams will be split into two groups of four each. Each team will play three fixtures in this phase, with the top two from each group securing their spots in the semifinals. As per the ICC board, the Super Eight stage promises to be a thrilling spectacle for cricket fans worldwide.

After the group stage in the USA, India will now shift their focus to the West Indies for the Super Eight phase. All three of India's Super Eight games will be held in the Caribbean, starting with their opening match against Afghanistan on Thursday, Jun 20, 2024, as per Sportskeeda.

T20 World Cup 2024 Super 8 Groups
Group 1: India, Australia, Afghanistan, Bangladesh
Group 2: South Africa, West Indies, United States of America, England
T20 World Cup 2024 India's Super 8 Date, Venues, And Timings
June 20: India vs Afghanistan, Kensington Oval, Barbados [8:00 PM IST]
June 22: India vs Bangladesh/Netherlands, Sir Vivian Richards Stadium, Antigua [8:00 PM IST]
June 24: India vs Australia, Daren Sammy Cricket Ground, St Lucia [8:00 PM IST]
