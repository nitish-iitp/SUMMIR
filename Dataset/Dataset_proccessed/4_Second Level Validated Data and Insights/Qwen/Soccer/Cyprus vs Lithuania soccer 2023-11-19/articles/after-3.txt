We said: Cyprus 1-1 Lithuania
Lithuania picked up some good results in qualifying, but the majority came at home, and they have been notoriously poor travellers of late. Ketsbaia would likely be happy to just stem the losing run for Cyprus and could well do so in what is expected to be a very low-key affair, with both sides playing for nothing in the immediate future.
