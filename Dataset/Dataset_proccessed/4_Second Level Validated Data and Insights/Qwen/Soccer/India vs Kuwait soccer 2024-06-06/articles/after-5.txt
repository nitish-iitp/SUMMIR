India vs Kuwait Football Match Highlights, Fifa World Cup: IND were held by KUW in a 0-0 draw in Kolkata, on Thursday.

India vs Kuwait Football Match Highlights, Fifa World Cup:

India vs Kuwait FIFA World Cup Qualifier Highlights: It was Sunil Chhetri's retirement match as India and Kuwait played out a 0-0 draw in their FIFA World Cup qualifier, at the Salt Lake Stadium in Kolkata on Thursday. The hosts missed plenty of chances in the first-half, with Anwar Ali coming the closest. The second-half turned out to be a cagey affair, and both sides failed to break the deadlock. India will next face Qatar in the final fixture of this round and need a win to progress.
