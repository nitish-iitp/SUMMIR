FIFA WORLD CUP 2026 QUALIFIERS: Details for Comoros vs Ghana game confirmed
 by Isaac Ganyo Tamakloe  November 18, 2023 in Black Stars, Featured, FIFA World Cup Finals, World  0  0  0
Share on Facebook
Share on Twitter

The Federation of International Football Association (FIFA) and the Confederation of African has confirmed the details for Tuesday’s World Cup qualifier between Comoros and Ghana.

According to FIFA, the Matchday two World Cup qualifier will take place at Moroni stadium – a multi-use stadium in Comoros that is currently used mostly for athletics competitions.

Ghana commenced their 2026 World Cup qualifiers with a vital 1-0 win over Madagascar at the Baba Yara Stadium on Friday, November 17, while Comoros Islands beat the Central African Republic 4-2 in an entertaining encounter.

DETAILS FOR THE GAME:

COMPETITION: WORLD CUP 2026 QUALIFIERS

MATCH: COMOROS VS GHANA

VENUE: MORONI STADIUM

DATE: TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 21, 2023

KICK-OFF: 4:00PM (16:00GMT)

MATCH OFFICIALS:

REFEREE: BOUH ABDEL AZIZ – MAURITANIA

ASSISTANT I: DIBA HAMEDINE – MAURITANIA

ASSISTANT II: YOUSSEF MOHAMED MAHMOUD – MAURITANIA

FOURTH OFFICIAL: DIOU MOUSSA ALASSANE – MAURITANIA

REFEREES ASSESSOR: EFONG NZOLO JÉRÔME – GABON

MATCH COMMISSIONER: SHONGWE WILLIAM MAKINATI – ESWATINI

LAST MEETING BETWEEN COMOROS AND GHANA

The two teams last met in the 2021 Africa Cup of Nations in Cameroon where Comoros beat Ghana 3-2 in a Group C encounter on January 19, 2023.
