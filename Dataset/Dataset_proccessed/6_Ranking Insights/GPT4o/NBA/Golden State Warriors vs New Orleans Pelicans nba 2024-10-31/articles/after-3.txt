5 things to know about the Pelicans on Oct. 31, 2024
Pelicans.com
October 31, 20249:41 AM CDT

New Orleans (2-3) dropped a third consecutive game after their 2-0 start on Wednesday with a 104-89 loss at Golden State (4-1) falling in both games in the 'baseball series' road back-to-back against the Warriors.

While the Pelicans offense struggled, guard Jordan Hawkins put up his second 20+ point outing on the season as he was called into starting duty with injuries to guard/forward Herb Jones and guard CJ McCollum. Hawkins scored 23 points on the night. Check out highlights of his performance.

The Pelicans head back home to begin a four-game homestand on Friday, Nov. 1 with a matchup against the Indiana Pacers to kick it off. Get tickets for the Pelicans matchup against the Pacers. The Pacers are also 2-3 to start the season.

The injury report for that contest will release later in the day which will include the game status for Daniel Theis who exited the Pelicans game against the Warriors with a right ankle injury as well as the status of the rest of the Pelicans recovering from injuries.

The Pelicans game against the Pacers will be broadcast on the Gulf Coast Sports and Entertainment Network and WWL 870 AM. Check out more broadcast information and for those looking to stream it, be sure to check out Pelicans+.
