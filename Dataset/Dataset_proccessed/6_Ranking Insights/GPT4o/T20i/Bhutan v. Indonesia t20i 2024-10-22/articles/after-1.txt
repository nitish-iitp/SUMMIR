PREVIEW

The hosts, Bhutan (BHU), will take on Indonesia (INA) in the sixth game of the ongoing Bhutan T20I Quadrangular Series 2024 2024. Geluphu International Cricket Ground in Geluphu will host this fixture on October 22 at 1:15 PM (IST). Both teams are currently struggling at the bottom of the tournament’s points table.

Bhutan are currently placed in the tally’s third spot, winning and losing a game each in their two matches so far. They are coming off a 4-wicket win against Maldives in the last game following a 77-run defeat against Thailand. They will eye moving to the second spot, surpassing Maldives (MLD) with a win in this game against Indonesia (INA).

Speaking of Indonesia, they are enduring a poor run of form in the tournament, with two successive defeats in the two matches they have played. INA are coming off a 6-wicket defeat against Thailand (TL) following a DLS-inflicted 23-run defeat against Maldives. They will aim to win this game and get off the mark in the tournament.
