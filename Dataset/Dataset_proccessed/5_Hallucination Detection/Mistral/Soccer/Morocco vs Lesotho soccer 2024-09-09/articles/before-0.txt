AFCON AFCON 2025Q: QUEST FOR MOROCCO 2025 TICKET ENTER MATCHDAY 2
8 SEP 2024
Like & Share
 
Tweet
 
+1
 
 

48 national teams from across the continent will continue their qualifying campaigns on Sunday, September 8, in an effort to advance to the TotalEnergies Africa Cup of Nations, Morocco 2025.

With 24 scheduled matches, Matchday Two of the qualifications is still ongoing.

Teams are split into 12 groups to compete in a series of thrilling matchups.

Read Also:AFCON 2025 qualifiers: Gambia to face Tunisia as Matchday 2 fixtures kick off

The top two finishers in the eleven (11) groups that do not include Morocco, the tournament host, will qualify for the competition, making this a tight race.

The 35th edition of Africa’s greatest sports event, which will take place in the country of North Africa from December 21 to January 18, will be decided by the qualifiers, which will choose the 24 teams who will compete.
