BOTTOM LINE: The Texas Rangers host the Toronto Blue Jays trying to extend a three-game home winning streak.

Texas has a 72-79 record overall and a 42-34 record at home. The Rangers have a 43-11 record in games when they scored at least five runs.

Toronto has a 72-79 record overall and a 34-42 record on the road. The Blue Jays have a 52-15 record in games when they have more hits than their opponents.

Wednesday's game is the fifth time these teams square off this season. The Blue Jays hold a 3-1 advantage in the season series.
