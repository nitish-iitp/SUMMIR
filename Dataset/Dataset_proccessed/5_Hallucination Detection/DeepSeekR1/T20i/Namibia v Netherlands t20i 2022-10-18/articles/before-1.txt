The Namibia Cricket Team will take on the Netherlands in Match 5 of the ICC T20 World Cup 2022 Qualifiers on Tuesday, October 18 at the Simonds Stadium in Geelong. Both teams are heading into the match on the back of brilliant wins in their first match at the same venue.

Namibia pulled off a great performance to defeat Sri Lanka on Sunday by 55 runs. Batting first, Namibia put on 163 runs with the loss of seven wickets. Jan Frylinck and JJ Smit played crucial knocks lower down the order to take Namibia to a good total. In response, Sri Lanka was bowled out for 108 runs. David Wiese, Bernard Scholtz, Ben Shikongo, and Jan Frylinck picked two wickets each.
