Georgia vs. Czech Republic – Prediction and Betting Tips | Jun 22, 2024
June 21, 2024
Bigb

Both pointless at the bottom of Group F, Georgia and Czech Republic aim to get off the mark at Euro 2024 when they meet on Saturday evening. After both were beaten in their opening game, Hamburg hosts the nations’ first encounter in any competition – and neither can afford to lose again.

Detailed Analysis
Georgia

Kicking off their first European Championship campaign in Dortmund, a spirited Georgia side put Turkey to the test throughout 90 pulsating minutes and a frantic stoppage-time finale. Pulling them level after Mert Muldur’s superb strike had put the Crescent-Stars in front, Georges Mikautadze scored his country’s historic maiden goal at a major tournament, sending the Georgian fans into raptures.

Key Players:

Georges Mikautadze: Scored Georgia’s first-ever goal at a major tournament.
Khvicha Kvaratskhelia: The star man who will be key in attack.

Expected Lineup: Mamardashvili; Dvali, Kashia, Kverkvelia; Kakabadze, Kochorashvili, Mekvabishvili, Chakvetadze, Tsitaishvili; Kvaratskhelia, Mikautadze

Czech Republic

Despite scoring the opener via Lukas Provod’s 62nd-minute strike, Ivan Hasek’s side then let Portugal back into their first Group F fixture. An unfortunate own goal from Robin Hranac leveled matters in Leipzig, and with time running out, substitute Francisco Conceicao broke Czech hearts by bagging the Selecao’s winner.

Key Players:

Patrik Schick: Top goalscorer in the squad with 19 goals.
Tomas Soucek: Influential midfielder from West Ham United.

Expected Lineup: Stanek; Holes, Hranac, Krejci; Soucek, Coufal, Barak, Provod, Doudera; Chytil, Schick
