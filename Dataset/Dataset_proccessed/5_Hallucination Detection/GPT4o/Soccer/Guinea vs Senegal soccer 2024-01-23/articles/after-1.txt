Guinea vs Senegal LIVE!

The holders look in ominous form as their title defence continues to thrive at the Africa Cup of Nations. Already through to the last 16 after back-to-back opening wins, Senegal confirmed their spot as Group C winners on Tuesday night and maintained their 100 per cent record in defence of their first title from 2021 as they brushed aside their west African neighbours in a feisty derby clash in Yamoussoukro.

After a torrid first half low on quality that featured no shortage of flashpoints, centre-back Abdoulaye Seck headed home a Krepin Diatta free-kick to give the Lions of Teranga a deserved lead shortly past the hour mark, with Sadio Mane missing a huge chance and also seeing a goal chalked off for offside.

Mane turned provider for substitute Iliman Ndiaye to wrap up the victory on the cusp of stoppage time, with Guinea pipped to second spot in Group C courtesy of Cameroon's dramatic late win over The Gambia in Bouake. However, Kaba Diawara's side will still advance with four points as one of the four best third-place group finishers at the tournament. Follow Guinea vs Senegal reaction from AFCON live below!
