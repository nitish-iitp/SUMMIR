South Africa vs Bangladesh Highlights, T20 World Cup 2024

South Africa vs Bangladesh Highlights, T20 World Cup 2024: South Africa sealed a four-run win against Bangladesh in their T20 World Cup fixture, in New York on Monday. Chasing 114, Bangladesh reached 109/7 in 20 overs, with Keshav Maharaj taking three wickets, including two in the final over. Meanwhile, Anrich Nortje and Kagiso Rabada struck twice for the Proteas. For Bangladesh, Towhid Hridoy smacked 37 off 34 balls. Initially, Heinrich Klaasen (46) took South Africa to 113/6 in 20 overs. For Bangladesh's bowling department, Tanzim Hasan Sakib took three wickets.
