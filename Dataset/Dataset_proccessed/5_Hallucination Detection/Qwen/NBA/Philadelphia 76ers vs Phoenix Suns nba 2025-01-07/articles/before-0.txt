Suns - 76ers Series Notes
The Suns have won nine of the last 11 over the 76ers, including six-straight wins in Phoenix … the two losses over the 11-game stretch were the last two meetings in Philadelphia
The Suns' six game win streak over Philadelphia spanning 11/4/19-3/27/22, was the second-longest win streak in the PHX/PHI all-time regular season series by either team, trailing only a 10-game win streak by the Suns spanning March 13, 1992 to March 15, 1996
The Suns are 31-8 in their last 39 games against the Sixers in Phoenix
Suns - 76ers Key Connections
76ers F Kelly Oubre Jr. played in 96 career games (67 starts) for the Suns, averaging 17.9 points and 5.8 rebounds from 2018-20
76ers G Eric Gordon played for the Suns during the 2023-24 season, averaging 11.0 points in 68 games (24 starts)
76ers AC Bryan Gates was an assistant coach for the Suns from 2021-23
76ers AC Matt Brase is the grandson of the late UofA men's basketball coach Lute Olson…he attended Catalina Foothills High School and finished his college basketball career at the University of Arizona, where he was a member of the 2004-05 team that reached the Elite 8

Suns Injury Report

Notes
The Suns dropped to 15-18 on the season following a 126-108 loss to the Pacers on Saturday night in Indianapolis
With a win tonight, Mike Budenholzer would win his 500th career regular season game as an NBA head coach, becoming the 38th coach in league history to the milestone
Kevin Durant moved into sole possession of 2nd place for the most 20+ point games in Christmas Day games (11) with his 27 points on Dec. 25
Devin Booker (34 points, 8 assists) and Kevin Durant (30 points, 8 assists) became the first Suns duo since the 1991-92 season (Jeff Hornacek & Dan Majerle) to record 30+ points and 8+ assists in the same game on Dec. 13, 2024 at Utah
Royce O’Neale scored a career-high 23 points on Nov. 7 in Miami, knocking down 5-of-8 from three-point range
With his first made basket early in the first quarter on Dec. 3 against the Spurs, Devin Booker became the 2nd player in franchise history to score 15,000 career points in a Suns jersey (Walter Davis) and became the 9th-youngest player in NBA history to reach the milestone
The Suns held the Spurs to 35% shooting (35-of-100) on Dec. 3, moving to 68-0 all-time when holding their opponent to 35% shooting or worse
After Grayson Allen ranked 2nd in the NBA in spot up shooting points per game last season, Royce O’Neale ranks 6th in the league this season in spot up shooting points per game (6.2)
The Suns have made 470 three-pointers in the first 33 games of this season, the most in franchise history in the first 33 games of a season
With his 21st point of the night on Oct. 26 against the Mavericks, Kevin Durant became the eighth player in NBA history to reach 29,000 career points
Devin Booker needs 450 more points to surpass Walter Davis as the Suns all-time leading scorer
The Suns enter this season having reached the playoffs in each of the last four seasons, matching their longest such stretch (four-straight from 2005-2008) since doing so in 13-straight from 1989-2001
Since the start of the 2020-21 season, the Suns hold a combined regular season record 224-127, the second-best record in the NBA over the last five seasons (trails Boston)
Last Game Recap

INDIANAPOLIS – The Suns (15-18) fell to the Pacers (18-18) on Jan. 4, 126-108, as the Pacers swept the 2024-25 regular season series over Phoenix, 2-0. Despite Phoenix shooting 48.7% from the field to Indiana’s 47.9%, the Pacers would out-rebound the Suns 47-36 and have the advantage in second-chance points, 17-6, and fast-break points, 16-8. With the game tied at halftime (56-56),
the Pacers outscored the Suns 40-28 in the third quarter alone, building up a lead to as many as 14 in the third quarter. Kevin Durant finished with a team-high 25 points on 9-of-14 shooting (6- for-6 FT), while also adding 7 rebounds and 7 assists. The game marked Durant’s 16th game this season of scoring 25+ points and his fifth-straight. Devin Booker posted 20 points to go along with a game-high 9 assists and 8 rebounds, while Ryan Dunn added 13 points on 6-of-11 shooting from the field, 4 rebounds, an assist and a block. The game marked the sixth time Dunn has posted double-digit points this season. Returning to court after missing the last five games, Grayson Allen led all Suns reserves with 13 points on 5-of-10 shooting from the field, including a 3-of-7 mark from three-point range. Monte Morris would be the only other Suns player to reach double figure scoring, tallying 12 points.
